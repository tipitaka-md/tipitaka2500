# 6.3 Channattheravatthu

### Paṇḍitavagga

### Channattheravatthu

(78.)

84. _Na bhaje pāpake mitte,_  
_na bhaje purisādhame;_  
_Bhajetha mitte kalyāṇe,_  
_bhajetha purisuttame._

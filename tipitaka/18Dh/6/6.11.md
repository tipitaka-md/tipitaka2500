# 6.11 Pañcasataāgantukabhikkhuvatthu

### Paṇḍitavagga

### Pañcasataāgantukabhikkhuvatthu

(87.)

93. _Kaṇhaṃ dhammaṃ vippahāya,_  
_Sukkaṃ bhāvetha paṇḍito;_  
_Okā anokamāgamma,_  
_Viveke yattha dūramaṃ._  


(88.)

94. _Tatrābhiratimiccheyya,_  
_hitvā kāme akiñcano;_  
_Pariyodapeyya attānaṃ,_  
_cittaklesehi paṇḍito._  


(89.)

95. _Yesaṃ sambodhiyaṅgesu,_  
_sammā cittaṃ subhāvitaṃ;_  
_Ādānapaṭinissagge,_  
_anupādāya ye ratā;_  
_Khīṇāsavā jutimanto,_  
_te loke parinibbutā._  


===

96. Paṇḍitavaggo chaṭṭho.

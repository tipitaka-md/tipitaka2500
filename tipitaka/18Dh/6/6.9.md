# 6.9 Dhammikattheravatthu

### Paṇḍitavagga

### Dhammikattheravatthu

(84.)

90\. _Na attahetu na parassa hetu,_  
_Na puttamicche na dhanaṃ na raṭṭhaṃ;_  
_Na iccheyya adhammena samiddhimattano,_  
_Sa sīlavā paññavā dhammiko siyā._

# 6.8 Pañcasatabhikkhuvatthu

### Paṇḍitavagga

### Pañcasatabhikkhuvatthu

(83.)

89. _Sabbattha ve sappurisā cajanti,_  
_Na kāmakāmā lapayanti santo;_  
_Sukhena phuṭṭhā atha vā dukhena,_  
_Na uccāvacaṃ paṇḍitā dassayanti._

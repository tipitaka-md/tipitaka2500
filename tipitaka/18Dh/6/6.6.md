# 6.6 Lakuṇḍakabhaddiyattheravatthu

* Paṇḍitavagga

* Lakuṇḍakabhaddiyattheravatthu

(81.)

87\. _Selo yathā ekaghano,_  
_vātena na samīrati;_  
_Evaṃ nindāpasaṃsāsu,_  
_na samiñjanti paṇḍitā._

# 3.6 Pañcasatabhikkhuvatthu

### Cittavagga

### Pañcasatabhikkhuvatthu

(40.)

43\. _Kumbhūpamaṃ kāyamimaṃ viditvā,_  
_Nagarūpamaṃ cittamidaṃ ṭhapetvā;_  
_Yodhetha māraṃ paññāvudhena,_  
_Jitañca rakkhe anivesano siyā._

# 25.1 Pañcabhikkhuvatthu

* Bhikkhuvagga

* Pañcabhikkhuvatthu

(360.)

387\. _Cakkhunā saṃvaro sādhu,_  
_sādhu sotena saṃvaro;_  
_Ghānena saṃvaro sādhu,_  
_sādhu jivhāya saṃvaro._  


(361.)

388\. _Kāyena saṃvaro sādhu,_  
_sādhu vācāya saṃvaro;_  
_Manasā saṃvaro sādhu,_  
_sādhu sabbattha saṃvaro;_  
_Sabbattha saṃvuto bhikkhu,_  
_sabbadukkhā pamuccati._

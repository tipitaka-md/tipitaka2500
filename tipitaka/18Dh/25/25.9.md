# 25.9 Santakāyattheravatthu

### Bhikkhuvagga

### Santakāyattheravatthu

(378.)

405. _Santakāyo santavāco,_  
_santavā susamāhito;_  
_Vantalokāmiso bhikkhu,_  
_“upasanto”ti vuccati._

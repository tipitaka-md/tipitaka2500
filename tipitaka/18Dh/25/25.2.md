# 25.2 Haṃsaghātakabhikkhuvatthu

### Bhikkhuvagga

### Haṃsaghātakabhikkhuvatthu

(362.)

389. _Hatthasaṃyato pādasaṃyato,_  
_Vācāsaṃyato saṃyatuttamo;_  
_Ajjhattarato samāhito,_  
_Eko santusito tamāhu bhikkhuṃ._

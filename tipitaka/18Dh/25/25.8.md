# 25.8 Pañcasatabhikkhuvatthu

### Bhikkhuvagga

### Pañcasatabhikkhuvatthu

(377.)

404. _Vassikā viya pupphāni,_  
_maddavāni pamuñcati;_  
_Evaṃ rāgañca dosañca,_  
_vippamuñcetha bhikkhavo._

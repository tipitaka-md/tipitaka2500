# 25.11 Vakkalittheravatthu

### Bhikkhuvagga

### Vakkalittheravatthu

(381.)

408. _Pāmojjabahulo bhikkhu,_  
_pasanno buddhasāsane;_  
_Adhigacche padaṃ santaṃ,_  
_saṅkhārūpasamaṃ sukhaṃ._

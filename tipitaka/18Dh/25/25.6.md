# 25.6 Pañcaggadāyakabrāhmaṇavatthu

* Bhikkhuvagga

* Pañcaggadāyakabrāhmaṇavatthu

(367.)

394\. _Sabbaso nāmarūpasmiṃ,_  
_yassa natthi mamāyitaṃ;_  
_Asatā ca na socati,_  
_sa ve “bhikkhū”ti vuccati._

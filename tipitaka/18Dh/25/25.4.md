# 25.4 Dhammārāmattheravatthu

### Bhikkhuvagga

### Dhammārāmattheravatthu

(364.)

391. _Dhammārāmo dhammarato,_  
_dhammaṃ anuvicintayaṃ;_  
_Dhammaṃ anussaraṃ bhikkhu,_  
_saddhammā na parihāyati._

# 25.12 Sumanasāmaṇeravatthu

* Bhikkhuvagga

* Sumanasāmaṇeravatthu

(382.)

409\. _Yo have daharo bhikkhu,_  
_yuñjati buddhasāsane;_  
_Somaṃ lokaṃ pabhāseti,_  
_abbhā muttova candimā._  


---

410\. Bhikkhuvaggo pañcavīsatimo.

# 25.3 Kokālikavatthu

* Bhikkhuvagga

* Kokālikavatthu

(363.)

390\. _Yo mukhasaṃyato bhikkhu,_  
_mantabhāṇī anuddhato;_  
_Atthaṃ dhammañca dīpeti,_  
_madhuraṃ tassa bhāsitaṃ._

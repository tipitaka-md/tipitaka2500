# 16.2 Aññatarakuṭumbikavatthu

### Piyavagga

### Aññatarakuṭumbikavatthu

(212.)

230. _Piyato jāyatī soko,_  
_piyato jāyatī bhayaṃ;_  
_Piyato vippamuttassa,_  
_natthi soko kuto bhayaṃ._

# 16.4 Licchavīvatthu

* Piyavagga

* Licchavīvatthu

(214.)

232\. _Ratiyā jāyatī soko,_  
_ratiyā jāyatī bhayaṃ;_  
_Ratiyā vippamuttassa,_  
_natthi soko kuto bhayaṃ._

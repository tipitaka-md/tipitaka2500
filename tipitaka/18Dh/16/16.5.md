# 16.5 Anitthigandhakumāravatthu

* Piyavagga

* Anitthigandhakumāravatthu

(215.)

233\. _Kāmato jāyatī soko,_  
_kāmato jāyatī bhayaṃ;_  
_Kāmato vippamuttassa,_  
_natthi soko kuto bhayaṃ._

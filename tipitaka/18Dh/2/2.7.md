# 2.7 Maghavatthu

* Appamādavagga

* Maghavatthu

(30.)

32\. _Appamādena maghavā,_  
_devānaṃ seṭṭhataṃ gato;_  
_Appamādaṃ pasaṃsanti,_  
_pamādo garahito sadā._

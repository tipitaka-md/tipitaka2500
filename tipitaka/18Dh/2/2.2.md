# 2.2 Kumbaghosakaseṭṭhivatthu

### Appamādavagga

### Kumbaghosakaseṭṭhivatthu

(24.)

26\. _Uṭṭhānavato satīmato,_  
_Sucikammassa nisammakārino;_  
_Saññatassa dhammajīvino,_  
_Appamattassa yasobhivaḍḍhati._

# 2.9 Nigamavāsitissattheravatthu

### Appamādavagga

### Nigamavāsitissattheravatthu

(32.)

34. _Appamādarato bhikkhu,_  
_pamāde bhayadassi vā;_  
_Abhabbo parihānāya,_  
_nibbānasseva santike._  


---

35. Appamādavaggo dutiyo.

# 2.8 Aññatarabhikkhuvatthu

### Appamādavagga

### Aññatarabhikkhuvatthu

(31.)

33. _Appamādarato bhikkhu,_  
_pamāde bhayadassi vā;_  
_Saṃyojanaṃ aṇuṃ thūlaṃ,_  
_ḍahaṃ aggīva gacchati._

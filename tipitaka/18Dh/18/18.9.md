# 18.9 Pañcaupāsakavatthu

* Malavagga

* Pañcaupāsakavatthu

(251.)

271\. _Natthi rāgasamo aggi,_  
_natthi dosasamo gaho;_  
_Natthi mohasamaṃ jālaṃ,_  
_natthi taṇhāsamā nadī._

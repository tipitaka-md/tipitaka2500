# 18.11 Ujjhānasaññittheravatthu

### Malavagga

### Ujjhānasaññittheravatthu

(253.)

273. _Paravajjānupassissa,_  
_niccaṃ ujjhānasaññino;_  
_Āsavā tassa vaḍḍhanti,_  
_ārā so āsavakkhayā._

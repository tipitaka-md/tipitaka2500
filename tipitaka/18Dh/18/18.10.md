# 18.10 Meṇḍakaseṭṭhivatthu

### Malavagga

### Meṇḍakaseṭṭhivatthu

(252.)

272. _Sudassaṃ vajjamaññesaṃ,_  
_attano pana duddasaṃ;_  
_Paresaṃ hi so vajjāni,_  
_opunāti yathā bhusaṃ;_  
_Attano pana chādeti,_  
_kaliṃva kitavā saṭho._

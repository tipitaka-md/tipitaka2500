# 21.2 Kukkuṭaṇḍakhādikāvatthu

* Pakiṇṇakavagga

* Kukkuṭaṇḍakhādikāvatthu

(291.)

314\. _Paradukkhūpadhānena,_  
_attano sukhamicchati;_  
_Verasaṃsaggasaṃsaṭṭho,_  
_verā so na parimuccati._

# 21.9 Ekavihārittheravatthu

### Pakiṇṇakavagga

### Ekavihārittheravatthu

(305.)

328. _Ekāsanaṃ ekaseyyaṃ,_  
_eko caramatandito;_  
_Eko damayamattānaṃ,_  
_vanante ramito siyā._  


===

329. Pakiṇṇakavaggo ekavīsatimo.

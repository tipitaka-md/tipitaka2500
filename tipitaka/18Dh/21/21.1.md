# 21.1 Attanopubbakammavatthu

### Pakiṇṇakavagga

### Attanopubbakammavatthu

(290.)

313. _Mattāsukhapariccāgā,_  
_passe ce vipulaṃ sukhaṃ;_  
_Caje mattāsukhaṃ dhīro,_  
_sampassaṃ vipulaṃ sukhaṃ._

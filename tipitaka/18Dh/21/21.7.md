# 21.7 Cittagahapativatthu

* Pakiṇṇakavagga

* Cittagahapativatthu

(303.)

326\. _Saddho sīlena sampanno,_  
_yasobhogasamappito;_  
_Yaṃ yaṃ padesaṃ bhajati,_  
_tattha tattheva pūjito._

# 21.8 Cūḷasubhaddāvatthu

* Pakiṇṇakavagga

* Cūḷasubhaddāvatthu

(304.)

327\. _Dūre santo pakāsenti,_  
_himavantova pabbato;_  
_Asantettha na dissanti,_  
_rattiṃ khittā yathā sarā._

# 15.7 Tissattheravatthu

* Sukhavagga

* Tissattheravatthu

(205.)

221\. _Pavivekarasaṃ pitvā,_  
_rasaṃ upasamassa ca;_  
_Niddaro hoti nippāpo,_  
_dhammapītirasaṃ pivaṃ._

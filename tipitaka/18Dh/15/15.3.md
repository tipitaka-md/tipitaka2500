# 15.3 Kosalaraññoparājayavatthu

* Sukhavagga

* Kosalaraññoparājayavatthu

(201.)

217\. _Jayaṃ veraṃ pasavati,_  
_dukkhaṃ seti parājito;_  
_Upasanto sukhaṃ seti,_  
_hitvā jayaparājayaṃ._

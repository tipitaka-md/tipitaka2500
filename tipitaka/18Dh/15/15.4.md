# 15.4 Aññatarakuladārikāvatthu

* Sukhavagga

* Aññatarakuladārikāvatthu

(202.)

218\. _Natthi rāgasamo aggi,_  
_Natthi dosasamo kali;_  
_Natthi khandhasamā dukkhā,_  
_Natthi santiparaṃ sukhaṃ._

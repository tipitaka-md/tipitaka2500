# 15.6 Pasenadikosalavatthu

* Sukhavagga

* Pasenadikosalavatthu

(204.)

220\. _Ārogyaparamā lābhā,_  
_Santuṭṭhiparamaṃ dhanaṃ;_  
_Vissāsaparamā ñāti,_  
_Nibbānaṃ paramaṃ sukhaṃ._

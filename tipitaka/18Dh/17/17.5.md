# 17.5 Buddhapitubrāhmaṇavatthu

* Kodhavagga

* Buddhapitubrāhmaṇavatthu

(225.)

244\. _Ahiṃsakā ye munayo,_  
_niccaṃ kāyena saṃvutā;_  
_Te yanti accutaṃ ṭhānaṃ,_  
_yattha gantvā na socare._

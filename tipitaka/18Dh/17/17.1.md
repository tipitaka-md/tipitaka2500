# 17.1 Rohinīkhattiyakaññāvatthu

### Kodhavagga

### Rohinīkhattiyakaññāvatthu

(221.)

240. _Kodhaṃ jahe vippajaheyya mānaṃ,_  
_Saṃyojanaṃ sabbamatikkameyya;_  
_Taṃ nāmarūpasmimasajjamānaṃ,_  
_Akiñcanaṃ nānupatanti dukkhā._

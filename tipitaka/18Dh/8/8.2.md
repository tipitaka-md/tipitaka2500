# 8.2 Bāhiyadārucīriyattheravatthu

* Sahassavagga

* Bāhiyadārucīriyattheravatthu

(101.)

109\. _Sahassamapi ce gāthā,_  
_anatthapadasaṃhitā;_  
_Ekaṃ gāthāpadaṃ seyyo,_  
_yaṃ sutvā upasammati._

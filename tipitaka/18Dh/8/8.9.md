# 8.9 Saṅkiccasāmaṇeravatthu

### Sahassavagga

### Saṅkiccasāmaṇeravatthu

(110.)

118. _Yo ca vassasataṃ jīve,_  
_dussīlo asamāhito;_  
_Ekāhaṃ jīvitaṃ seyyo,_  
_sīlavantassa jhāyino._

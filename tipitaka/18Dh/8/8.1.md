# 8.1 Tambadāṭhikacoraghātakavatthu

* Sahassavagga

* Tambadāṭhikacoraghātakavatthu

(100.)

108\. _Sahassamapi ce vācā,_  
_anatthapadasaṃhitā;_  
_Ekaṃ atthapadaṃ seyyo,_  
_yaṃ sutvā upasammati._

# 8.8 Āyuvaḍḍhanakumāravatthu

### Sahassavagga

### Āyuvaḍḍhanakumāravatthu

(109.)

117. _Abhivādanasīlissa,_  
_niccaṃ vuḍḍhāpacāyino;_  
_Cattāro dhammā vaḍḍhanti,_  
_āyu vaṇṇo sukhaṃ balaṃ._

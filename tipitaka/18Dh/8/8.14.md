# 8.14 Bahuputtikattherīvatthu

* Sahassavagga

* Bahuputtikattherīvatthu

(115.)

123\. _Yo ca vassasataṃ jīve,_  
_apassaṃ dhammamuttamaṃ;_  
_Ekāhaṃ jīvitaṃ seyyo,_  
_passato dhammamuttamaṃ._  


---

124\. Sahassavaggo aṭṭhamo.

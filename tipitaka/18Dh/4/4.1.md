# 4.1 Pathavikathāpasutapañcasatabhikkhuvatthu

* Pupphavagga

* Pathavikathāpasutapañcasatabhikkhuvatthu

(44.)

48\. _Ko imaṃ pathaviṃ vicessati,_  
_Yamalokañca imaṃ sadevakaṃ;_  
_Ko dhammapadaṃ sudesitaṃ,_  
_Kusalo pupphamiva pacessati._  


(45.)

49\. _Sekho pathaviṃ vicessati,_  
_Yamalokañca imaṃ sadevakaṃ;_  
_Sekho dhammapadaṃ sudesitaṃ,_  
_Kusalo pupphamiva pacessati._

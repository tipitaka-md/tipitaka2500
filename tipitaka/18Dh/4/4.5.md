# 4.5 Macchariyakosiyaseṭṭhivatthu

* Pupphavagga

* Macchariyakosiyaseṭṭhivatthu

(49.)

53\. _Yathāpi bhamaro pupphaṃ,_  
_vaṇṇagandhamaheṭhayaṃ;_  
_Paleti rasamādāya,_  
_evaṃ gāme munī care._

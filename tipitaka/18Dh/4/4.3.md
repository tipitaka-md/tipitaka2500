# 4.3 Viṭaṭūbhavatthu

### Pupphavagga

### Viṭaṭūbhavatthu

(47.)

51. _Pupphāni heva pacinantaṃ,_  
_byāsattamanasaṃ naraṃ;_  
_Suttaṃ gāmaṃ mahoghova,_  
_maccu ādāya gacchati._

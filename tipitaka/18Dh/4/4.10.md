# 4.10 Mahākassapattherapiṇḍapātadinnavatthu

### Pupphavagga

### Mahākassapattherapiṇḍapātadinnavatthu

(56.)

60. _Appamatto ayaṃ gandho,_  
_yāyaṃ tagaracandanī;_  
_Yo ca sīlavataṃ gandho,_  
_vāti devesu uttamo._

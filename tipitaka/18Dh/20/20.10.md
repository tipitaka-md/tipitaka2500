# 20.10 Mahādhanavāṇijavatthu

### Maggavagga

### Mahādhanavāṇijavatthu

(286.)

308\. _Idha vassaṃ vasissāmi,_  
_idha hemantagimhisu;_  
_Iti bālo vicinteti,_  
_antarāyaṃ na bujjhati._

# 20.8 Pañcamahallakabhikkhuvatthu

### Maggavagga

### Pañcamahallakabhikkhuvatthu

(283.)

305. _Vanaṃ chindatha mā rukkhaṃ,_  
_vanato jāyate bhayaṃ;_  
_Chetvā vanañca vanathañca,_  
_nibbanā hotha bhikkhavo._  


(284.)

306. _Yāva hi vanatho na chijjati,_  
_Aṇumattopi narassa nārisu;_  
_Paṭibaddhamanova tāva so,_  
_Vaccho khīrapakova mātari._

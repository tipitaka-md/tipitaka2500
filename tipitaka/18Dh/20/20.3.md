# 20.3 Dukkhalakkhaṇavatthu

### Maggavagga

### Dukkhalakkhaṇavatthu

(278.)

300. _“Sabbe saṅkhārā dukkhā”ti,_  
_yadā paññāya passati;_  
_Atha nibbindati dukkhe,_  
_esa maggo visuddhiyā._

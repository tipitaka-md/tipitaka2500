# 20.9 Suvaṇṇakārattheravatthu

### Maggavagga

### Suvaṇṇakārattheravatthu

(285.)

307. _Ucchinda sinehamattano,_  
_Kumudaṃ sāradikaṃva pāṇinā;_  
_Santimaggameva brūhaya,_  
_Nibbānaṃ sugatena desitaṃ._

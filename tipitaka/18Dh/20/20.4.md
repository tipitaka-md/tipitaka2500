# 20.4 Anattalakkhaṇavatthu

### Maggavagga

### Anattalakkhaṇavatthu

(279.)

301. _“Sabbe dhammā anattā”ti,_  
_yadā paññāya passati;_  
_Atha nibbindati dukkhe,_  
_esa maggo visuddhiyā._

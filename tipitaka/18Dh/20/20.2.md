# 20.2 Aniccalakkhaṇavatthu

* Maggavagga

* Aniccalakkhaṇavatthu

(277.)

299\. _“Sabbe saṅkhārā aniccā”ti,_  
_yadā paññāya passati;_  
_Atha nibbindati dukkhe,_  
_esa maggo visuddhiyā._

# 14.8 Sambahulabhikkhuvatthu

### Buddhavagga

### Sambahulabhikkhuvatthu

(194.)

208. _Sukho buddhānamuppādo,_  
_sukhā saddhammadesanā;_  
_Sukhā saṃghassa sāmaggī,_  
_samaggānaṃ tapo sukho._

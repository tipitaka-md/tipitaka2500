# 14.3 Erakapattanāgarājavatthu

* Buddhavagga

* Erakapattanāgarājavatthu

(182.)

196\. _Kiccho manussapaṭilābho,_  
_kicchaṃ maccāna jīvitaṃ;_  
_Kicchaṃ saddhammassavanaṃ,_  
_kiccho buddhānamuppādo._

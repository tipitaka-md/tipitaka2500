# 12.1 Bodhirājakumāravatthu

* Attavagga

* Bodhirājakumāravatthu

(157.)

169\. _Attānañce piyaṃ jaññā,_  
_rakkheyya naṃ surakkhitaṃ;_  
_Tiṇṇaṃ aññataraṃ yāmaṃ,_  
_paṭijaggeyya paṇḍito._

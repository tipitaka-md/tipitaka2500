# 12.6 Devadattavatthu

### Attavagga

### Devadattavatthu

(162.)

174\. _Yassa accantadussilyaṃ,_  
_māluvā sālamivotthataṃ;_  
_Karoti so tathattānaṃ,_  
_yathā naṃ icchatī diso._

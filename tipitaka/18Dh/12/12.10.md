# 12.10 Attadatthattheravatthu

### Attavagga

### Attadatthattheravatthu

(166.)

178. _Attadatthaṃ paratthena,_  
_bahunāpi na hāpaye;_  
_Attadatthamabhiññāya,_  
_sadatthapasuto siyā._  


===

179. Attavaggo dvādasamo.

# 12.8 Kālattheravatthu

### Attavagga

### Kālattheravatthu

(164.)

176. _Yo sāsanaṃ arahataṃ,_  
_ariyānaṃ dhammajīvinaṃ;_  
_Paṭikkosati dummedho,_  
_diṭṭhiṃ nissāya pāpikaṃ;_  
_Phalāni kaṭṭhakasseva,_  
_attaghātāya phallati._

# 13.8 Tiṃsabhikkhuvatthu

* Lokavagga

* Tiṃsabhikkhuvatthu

(175.)

188\. _Haṃsādiccapathe yanti,_  
_ākāse yanti iddhiyā;_  
_Nīyanti dhīrā lokamhā,_  
_jetvā māraṃ savāhiniṃ._

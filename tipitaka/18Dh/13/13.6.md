# 13.6 Aṅgulimālattheravatthu

### Lokavagga

### Aṅgulimālattheravatthu

(173.)

186. _Yassa pāpaṃ kataṃ kammaṃ,_  
_kusalena pidhīyati;_  
_Somaṃ lokaṃ pabhāseti,_  
_abbhā muttova candimā._

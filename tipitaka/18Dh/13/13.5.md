# 13.5 Sammajjanattheravatthu

### Lokavagga

### Sammajjanattheravatthu

(172.)

185. _Yo ca pubbe pamajjitvā,_  
_pacchā so nappamajjati;_  
_Somaṃ lokaṃ pabhāseti,_  
_abbhā muttova candimā._

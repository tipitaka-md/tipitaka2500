# 13.1 Daharabhikkhuvatthu

* Lokavagga

* Daharabhikkhuvatthu

(167.)

180\. _Hīnaṃ dhammaṃ na seveyya,_  
_pamādena na saṃvase;_  
_Micchādiṭṭhiṃ na seveyya,_  
_na siyā lokavaḍḍhano._

# 13.3 Pañcasatavipassakabhikkhuvatthu

* Lokavagga

* Pañcasatavipassakabhikkhuvatthu

(170.)

183\. _Yathā pubbuḷakaṃ passe,_  
_yathā passe marīcikaṃ;_  
_Evaṃ lokaṃ avekkhantaṃ,_  
_maccurājā na passati._

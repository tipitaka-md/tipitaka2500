# 7.4 Anuruddhattheravatthu

### Arahantavagga

### Anuruddhattheravatthu

(93.)

100. _Yassāsavā parikkhīṇā,_  
_āhāre ca anissito;_  
_Suññato animitto ca,_  
_vimokkho yassa gocaro;_  
_Ākāseva sakuntānaṃ,_  
_padaṃ tassa durannayaṃ._

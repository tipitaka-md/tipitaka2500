# 7.2 Mahākassapattheravatthu

* Arahantavagga

* Mahākassapattheravatthu

(91.)

98\. _Uyyuñjanti satīmanto,_  
_na nikete ramanti te;_  
_Haṃsāva pallalaṃ hitvā,_  
_okamokaṃ jahanti te._

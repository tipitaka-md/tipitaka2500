# 7.8 Sāriputtattheravatthu

* Arahantavagga

* Sāriputtattheravatthu

(97.)

104\. _Assaddho akataññū ca,_  
_sandhicchedo ca yo naro;_  
_Hatāvakāso vantāso,_  
_sa ve uttamaporiso._

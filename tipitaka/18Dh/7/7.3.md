# 7.3 Belaṭṭhasīsattheravatthu

### Arahantavagga

### Belaṭṭhasīsattheravatthu

(92.)

99. _Yesaṃ sannicayo natthi,_  
_ye pariññātabhojanā;_  
_Suññato animitto ca,_  
_vimokkho yesaṃ gocaro;_  
_Ākāseva sakuntānaṃ,_  
_gati tesaṃ durannayā._

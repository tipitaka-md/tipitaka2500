# 7.7 Kosambivāsitissattherasāmaṇeravatthu

### Arahantavagga

### Kosambivāsitissattherasāmaṇeravatthu

(96.)

103. _Santaṃ tassa manaṃ hoti,_  
_santā vācā ca kamma ca;_  
_Sammadaññāvimuttassa,_  
_upasantassa tādino._

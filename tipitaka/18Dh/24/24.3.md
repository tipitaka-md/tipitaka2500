# 24.3 Vibbhantabhikkhuvatthu

* Taṇhāvagga

* Vibbhantabhikkhuvatthu

(344.)

370\. _Yo nibbanatho vanādhimutto,_  
_Vanamutto vanameva dhāvati;_  
_Taṃ puggalametha passatha,_  
_Mutto bandhanameva dhāvati._

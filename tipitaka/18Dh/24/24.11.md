# 24.11 Aputtakaseṭṭhivatthu

### Taṇhāvagga

### Aputtakaseṭṭhivatthu

(355.)

381. _Hananti bhogā dummedhaṃ,_  
_no ca pāragavesino;_  
_Bhogataṇhāya dummedho,_  
_hanti aññeva attanaṃ._

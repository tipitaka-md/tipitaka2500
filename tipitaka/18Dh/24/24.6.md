# 24.6 Uggasenavatthu

* Taṇhāvagga

* Uggasenavatthu

(348.)

374\. _Muñca pure muñca pacchato,_  
_Majjhe muñca bhavassa pāragū;_  
_Sabbattha vimuttamānaso,_  
_Na punaṃ jātijaraṃ upehisi._

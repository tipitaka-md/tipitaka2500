# 22.8 Nigaṇṭhavatthu

* Nirayavagga

* Nigaṇṭhavatthu

(316.)

340\. _Alajjitāye lajjanti,_  
_lajjitāye na lajjare;_  
_Micchādiṭṭhisamādānā,_  
_sattā gacchanti duggatiṃ._  


(317.)

341\. _Abhaye bhayadassino,_  
_bhaye cābhayadassino;_  
_Micchādiṭṭhisamādānā,_  
_sattā gacchanti duggatiṃ._

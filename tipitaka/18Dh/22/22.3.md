# 22.3 Vaggumudātīriyabhikkhuvatthu

### Nirayavagga

### Vaggumudātīriyabhikkhuvatthu

(308.)

332. _Seyyo ayoguḷo bhutto,_  
_tatto aggisikhūpamo;_  
_Yañce bhuñjeyya dussīlo,_  
_raṭṭhapiṇḍamasaññato._

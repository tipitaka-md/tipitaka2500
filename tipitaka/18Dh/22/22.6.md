# 22.6 Issāpakataitthivatthu

* Nirayavagga

* Issāpakataitthivatthu

(314.)

338\. _Akataṃ dukkaṭaṃ seyyo,_  
_pacchā tappati dukkaṭaṃ;_  
_Katañca sukataṃ seyyo,_  
_yaṃ katvā nānutappati._

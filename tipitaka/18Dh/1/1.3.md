# 1.3 Tissattheravatthu

### Yamakavagga

### Tissattheravatthu

(3.)

4. _Akkocchi maṃ avadhi maṃ,_  
_ajini maṃ ahāsi me;_  
_Ye ca taṃ upanayhanti,_  
_veraṃ tesaṃ na sammati._  


(4.)

5. _Akkocchi maṃ avadhi maṃ,_  
_ajini maṃ ahāsi me;_  
_Ye ca taṃ nupanayhanti,_  
_veraṃ tesūpasammati._

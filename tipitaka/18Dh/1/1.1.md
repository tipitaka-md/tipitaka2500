# 1.1 Cakkhupālattheravatthu

* Yamakavagga

* Cakkhupālattheravatthu

(1.)

2\. _Manopubbaṅgamā dhammā,_  
_manoseṭṭhā manomayā;_  
_Manasā ce paduṭṭhena,_  
_bhāsati vā karoti vā;_  
_Tato naṃ dukkhamanveti,_  
_cakkaṃva vahato padaṃ._

# 1.10 Cundasūkarikavatthu

### Yamakavagga

### Cundasūkarikavatthu

(15.)

16. _Idha socati pecca socati,_  
_Pāpakārī ubhayattha socati;_  
_So socati so vihaññati,_  
_Disvā kammakiliṭṭhamattano._

# 1.9 Nandattheravatthu

* Yamakavagga

* Nandattheravatthu

(13.)

14\. _Yathā agāraṃ ducchannaṃ,_  
_vuṭṭhī samativijjhati;_  
_Evaṃ abhāvitaṃ cittaṃ,_  
_rāgo samativijjhati._  


(14.)

15\. _Yathā agāraṃ suchannaṃ,_  
_vuṭṭhī na samativijjhati;_  
_Evaṃ subhāvitaṃ cittaṃ,_  
_rāgo na samativijjhati._

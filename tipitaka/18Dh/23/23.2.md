# 23.2 Hatthācariyapubbakabhikkhuvatthu

### Nāgavagga

### Hatthācariyapubbakabhikkhuvatthu

(323.)

348\. _Na hi etehi yānehi,_  
_gaccheyya agataṃ disaṃ;_  
_Yathāttanā sudantena,_  
_danto dantena gacchati._

# 23.6 Pāveyyakahatthivatthu

### Nāgavagga

### Pāveyyakahatthivatthu

(327.)

352. _Appamādaratā hotha,_  
_sacittamanurakkhatha;_  
_Duggā uddharathattānaṃ,_  
_paṅke sannova kuñjaro._

# 23.3 Parijiṇṇabrāhmaṇaputtavatthu

### Nāgavagga

### Parijiṇṇabrāhmaṇaputtavatthu

(324.)

349. _Dhanapālo nāma kuñjaro,_  
_Kaṭukabhedano dunnivārayo;_  
_Baddho kabaḷaṃ na bhuñjati,_  
_Sumarati nāgavanassa kuñjaro._

# 10.11 Sukhasāmaṇeravatthu

### Daṇḍavagga

### Sukhasāmaṇeravatthu

(145.)

155. _Udakañhi nayanti nettikā,_  
_Usukārā namayanti tejanaṃ;_  
_Dāruṃ namayanti tacchakā,_  
_Attānaṃ damayanti subbatā._  


===

156. Daṇḍavaggo dasamo.

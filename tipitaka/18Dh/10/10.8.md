# 10.8 Bahubhaṇḍikabhikkhuvatthu

### Daṇḍavagga

### Bahubhaṇḍikabhikkhuvatthu

(141.)

151. _Na naggacariyā na jaṭā na paṅkā,_  
_Nānāsakā thaṇḍilasāyikā vā;_  
_Rajojallaṃ ukkuṭikappadhānaṃ,_  
_Sodhenti maccaṃ avitiṇṇakaṅkhaṃ._

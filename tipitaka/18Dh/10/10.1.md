# 10.1 Chabbaggiyabhikkhuvatthu

### Daṇḍavagga

### Chabbaggiyabhikkhuvatthu

(129.)

139. _Sabbe tasanti daṇḍassa,_  
_sabbe bhāyanti maccuno;_  
_Attānaṃ upamaṃ katvā,_  
_na haneyya na ghātaye._

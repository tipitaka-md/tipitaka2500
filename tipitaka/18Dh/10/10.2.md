# 10.2 Chabbaggiyavatthu

### Daṇḍavagga

### Chabbaggiyavatthu

(130.)

140. _Sabbe tasanti daṇḍassa,_  
_sabbesaṃ jīvitaṃ piyaṃ;_  
_Attānaṃ upamaṃ katvā,_  
_na haneyya na ghātaye._

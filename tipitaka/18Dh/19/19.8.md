# 19.8 Titthiyavatthu

* Dhammaṭṭhavagga

* Titthiyavatthu

(268.)

289\. _Na monena munī hoti,_  
_mūḷharūpo aviddasu;_  
_Yo ca tulaṃva paggayha,_  
_varamādāya paṇḍito._  


(269.)

290\. _Pāpāni parivajjeti,_  
_sa munī tena so muni;_  
_Yo munāti ubho loke,_  
_“muni” tena pavuccati._

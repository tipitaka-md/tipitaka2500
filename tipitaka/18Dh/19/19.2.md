# 19.2 Chabbaggiyavatthu

### Dhammaṭṭhavagga

### Chabbaggiyavatthu

(258.)

279. _Na tena paṇḍito hoti,_  
_yāvatā bahu bhāsati;_  
_Khemī averī abhayo,_  
_“paṇḍito”ti pavuccati._

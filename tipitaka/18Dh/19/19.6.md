# 19.6 Hatthakavatthu

* Dhammaṭṭhavagga

* Hatthakavatthu

(264.)

285\. _Na muṇḍakena samaṇo,_  
_abbato alikaṃ bhaṇaṃ;_  
_Icchālobhasamāpanno,_  
_samaṇo kiṃ bhavissati._  


(265.)

286\. _Yo ca sameti pāpāni,_  
_aṇuṃthūlāni sabbaso;_  
_Samitattā hi pāpānaṃ,_  
_“samaṇo”ti pavuccati._

# 19.9 Bālisikavatthu

### Dhammaṭṭhavagga

### Bālisikavatthu

(270.)

291. _Na tena ariyo hoti,_  
_yena pāṇāni hiṃsati;_  
_Ahiṃsā sabbapāṇānaṃ,_  
_“ariyo”ti pavuccati._

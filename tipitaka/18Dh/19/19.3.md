# 19.3 Ekudānakhīṇāsavavatthu

### Dhammaṭṭhavagga

### Ekudānakhīṇāsavavatthu

(259.)

280. _Na tāvatā dhammadharo,_  
_yāvatā bahu bhāsati;_  
_Yo ca appampi sutvāna,_  
_dhammaṃ kāyena passati;_  
_Sa ve dhammadharo hoti,_  
_yo dhammaṃ nappamajjati._

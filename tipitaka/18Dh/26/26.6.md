# 26.6 Aññatarabrāhmaṇapabbajitavatthu

### Brāhmaṇavagga

### Aññatarabrāhmaṇapabbajitavatthu

(388.)

416. _Bāhitapāpoti brāhmaṇo,_  
_Samacariyā samaṇoti vuccati;_  
_Pabbājayamattano malaṃ,_  
_Tasmā “pabbajito”ti vuccati._

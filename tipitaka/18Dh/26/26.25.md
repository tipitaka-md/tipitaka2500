# 26.25 Pilindavacchattheravatthu

* Brāhmaṇavagga

* Pilindavacchattheravatthu

(408.)

436\. _Akakkasaṃ viññāpaniṃ,_  
_giraṃ saccamudīraye;_  
_Yāya nābhisaje kañci,_  
_tamahaṃ brūmi brāhmaṇaṃ._

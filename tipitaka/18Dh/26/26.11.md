# 26.11 Kuhakabrāhmaṇavatthu

### Brāhmaṇavagga

### Kuhakabrāhmaṇavatthu

(394.)

422. _Kiṃ te jaṭāhi dummedha,_  
_kiṃ te ajinasāṭiyā;_  
_Abbhantaraṃ te gahanaṃ,_  
_bāhiraṃ parimajjasi._

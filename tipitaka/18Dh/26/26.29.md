# 26.29 Revatattheravatthu

### Brāhmaṇavagga

### Revatattheravatthu

(412.)

440. _Yodha puññañca pāpañca,_  
_ubho saṅgamupaccagā;_  
_Asokaṃ virajaṃ suddhaṃ,_  
_tamahaṃ brūmi brāhmaṇaṃ._

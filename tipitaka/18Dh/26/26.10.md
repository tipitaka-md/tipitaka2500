# 26.10 Jaṭilabrāhmaṇavatthu

### Brāhmaṇavagga

### Jaṭilabrāhmaṇavatthu

(393.)

421. _Na jaṭāhi na gottena,_  
_na jaccā hoti brāhmaṇo;_  
_Yamhi saccañca dhammo ca,_  
_so sucī so ca brāhmaṇo._

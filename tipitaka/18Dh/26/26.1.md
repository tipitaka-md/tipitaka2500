# 26.1 Pasādabahulabrāhmaṇavatthu

### Brāhmaṇavagga

### Pasādabahulabrāhmaṇavatthu

(383.)

411. _Chinda sotaṃ parakkamma,_  
_kāme panuda brāhmaṇa;_  
_Saṅkhārānaṃ khayaṃ ñatvā,_  
_akataññūsi brāhmaṇa._

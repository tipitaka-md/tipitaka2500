# 26.39 Aṅgulimālattheravatthu

### Brāhmaṇavagga

### Aṅgulimālattheravatthu

(423.)

451. _Usabhaṃ pavaraṃ vīraṃ,_  
_mahesiṃ vijitāvinaṃ;_  
_Anejaṃ nhātakaṃ buddhaṃ,_  
_tamahaṃ brūmi brāhmaṇaṃ._

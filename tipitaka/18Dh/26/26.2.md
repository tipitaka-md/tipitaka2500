# 26.2 Sambahulabhikkhuvatthu

### Brāhmaṇavagga

### Sambahulabhikkhuvatthu

(384.)

412. _Yadā dvayesu dhammesu,_  
_pāragū hoti brāhmaṇo;_  
_Athassa sabbe saṃyogā,_  
_atthaṃ gacchanti jānato._

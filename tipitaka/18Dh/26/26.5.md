# 26.5 Ānandattheravatthu

### Brāhmaṇavagga

### Ānandattheravatthu

(387.)

415. _Divā tapati ādicco,_  
_rattimābhāti candimā;_  
_Sannaddho khattiyo tapati,_  
_jhāyī tapati brāhmaṇo;_  
_Atha sabbamahorattiṃ,_  
_buddho tapati tejasā._

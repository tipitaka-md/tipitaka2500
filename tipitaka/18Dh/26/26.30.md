# 26.30 Candābhattheravatthu

* Brāhmaṇavagga

* Candābhattheravatthu

(413.)

441\. _Candaṃva vimalaṃ suddhaṃ,_  
_vippasannamanāvilaṃ;_  
_Nandībhavaparikkhīṇaṃ,_  
_tamahaṃ brūmi brāhmaṇaṃ._

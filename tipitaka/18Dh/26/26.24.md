# 26.24 Mahāpanthakattheravatthu

* Brāhmaṇavagga

* Mahāpanthakattheravatthu

(407.)

435\. _Yassa rāgo ca doso ca,_  
_māno makkho ca pātito;_  
_Sāsaporiva āraggā,_  
_tamahaṃ brūmi brāhmaṇaṃ._

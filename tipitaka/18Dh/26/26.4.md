# 26.4 Aññatarabrāhmaṇavatthu

### Brāhmaṇavagga

### Aññatarabrāhmaṇavatthu

(386.)

414\. _Jhāyiṃ virajamāsīnaṃ,_  
_katakiccamanāsavaṃ;_  
_Uttamatthamanuppattaṃ,_  
_tamahaṃ brūmi brāhmaṇaṃ._

# 26.35 Naṭaputtakattheravatthu

### Brāhmaṇavagga

### Naṭaputtakattheravatthu

(418.)

446. _Hitvā mānusakaṃ yogaṃ,_  
_dibbaṃ yogaṃ upaccagā;_  
_Sabbayogavisaṃyuttaṃ,_  
_tamahaṃ brūmi brāhmaṇaṃ._

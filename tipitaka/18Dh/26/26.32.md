# 26.32 Sundarasamuddattheravatthu

### Brāhmaṇavagga

### Sundarasamuddattheravatthu

(415.)

443. _Yodha kāme pahantvāna,_  
_anāgāro paribbaje;_  
_Kāmabhavaparikkhīṇaṃ,_  
_tamahaṃ brūmi brāhmaṇaṃ._

# 26.36 Naṭaputtakattheravatthu

### Brāhmaṇavagga

### Naṭaputtakattheravatthu

(419.)

447. _Hitvā ratiñca aratiñca,_  
_sītibhūtaṃ nirūpadhiṃ;_  
_Sabbalokābhibhuṃ vīraṃ,_  
_tamahaṃ brūmi brāhmaṇaṃ._

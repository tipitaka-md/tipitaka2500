# 26.21 Pabbhāravāsitissattheravatthu

* Brāhmaṇavagga

* Pabbhāravāsitissattheravatthu

(404.)

432\. _Asaṃsaṭṭhaṃ gahaṭṭhehi,_  
_anāgārehi cūbhayaṃ;_  
_Anokasārimappicchaṃ,_  
_tamahaṃ brūmi brāhmaṇaṃ._

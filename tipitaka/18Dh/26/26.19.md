# 26.19 Aññatarabrāhmaṇavatthu

* Brāhmaṇavagga

* Aññatarabrāhmaṇavatthu

(402.)

430\. _Yo dukkhassa pajānāti,_  
_idheva khayamattano;_  
_Pannabhāraṃ visaṃyuttaṃ,_  
_tamahaṃ brūmi brāhmaṇaṃ._

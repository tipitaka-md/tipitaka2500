# 11.5 Janapadakalyāṇīrūpanandātherīvatthu

### Jarāvagga

### Janapadakalyāṇīrūpanandātherīvatthu

(150.)

161. _Aṭṭhīnaṃ nagaraṃ kataṃ,_  
_maṃsalohitalepanaṃ;_  
_Yattha jarā ca maccu ca,_  
_māno makkho ca ohito._

# 5.3 Ānandaseṭṭhivatthu

### Bālavagga

### Ānandaseṭṭhivatthu

(62.)

67. _Puttā matthi dhanaṃ matthi,_  
_iti bālo vihaññati;_  
_Attā hi attano natthi,_  
_kuto puttā kuto dhanaṃ._

# 5.7 Suppabuddhakuṭṭhivatthu

* Bālavagga

* Suppabuddhakuṭṭhivatthu

(66.)

71\. _Caranti bālā dummedhā,_  
_amitteneva attanā;_  
_Karontā pāpakaṃ kammaṃ,_  
_yaṃ hoti kaṭukapphalaṃ._

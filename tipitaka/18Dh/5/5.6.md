# 5.6 Tiṃsapāveyyakabhikkhuvatthu

### Bālavagga

### Tiṃsapāveyyakabhikkhuvatthu

(65.)

70. _Muhuttamapi ce viññū,_  
_paṇḍitaṃ payirupāsati;_  
_Khippaṃ dhammaṃ vijānāti,_  
_jivhā sūparasaṃ yathā._

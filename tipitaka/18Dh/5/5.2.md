# 5.2 Mahākassapasaddhivihārikavatthu

* Bālavagga

* Mahākassapasaddhivihārikavatthu

(61.)

66\. _Carañce nādhigaccheyya,_  
_Seyyaṃ sadisamattano;_  
_Ekacariyaṃ daḷhaṃ kayirā,_  
_Natthi bāle sahāyatā._

# 5.11 Jambukattheravatthu

### Bālavagga

### Jambukattheravatthu

(70.)

75. _Māse māse kusaggena,_  
_bālo bhuñjeyya bhojanaṃ;_  
_Na so saṅkhātadhammānaṃ,_  
_kalaṃ agghati soḷasiṃ._

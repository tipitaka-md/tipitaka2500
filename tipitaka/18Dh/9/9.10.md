# 9.10 Maṇikārakulūpakatissattheravatthu

* Pāpavagga

* Maṇikārakulūpakatissattheravatthu

(126.)

135\. _Gabbhameke uppajjanti,_  
_nirayaṃ pāpakammino;_  
_Saggaṃ sugatino yanti,_  
_parinibbanti anāsavā._

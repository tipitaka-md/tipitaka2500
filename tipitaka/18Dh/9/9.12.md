# 9.12 Suppabuddhasakyavatthu

### Pāpavagga

### Suppabuddhasakyavatthu

(128.)

137. _Na antalikkhe na samuddamajjhe,_  
_Na pabbatānaṃ vivaraṃ pavissa;_  
_Na vijjatī so jagatippadeso,_  
_Yatthaṭṭhitaṃ nappasaheyya maccu._  


===

138. Pāpavaggo navamo.

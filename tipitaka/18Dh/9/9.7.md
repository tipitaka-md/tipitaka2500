# 9.7 Mahādhanavāṇijavatthu

* Pāpavagga

* Mahādhanavāṇijavatthu

(123.)

132\. _Vāṇijova bhayaṃ maggaṃ,_  
_appasattho mahaddhano;_  
_Visaṃ jīvitukāmova,_  
_pāpāni parivajjaye._

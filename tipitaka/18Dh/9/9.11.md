# 9.11 Tayojanavatthu

### Pāpavagga

### Tayojanavatthu

(127.)

136. _Na antalikkhe na samuddamajjhe,_  
_Na pabbatānaṃ vivaraṃ pavissa;_  
_Na vijjatī so jagatippadeso,_  
_Yatthaṭṭhito mucceyya pāpakammā._

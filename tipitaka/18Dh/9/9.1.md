# 9.1 Cūḷekasāṭakabrāhmaṇavatthu

### Pāpavagga

### Cūḷekasāṭakabrāhmaṇavatthu

(116.)

125\. _Abhittharetha kalyāṇe,_  
_pāpā cittaṃ nivāraye;_  
_Dandhañhi karoto puññaṃ,_  
_pāpasmiṃ ramatī mano._

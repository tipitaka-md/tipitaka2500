# 9.2 Seyyasakattheravatthu

### Pāpavagga

### Seyyasakattheravatthu

(117.)

126. _Pāpañce puriso kayirā,_  
_Na naṃ kayirā punappunaṃ;_  
_Na tamhi chandaṃ kayirātha,_  
_Dukkho pāpassa uccayo._

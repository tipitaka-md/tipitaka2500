# 3.5.4 Dhammadiṭṭhapañha

### Milindapañha

### Buddhavagga

### Dhammadiṭṭhapañha

(76.)

369\. Rājā āha—  “bhante nāgasena, dhammo tayā diṭṭho”ti. “Buddhanettiyā kho, mahārāja, buddhapaññattiyā yāvajīvaṃ sāvakehi vattitabban”ti.

370\. “Kallosi, bhante nāgasenā”ti.

---

371\. Dhammadiṭṭhapañho catuttho.

# 14.1 Abhidhammabhājanīya

* Sikkhāpadavibhaṅga

* Abhidhammabhājanīya

(703.)

2062\. Pañca sikkhāpadāni—  pāṇātipātā veramaṇī sikkhāpadaṃ, adinnādānā veramaṇī sikkhāpadaṃ, kāmesumicchācārā veramaṇī sikkhāpadaṃ, musāvādā veramaṇī sikkhāpadaṃ, surāmerayamajjapamādaṭṭhānā veramaṇī sikkhāpadaṃ.

(704.)

2063\. Tattha katamaṃ pāṇātipātā veramaṇī sikkhāpadaṃ? Yasmiṃ samaye kāmāvacaraṃ kusalaṃ cittaṃ uppannaṃ hoti somanassasahagataṃ ñāṇasampayuttaṃ pāṇātipātā viramantassa, yā tasmiṃ samaye pāṇātipātā ārati virati paṭivirati veramaṇī akiriyā akaraṇaṃ anajjhāpatti velāanatikkamo setughāto—  idaṃ vuccati “pāṇātipātā veramaṇī sikkhāpadaṃ”. Avasesā dhammā veramaṇiyā sampayuttā.

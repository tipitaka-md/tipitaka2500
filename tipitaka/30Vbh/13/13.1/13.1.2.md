# 13.1.2 Karuṇā

* Appamaññāvibhaṅga

* Suttantabhājanīya

* Karuṇā

(653.)

1950\. Kathañca bhikkhu karuṇāsahagatena cetasā ekaṃ disaṃ pharitvā viharati? Seyyathāpi nāma ekaṃ puggalaṃ duggataṃ durūpetaṃ disvā karuṇāyeyya; evameva sabbe satte karuṇāya pharati.

1951\. Tattha katamā karuṇā? Yā sattesu karuṇā karuṇāyanā karuṇāyitattaṃ karuṇācetovimutti—  ayaṃ vuccati “karuṇā”.

1952\. Tattha katamaṃ cittaṃ? Yaṃ cittaṃ mano mānasaṃ…pe…  tajjāmanoviññāṇadhātu—  idaṃ vuccati “cittaṃ”. Idaṃ cittaṃ imāya karuṇāya sahagataṃ hoti sahajātaṃ saṃsaṭṭhaṃ sampayuttaṃ. Tena vuccati “karuṇāsahagatena cetasā”ti.

(654.)

1953\. “Ekaṃ disan”ti puratthimaṃ vā disaṃ pacchimaṃ vā disaṃ uttaraṃ vā disaṃ dakkhiṇaṃ vā disaṃ uddhaṃ vā adho vā tiriyaṃ vā vidisaṃ vā.

(655.)

1954\. “Pharitvā”ti pharitvā adhimuccitvā.

(656.)

1955\. “Viharatī”ti iriyati vattati pāleti yapeti yāpeti carati viharati. Tena vuccati “viharatī”ti.

(657.)

1956\. “Tathā dutiyan”ti yatheva ekaṃ disaṃ tathā dutiyaṃ disaṃ tathā tatiyaṃ disaṃ tathā catutthaṃ disaṃ tathā uddhaṃ tathā adho tathā tiriyaṃ tathā vidisaṃ.

(658.)

1957\. “Sabbadhi sabbattatāya sabbāvantaṃ lokan”ti sabbena sabbaṃ sabbathā sabbaṃ asesaṃ nissesaṃ. Pariyādāyavacanametaṃ—  “sabbadhi sabbattatāya sabbāvantaṃ lokan”ti.

(659.)

1958\. “Karuṇāsahagatena cetasā”ti tattha katamā karuṇā? Yā sattesu karuṇā karuṇāyanā karuṇāyitattaṃ karuṇācetovimutti—  ayaṃ vuccati “karuṇā”.

1959\. Tattha katamaṃ cittaṃ? Yaṃ cittaṃ mano mānasaṃ…pe…  tajjāmanoviññāṇadhātu—  idaṃ vuccati “cittaṃ”. Idaṃ cittaṃ imāya karuṇāya sahagataṃ hoti sahajātaṃ saṃsaṭṭhaṃ sampayuttaṃ. Tena vuccati “karuṇāsahagatena cetasā”ti.

(660.)

1960\. “Vipulenā”ti yaṃ vipulaṃ taṃ mahaggataṃ, yaṃ mahaggataṃ taṃ appamāṇaṃ, yaṃ appamāṇaṃ so avero, yo avero so abyāpajjo.

(661.)

1961\. “Pharitvā”ti pharitvā adhimuccitvā.

(662.)

1962\. “Viharatī”ti…pe…  tena vuccati “viharatī”ti.

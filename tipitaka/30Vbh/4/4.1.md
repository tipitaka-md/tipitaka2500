# 4.1 Suttantabhājanīya

* Saccavibhaṅga

* Suttantabhājanīya

(189.)

714\. Cattāri ariyasaccāni—  dukkhaṃ ariyasaccaṃ, dukkhasamudayaṃ ariyasaccaṃ, dukkhanirodhaṃ ariyasaccaṃ, dukkhanirodhagāminī paṭipadā ariyasaccaṃ.

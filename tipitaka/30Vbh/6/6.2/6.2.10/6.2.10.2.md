# 6.2.10.2 Rūpāvacarakusalacitta

### Paṭiccasamuppādavibhaṅga

### Abhidhammabhājanīya

### Kusalaniddesa

### Rūpāvacarakusalacitta

(300.)

1212. Katame dhammā kusalā? Yasmiṃ samaye rūpūpapattiyā maggaṃ bhāveti vivicceva kāmehi…pe…  paṭhamaṃ jhānaṃ upasampajja viharati pathavīkasiṇaṃ, tasmiṃ samaye kusalamūlapaccayā saṅkhāro, saṅkhārapaccayā viññāṇaṃ, viññāṇapaccayā nāmaṃ, nāmapaccayā chaṭṭhāyatanaṃ, chaṭṭhāyatanapaccayā phasso, phassapaccayā vedanā, vedanāpaccayā pasādo, pasādapaccayā adhimokkho, adhimokkhapaccayā bhavo, bhavapaccayā jāti, jātipaccayā jarāmaraṇaṃ. Evametassa kevalassa dukkhakkhandhassa samudayo hoti.

(301.)

1213. Tattha katame kusalamūlā? Alobho, adoso, amoho—  ime vuccanti “kusalamūlā”.

1214. Tattha katamo kusalamūlapaccayā saṅkhāro? Yā cetanā sañcetanā sañcetayitattaṃ—  ayaṃ vuccati “kusalamūlapaccayā saṅkhāro” …pe…  tena vuccati “evametassa kevalassa dukkhakkhandhassa samudayo hotī”ti.

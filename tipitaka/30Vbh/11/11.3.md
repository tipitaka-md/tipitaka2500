# 11.3 Pañhāpucchaka

### Maggaṅgavibhaṅga

### Pañhāpucchaka

(504.)

1667\. Ariyo aṭṭhaṅgiko maggo, seyyathidaṃ—  sammādiṭṭhi, sammāsaṅkappo, sammāvācā, sammākammanto, sammāājīvo, sammāvāyāmo, sammāsati, sammāsamādhi.

### Maggaṅgavibhaṅga

### Pañhāpucchaka

(505.)

1668\. Aṭṭhannaṃ maggaṅgānaṃ kati kusalā, kati akusalā, kati abyākatā…pe…  kati saraṇā, kati araṇā?

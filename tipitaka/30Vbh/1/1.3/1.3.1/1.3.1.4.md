### Upādinnattika

503. Rūpakkhandho siyā upādinnupādāniyo, siyā anupādinnupādāniyo. Cattāro khandhā siyā upādinnupādāniyā, siyā anupādinnupādāniyā, siyā anupādinnaanupādāniyā.

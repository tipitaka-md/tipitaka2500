# 1.3.1.9 Dassanenapahātabbahetukattika

### Khandhavibhaṅga

### Pañhāpucchaka

### Tika

### Dassanenapahātabbahetukattika

508. Rūpakkhandho neva dassanena na bhāvanāya pahātabbahetuko. Cattāro khandhā siyā dassanena pahātabbahetukā, siyā bhāvanāya pahātabbahetukā, siyā neva dassanena na bhāvanāya pahātabbahetukā.

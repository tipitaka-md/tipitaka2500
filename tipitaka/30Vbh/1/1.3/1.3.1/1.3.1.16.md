# 1.3.1.16 Maggārammaṇattika

* Khandhavibhaṅga

* Pañhāpucchaka

* Tika

* Maggārammaṇattika

515\. Rūpakkhandho anārammaṇo. Cattāro khandhā siyā maggārammaṇā, siyā maggahetukā, siyā maggādhipatino, siyā na vattabbā—  “maggārammaṇā”tipi, “maggahetukā”tipi, “maggādhipatino”tipi.

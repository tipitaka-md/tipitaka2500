# 9.3 Pañhāpucchaka

* Iddhipādavibhaṅga

* Pañhāpucchaka

(462.)

1525\. Cattāro iddhipādā—  idha bhikkhu chandasamādhipadhānasaṅkhārasamannāgataṃ iddhipādaṃ bhāveti, vīriyasamādhi…pe…  cittasamādhi…pe…  vīmaṃsāsamādhipadhānasaṅkhārasamannāgataṃ iddhipādaṃ bhāveti.

* Iddhipādavibhaṅga

* Pañhāpucchaka

(463.)

1526\. Catunnaṃ iddhipādānaṃ kati kusalā, kati akusalā, kati abyākatā…pe…  kati saraṇā, kati araṇā?

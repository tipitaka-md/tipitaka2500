# 1.7.5.3 Sakabaḷasikkhāpada

### Mahāvibhaṅga

### Sekhiyakaṇḍa

### Kabaḷavagga

### Sakabaḷasikkhāpada

(619.)

1899\. Tena samayena buddho bhagavā sāvatthiyaṃ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena chabbaggiyā bhikkhū sakabaḷena mukhena byāharanti…pe… .

1900\. **“Na sakabaḷena mukhena byāharissāmīti sikkhā karaṇīyā”**ti. (*43*:**188**)

1901\. Na sakabaḷena mukhena byāharitabbaṃ. Yo anādariyaṃ paṭicca sakabaḷena mukhena byāharati, āpatti dukkaṭassa.

1902\. Anāpatti— asañcicca…pe… ādikammikassāti.

---

1903\. Tatiyasikkhāpadaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.

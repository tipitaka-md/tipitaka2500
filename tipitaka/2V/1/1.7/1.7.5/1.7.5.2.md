# 1.7.5.2 Dutiyaanāhaṭasikkhāpada

* Mahāvibhaṅga

* Sekhiyakaṇḍa

* Kabaḷavagga

* Dutiyaanāhaṭasikkhāpada

(618.)

1894\. Tena samayena buddho bhagavā sāvatthiyaṃ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena chabbaggiyā bhikkhū bhuñjamānā sabbaṃ hatthaṃ mukhe pakkhipanti…pe… .

1895\. **“Na bhuñjamāno sabbaṃ hatthaṃ mukhe pakkhipissāmīti sikkhā karaṇīyā”**ti. (*42*:**187**)

1896\. Na bhuñjamānena sabbo hattho mukhe pakkhipitabbo. Yo anādariyaṃ paṭicca bhuñjamāno sabbaṃ hatthaṃ mukhe pakkhipati, āpatti dukkaṭassa.

1897\. Anāpatti— asañcicca…pe… ādikammikassāti.

---

1898\. Dutiyasikkhāpadaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.

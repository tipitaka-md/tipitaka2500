# 1.7.3.1 Khambhakatasikkhāpada

### Mahāvibhaṅga

### Sekhiyakaṇḍa

### Khambhakatavagga

### Khambhakatasikkhāpada

(596.)

1785. Tena samayena buddho bhagavā sāvatthiyaṃ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena chabbaggiyā bhikkhū khambhakatā antaraghare gacchanti…pe… .

1786. **“Na khambhakato antaraghare gamissāmīti sikkhā karaṇīyā”**ti. (*21*:**166**)

1787. Na khambhakatena antaraghare gantabbaṃ. Yo anādariyaṃ paṭicca ekato vā ubhato vā khambhaṃ katvā antaraghare gacchati, āpatti dukkaṭassa.

1788. Anāpatti—  asañcicca…pe…  ādikammikassāti.

---

1789. Paṭhamasikkhāpadaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.

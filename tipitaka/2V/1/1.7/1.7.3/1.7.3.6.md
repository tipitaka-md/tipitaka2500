# 1.7.3.6 Pallatthikasikkhāpada

### Mahāvibhaṅga

### Sekhiyakaṇḍa

### Khambhakatavagga

### Pallatthikasikkhāpada

(601.)

1810. Tena samayena buddho bhagavā sāvatthiyaṃ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena chabbaggiyā bhikkhū pallatthikāya antaraghare nisīdanti…pe… .

1811. **“Na pallatthikāya antaraghare nisīdissāmīti sikkhā karaṇīyā”**ti. (*26*:**171**)

1812. Na pallatthikāya antaraghare nisīditabbaṃ. Yo anādariyaṃ paṭicca hatthapallatthikāya vā dussapallatthikāya vā antaraghare nisīdati, āpatti dukkaṭassa.

1813. Anāpatti— asañcicca, assatiyā, ajānantassa, gilānassa, vāsūpagatassa, āpadāsu, ummattakassa, ādikammikassāti.

===

1814. Chaṭṭhasikkhāpadaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.

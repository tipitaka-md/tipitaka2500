# 1.7.3.5 Ukkuṭikasikkhāpada

* Mahāvibhaṅga

* Sekhiyakaṇḍa

* Khambhakatavagga

* Ukkuṭikasikkhāpada

(600.)

1805\. Tena samayena buddho bhagavā sāvatthiyaṃ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena chabbaggiyā bhikkhū ukkuṭikāya antaraghare gacchanti…pe… .

1806\. **“Na ukkuṭikāya antaraghare gamissāmīti sikkhā karaṇīyā”**ti. (*25*:**170**)

1807\. Na ukkuṭikāya antaraghare gantabbaṃ. Yo anādariyaṃ paṭicca ukkuṭikāya antaraghare gacchati, āpatti dukkaṭassa.

1808\. Anāpatti— asañcicca…pe… ādikammikassāti.

---

1809\. Pañcamasikkhāpadaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.

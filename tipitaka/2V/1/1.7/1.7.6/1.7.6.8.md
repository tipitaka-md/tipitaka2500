# 1.7.6.8 Daṇḍapāṇisikkhāpada

### Mahāvibhaṅga

### Sekhiyakaṇḍa

### Surusuruvagga

### Daṇḍapāṇisikkhāpada

(635.)

1984. Tena samayena buddho bhagavā sāvatthiyaṃ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena chabbaggiyā bhikkhū daṇḍapāṇissa dhammaṃ desenti…pe… .

1985. **“Na daṇḍapāṇissa agilānassa dhammaṃ desessāmīti sikkhā karaṇīyā”**ti. (*58*:**203**)

1986. **Daṇḍo** nāma majjhimassa purisassa catuhattho daṇḍo. Tato ukkaṭṭho adaṇḍo, omako adaṇḍo.

1987. Na daṇḍapāṇissa agilānassa dhammo desetabbo. Yo anādariyaṃ paṭicca daṇḍapāṇissa agilānassa dhammaṃ deseti, āpatti dukkaṭassa.

1988. Anāpatti—  asañcicca…pe…  ādikammikassāti.

===

1989. Aṭṭhamasikkhāpadaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.

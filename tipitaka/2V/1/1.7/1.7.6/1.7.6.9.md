# 1.7.6.9 Satthapāṇisikkhāpada

### Mahāvibhaṅga

### Sekhiyakaṇḍa

### Surusuruvagga

### Satthapāṇisikkhāpada

(636.)

1990\. Tena samayena buddho bhagavā sāvatthiyaṃ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena chabbaggiyā bhikkhū satthapāṇissa dhammaṃ desenti…pe… .

1991\. **“Na satthapāṇissa agilānassa dhammaṃ desessāmīti sikkhā karaṇīyā”**ti. (*59*:**204**)

1992\. **Satthaṃ** nāma ekatodhāraṃ ubhatodhāraṃ paharaṇaṃ.

1993\. Na satthapāṇissa agilānassa dhammo desetabbo. Yo anādariyaṃ paṭicca satthapāṇissa agilānassa dhammaṃ deseti, āpatti dukkaṭassa.

1994\. Anāpatti—  asañcicca…pe…  ādikammikassāti.

---

1995\. Navamasikkhāpadaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.

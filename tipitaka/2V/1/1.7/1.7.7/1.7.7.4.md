# 1.7.7.4 Sayanasikkhāpada

### Mahāvibhaṅga

### Sekhiyakaṇḍa

### Pādukavagga

### Sayanasikkhāpada

(641.)

2018\. Tena samayena buddho bhagavā sāvatthiyaṃ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena chabbaggiyā bhikkhū sayanagatassa dhammaṃ desenti…pe… .

2019\. **“Na sayanagatassa agilānassa dhammaṃ desessāmīti sikkhā karaṇīyā”**ti. (*64*:**209**)

2020\. Na sayanagatassa agilānassa dhammo desetabbo. Yo anādariyaṃ paṭicca antamaso chamāyampi nipannassa sayanagatassa agilānassa dhammaṃ deseti, āpatti dukkaṭassa.

2021\. Anāpatti—  asañcicca…pe…  ādikammikassāti.

---

2022\. Catutthasikkhāpadaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.

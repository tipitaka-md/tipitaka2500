# 1.7.7.10 Ṭhitasikkhāpada

* Mahāvibhaṅga

* Sekhiyakaṇḍa

* Pādukavagga

* Ṭhitasikkhāpada

(648.)

2057\. Tena samayena buddho bhagavā sāvatthiyaṃ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena chabbaggiyā bhikkhū ṭhitā nisinnassa dhammaṃ desenti…pe… .

2058\. **“Na ṭhito nisinnassa agilānassa dhammaṃ desessāmīti sikkhā karaṇīyā”**ti. (*70*:**215**)

2059\. Na ṭhitena nisinnassa agilānassa dhammo desetabbo. Yo anādariyaṃ paṭicca ṭhito nisinnassa agilānassa dhammaṃ deseti, āpatti dukkaṭassa.

2060\. Anāpatti—  asañcicca…pe…  ādikammikassāti.

---

2061\. Dasamasikkhāpadaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.

# 1.7.7.11 Pacchatogamanasikkhāpada

### Mahāvibhaṅga

### Sekhiyakaṇḍa

### Pādukavagga

### Pacchatogamanasikkhāpada

(649.)

2062. Tena samayena buddho bhagavā sāvatthiyaṃ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena chabbaggiyā bhikkhū pacchato gacchantā purato gacchantassa dhammaṃ desenti…pe… .

2063. **“Na pacchato gacchanto purato gacchantassa agilānassa dhammaṃ desessāmīti sikkhā karaṇīyā”**ti. (*71*:**216**)

2064. Na pacchato gacchantena purato gacchantassa agilānassa dhammo desetabbo. Yo anādariyaṃ paṭicca pacchato gacchanto purato gacchantassa agilānassa dhammaṃ deseti, āpatti dukkaṭassa.

2065. Anāpatti—  asañcicca…pe…  ādikammikassāti.

===

2066. Ekādasamasikkhāpadaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.

# 1.7.7.3 Yānasikkhāpada

### Mahāvibhaṅga

### Sekhiyakaṇḍa

### Pādukavagga

### Yānasikkhāpada

(640.)

2012. Tena samayena buddho bhagavā sāvatthiyaṃ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena chabbaggiyā bhikkhū yānagatassa dhammaṃ desenti…pe… .

2013. **“Na yānagatassa agilānassa dhammaṃ desessāmīti sikkhā karaṇīyā”**ti. (*63*:**208**)

2014. **Yānaṃ** nāma vayhaṃ ratho sakaṭaṃ sandamānikā sivikā pāṭaṅkī.

2015. Na yānagatassa agilānassa dhammo desetabbo. Yo anādariyaṃ paṭicca yānagatassa agilānassa dhammaṃ deseti, āpatti dukkaṭassa.

2016. Anāpatti—  asañcicca…pe…  ādikammikassāti.

===

2017. Tatiyasikkhāpadaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.

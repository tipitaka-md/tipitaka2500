# 1.7.1.5 Susaṃvutasikkhāpada

### Mahāvibhaṅga

### Sekhiyakaṇḍa

### Parimaṇḍalavagga

### Susaṃvutasikkhāpada

(580.)

1705. Tena samayena buddho bhagavā sāvatthiyaṃ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena chabbaggiyā bhikkhū hatthampi pādampi kīḷāpentā antaraghare gacchanti…pe… .

1706. **“Susaṃvuto antaraghare gamissāmīti sikkhā karaṇīyā”**ti. (*5*:**150**)

1707. Susaṃvutena antaraghare gantabbaṃ. Yo anādariyaṃ paṭicca hatthaṃ vā pādaṃ vā kīḷāpento antaraghare gacchati, āpatti dukkaṭassa.

1708. Anāpatti—  asañcicca, assatiyā, ajānantassa, gilānassa, ummattakassa, ādikammikassāti.

===

1709. Pañcamasikkhāpadaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.

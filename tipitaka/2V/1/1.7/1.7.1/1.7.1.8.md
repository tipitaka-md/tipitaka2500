# 1.7.1.8 Dutiyaokkhittacakkhusikkhāpada

* Mahāvibhaṅga

* Sekhiyakaṇḍa

* Parimaṇḍalavagga

* Dutiyaokkhittacakkhusikkhāpada

(583.)

1720\. Tena samayena buddho bhagavā sāvatthiyaṃ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena chabbaggiyā bhikkhū tahaṃ tahaṃ olokentā antaraghare nisīdanti…pe… .

1721\. **“Okkhittacakkhu antaraghare nisīdissāmīti sikkhā karaṇīyā”**ti. (*8*:**153**)

1722\. Okkhittacakkhunā antaraghare nisīditabbaṃ yugamattaṃ pekkhantena. Yo anādariyaṃ paṭicca tahaṃ tahaṃ olokento antaraghare nisīdati, āpatti dukkaṭassa.

1723\. Anāpatti—  asañcicca, assatiyā, ajānantassa, gilānassa, āpadāsu, ummattakassa, ādikammikassāti.

---

1724\. Aṭṭhamasikkhāpadaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.

# 1.7.1.6 Dutiyasusaṃvutasikkhāpada

* Mahāvibhaṅga

* Sekhiyakaṇḍa

* Parimaṇḍalavagga

* Dutiyasusaṃvutasikkhāpada

(581.)

1710\. Tena samayena buddho bhagavā sāvatthiyaṃ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena chabbaggiyā bhikkhū hatthampi pādampi kīḷāpentā antaraghare nisīdanti…pe… .

1711\. **“Susaṃvuto antaraghare nisīdissāmīti sikkhā karaṇīyā”**ti. (*6*:**151**)

1712\. Susaṃvutena antaraghare nisīditabbaṃ. Yo anādariyaṃ paṭicca hatthaṃ vā pādaṃ vā kīḷāpento antaraghare nisīdati, āpatti dukkaṭassa.

1713\. Anāpatti—  asañcicca, assatiyā, ajānantassa, gilānassa, ummattakassa, ādikammikassāti.

---

1714\. Chaṭṭhasikkhāpadaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.

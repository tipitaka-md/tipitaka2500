# 1.7.1.9 Ukkhittakasikkhāpada

### Mahāvibhaṅga

### Sekhiyakaṇḍa

### Parimaṇḍalavagga

### Ukkhittakasikkhāpada

(584.)

1725\. Tena samayena buddho bhagavā sāvatthiyaṃ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena chabbaggiyā bhikkhū ukkhittakāya antaraghare gacchanti…pe… .

1726\. **“Na ukkhittakāya antaraghare gamissāmīti sikkhā karaṇīyā”**ti. (*9*:**154**)

1727\. Na ukkhittakāya antaraghare gantabbaṃ. Yo anādariyaṃ paṭicca ekato vā ubhato vā ukkhipitvā antaraghare gacchati, āpatti dukkaṭassa.

1728\. Anāpatti—  asañcicca, assatiyā, ajānantassa, gilānassa, āpadāsu, ummattakassa, ādikammikassāti.

---

1729\. Navamasikkhāpadaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.

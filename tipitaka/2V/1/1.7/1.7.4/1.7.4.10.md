# 1.7.4.10 Ālopasikkhāpada

### Mahāvibhaṅga

### Sekhiyakaṇḍa

### Sakkaccavagga

### Ālopasikkhāpada

(616.)

1884. Tena samayena buddho bhagavā sāvatthiyaṃ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena chabbaggiyā bhikkhū dīghaṃ ālopaṃ karonti…pe… .

1885. **“Parimaṇḍalaṃ ālopaṃ karissāmīti sikkhā karaṇīyā”**ti. (*40*:**185**)

1886. Parimaṇḍalo ālopo kātabbo. Yo anādariyaṃ paṭicca dīghaṃ ālopaṃ karoti, āpatti dukkaṭassa.

1887. Anāpatti— asañcicca, assatiyā, ajānantassa, gilānassa, khajjake, phalāphale, uttaribhaṅge, āpadāsu, ummattakassa, ādikammikassāti.

===

1888. Dasamasikkhāpadaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.

  
Sakkaccavaggo catuttho.

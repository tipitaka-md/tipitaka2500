# 2.4.16.10 Nandasikkhāpada

### Bhikkhunivibhaṅga

### Pācittiyakaṇḍa

### Dhammikavagga

### Nandasikkhāpada

3526. Uddiṭṭhā kho, ayyāyo, chasaṭṭhisatā pācittiyā dhammā. Tatthāyyāyo pucchāmi—  “kaccittha parisuddhā”? Dutiyampi pucchāmi—  “kaccittha parisuddhā”? Tatiyampi pucchāmi—  “kaccittha parisuddhā”? Parisuddhetthāyyāyo, tasmā tuṇhī, evametaṃ dhārayāmīti.

===

3527. Khuddakaṃ samattaṃ.

  
Bhikkhunivibhaṅge pācittiyakaṇḍaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ. ( )

# 2.4.4.8 Tiroraṭṭhasikkhāpada

* Bhikkhunivibhaṅga

* Pācittiyakaṇḍa

* Tuvaṭṭavagga

* Tiroraṭṭhasikkhāpada

(965.)

2963\. Tena samayena buddho bhagavā sāvatthiyaṃ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena bhikkhuniyo tiroraṭṭhe sāsaṅkasammate sappaṭibhaye asatthikā cārikaṃ caranti. Dhuttā dūsenti.

2964\. Yā tā bhikkhuniyo appicchā…pe…  tā ujjhāyanti khiyyanti vipācenti—  “kathañhi nāma bhikkhuniyo tiroraṭṭhe sāsaṅkasammate sappaṭibhaye asatthikā cārikaṃ carissantī”ti…pe…  “saccaṃ kira, bhikkhave, bhikkhuniyo tiroraṭṭhe sāsaṅkasammate sappaṭibhaye asatthikā cārikaṃ carantī”ti? “Saccaṃ, bhagavā”ti. Vigarahi buddho bhagavā…pe…  kathañhi nāma, bhikkhave, bhikkhuniyo tiroraṭṭhe sāsaṅkasammate sappaṭibhaye asatthikā cārikaṃ carissanti. Netaṃ, bhikkhave, appasannānaṃ vā pasādāya…pe…  evañca pana, bhikkhave, bhikkhuniyo imaṃ sikkhāpadaṃ uddisantu—

(966.)

2965\. **“Yā pana bhikkhunī tiroraṭṭhe sāsaṅkasammate sappaṭibhaye asatthikā cārikaṃ careyya, pācittiyan”**ti. (*38*:**93**)

(967.)

2966\. **Yā panā**ti yā yādisā…pe…  **bhikkhunī**ti…pe…  ayaṃ imasmiṃ atthe adhippetā bhikkhunīti.

2967\. **Tiroraṭṭhe**ti yassa vijite viharati, taṃ ṭhapetvā aññassa raṭṭhe.

2968\. **Sāsaṅkaṃ** nāma tasmiṃ magge corānaṃ niviṭṭhokāso dissati, bhuttokāso dissati, ṭhitokāso dissati, nisinnokāso dissati, nipannokāso dissati.

2969\. **Sappaṭibhayaṃ** nāma tasmiṃ magge corehi manussā hatā dissanti, viluttā dissanti, ākoṭitā dissanti.

2970\. **Asatthikā** nāma vinā satthena.

2971\. **Cārikaṃ careyyā**ti kukkuṭasampāte gāme gāmantare gāmantare, āpatti pācittiyassa. Agāmake araññe addhayojane addhayojane āpatti pācittiyassa.

(968.)

2972\. Anāpatti—  satthena saha gacchati, kheme appaṭibhaye gacchati, āpadāsu, ummattikāya, ādikammikāyāti.

---

2973\. Aṭṭhamasikkhāpadaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.

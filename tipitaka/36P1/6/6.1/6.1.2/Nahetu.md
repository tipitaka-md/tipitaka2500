2817\. Saṃkiliṭṭhasaṃkilesikaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca saṃkiliṭṭhasaṃkilesiko dhammo uppajjati nahetupaccayā—  vicikicchāsahagate uddhaccasahagate khandhe paṭicca vicikicchāsahagato uddhaccasahagato moho. (1)

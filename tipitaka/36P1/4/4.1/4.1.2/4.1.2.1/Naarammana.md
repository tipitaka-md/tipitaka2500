# Naārammaṇa

### Paṭiccavāra

### Paccayapaccanīya

### Vibhaṅgavāra

### Naārammaṇa

(27.)

2100. Vipākaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nevavipākanavipākadhammadhammo uppajjati naārammaṇapaccayā—  vipāke khandhe paṭicca cittasamuṭṭhānaṃ rūpaṃ; paṭisandhikkhaṇe vipāke khandhe paṭicca kaṭattārūpaṃ, khandhe paṭicca vatthu. (1)

(28.)

2101. Vipākadhammadhammaṃ paṭicca nevavipākanavipākadhammadhammo uppajjati naārammaṇapaccayā—  vipākadhammadhamme khandhe paṭicca cittasamuṭṭhānaṃ rūpaṃ. (1)

(29.)

2102. Nevavipākanavipākadhammadhammaṃ paṭicca nevavipākanavipākadhammadhammo uppajjati naārammaṇapaccayā—  nevavipākanavipākadhammadhamme khandhe paṭicca cittasamuṭṭhānaṃ rūpaṃ, ekaṃ mahābhūtaṃ paṭicca tayo mahābhūtā…pe…  mahābhūte paṭicca cittasamuṭṭhānaṃ rūpaṃ kaṭattārūpaṃ upādārūpaṃ; bāhiraṃ…  āhārasamuṭṭhānaṃ…  utusamuṭṭhānaṃ…  asaññasattānaṃ ekaṃ mahābhūtaṃ paṭicca tayo mahābhūtā…  mahābhūte paṭicca kaṭattārūpaṃ upādārūpaṃ. (1)

(30.)

2103. Vipākañca nevavipākanavipākadhammadhammañca dhammaṃ paṭicca nevavipākanavipākadhammadhammo uppajjati naārammaṇapaccayā—  vipāke khandhe ca mahābhūte ca paṭicca cittasamuṭṭhānaṃ rūpaṃ; paṭisandhikkhaṇe vipāke khandhe ca mahābhūte ca paṭicca kaṭattārūpaṃ. (1)

(31.)

2104. Vipākadhammadhammañca nevavipākanavipākadhammadhammañca dhammaṃ paṭicca nevavipākanavipākadhammadhammo uppajjati naārammaṇapaccayā—  vipākadhammadhamme khandhe ca mahābhūte ca paṭicca cittasamuṭṭhānaṃ rūpaṃ. (1)

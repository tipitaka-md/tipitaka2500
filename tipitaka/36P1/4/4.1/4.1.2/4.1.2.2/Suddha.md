### Suddha

(49.)

2142. Nahetuyā dasa, naārammaṇe pañca, naadhipatiyā terasa, naanantare pañca, nasamanantare pañca, naaññamaññe pañca, naupanissaye pañca, napurejāte dvādasa, napacchājāte terasa, naāsevane terasa, nakamme dve, navipāke pañca, naāhāre ekaṃ, naindriye ekaṃ, najhāne dve, namagge nava, nasampayutte pañca, navippayutte tīṇi, nonatthiyā pañca, novigate pañca.

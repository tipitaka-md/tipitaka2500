(17.)

2076. Vipākadhammadhammaṃ paṭicca vipākadhammadhammo uppajjati āsevanapaccayā—  vipākadhammadhammaṃ ekaṃ khandhaṃ paṭicca tayo khandhā…pe…  dve khandhe paṭicca dve khandhā.

### Nissayavāra

### Paccayacatukka

(82.)

2247. Vipākaṃ dhammaṃ nissāya vipāko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā—  vipākaṃ ekaṃ khandhaṃ nissāya tayo khandhā…pe… .

2248. Hetuyā sattarasa…pe… .

2249. Nahetuyā dvādasa…pe…  novigate pañca.

2250. Hetupaccayā naārammaṇe pañca…pe…  navippayutte tīṇi.

2251. Nahetupaccayā ārammaṇe satta…pe…  avigate dvādasa.

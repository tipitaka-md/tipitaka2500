### Paccayapaccanīyānuloma

### Naaññamaññaduka

(500.)

1722. Naaññamaññapaccayā hetuyā tīṇi, ārammaṇe nava, adhipatiyā aṭṭha, anantare satta, samanantare satta, sahajāte pañca, nissaye satta, upanissaye nava, purejāte tīṇi, pacchājāte tīṇi, āsevane tīṇi, kamme tīṇi, vipāke ekaṃ, āhāre tīṇi, indriye tīṇi, jhāne tīṇi, magge tīṇi, vippayutte pañca, atthiyā satta, natthiyā satta, vigate satta, avigate satta…pe… .

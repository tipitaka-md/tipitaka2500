### Naanantara-nasamanantaraduka

### Aṭṭhaka

1716\. Nasamanantarapaccayā nahetupaccayā naārammaṇapaccayā naadhipatipaccayā naanantarapaccayā nasahajātapaccayā naaññamaññapaccayā nissaye tīṇi, upanissaye nava, purejāte tīṇi, pacchājāte tīṇi, kamme dve, āhāre ekaṃ, indriye ekaṃ, vippayutte pañca, atthiyā satta, avigate satta. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

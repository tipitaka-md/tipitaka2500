### Āhāraduka

(452.)

1431. Āhārapaccayā nahetuyā satta, naārammaṇe satta, naadhipatiyā satta, naanantare satta, nasamanantare satta, nasahajāte ekaṃ, naaññamaññe tīṇi, nanissaye ekaṃ, naupanissaye satta, napurejāte satta, napacchājāte satta, naāsevane satta, nakamme satta, navipāke satta, naindriye satta, najhāne satta, namagge satta, nasampayutte tīṇi, navippayutte tīṇi, nonatthiyā satta, novigate satta. (21)

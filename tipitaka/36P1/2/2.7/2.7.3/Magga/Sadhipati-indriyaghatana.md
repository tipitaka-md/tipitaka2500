### Sādhipati-indriyaghaṭanā

(480.)

1620. Magga adhipati sahajāta nissaya indriya atthi avigatanti nahetuyā satta…pe…  naaññamaññe tīṇi, naupanissaye satta…pe…  nasampayutte tīṇi, navippayutte tīṇi, nonatthiyā satta, novigate satta.

1621. Magga adhipati sahajāta aññamañña nissaya indriya sampayutta atthi avigatanti nahetuyā tīṇi…pe…  novigate tīṇi.

1622. Magga adhipati sahajāta nissaya indriya vippayutta atthi avigatanti nahetuyā tīṇi…pe…  novigate tīṇi. [Avipāka—  3]

1623. Magga adhipati sahajāta nissaya vipāka indriya atthi avigatanti nahetuyā ekaṃ…pe…  novigate ekaṃ.

1624. Magga adhipati sahajāta aññamañña nissaya vipāka indriya sampayutta atthi avigatanti nahetuyā ekaṃ…pe…  novigate ekaṃ.

1625. Magga adhipati sahajāta nissaya vipāka indriya vippayutta atthi avigatanti nahetuyā ekaṃ…pe…  novigate ekaṃ. [Savipāka—  3]

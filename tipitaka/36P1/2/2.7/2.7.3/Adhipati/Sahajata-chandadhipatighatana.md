* Sahajāta-chandādhipatighaṭanā

(423.)

1310\. Adhipati sahajāta nissaya atthi avigatanti nahetuyā satta, naārammaṇe satta, naanantare satta, nasamanantare satta, naaññamaññe tīṇi, naupanissaye satta, napurejāte satta, napacchājāte satta, naāsevane satta, nakamme satta, navipāke satta, naāhāre satta, naindriye satta, najhāne satta, namagge satta, nasampayutte tīṇi, navippayutte tīṇi, nonatthiyā satta, novigate satta.

1311\. Adhipati sahajāta aññamañña nissaya sampayutta atthi avigatanti nahetuyā tīṇi, naārammaṇe tīṇi, naanantare tīṇi, nasamanantare tīṇi, naupanissaye tīṇi, napurejāte tīṇi, napacchājāte tīṇi, naāsevane tīṇi, nakamme tīṇi, navipāke tīṇi, naāhāre tīṇi, naindriye tīṇi, najhāne tīṇi, namagge tīṇi, navippayutte tīṇi, nonatthiyā tīṇi, novigate tīṇi.

1312\.

### Najhānaduka

### Sattaka

810. Najhānapaccayā nahetupaccayā naadhipatipaccayā napacchājātapaccayā naāsevanapaccayā namaggapaccayā ārammaṇe ekaṃ, anantare ekaṃ, samanantare ekaṃ, sahajāte ekaṃ, aññamaññe ekaṃ, nissaye ekaṃ, upanissaye ekaṃ, purejāte ekaṃ, kamme ekaṃ, vipāke ekaṃ, āhāre ekaṃ, indriye ekaṃ, sampayutte ekaṃ, vippayutte ekaṃ, atthiyā ekaṃ, natthiyā ekaṃ, vigate ekaṃ, avigate ekaṃ.

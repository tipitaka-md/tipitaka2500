### Paccayānulomapaccanīya

### Aññamaññaduka

(166.)

592. Aññamaññapaccayā nahetuyā cattāri, naārammaṇe ekaṃ, naadhipatiyā satta, naanantare ekaṃ, nasamanantare ekaṃ, naupanissaye ekaṃ, napurejāte tīṇi, napacchājāte satta, naāsevane satta, nakamme satta, navipāke satta, naāhāre ekaṃ, naindriye ekaṃ, najhāne ekaṃ, namagge ekaṃ, nasampayutte ekaṃ, navippayutte tīṇi, nonatthiyā ekaṃ, novigate ekaṃ.

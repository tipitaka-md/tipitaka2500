# Paccanīyuddhāra

### Vedanāttika

### Pañhāvāra

### Paccanīyuddhāra

(81.)

2006. Sukhāya vedanāya sampayutto dhammo sukhāya vedanāya sampayuttassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo…  sahajātapaccayena paccayo…  upanissayapaccayena paccayo…  kammapaccayena paccayo. (1)

2007. Sukhāya vedanāya sampayutto dhammo dukkhāya vedanāya sampayuttassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo…  upanissayapaccayena paccayo…  kammapaccayena paccayo. (2)

2008. Sukhāya vedanāya sampayutto dhammo adukkhamasukhāya vedanāya sampayuttassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo…  upanissayapaccayena paccayo…  kammapaccayena paccayo. (3)

(82.)

2009. Dukkhāya vedanāya sampayutto dhammo dukkhāya vedanāya sampayuttassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo…  sahajātapaccayena paccayo…  upanissayapaccayena paccayo…  kammapaccayena paccayo. (1)

2010. Dukkhāya vedanāya sampayutto dhammo sukhāya vedanāya sampayuttassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo…  upanissayapaccayena paccayo. (2)

2011. Dukkhāya vedanāya sampayutto dhammo adukkhamasukhāya vedanāya sampayuttassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo…  upanissayapaccayena paccayo…  kammapaccayena paccayo. (3)

(83.)

2012. Adukkhamasukhāya vedanāya sampayutto dhammo adukkhamasukhāya vedanāya sampayuttassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo…  sahajātapaccayena paccayo…  upanissayapaccayena paccayo…  kammapaccayena paccayo. (1)

2013. Adukkhamasukhāya vedanāya sampayutto dhammo sukhāya vedanāya sampayuttassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo…  upanissayapaccayena paccayo…  kammapaccayena paccayo. (2)

2014. Adukkhamasukhāya vedanāya sampayutto dhammo dukkhāya vedanāya sampayuttassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo…  upanissayapaccayena paccayo…  kammapaccayena paccayo. (3)

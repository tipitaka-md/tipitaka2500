### Kusalapada

(26.)

60. Siyā kusalaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca kusalo dhammo uppajjeyya hetupaccayā. Siyā kusalaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca akusalo dhammo uppajjeyya hetupaccayā. Siyā kusalaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca abyākato dhammo uppajjeyya hetupaccayā. Siyā kusalaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca kusalo ca abyākato ca dhammā uppajjeyyuṃ hetupaccayā. Siyā kusalaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca akusalo ca abyākato ca dhammā uppajjeyyuṃ hetupaccayā. Siyā kusalaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca kusalo ca akusalo ca dhammā uppajjeyyuṃ hetupaccayā. Siyā kusalaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca kusalo ca akusalo ca abyākato ca dhammā uppajjeyyuṃ hetupaccayā.

# 1.54 Ubhatobyañjanakavatthu

* Mahākhandhaka

* Ubhatobyañjanakavatthu

(116.)

490\. Tena kho pana samayena aññataro ubhatobyañjanako bhikkhūsu pabbajito hoti. So karotipi kārāpetipi. Bhagavato etamatthaṃ ārocesuṃ. “Ubhatobyañjanako, bhikkhave, anupasampanno na upasampādetabbo, upasampanno nāsetabbo”ti. (11)

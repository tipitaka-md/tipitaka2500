# 1.31 Kasāhatavatthu

### Mahākhandhaka

### Kasāhatavatthu

(94.)

432\. Tena kho pana samayena aññataro puriso kasāhato katadaṇḍakammo bhikkhūsu pabbajito hoti. Manussā ujjhāyanti khiyyanti vipācenti—  “kathañhi nāma samaṇā sakyaputtiyā kasāhataṃ katadaṇḍakammaṃ pabbājessantī”ti. Bhagavato etamatthaṃ ārocesuṃ. “Na, bhikkhave, kasāhato katadaṇḍakammo pabbājetabbo. Yo pabbājeyya, āpatti dukkaṭassā”ti.

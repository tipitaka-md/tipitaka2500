# 7.7 Samādāyapannarasakādi

* Kathinakkhandhaka

* Samādāyapannarasakādi

(316.)

1446\. Bhikkhu atthatakathino cīvaraṃ samādāya pakkamati…pe… .

---

1447\. (Ādāyavārasadisaṃ evaṃ vitthāretabbaṃ.)



1448\. Bhikkhu atthatakathino vippakatacīvaraṃ ādāya pakkamati. Tassa bahisīmagatassa evaṃ hoti—  “idhevimaṃ cīvaraṃ kāressaṃ, na paccessan”ti. So taṃ cīvaraṃ kāreti. Tassa bhikkhuno niṭṭhānantiko kathinuddhāro…pe… .

---

1449\. (Samādāyavārasadisaṃ evaṃ vitthāretabbaṃ.)

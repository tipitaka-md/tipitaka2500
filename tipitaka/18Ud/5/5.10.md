# 5.10 Cūḷapanthakasutta

### Soṇavagga

### Cūḷapanthakasutta

(50.)

449. Evaṃ me sutaṃ—  ekaṃ samayaṃ bhagavā sāvatthiyaṃ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena āyasmā cūḷapanthako bhagavato avidūre nisinno hoti pallaṅkaṃ ābhujitvā ujuṃ kāyaṃ paṇidhāya parimukhaṃ satiṃ upaṭṭhapetvā.

450. Addasā kho bhagavā āyasmantaṃ cūḷapanthakaṃ avidūre nisinnaṃ pallaṅkaṃ ābhujitvā ujuṃ kāyaṃ paṇidhāya parimukhaṃ satiṃ upaṭṭhapetvā.

451. Atha kho bhagavā etamatthaṃ viditvā tāyaṃ velāyaṃ imaṃ udānaṃ udānesi—

452. _“Ṭhitena kāyena ṭhitena cetasā,_  
_Tiṭṭhaṃ nisinno uda vā sayāno;_  
_Etaṃ satiṃ bhikkhu adhiṭṭhahāno,_  
_Labhetha pubbāpariyaṃ visesaṃ;_  
_Laddhāna pubbāpariyaṃ visesaṃ,_  
_Adassanaṃ maccurājassa gacche”ti. (61)_  


===

453. Dasamaṃ.



===

454. Soṇavaggo pañcamo.

455. Tassuddānaṃ



456. _Piyo appāyukā kuṭṭhī,_  
_kumārakā uposatho;_  
_Soṇo ca revato bhedo,_  
_sadhāya panthakena cāti._

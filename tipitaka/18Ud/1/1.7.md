# 1.7 Ajakalāpakasutta

* Bodhivagga

* Ajakalāpakasutta

(7.)

33\. Evaṃ me sutaṃ—  ekaṃ samayaṃ bhagavā pāvāyaṃ viharati ajakalāpake cetiye, ajakalāpakassa yakkhassa bhavane. Tena kho pana samayena bhagavā rattandhakāratimisāyaṃ abbhokāse nisinno hoti; devo ca ekamekaṃ phusāyati. Atha kho ajakalāpako yakkho bhagavato bhayaṃ chambhitattaṃ lomahaṃsaṃ uppādetukāmo yena bhagavā tenupasaṅkami; upasaṅkamitvā bhagavato avidūre tikkhattuṃ “akkulo pakkulo”ti akkulapakkulikaṃ akāsi—  “eso te, samaṇa, pisāco”ti.

34\. Atha kho bhagavā etamatthaṃ viditvā tāyaṃ velāyaṃ imaṃ udānaṃ udānesi—

35\. _“Yadā sakesu dhammesu,_  
_pāragū hoti brāhmaṇo;_  
_Atha etaṃ pisācañca,_  
_pakkulañcātivattatī”ti. (7)_  


---

36\. Sattamaṃ.

# 2.2.8 Uddhambhāgiyasutta

### Dutiyapaṇṇāsaka

### Satipaṭṭhānavagga

### Uddhambhāgiyasutta

(70.)

457. “Pañcimāni, bhikkhave, uddhambhāgiyāni saṃyojanāni. Katamāni pañca? Rūparāgo, arūparāgo, māno, uddhaccaṃ, avijjā—  imāni kho, bhikkhave, pañcuddhambhāgiyāni saṃyojanāni.

458. Imesaṃ kho, bhikkhave, pañcannaṃ uddhambhāgiyānaṃ saṃyojanānaṃ pahānāya…pe…  ime cattāro satipaṭṭhānā bhāvetabbā”ti.

===

459. Aṭṭhamaṃ.

# 8.1.8 Sūcilomasutta

### Lakkhaṇasaṃyutta

### Paṭhamavagga

### Sūcilomasutta

(209.)

1175. “Idhāhaṃ, āvuso, gijjhakūṭā pabbatā orohanto addasaṃ sūcilomaṃ purisaṃ vehāsaṃ gacchantaṃ. Tassa tā sūciyo uppatitvā uppatitvā tasseva kāye nipatanti. So sudaṃ aṭṭassaraṃ karoti…pe…  eso, bhikkhave, satto imasmiṃyeva rājagahe sūto ahosi…pe… .

===

1176. Aṭṭhamaṃ.

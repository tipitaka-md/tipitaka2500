# 8.1.3 Piṇḍasutta

### Lakkhaṇasaṃyutta

### Paṭhamavagga

### Piṇḍasutta

(204.)

1165. “Idhāhaṃ, āvuso, gijjhakūṭā pabbatā orohanto addasaṃ maṃsapiṇḍaṃ vehāsaṃ gacchantaṃ. Tamenaṃ gijjhāpi kākāpi kulalāpi anupatitvā anupatitvā vitacchenti virājenti. Sā sudaṃ aṭṭassaraṃ karoti…pe…  eso, bhikkhave, satto imasmiṃyeva rājagahe sākuṇiko ahosi…pe… .

===

1166. Tatiyaṃ.

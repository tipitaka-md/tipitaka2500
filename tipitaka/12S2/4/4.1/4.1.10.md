# 4.1.10 Puggalasutta

### Anamataggasaṃyutta

### Paṭhamavagga

### Puggalasutta

(133.)

853. Ekaṃ samayaṃ bhagavā rājagahe viharati gijjhakūṭe pabbate. Tatra kho bhagavā bhikkhū āmantesi—  “bhikkhavo”ti. “Bhadante”ti te bhikkhū bhagavato paccassosuṃ. Bhagavā etadavoca—

854. “Anamataggoyaṃ, bhikkhave, saṃsāro…pe…  ekapuggalassa, bhikkhave, kappaṃ sandhāvato saṃsarato siyā evaṃ mahā aṭṭhikaṅkalo aṭṭhipuñjo aṭṭhirāsi yathāyaṃ vepullo pabbato, sace saṃhārako assa, sambhatañca na vinasseyya. Taṃ kissa hetu? Anamataggoyaṃ, bhikkhave, saṃsāro…pe…  alaṃ vimuccitun”ti.

855. Idamavoca bhagavā. Idaṃ vatvāna sugato athāparaṃ etadavoca satthā—

856. _“Ekassekena kappena,_  
_puggalassaṭṭhisañcayo;_  
_Siyā pabbatasamo rāsi,_  
_iti vuttaṃ mahesinā._  


857. _So kho panāyaṃ akkhāto,_  
_vepullo pabbato mahā;_  
_Uttaro gijjhakūṭassa,_  
_magadhānaṃ giribbaje._  


858. _Yato ca ariyasaccāni,_  
_sammappaññāya passati;_  
_Dukkhaṃ dukkhasamuppādaṃ,_  
_dukkhassa ca atikkamaṃ;_  
_Ariyaṃ caṭṭhaṅgikaṃ maggaṃ,_  
_dukkhūpasamagāminaṃ._  


859. _Sa sattakkhattuṃparamaṃ,_  
_sandhāvitvāna puggalo;_  
_Dukkhassantakaro hoti,_  
_sabbasaṃyojanakkhayā”ti._

862. Tassuddānaṃ



863. _Tiṇakaṭṭhañca pathavī,_  
_assu khīrañca pabbataṃ;_  
_Sāsapā sāvakā gaṅgā,_  
_daṇḍo ca puggalena cāti._

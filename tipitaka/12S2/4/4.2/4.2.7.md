# 4.2.7 Bhaginisutta

### Anamataggasaṃyutta

### Dutiyavagga

### Bhaginisutta

(140.)

879. Sāvatthiyaṃ viharati…pe…  “na so, bhikkhave, satto sulabharūpo yo nabhaginibhūtapubbo…pe…  alaṃ vimuccitun”ti.

===

880. Sattamaṃ.

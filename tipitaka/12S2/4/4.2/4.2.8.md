# 4.2.8 Puttasutta

* Anamataggasaṃyutta

* Dutiyavagga

* Puttasutta

(141.)

881\. Sāvatthiyaṃ viharati…pe…  “na so, bhikkhave, satto sulabharūpo yo naputtabhūtapubbo…pe…  alaṃ vimuccitun”ti.

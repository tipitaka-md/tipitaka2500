# 4.2.5 Pitusutta

### Anamataggasaṃyutta

### Dutiyavagga

### Pitusutta

(138.)

875\. Sāvatthiyaṃ viharati. “Anamataggoyaṃ, bhikkhave, saṃsāro…pe…  na so, bhikkhave, satto sulabharūpo yo napitābhūtapubbo…pe…  alaṃ vimuccitun”ti.

---

876\. Pañcamaṃ.

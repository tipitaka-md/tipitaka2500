# 6.3.6 Dutiyasamaṇabrāhmaṇasutta

### Lābhasakkārasaṃyutta

### Tatiyavagga

### Dutiyasamaṇabrāhmaṇasutta

(175.)

1070. Sāvatthiyaṃ viharati. “Ye hi keci, bhikkhave, samaṇā vā brāhmaṇā vā lābhasakkārasilokassa samudayañca atthaṅgamañca assādañca ādīnavañca nissaraṇañca yathābhūtaṃ nappajānanti…pe…  pajānanti…pe…  sayaṃ abhiññā sacchikatvā upasampajja viharantī”ti.

===

1071. Chaṭṭhaṃ.

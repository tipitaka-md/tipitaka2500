# 6.1.6 Asanisutta

### Lābhasakkārasaṃyutta

### Paṭhamavagga

### Asanisutta

(162.)

1033. Sāvatthiyaṃ viharati. “Dāruṇo, bhikkhave, lābhasakkārasiloko…pe…  adhigamāya. Kaṃ, bhikkhave, asanivicakkaṃ āgacchatu, sekhaṃ appattamānasaṃ lābhasakkārasiloko anupāpuṇātu.

1034. Asanivicakkanti kho, bhikkhave, lābhasakkārasilokassetaṃ adhivacanaṃ. Evaṃ dāruṇo kho, bhikkhave, lābhasakkārasiloko…pe…  evañhi vo, bhikkhave, sikkhitabban”ti.

---

1035. Chaṭṭhaṃ.

# 1.16 Hetuduka, āsavasāsavaduka

### Hetuduka, Duka

### Hetuduka, āsavasāsavaduka

(38.)

111. Hetuṃ āsavañceva sāsavañca dhammaṃ paṭicca hetu āsavo ceva sāsavo ca dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

112. Hetuyā pañca…pe…  avigate pañca. (Sabbattha vitthāro.)

(39.)

113. Hetuṃ sāsavañceva no ca āsavaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca hetu sāsavo ceva no ca āsavo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

114. Hetuyā nava…pe…  avigate nava. (Sabbattha vitthāro.)

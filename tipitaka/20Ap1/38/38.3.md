# 38.3 Tīṇuppalamāliyattheraapadāna

* Bodhivandanavagga

* Tīṇuppalamāliyattheraapadāna

(13.)

4112\. _“Candabhāgānadītīre,_  
_ahosiṃ vānaro tadā;_  
_Addasaṃ virajaṃ buddhaṃ,_  
_nisinnaṃ pabbatantare._  


(14.)

4113\. _Obhāsentaṃ disā sabbā,_  
_sālarājaṃva phullitaṃ;_  
_Lakkhaṇabyañjanūpetaṃ,_  
_disvānattamano ahaṃ._  


(15.)

4114\. _Udaggacitto sumano,_  
_pītiyā haṭṭhamānaso;_  
_Tīṇi uppalapupphāni,_  
_matthake abhiropayiṃ._  


(16.)

4115\. _Pūjayitvāna pupphāni,_  
_phussassāhaṃ mahesino;_  
_Sagāravo bhavitvāna,_  
_pakkāmiṃ uttarāmukho._  


(17.)

4116\. _Gacchanto paṭikuṭiko,_  
_vippasannena cetasā;_  
_Selantare patitvāna,_  
_pāpuṇiṃ jīvitakkhayaṃ._  


(18.)

4117\. _Tena kammena sukatena,_  
_cetanāpaṇidhīhi ca;_  
_Jahitvā purimaṃ jātiṃ,_  
_tāvatiṃsamagacchahaṃ._  


(19.)

4118\. _Satānaṃ tīṇikkhattuñca,_  
_devarajjamakārayiṃ;_  
_Satānaṃ pañcakkhattuñca,_  
_cakkavattī ahosahaṃ._  


(20.)

4119\. _Dvenavute ito kappe,_  
_yaṃ pupphamabhipūjayiṃ;_  
_Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,_  
_buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ._  


(21.)

4120\. _Paṭisambhidā catasso,_  
_…pe…_  
_kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”._  


4121\. Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā tīṇuppalamāliyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

---

4122\. Tīṇuppalamāliyattherassāpadānaṃ tatiyaṃ.

# 38.5 Sattapaṇṇiyattheraapadāna

* Bodhivandanavagga

* Sattapaṇṇiyattheraapadāna

(28.)

4131\. _“Sumano nāma sambuddho,_  
_uppajji lokanāyako;_  
_Pasannacitto sumano,_  
_sattapaṇṇimapūjayiṃ._  


(29.)

4132\. _Satasahassito kappe,_  
_Sattapaṇṇimapūjayiṃ;_  
_Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,_  
_Sattapaṇṇipūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ._  


(30.)

4133\. _Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,_  
_…pe…_  
_viharāmi anāsavo._  


(31.)

4134\. _Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,_  
_…pe…_  
_kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ._  


(32.)

4135\. _Paṭisambhidā catasso,_  
_…pe…_  
_kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”._  


4136\. Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā sattapaṇṇiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

---

4137\. Sattapaṇṇiyattherassāpadānaṃ pañcamaṃ.

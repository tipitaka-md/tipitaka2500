# 51.6 Mātuluṅgaphaladāyakattheraapadāna

### Kaṇikāravagga

### Mātuluṅgaphaladāyakattheraapadāna

(68.)

6645. _“Kaṇikāraṃva jalitaṃ,_  
_puṇṇamāyeva candimaṃ;_  
_Jalantaṃ dīparukkhaṃva,_  
_addasaṃ lokanāyakaṃ._  


(69.)

6646. _Mātuluṅgaphalaṃ gayha,_  
_adāsiṃ satthuno ahaṃ;_  
_Dakkhiṇeyyassa vīrassa,_  
_pasanno sehi pāṇibhi._  


(70.)

6647. _Ekatiṃse ito kappe,_  
_yaṃ phalaṃ adadiṃ tadā;_  
_Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,_  
_phaladānassidaṃ phalaṃ._  


(71.)

6648. _Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,_  
_…pe…_  
_viharāmi anāsavo._  


(72.)

6649. _Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,_  
_…pe…_  
_kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ._  


(73.)

6650. _Paṭisambhidā catasso,_  
_…pe…_  
_kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”._  


6651. Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā mātuluṅgaphaladāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

===

6652. Mātuluṅgaphaladāyakattherassāpadānaṃ chaṭṭhaṃ.

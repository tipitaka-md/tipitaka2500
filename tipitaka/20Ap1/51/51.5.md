# 51.5 Pādaphaliyattheraapadāna

### Kaṇikāravagga

### Pādaphaliyattheraapadāna

(63.)

6638. _“Suvaṇṇavaṇṇaṃ sambuddhaṃ,_  
_Āhutīnaṃ paṭiggahaṃ;_  
_Rathiyaṃ paṭipajjantaṃ,_  
_Pādaphalaṃ adāsahaṃ._  


(64.)

6639. _Ekanavutito kappe,_  
_yaṃ phalaṃ adadiṃ tadā;_  
_Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,_  
_phaladānassidaṃ phalaṃ._  


(65.)

6640. _Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,_  
_…pe…_  
_viharāmi anāsavo._  


(66.)

6641. _Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,_  
_…pe…_  
_kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ._  


(67.)

6642. _Paṭisambhidā catasso,_  
_…pe…_  
_kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”._  


6643. Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā pādaphaliyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

===

6644. Pādaphaliyattherassāpadānaṃ pañcamaṃ.

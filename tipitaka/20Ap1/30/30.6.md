# 30.6 Pādapūjakattheraapadāna

### Citakapūjakavagga

### Pādapūjakattheraapadāna

(27.)

3394. _“Pabbate himavantamhi,_  
_ahosiṃ kinnaro tadā;_  
_Addasaṃ virajaṃ buddhaṃ,_  
_pītaraṃsiṃva bhāṇumaṃ._  


(28.)

3395. _Upetaṃ tamahaṃ buddhaṃ,_  
_vipassiṃ lokanāyakaṃ;_  
_Candanaṃ tagarañcāpi,_  
_pāde osiñcahaṃ tadā._  


(29.)

3396. _Ekanavutito kappe,_  
_yaṃ pādaṃ abhipūjayiṃ;_  
_Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,_  
_pādapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ._  


(30.)

3397. _Paṭisambhidā catasso,_  
_…pe…_  
_kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”._  


3398. Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā pādapūjako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

===

3399. Pādapūjakattherassāpadānaṃ chaṭṭhaṃ.

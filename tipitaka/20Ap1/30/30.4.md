# 30.4 Saddasaññakattheraapadāna

* Citakapūjakavagga

* Saddasaññakattheraapadāna

(17.)

3380\. _“Anuggatamhi ādicce,_  
_panādo vipulo ahu;_  
_Buddhaseṭṭhassa lokamhi,_  
_pātubhāvo mahesino._  


(18.)

3381\. _Ghosamassosahaṃ tattha,_  
_na ca passāmi taṃ jinaṃ;_  
_Maraṇañca anuppatto,_  
_buddhasaññamanussariṃ._  


(19.)

3382\. _Catunnavutito kappe,_  
_yaṃ saññamalabhiṃ tadā;_  
_Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,_  
_buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ._  


(20.)

3383\. _Paṭisambhidā catasso,_  
_…pe…_  
_kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”._  


3384\. Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā saddasaññako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

---

3385\. Saddasaññakattherassāpadānaṃ catutthaṃ.

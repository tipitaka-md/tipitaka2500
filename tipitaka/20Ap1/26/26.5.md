# 26.5 Sattapāṭaliyattheraapadāna

### Thomakavagga

### Sattapāṭaliyattheraapadāna

(17.)

3118. _“Kaṇikāraṃva jotantaṃ,_  
_nisinnaṃ pabbatantare;_  
_Satta pāṭalipupphāni,_  
_buddhassa abhiropayiṃ._  


(18.)

3119. _Catunnavutito kappe,_  
_yaṃ pupphamabhiropayiṃ;_  
_Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,_  
_buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ._  


(19.)

3120. _Paṭisambhidā catasso,_  
_…pe…_  
_kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”._  


3121. Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā sattapāṭaliyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

===

3122. Sattapāṭaliyattherassāpadānaṃ pañcamaṃ.

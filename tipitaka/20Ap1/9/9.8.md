# 9.8 Saddasaññakattheraapadāna

* Timiravagga

* Saddasaññakattheraapadāna

(43.)

1742\. _“Pabbate himavantamhi,_  
_vasāmi paṇṇasanthare;_  
_Phussassa dhammaṃ bhaṇato,_  
_sadde cittaṃ pasādayiṃ._  


(44.)

1743\. _Dvenavute ito kappe,_  
_yaṃ kammamakariṃ tadā;_  
_Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,_  
_puññakammassidaṃ phalaṃ._  


(45.)

1744\. _Paṭisambhidā catasso,_  
_…pe…_  
_kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”._  


1745\. Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā saddasaññako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

---

1746\. Saddasaññakattherassāpadānaṃ aṭṭhamaṃ.

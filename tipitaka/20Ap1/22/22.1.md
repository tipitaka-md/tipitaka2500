# 22.1 Hatthidāyakattheraapadāna

### Hatthivagga

### Hatthidāyakattheraapadāna

(1.)

2822. _“Siddhatthassa bhagavato,_  
_dvipadindassa tādino;_  
_Nāgaseṭṭho mayā dinno,_  
_īsādanto urūḷhavā._  


(2.)

2823. _Uttamatthaṃ anubhomi,_  
_santipadamanuttaraṃ;_  
_Nāgadānaṃ mayā dinnaṃ,_  
_sabbalokahitesino._  


(3.)

2824. _Catunnavutito kappe,_  
_yaṃ nāga madadiṃ tadā;_  
_Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,_  
_nāgadānassidaṃ phalaṃ._  


(4.)

2825. _Aṭṭhasattatikappamhi,_  
_soḷasāsiṃsu khattiyā;_  
_Samantapāsādikā nāma,_  
_cakkavattī mahabbalā._  


(5.)

2826. _Paṭisambhidā catasso,_  
_vimokkhāpi ca aṭṭhime;_  
_Chaḷabhiññā sacchikatā,_  
_kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”._  


2827. Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā hatthidāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

===

2828. Hatthidāyakattherassāpadānaṃ paṭhamaṃ.

# 40.1.8.35 Bhisiānisaṃsa

* Pilindavacchavagga

* Pilindavacchattheraapadāna

* Dānānisaṃsakathā

* Bhisiānisaṃsa

(175.)

4472\. _Bhisiyo sugate datvā,_  
_saṃghe gaṇavaruttame;_  
_Chānisaṃse anubhomi,_  
_kammānucchavike mama._  


(176.)

4473\. _Samasugattopacito,_  
_muduko cārudassano;_  
_Labhāmi ñāṇaparivāraṃ,_  
_bhisidānassidaṃ phalaṃ._  


(177.)

4474\. _Tūlikā vikatikāyo,_  
_kaṭṭissā cittakā bahū;_  
_Varapotthake kambale ca,_  
_labhāmi vividhe ahaṃ._  


(178.)

4475\. _Pāvārike ca muduke,_  
_mudukājinaveṇiyo;_  
_Labhāmi vividhatthāre,_  
_bhisidānassidaṃ phalaṃ._  


(179.)

4476\. _Yato sarāmi attānaṃ,_  
_yato pattosmi viññutaṃ;_  
_Atuccho jhānamañcomhi,_  
_bhisidānassidaṃ phalaṃ._

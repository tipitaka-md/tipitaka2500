# 40.1.8.26 Āyogānisaṃsa

* Pilindavacchavagga

* Pilindavacchattheraapadāna

* Dānānisaṃsakathā

* Āyogānisaṃsa

(156.)

4453\. _Āyoge sugate datvā,_  
_saṃghe gaṇavaruttame;_  
_Pañcānisaṃse anubhomi,_  
_kammānucchavike mama._  


(157.)

4454\. _Samādhīsu na kampāmi,_  
_vasī homi samādhisu;_  
_Abhejjapariso homi,_  
_ādeyyavacano sadā;_  
_Jāyati bhogasampatti,_  
_bhave saṃsarato mama._

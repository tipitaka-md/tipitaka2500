# 40.1.8.21 Udakapuñchanānisaṃsa

* Pilindavacchavagga

* Pilindavacchattheraapadāna

* Dānānisaṃsakathā

* Udakapuñchanānisaṃsa

(147.)

4444\. _Udakapuñchanacoḷe,_  
_datvā buddhe gaṇuttame;_  
_Pañcānisaṃse anubhomi,_  
_kammānucchavike mama._  


(148.)

4445\. _Suvaṇṇavaṇṇo virajo,_  
_sappabhāso patāpavā;_  
_Siniddhaṃ hoti me gattaṃ,_  
_rajojallaṃ na limpati;_  
_Ime guṇe paṭilabhe,_  
_tassa nissandato ahaṃ._

# 40.1.8.28 Dīpadhārānisaṃsa

* Pilindavacchavagga

* Pilindavacchattheraapadāna

* Dānānisaṃsakathā

* Dīpadhārānisaṃsa

(160.)

4457\. _Dīpadhāre jine datvā,_  
_saṃghe gaṇavaruttame;_  
_Tīṇānisaṃse anubhomi,_  
_kammānucchavike mama._  


(161.)

4458\. _Jātimā aṅgasampanno,_  
_paññavā buddhasammato;_  
_Ime guṇe paṭilabhe,_  
_tassa nissandato ahaṃ._

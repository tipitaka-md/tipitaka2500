# 40.1.8.42 Dadhiānisaṃsa

* Pilindavacchavagga

* Pilindavacchattheraapadāna

* Dānānisaṃsakathā

* Dadhiānisaṃsa

(197.)

4494\. _Dadhiṃ datvāna sampannaṃ,_  
_buddhe saṃghe gaṇuttame;_  
_Bhuñjāmi amataṃ bhattaṃ,_  
_varaṃ kāyagatāsatiṃ._

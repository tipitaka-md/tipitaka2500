### Saṇḍāsānisaṃsa

(169.)

4466. _Saṇḍāse sugate datvā,_  
_saṃghe gaṇavaruttame;_  
_Kilesabhañjanaṃ ñāṇaṃ,_  
_labhāmi atulaṃ suciṃ._

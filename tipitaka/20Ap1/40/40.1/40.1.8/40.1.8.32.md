# 40.1.8.32 Saṇḍāsānisaṃsa

### Pilindavacchavagga

### Pilindavacchattheraapadāna

### Dānānisaṃsakathā

### Saṇḍāsānisaṃsa

(169.)

4466. _Saṇḍāse sugate datvā,_  
_saṃghe gaṇavaruttame;_  
_Kilesabhañjanaṃ ñāṇaṃ,_  
_labhāmi atulaṃ suciṃ._

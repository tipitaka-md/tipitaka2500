# 40.1.8.13 Aṃsabaddhānisaṃsa

### Pilindavacchavagga

### Pilindavacchattheraapadāna

### Dānānisaṃsakathā

### Aṃsabaddhānisaṃsa

(125.)

4422. _Aṃsabaddhe jine datvā,_  
_saṃghe gaṇavaruttame;_  
_Tīṇānisaṃse anubhomi,_  
_kammānucchavike mama._  


(126.)

4423. _Saddhamme gādhaṃ vindāmi,_  
_Sarāmi dutiyaṃ bhavaṃ;_  
_Sabbattha succhavī homi,_  
_Tassa nissandato ahaṃ._

# 40.1.8.24 Kuñcikānisaṃsa

* Pilindavacchavagga

* Pilindavacchattheraapadāna

* Dānānisaṃsakathā

* Kuñcikānisaṃsa

(154.)

4451\. _Kuñcike sugate datvā,_  
_saṃghe gaṇavaruttame;_  
_Dhammadvāravivaraṇaṃ,_  
_labhāmi ñāṇakuñcikaṃ._

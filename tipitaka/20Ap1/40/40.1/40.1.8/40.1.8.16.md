# 40.1.8.16 Bhājanānisaṃsa

### Pilindavacchavagga

### Pilindavacchattheraapadāna

### Dānānisaṃsakathā

### Bhājanānisaṃsa

(132.)

4429. _Bhājane paribhoge ca,_  
_datvā buddhe gaṇuttame;_  
_Tīṇānisaṃse anubhomi,_  
_kammānucchavike mama._  


(133.)

4430. _Soṇṇamaye maṇimaye,_  
_athopi phalikāmaye;_  
_Lohitaṅgamaye ceva,_  
_labhāmi bhājane ahaṃ._  


(134.)

4431. _Bhariyā dāsadāsī ca,_  
_hatthissarathapattike;_  
_Itthī patibbatā ceva,_  
_paribhogāni sabbadā._  


(135.)

4432. _Vijjā mantapade ceva,_  
_vividhe āgame bahū;_  
_Sabbaṃ sippaṃ nisāmemi,_  
_paribhogāni sabbadā._

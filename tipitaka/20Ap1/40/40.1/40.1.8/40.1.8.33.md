# 40.1.8.33 Natthukānisaṃsa

* Pilindavacchavagga

* Pilindavacchattheraapadāna

* Dānānisaṃsakathā

* Natthukānisaṃsa

(170.)

4467\. _Natthuke sugate datvā,_  
_saṃghe gaṇavaruttame;_  
_Aṭṭhānisaṃse anubhomi,_  
_kammānucchavike mama._  


(171.)

4468\. _Saddhaṃ sīlaṃ hiriñcāpi,_  
_atha ottappiyaṃ guṇaṃ;_  
_Sutaṃ cāgañca khantiñca,_  
_paññaṃ me aṭṭhamaṃ guṇaṃ._

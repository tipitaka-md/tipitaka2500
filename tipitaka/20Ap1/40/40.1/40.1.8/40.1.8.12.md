# 40.1.8.12 Sūcigharānisaṃsa

### Pilindavacchavagga

### Pilindavacchattheraapadāna

### Dānānisaṃsakathā

### Sūcigharānisaṃsa

(123.)

4420. _Sūcighare sugate datvā,_  
_saṃghe gaṇavaruttame;_  
_Tīṇānisaṃse anubhomi,_  
_kammānucchavike mama._  


(124.)

4421. _Cetosukhaṃ kāyasukhaṃ,_  
_iriyāpathajaṃ sukhaṃ;_  
_Ime guṇe paṭilabhe,_  
_tassa nissandato ahaṃ._

# 40.1.8.31 Pipphalānisaṃsa

### Pilindavacchavagga

### Pilindavacchattheraapadāna

### Dānānisaṃsakathā

### Pipphalānisaṃsa

(168.)

4465. _Taṇudhāre sunisite,_  
_saṃghe datvāna pipphale;_  
_Kilesakantanaṃ ñāṇaṃ,_  
_labhāmi atulaṃ suciṃ._

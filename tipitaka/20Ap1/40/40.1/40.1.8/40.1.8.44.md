# 40.1.8.44 Rasānisaṃsa

### Pilindavacchavagga

### Pilindavacchattheraapadāna

### Dānānisaṃsakathā

### Rasānisaṃsa

(199.)

4496. _Yathābhūtaṃ rasaṃ datvā,_  
_buddhe saṃghe gaṇuttame;_  
_Caturo phale anubhomi,_  
_kammānucchavike mama._

# 40.1.6 Dānakathā

* Pilindavacchavagga

* Pilindavacchattheraapadāna

* Dānakathā

(66.)

4363\. _Bhikkhuno ekamekassa,_  
_Paccekaṃ pattamadāsahaṃ;_  
_Jahiṃsu sumbhakaṃ pattaṃ,_  
_Lohapattaṃ adhārayuṃ._  


(67.)

4364\. _Sattarattindivaṃ buddho,_  
_nisīdi pupphamaṇḍape;_  
_Bodhayanto bahū satte,_  
_dhammacakkaṃ pavattayi._  


(68.)

4365\. _Dhammacakkaṃ pavattento,_  
_heṭṭhato pupphamaṇḍape;_  
_Cullāsītisahassānaṃ,_  
_dhammābhisamayo ahu._  


(69.)

4366\. _Sattame divase patte,_  
_padumuttaro mahāmuni;_  
_Chattacchāyāyamāsīno,_  
_imā gāthā abhāsatha._

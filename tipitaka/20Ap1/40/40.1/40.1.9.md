# 40.1.9 Sādhāraṇānisaṃsa

### Pilindavacchavagga

### Pilindavacchattheraapadāna

### Sādhāraṇānisaṃsa

(205.)

4502. _Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,_  
_bhavā sabbe samūhatā;_  
_Nāgova bandhanaṃ chetvā,_  
_viharāmi anāsavo._  


(206.)

4503. _Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,_  
_mama buddhassa santike;_  
_Tisso vijjā anuppattā,_  
_kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ._  


(207.)

4504. _Paṭisambhidā catasso,_  
_vimokkhāpi ca aṭṭhime;_  
_Chaḷabhiññā sacchikatā,_  
_kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”._  


4505. Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā pilindavaccho thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

---

4506. Pilindavacchattherassāpadānaṃ paṭhamaṃ.

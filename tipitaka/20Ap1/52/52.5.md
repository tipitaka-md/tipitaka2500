# 52.5 Nāgapupphiyattheraapadāna

### Phaladāyakavagga

### Nāgapupphiyattheraapadāna

(23.)

6721. _“Suvaṇṇavaṇṇaṃ sambuddhaṃ,_  
_Āhutīnaṃ paṭiggahaṃ;_  
_Rathiyaṃ paṭipajjantaṃ,_  
_Nāgapupphaṃ apūjayiṃ._  


(24.)

6722. _Ekanavutito kappe,_  
_yaṃ pupphamabhipūjayiṃ;_  
_Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,_  
_buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ._  


(25.)

6723. _Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,_  
_…pe…_  
_viharāmi anāsavo._  


(26.)

6724. _Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,_  
_…pe…_  
_kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ._  


(27.)

6725. _Paṭisambhidā catasso,_  
_…pe…_  
_kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”._  


6726. Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā nāgapupphiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

===

6727. Nāgapupphiyattherassāpadānaṃ pañcamaṃ.

# 39.10.11

* Avaṭaphalavagga

* Pubbakammapilotikabuddhaapadāna

(91.)

4285\. _Tikicchako ahaṃ āsiṃ,_  
_seṭṭhiputtaṃ virecayiṃ;_  
_Tena kammavipākena,_  
_hoti pakkhandikā mama._

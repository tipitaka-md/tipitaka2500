# 39.10.10

### Avaṭaphalavagga

### Pubbakammapilotikabuddhaapadāna

(90.)

4284\. _Nibbuddhe vattamānamhi,_  
_mallaputtaṃ niheṭhayiṃ;_  
_Tena kammavipākena,_  
_piṭṭhidukkhaṃ ahū mama._

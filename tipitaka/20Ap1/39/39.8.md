# 39.8 Panasaphaladāyakattheraapadāna

### Avaṭaphalavagga

### Panasaphaladāyakattheraapadāna

(43.)

4233. _“Ajjuno nāma sambuddho,_  
_himavante vasī tadā;_  
_Caraṇena ca sampanno,_  
_samādhikusalo muni._  


(44.)

4234. _Kumbhamattaṃ gahetvāna,_  
_panasaṃ jīvajīvakaṃ;_  
_Chattapaṇṇe ṭhapetvāna,_  
_adāsiṃ satthuno ahaṃ._  


(45.)

4235. _Ekattiṃse ito kappe,_  
_yaṃ phalamadadiṃ tadā;_  
_Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,_  
_phaladānassidaṃ phalaṃ._  


(46.)

4236. _Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,_  
_…pe…_  
_viharāmi anāsavo._  


(47.)

4237. _Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,_  
_…pe…_  
_kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ._  


(48.)

4238. _Paṭisambhidā catasso,_  
_…pe…_  
_kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”._  


4239. Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā panasaphaladāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

---

4240. Panasaphaladāyakattherassāpadānaṃ aṭṭhamaṃ.

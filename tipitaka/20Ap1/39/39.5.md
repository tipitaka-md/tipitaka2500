# 39.5 Phārusaphaladāyakattheraapadāna

* Avaṭaphalavagga

* Phārusaphaladāyakattheraapadāna

(26.)

4210\. _“Suvaṇṇavaṇṇaṃ sambuddhaṃ,_  
_Āhutīnaṃ paṭiggahaṃ;_  
_Rathiyaṃ paṭipajjantaṃ,_  
_Phārusaphalamadāsahaṃ._  


(27.)

4211\. _Ekanavutito kappe,_  
_yaṃ phalamadadiṃ ahaṃ;_  
_Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,_  
_phaladānassidaṃ phalaṃ._  


(28.)

4212\. _Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,_  
_…pe…_  
_viharāmi anāsavo._  


(29.)

4213\. _Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,_  
_…pe…_  
_kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ._  


(30.)

4214\. _Paṭisambhidā catasso,_  
_…pe…_  
_kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”._  


4215\. Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā phārusaphaladāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

---

4216\. Phārusaphaladāyakattherassāpadānaṃ pañcamaṃ.

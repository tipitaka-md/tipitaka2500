# 25.8 Sattalipupphapūjakattheraapadāna

* Tuvaradāyakavagga

* Sattalipupphapūjakattheraapadāna

(27.)

3074\. _“Satta sattalipupphāni,_  
_sīse katvānahaṃ tadā;_  
_Buddhassa abhiropesiṃ,_  
_vessabhumhi naruttame._  


(28.)

3075\. _Ekattiṃse ito kappe,_  
_yaṃ pupphamabhipūjayiṃ;_  
_Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,_  
_buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ._  


(29.)

3076\. _Paṭisambhidā catasso,_  
_…pe…_  
_kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”._  


3077\. Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā sattalipupphapūjako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

---

3078\. Sattalipupphapūjakattherassāpadānaṃ aṭṭhamaṃ.

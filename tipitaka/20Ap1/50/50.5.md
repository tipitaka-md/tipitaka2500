# 50.5 Upaḍḍhadussadāyakattheraapadāna

### Kiṅkaṇipupphavagga

### Upaḍḍhadussadāyakattheraapadāna

(31.)

6468. _“Padumuttarabhagavato,_  
_sujāto nāma sāvako;_  
_Paṃsukūlaṃ gavesanto,_  
_saṅkāre carate tadā._  


(32.)

6469. _Nagare haṃsavatiyā,_  
_paresaṃ bhatako ahaṃ;_  
_Upaḍḍhadussaṃ datvāna,_  
_sirasā abhivādayiṃ._  


(33.)

6470. _Tena kammena sukatena,_  
_cetanāpaṇidhīhi ca;_  
_Jahitvā mānusaṃ dehaṃ,_  
_tāvatiṃsamagacchahaṃ._  


(34.)

6471. _Tettiṃsakkhattuṃ devindo,_  
_devarajjamakārayiṃ;_  
_Sattasattatikkhattuñca,_  
_cakkavattī ahosahaṃ._  


(35.)

6472. _Padesarajjaṃ vipulaṃ,_  
_gaṇanāto asaṅkhiyaṃ;_  
_Upaḍḍhadussadānena,_  
_modāmi akutobhayo._  


(36.)

6473. _Icchamāno cahaṃ ajja,_  
_sakānanaṃ sapabbataṃ;_  
_Khomadussehi chādeyyaṃ,_  
_aḍḍhadussassidaṃ phalaṃ._  


(37.)

6474. _Satasahassito kappe,_  
_yaṃ dānamadadiṃ tadā;_  
_Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,_  
_aḍḍhadussassidaṃ phalaṃ._  


(38.)

6475. _Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,_  
_…pe…_  
_viharāmi anāsavo._  


(39.)

6476. _Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,_  
_…pe…_  
_kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ._  


(40.)

6477. _Paṭisambhidā catasso,_  
_…pe…_  
_kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”._  


6478. Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā upaḍḍhadussadāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

===

6479. Upaḍḍhadussadāyakattherassāpadānaṃ pañcamaṃ.

# 42.9 Patthodanadāyakattheraapadāna

### Bhaddālivagga

### Patthodanadāyakattheraapadāna

(170.)

5435. _“Vanacārī pure āsiṃ,_  
_satataṃ vanakammiko;_  
_Patthodanaṃ gahetvāna,_  
_kammantaṃ agamāsahaṃ._  


(171.)

5436. _Tatthaddasāsiṃ sambuddhaṃ,_  
_sayambhuṃ aparājitaṃ;_  
_Vanā piṇḍāya nikkhantaṃ,_  
_disvā cittaṃ pasādayiṃ._  


(172.)

5437. _Parakammāyane yutto,_  
_puññañca me na vijjati;_  
_Ayaṃ patthodano atthi,_  
_bhojayissāmahaṃ muniṃ._  


(173.)

5438. _Patthodanaṃ gahetvāna,_  
_sayambhussa adāsahaṃ;_  
_Mama nijjhāyamānassa,_  
_paribhuñji tadā muni._  


(174.)

5439. _Tena kammena sukatena,_  
_cetanāpaṇidhīhi ca;_  
_Jahitvā mānusaṃ dehaṃ,_  
_tāvatiṃsamagacchahaṃ._  


(175.)

5440. _Chattiṃsakkhattuṃ devindo,_  
_devarajjamakārayiṃ;_  
_Tettiṃsakkhattuṃ rājā ca,_  
_cakkavattī ahosahaṃ._  


(176.)

5441. _Padesarajjaṃ vipulaṃ,_  
_gaṇanāto asaṅkhiyaṃ;_  
_Sukhito yasavā homi,_  
_patthodanassidaṃ phalaṃ._  


(177.)

5442. _Bhavābhave saṃsaranto,_  
_labhāmi amitaṃ dhanaṃ;_  
_Bhoge me ūnatā natthi,_  
_patthodanassidaṃ phalaṃ._  


(178.)

5443. _Nadīsotapaṭibhāgā,_  
_bhogā nibbattare mama;_  
_Parimetuṃ na sakkomi,_  
_patthodanassidaṃ phalaṃ._  


(179.)

5444. _Imaṃ khāda imaṃ bhuñja,_  
_imamhi sayane saya;_  
_Tenāhaṃ sukhito homi,_  
_patthodanassidaṃ phalaṃ._  


(180.)

5445. _Catunnavutito kappe,_  
_yaṃ dānamadadiṃ tadā;_  
_Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,_  
_patthodanassidaṃ phalaṃ._  


(181.)

5446. _Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,_  
_…pe…_  
_viharāmi anāsavo._  


(182.)

5447. _Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,_  
_…pe…_  
_kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ._  


(183.)

5448. _Paṭisambhidā catasso,_  
_…pe…_  
_kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”._  


5449. Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā patthodanadāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

---

5450. Patthodanadāyakattherassāpadānaṃ navamaṃ.

# 37.2 Kakkārupupphiyattheraapadāna

### Mandāravapupphiyavagga

### Kakkārupupphiyattheraapadāna

(5.)

4039. _“Yāmā devā idhāgantvā,_  
_gotamaṃ sirivacchasaṃ;_  
_Kakkārumālaṃ paggayha,_  
_buddhassa abhiropayiṃ._  


(6.)

4040. _Dvenavute ito kappe,_  
_yaṃ buddhamabhipūjayiṃ;_  
_Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,_  
_buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ._  


(7.)

4041. _Paṭisambhidā catasso,_  
_…pe…_  
_kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”._  


4042. Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā kakkārupupphiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

===

4043. Kakkārupupphiyattherassāpadānaṃ dutiyaṃ.

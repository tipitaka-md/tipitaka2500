# 37.9 Timirapupphiyattheraapadāna

### Mandāravapupphiyavagga

### Timirapupphiyattheraapadāna

(31.)

4079. _“Candabhāgānadītīre,_  
_anusotaṃ vajāmahaṃ;_  
_Addasaṃ virajaṃ buddhaṃ,_  
_sālarājaṃva phullitaṃ._  


(32.)

4080. _Pasannacitto sumano,_  
_paccekamunimuttamaṃ;_  
_Gahetvā timiraṃ pupphaṃ,_  
_matthake okiriṃ ahaṃ._  


(33.)

4081. _Ekanavutito kappe,_  
_yaṃ pupphamabhipūjayiṃ;_  
_Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,_  
_buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ._  


(34.)

4082. _Paṭisambhidā catasso,_  
_…pe…_  
_kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”._  


4083. Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā timirapupphiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

===

4084. Timirapupphiyattherassāpadānaṃ navamaṃ.

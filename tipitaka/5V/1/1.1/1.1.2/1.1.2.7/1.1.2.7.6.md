# 1.1.2.7.6 Surusuruvagga

### Bhikkhuvibhaṅga

### Paṭhamabhāga

### Katāpattivāra

### Sekhiyakaṇḍa

### Surusuruvagga

(180.)

546. Anādariyaṃ paṭicca surusurukārakaṃ bhuñjanto ekaṃ āpattiṃ āpajjati. Dukkaṭaṃ. (1)

547. Anādariyaṃ paṭicca hatthanillehakaṃ bhuñjanto ekaṃ āpattiṃ āpajjati. Dukkaṭaṃ. (2)

548. Anādariyaṃ paṭicca pattanillehakaṃ bhuñjanto ekaṃ āpattiṃ āpajjati. Dukkaṭaṃ. (3)

549. Anādariyaṃ paṭicca oṭṭhanillehakaṃ bhuñjanto ekaṃ āpattiṃ āpajjati. Dukkaṭaṃ. (4)

550. Anādariyaṃ paṭicca sāmisena hatthena pānīyathālakaṃ paṭiggaṇhanto ekaṃ āpattiṃ āpajjati. Dukkaṭaṃ. (5)

551. Anādariyaṃ paṭicca sasitthakaṃ pattadhovanaṃ antaraghare chaḍḍento ekaṃ āpattiṃ āpajjati. Dukkaṭaṃ. (6)

552. Anādariyaṃ paṭicca chattapāṇissa dhammaṃ desento ekaṃ āpattiṃ āpajjati. Dukkaṭaṃ. (7)

553. Anādariyaṃ paṭicca daṇḍapāṇissa dhammaṃ desento ekaṃ āpattiṃ āpajjati. Dukkaṭaṃ. (8)

554. Anādariyaṃ paṭicca satthapāṇissa dhammaṃ desento ekaṃ āpattiṃ āpajjati. Dukkaṭaṃ. (9)

555. Anādariyaṃ paṭicca āvudhapāṇissa dhammaṃ desento ekaṃ āpattiṃ āpajjati. Dukkaṭaṃ. (10)

===

556. Surusuruvaggo chaṭṭho.

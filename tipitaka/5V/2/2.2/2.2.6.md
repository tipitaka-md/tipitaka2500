# 2.2.6 Adhikaraṇavāra

### Bhikkhunivibhaṅga

### Dutiyabhāga

### Adhikaraṇavāra

(254.)

1064. Kāyasaṃsaggaṃ sādiyanapaccayā āpattiyo catunnaṃ adhikaraṇānaṃ katamaṃ adhikaraṇaṃ? Kāyasaṃsaggaṃ sādiyanapaccayā āpattiyo catunnaṃ adhikaraṇānaṃ, āpattādhikaraṇaṃ…pe… . (1)

1065. Dadhiṃ viññāpetvā bhuñjanapaccayā āpattiyo catunnaṃ adhikaraṇānaṃ katamaṃ adhikaraṇaṃ? Dadhiṃ viññāpetvā bhuñjanapaccayā āpattiyo catunnaṃ adhikaraṇānaṃ—  āpattādhikaraṇaṃ. (2)

---

1066. Adhikaraṇavāro niṭṭhito chaṭṭho.

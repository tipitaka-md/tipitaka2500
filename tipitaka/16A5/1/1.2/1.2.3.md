# 1.2.3 Saṃkhittasutta

### Paṭhamapaṇṇāsaka

### Balavagga

### Saṃkhittasutta

(13.)

50. “Pañcimāni, bhikkhave, balāni. Katamāni pañca? Saddhābalaṃ, vīriyabalaṃ, satibalaṃ, samādhibalaṃ, paññābalaṃ—  imāni kho, bhikkhave, pañca balānī”ti.

===

51. Tatiyaṃ.

# 6.4.2

### Chaṭṭhapaṇṇāsaka

### Rāgapeyyāla

(304.)

1407. “Rāgassa, bhikkhave, abhiññāya pañca dhammā bhāvetabbā. Katame pañca? Aniccasaññā, anattasaññā, maraṇasaññā, āhāre paṭikūlasaññā, sabbaloke anabhiratasaññā—  rāgassa, bhikkhave, abhiññāya ime pañca dhammā bhāvetabbā”ti. (2)

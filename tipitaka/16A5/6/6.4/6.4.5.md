# 6.4.5

* Chaṭṭhapaṇṇāsaka

* Rāgapeyyāla

(307.)

1410\. “Rāgassa, bhikkhave, abhiññāya pañca dhammā bhāvetabbā. Katame pañca? Saddhābalaṃ, vīriyabalaṃ, satibalaṃ, samādhibalaṃ, paññābalaṃ—  rāgassa, bhikkhave, abhiññāya ime pañca dhammā bhāvetabbā”ti. (5)

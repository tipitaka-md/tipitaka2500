# 2.2.2 Dutiyasaññāsutta

### Dutiyapaṇṇāsaka

### Saññāvagga

### Dutiyasaññāsutta

(62.)

401. “Pañcimā, bhikkhave, saññā bhāvitā bahulīkatā mahapphalā honti mahānisaṃsā amatogadhā amatapariyosānā. Katamā pañca? Aniccasaññā, anattasaññā, maraṇasaññā, āhāre paṭikūlasaññā, sabbaloke anabhiratasaññā—  imā kho, bhikkhave, pañca saññā bhāvitā bahulīkatā mahapphalā honti mahānisaṃsā amatogadhā amatapariyosānā”ti.

---

402. Dutiyaṃ.

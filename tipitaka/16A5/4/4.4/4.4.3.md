# 4.4.3 Rukkhamūlikasutta

### Catutthapaṇṇāsaka

### Araññavagga

### Rukkhamūlikasutta

(183.)

1078\. “Pañcime, bhikkhave, rukkhamūlikā. Katame pañca? Mandattā momūhattā rukkhamūliko hoti…pe…  idamatthitaṃyeva nissāya rukkhamūliko hoti. Ime kho, bhikkhave, pañca rukkhamūlikā”ti.

---

1079\. Tatiyaṃ.

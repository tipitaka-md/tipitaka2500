# 4.4.2 Cīvarasutta

### Catutthapaṇṇāsaka

### Araññavagga

### Cīvarasutta

(182.)

1076. “Pañcime, bhikkhave, paṃsukūlikā. Katame pañca? Mandattā momūhattā paṃsukūliko hoti…pe…  idamatthitaṃyeva nissāya paṃsukūliko hoti. Ime kho, bhikkhave, pañca paṃsukūlikā”ti.

===

1077. Dutiyaṃ.

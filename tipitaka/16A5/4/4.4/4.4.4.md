# 4.4.4 Sosānikasutta

### Catutthapaṇṇāsaka

### Araññavagga

### Sosānikasutta

(184.)

1080. “Pañcime, bhikkhave, sosānikā. Katame pañca? Mandattā momūhattā sosāniko hoti…pe…  idamatthitaṃyeva nissāya sosāniko hoti. Ime kho, bhikkhave, pañca sosānikā”ti.

===

1081. Catutthaṃ.

# 5.2.9 Aggisutta

### Pañcamapaṇṇāsaka

### Akkosakavagga

### Aggisutta

(219.)

1251. “Pañcime, bhikkhave, ādīnavā aggismiṃ. Katame pañca? Acakkhusso, dubbaṇṇakaraṇo, dubbalakaraṇo, saṅgaṇikāpavaḍḍhano, tiracchānakathāpavattaniko hoti. Ime kho, bhikkhave, pañca ādīnavā aggismin”ti.

===

1252. Navamaṃ.

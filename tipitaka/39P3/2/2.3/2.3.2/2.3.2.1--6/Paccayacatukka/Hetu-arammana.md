1322\. Hetusampayuttaṃ akusalaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca hetusampayutto akusalo dhammo uppajjati ārammaṇapaccayā…  tīṇi. (3)

1325\. Hetusampayuttaṃ akusalaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca hetusampayutto akusalo dhammo uppajjati adhipatipaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

1326\.

### Hetu-ārammaṇa

(31.)

1319. Hetusampayuttaṃ akusalaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca hetusampayutto akusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (1)

1320. Hetuvippayuttaṃ akusalaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca hetusampayutto akusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (1)

1321. Hetusampayuttaṃ akusalañca hetuvippayuttaṃ akusalañca dhammaṃ paṭicca hetusampayutto akusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (1)

1322. Hetusampayuttaṃ akusalaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca hetusampayutto akusalo dhammo uppajjati ārammaṇapaccayā…  tīṇi. (3)

1323. Hetuvippayuttaṃ akusalaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca hetusampayutto akusalo dhammo uppajjati ārammaṇapaccayā. (1)

1324. Hetusampayuttaṃ akusalañca hetuvippayuttaṃ akusalañca dhammaṃ paṭicca hetusampayutto akusalo dhammo uppajjati ārammaṇapaccayā. (1)

1325. Hetusampayuttaṃ akusalaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca hetusampayutto akusalo dhammo uppajjati adhipatipaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

(32.)

1326. Hetuyā tīṇi, ārammaṇe pañca, adhipatiyā ekaṃ, anantare pañca, samanantare pañca, sahajāte pañca, aññamaññe pañca, nissaye pañca, upanissaye pañca, purejāte pañca, āsevane pañca, kamme pañca, āhāre pañca, indriye pañca, jhāne pañca, magge pañca, sampayutte pañca, vippayutte pañca, atthiyā pañca…pe…  avigate pañca. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

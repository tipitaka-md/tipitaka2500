### Hetu-ārammaṇa

(35.)

1333. Hetusampayutto akusalo dhammo hetusampayuttassa akusalassa dhammassa hetupaccayena paccayo…  dve.

1334. Hetusampayutto akusalo dhammo hetusampayuttassa akusalassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo…  tīṇi.

1335. Hetuvippayutto akusalo dhammo hetuvippayuttassa akusalassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo…  tīṇi.

1336. Hetusampayutto akusalo ca hetuvippayutto akusalo ca dhammā hetusampayuttassa akusalassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo…  tīṇi. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

(36.)

1337. Hetuyā dve, ārammaṇe nava, adhipatiyā ekaṃ, anantare nava, samanantare nava, sahajāte pañca, aññamaññe pañca, nissaye pañca, upanissaye nava, āsevane nava, kamme tīṇi, āhāre tīṇi, indriye tīṇi, jhāne tīṇi, magge tīṇi, sampayutte pañca, atthiyā pañca…pe…  avigate pañca. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

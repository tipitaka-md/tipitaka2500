# Paccanīyuddhāra

### Hetugocchaka, Kusalattika

### Hetuhetusampayuttaduka, Kusalattika

### Abyākatapada

### Pañhāvāra

### Paccanīyuddhāra

(91.)

1444. Hetu ceva hetusampayutto ca abyākato dhammo hetussa ceva hetusampayuttassa ca abyākatassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo…  sahajātapaccayena paccayo…  upanissayapaccayena paccayo. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

(92.)

1445. Nahetuyā nava, naārammaṇe nava. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

1446. Hetupaccayā naārammaṇe tīṇi. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

1447. Nahetupaccayā ārammaṇe nava. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

1448. (Yathā kusalattike pañhāvārassa anulomampi paccanīyampi anulomapaccanīyampi paccanīyānulomampi gaṇitaṃ, evaṃ gaṇetabbaṃ.)

===

1449. Hetuhetusampayuttadukakusalattikaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.

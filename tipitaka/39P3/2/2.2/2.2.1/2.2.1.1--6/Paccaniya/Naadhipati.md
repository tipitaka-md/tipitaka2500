(3.)

1246. Sahetukaṃ kusalaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca sahetuko kusalo dhammo uppajjati naadhipatipaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

1249. Naadhipatipaccayā hetuyā ekaṃ. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

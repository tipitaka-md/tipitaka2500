### Hetu

(67.)

1399. Hetu ceva sahetuko ca abyākato dhammo hetussa ceva sahetukassa ca abyākatassa dhammassa hetupaccayena paccayo. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

(68.)

1400. Hetuyā tīṇi, ārammaṇe nava, adhipatiyā nava, anantare nava, samanantare nava, sahajāte nava, aññamaññe nava, nissaye nava, upanissaye nava, āsevane nava, kamme tīṇi, vipāke nava, āhāre tīṇi, indriye nava, jhāne tīṇi, magge sampayutte nava…pe…  avigate nava. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

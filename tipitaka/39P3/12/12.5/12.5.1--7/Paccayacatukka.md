# Paccayacatukka

### Kilesagocchaka, Kusalattika

### Saṃkilesasaṃkilesikaduka, Kusalattika

### Paṭiccādivāra

### Paccayacatukka

(20.)

2346. Saṃkilesikañceva no ca kilesaṃ kusalaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca saṃkilesiko ceva no ca kileso kusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

2347. Hetuyā ekaṃ, ārammaṇe ekaṃ…pe…  avigate ekaṃ. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

2348. (Sahajātavārepi…pe…  pañhāvārepi sabbattha ekaṃ.)

(21.)

2349. Kilesañceva saṃkilesikañca akusalaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca kileso ceva saṃkilesiko ca akusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā…  tīṇi.

2350. Saṃkilesikañceva no ca kilesaṃ akusalaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca saṃkilesiko ceva no ca kileso akusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā…  tīṇi.

2351. Kilesañceva saṃkilesikaṃ akusalañca saṃkilesikañceva no ca kilesaṃ akusalañca dhammaṃ paṭicca kileso ceva saṃkilesiko ca akusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā…  tīṇi. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

(22.)

2352. Hetuyā nava, ārammaṇe nava…pe…  avigate nava. (Saṃkhittaṃ, kilesadukaakusalasadisaṃ. Sahajātavārepi…pe…  pañhāvārepi sabbattha vitthāretabbaṃ.)

(23.)

2353. Saṃkilesikañceva no ca kilesaṃ abyākataṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca saṃkilesiko ceva no ca kileso abyākato dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

2354. Hetuyā ekaṃ, ārammaṇe ekaṃ…pe…  avigate ekaṃ. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

2355. (Sahajātavārepi…pe…  pañhāvārepi sabbattha ekaṃ.)

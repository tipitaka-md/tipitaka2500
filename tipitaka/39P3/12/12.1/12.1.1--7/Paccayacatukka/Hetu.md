# Hetu

* Kilesagocchaka, Kusalattika

* Kilesaduka, Kusalattika

* Paṭiccādivāra

* Paccayacatukka

* Hetu

(1.)

2302\. Nokilesaṃ kusalaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nokileso kusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

2303\. Hetuyā ekaṃ, ārammaṇe ekaṃ…pe…  avigate ekaṃ. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

2304\. (Sahajātavārepi…pe…  pañhāvārepi sabbattha ekaṃ.)

(2.)

2305\. Kilesaṃ akusalaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca kileso akusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā…  tīṇi.

2306\. Nokilesaṃ akusalaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nokileso akusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā…  tīṇi.

2307\. Kilesaṃ akusalañca nokilesaṃ akusalañca dhammaṃ paṭicca kileso akusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā…  tīṇi. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

(3.)

2308\. Hetuyā nava, ārammaṇe nava (sabbattha nava), avigate nava. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

(4.)

2309\. Kilesaṃ akusalaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca kileso akusalo dhammo uppajjati nahetupaccayā—  vicikicchaṃ paṭicca vicikicchāsahagato moho, uddhaccaṃ paṭicca uddhaccasahagato moho. (1)

2310\. Nokilesaṃ akusalaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca kileso akusalo dhammo uppajjati nahetupaccayā—  vicikicchāsahagate uddhaccasahagate khandhe paṭicca vicikicchāsahagato uddhaccasahagato moho. (1)

2311\. Kilesaṃ akusalañca nokilesaṃ akusalañca dhammaṃ paṭicca kileso akusalo dhammo uppajjati nahetupaccayā—  vicikicchāsahagate khandhe ca vicikicchañca paṭicca vicikicchāsahagato moho, uddhaccasahagate khandhe ca uddhaccañca paṭicca uddhaccasahagato moho. (1) (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

(5.)

2312\. Nahetuyā tīṇi, naadhipatiyā nava, napurejāte nava, napacchājāte nava, naāsevane nava, nakamme tīṇi, navipāke nava, navippayutte nava. (Saṃkhittaṃ, sahajātavārādi vitthāretabbo.)

(6.)

2313\. Kileso akusalo dhammo kilesassa akusalassa dhammassa hetupaccayena paccayo. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

(7.)

2314\. Hetuyā tīṇi, ārammaṇe nava, adhipatiyā nava (majjhe tīṇi sahajātādhipati), anantare nava, samanantare nava…pe…  upanissaye nava, āsevane nava, kamme tīṇi, āhāre tīṇi, indriye tīṇi, jhāne tīṇi, magge nava, sampayutte nava…pe…  avigate nava. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

2315\. Nokilesaṃ abyākataṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nokileso abyākato dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

(8.)

2316\. Hetuyā ekaṃ, ārammaṇe ekaṃ…pe…  avigate ekaṃ. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

2317\. (Sahajātavārepi…pe…  pañhāvārepi sabbattha ekaṃ.)

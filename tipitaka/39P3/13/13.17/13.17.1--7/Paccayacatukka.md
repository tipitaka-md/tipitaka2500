# Paccayacatukka

### Piṭṭhiduka, Kusalattika

### Saraṇaduka, Kusalattika

### Paṭiccādivāra

### Paccayacatukka

(105.)

2597. Araṇaṃ kusalaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca araṇo kusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

2598. Hetuyā ekaṃ, ārammaṇe ekaṃ…pe…  avigate ekaṃ. (Saṃkhittaṃ. Sahajātavārepi…pe…  pañhāvārepi sabbattha ekaṃ.)

(106.)

2599. Saraṇaṃ akusalaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca saraṇo akusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

2600. Hetuyā ekaṃ, ārammaṇe ekaṃ…pe…  avigate ekaṃ. (Saṃkhittaṃ. Sahajātavārepi…pe…  pañhāvārepi sabbattha ekaṃ.)

(107.)

2601. Araṇaṃ abyākataṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca araṇo abyākato dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

2602. Hetuyā ekaṃ, ārammaṇe ekaṃ…pe…  avigate ekaṃ. (Saṃkhittaṃ. Sahajātavārepi…pe…  sampayuttavārepi sabbattha ekaṃ.)

(108.)

2603. Araṇo abyākato dhammo araṇassa abyākatassa dhammassa hetupaccayena paccayo. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

2604. Hetuyā ekaṃ, ārammaṇe ekaṃ…pe…  avigate ekaṃ. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

2605. (Yathā kusalattike pañhāvāraṃ evaṃ vitthāretabbaṃ.)

===

2606. Piṭṭhidukakusalattikaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.

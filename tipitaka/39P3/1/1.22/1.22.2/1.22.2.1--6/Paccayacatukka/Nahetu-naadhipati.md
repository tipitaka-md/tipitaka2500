### Nahetu-naadhipati

(547.)

1224. Nahetuṃ anidassanaappaṭighaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nahetu anidassanaappaṭigho dhammo uppajjati nahetupaccayā. Nahetuṃ anidassanaappaṭighaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca hetu anidassanaappaṭigho dhammo uppajjati nahetupaccayā. (2)

1225. Hetuṃ anidassanaappaṭighaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nahetu anidassanaappaṭigho dhammo uppajjati naārammaṇapaccayā…  tīṇi.

1226. Hetuṃ anidassanaappaṭighaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca hetu anidassanaappaṭigho dhammo uppajjati naadhipatipaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

(548.)

1227. Nahetuyā dve, naārammaṇe tīṇi, naadhipatiyā nava…pe…  napurejāte nava, napacchājāte nava, naāsevane nava, nakamme tīṇi, navipāke nava, naāhāre ekaṃ, naindriye ekaṃ, najhāne ekaṃ, namagge ekaṃ, nasampayutte tīṇi, navippayutte nava, nonatthiyā tīṇi, novigate tīṇi. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

1228. Hetupaccayā naārammaṇe tīṇi. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

1229. Nahetupaccayā ārammaṇe dve. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

1230. (Sahajātavāropi paccayavāropi nissayavāropi saṃsaṭṭhavāropi sampayuttavāropi paṭiccavārasadisā vitthāretabbā.)

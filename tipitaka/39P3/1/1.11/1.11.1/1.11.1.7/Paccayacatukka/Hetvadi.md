### Hetvādi

(334.)

697. Hetu sekkho dhammo hetussa sekkhassa dhammassa hetupaccayena paccayo…  tīṇi.

698. Hetu sekkho dhammo hetussa sekkhassa dhammassa adhipatipaccayena paccayo—  sahajātādhipati…  tīṇi.

699. Nahetu sekkho dhammo nahetussa sekkhassa dhammassa adhipatipaccayena paccayo—  sahajātādhipati…  tīṇi.

700. Hetu sekkho dhammo hetussa sekkhassa dhammassa anantarapaccayena paccayo. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

(335.)

701. Hetuyā tīṇi, adhipatiyā cha, anantare nava, samanantare nava, sahajāte nava, aññamaññe nava, nissaye nava, upanissaye nava, kamme tīṇi, vipāke nava, āhāre tīṇi, indriye nava, jhāne tīṇi, magge nava, sampayutte nava, atthiyā nava, natthiyā nava, vigate nava, avigate nava. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

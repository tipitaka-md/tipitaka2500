# Nahetvādi

* Hetuduka, Tika

* Hetuduka, Dassanenapahātabbattika

* Nevadassanenanabhāvanāyapahātabbapada

* Paṭiccādivāra

* Paccayacatukka

* Nahetvādi

(276.)

557\. Nahetuṃ nevadassanena nabhāvanāya pahātabbaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nahetu nevadassanena nabhāvanāya pahātabbo dhammo uppajjati nahetupaccayā.

558\. Hetuṃ nevadassanena nabhāvanāya pahātabbaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nahetu nevadassanena nabhāvanāya pahātabbo dhammo uppajjati naārammaṇapaccayā. (1)

559\. Nahetuṃ nevadassanena nabhāvanāya pahātabbaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nahetu nevadassanena nabhāvanāya pahātabbo dhammo uppajjati naārammaṇapaccayā. (1)

560\. Hetuṃ nevadassanena nabhāvanāya pahātabbañca nahetuṃ nevadassanena nabhāvanāya pahātabbañca dhammaṃ paṭicca nahetu nevadassanena nabhāvanāya pahātabbo dhammo uppajjati naārammaṇapaccayā. (1)

561\. Hetuṃ nevadassanena nabhāvanāya pahātabbaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca hetu nevadassanena nabhāvanāya pahātabbo dhammo uppajjati naadhipatipaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

(277.)

562\. Nahetuyā ekaṃ, naārammaṇe tīṇi, naadhipatiyā nava, naanantare tīṇi, nasamanantare tīṇi, naaññamaññe tīṇi, naupanissaye tīṇi, napurejāte nava, napacchājāte nava, naāsevane nava, nakamme tīṇi, navipāke nava, naāhāre ekaṃ, naindriye ekaṃ, najhāne ekaṃ, namagge ekaṃ, nasampayutte tīṇi, navippayutte nava, nonatthiyā tīṇi, novigate tīṇi. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

563\. Hetupaccayā naārammaṇe tīṇi. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

564\. Nahetupaccayā ārammaṇe ekaṃ. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

565\. (Sahajātavāropi paccayavāropi nissayavāropi saṃsaṭṭhavāropi sampayuttavāropi paṭiccavārasadisā vitthāretabbā.)

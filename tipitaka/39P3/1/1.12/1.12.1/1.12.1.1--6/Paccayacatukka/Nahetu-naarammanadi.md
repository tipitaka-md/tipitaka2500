755. Nahetuṃ parittaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nahetu paritto dhammo uppajjati nahetupaccayā. Nahetuṃ parittaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca hetu paritto dhammo uppajjati nahetupaccayā. (2)

757. Nahetuṃ parittaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nahetu paritto dhammo uppajjati naārammaṇapaccayā. (1)

758. Hetuṃ parittañca nahetuṃ parittañca dhammaṃ paṭicca nahetu paritto dhammo uppajjati naārammaṇapaccayā. (1)

759. Hetuṃ parittaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca hetu paritto dhammo uppajjati naadhipatipaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

(358.)

760. Nahetuyā dve, naārammaṇe tīṇi, naadhipatiyā nava, naanantare tīṇi, nasamanantare tīṇi, naaññamaññe tīṇi, naupanissaye tīṇi, napurejāte nava, napacchājāte nava, naāsevane nava, nakamme tīṇi, navipāke nava, naāhāre ekaṃ, naindriye ekaṃ, najhāne ekaṃ, namagge ekaṃ, nasampayutte tīṇi, navippayutte nava, nonatthiyā tīṇi, novigate tīṇi. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

761. Hetupaccayā naārammaṇe tīṇi. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

762. Nahetupaccayā ārammaṇe dve. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

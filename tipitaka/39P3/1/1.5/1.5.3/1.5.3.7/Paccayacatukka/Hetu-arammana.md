### Hetu-ārammaṇa

(206.)

386. Hetu asaṃkiliṭṭhaasaṃkilesiko dhammo hetussa asaṃkiliṭṭhaasaṃkilesikassa dhammassa hetupaccayena paccayo…  tīṇi.

387. Nahetu asaṃkiliṭṭhaasaṃkilesiko dhammo nahetussa asaṃkiliṭṭhaasaṃkilesikassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo…  tīṇi. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

(207.)

388. Hetuyā tīṇi, ārammaṇe tīṇi, adhipatiyā cha, anantare nava, samanantare nava, sahajāte nava, aññamaññe nava, nissaye nava, upanissaye nava, kamme tīṇi, vipāke nava, āhāre tīṇi, indriye nava, jhāne tīṇi, magge nava, sampayutte nava, atthiyā nava, natthiyā nava, vigate nava, avigate nava. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

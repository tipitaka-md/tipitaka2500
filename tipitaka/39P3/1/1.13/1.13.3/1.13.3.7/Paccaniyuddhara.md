### Paccanīyuddhāra

(403.)

871. Hetu appamāṇārammaṇo dhammo hetussa appamāṇārammaṇassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo…  sahajātapaccayena paccayo…  upanissayapaccayena paccayo. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

(404.)

872. Nahetuyā nava, naārammaṇe nava. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

873. Hetupaccayā naārammaṇe tīṇi. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

874. Nahetupaccayā ārammaṇe nava. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

875. (Yathā kusalattike pañhāvārassa anulomampi paccanīyampi anulomapaccanīyampi paccanīyānulomampi gaṇitaṃ, evaṃ gaṇetabbaṃ.)

===

876. Hetudukaparittārammaṇattikaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.

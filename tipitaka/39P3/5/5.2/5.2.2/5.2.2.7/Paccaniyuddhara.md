### Paccanīyuddhāra

(18.)

1735. Saññojaniyo abyākato dhammo saññojaniyassa abyākatassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo…  sahajātapaccayena paccayo…  upanissayapaccayena paccayo…  purejātapaccayena paccayo…  pacchājātapaccayena paccayo…  āhārapaccayena paccayo…  indriyapaccayena paccayo. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

(19.)

1736. Nahetuyā satta, naārammaṇe satta. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

1737. Hetupaccayā naārammaṇe cattāri. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

1738. Nahetupaccayā ārammaṇe tīṇi. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

1739. (Yathā kusalattike pañhāvārassa anulomampi paccanīyampi anulomapaccanīyampi paccanīyānulomampi gaṇitaṃ, evaṃ gaṇetabbaṃ.)

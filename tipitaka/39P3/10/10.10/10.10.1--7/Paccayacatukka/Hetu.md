* Hetu

(73.)

2163\. Cittasaṃsaṭṭhasamuṭṭhānasahabhuṃ kusalaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca cittasaṃsaṭṭhasamuṭṭhānasahabhū kusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā…  tīṇi.

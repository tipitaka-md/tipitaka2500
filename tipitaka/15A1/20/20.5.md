# 20.5

### Amatavagga

(604.)

473. “Amataṃ te, bhikkhave, pamādiṃsu ye kāyagatāsatiṃ pamādiṃsu. Amataṃ te, bhikkhave, na pamādiṃsu ye kāyagatāsatiṃ na pamādiṃsu”.

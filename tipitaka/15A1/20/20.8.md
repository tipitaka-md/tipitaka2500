# 20.8

### Amatavagga

(607.)

476. “Amataṃ tesaṃ, bhikkhave, abhāvitaṃ yesaṃ kāyagatāsati abhāvitā. Amataṃ tesaṃ, bhikkhave, bhāvitaṃ yesaṃ kāyagatāsati bhāvitā”ti.

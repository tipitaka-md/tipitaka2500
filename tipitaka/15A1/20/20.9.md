# 20.9

### Amatavagga

(608.)

477. “Amataṃ tesaṃ, bhikkhave, abahulīkataṃ yesaṃ kāyagatāsati abahulīkatā. Amataṃ tesaṃ, bhikkhave, bahulīkataṃ yesaṃ kāyagatāsati bahulīkatā”ti.

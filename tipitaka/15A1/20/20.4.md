# 20.4

### Amatavagga

(603.)

472. “Amataṃ tesaṃ, bhikkhave, viraddhaṃ yesaṃ kāyagatāsati viraddhā. Amataṃ tesaṃ, bhikkhave, āraddhaṃ yesaṃ kāyagatāsati āraddhā”ti.

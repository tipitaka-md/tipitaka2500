# 20.6

### Amatavagga

(605.)

474\. “Amataṃ tesaṃ, bhikkhave, pamuṭṭhaṃ yesaṃ kāyagatāsati pamuṭṭhā. Amataṃ tesaṃ, bhikkhave, appamuṭṭhaṃ yesaṃ kāyagatāsati appamuṭṭhā”ti.

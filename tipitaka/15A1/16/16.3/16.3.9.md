# 16.3.9

### Ekadhammapāḷi

### Tatiyavagga

(316.)

405\. “Durakkhāte, bhikkhave, dhammavinaye yo āraddhavīriyo so dukkhaṃ viharati. Taṃ kissa hetu? Durakkhātattā, bhikkhave, dhammassā”ti.

# 16.3.7

* Ekadhammapāḷi

* Tatiyavagga

(314.)

403\. “Durakkhāte, bhikkhave, dhammavinaye dāyakena mattā jānitabbā, no paṭiggāhakena. Taṃ kissa hetu? Durakkhātattā, bhikkhave, dhammassā”ti.

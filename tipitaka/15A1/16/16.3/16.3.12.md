# 16.3.12

* Ekadhammapāḷi

* Tatiyavagga

(319.)

408\. “Svākkhāte, bhikkhave, dhammavinaye yo āraddhavīriyo so sukhaṃ viharati. Taṃ kissa hetu? Svākkhātattā, bhikkhave, dhammassā”ti.

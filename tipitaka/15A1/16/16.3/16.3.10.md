# 16.3.10

### Ekadhammapāḷi

### Tatiyavagga

(317.)

406. “Svākkhāte, bhikkhave, dhammavinaye yo kusīto so dukkhaṃ viharati. Taṃ kissa hetu? Svākkhātattā, bhikkhave, dhammassā”ti.

# 16.3.11

### Ekadhammapāḷi

### Tatiyavagga

(318.)

407. “Durakkhāte, bhikkhave, dhammavinaye yo kusīto so sukhaṃ viharati. Taṃ kissa hetu? Durakkhātattā, bhikkhave, dhammassā”ti.

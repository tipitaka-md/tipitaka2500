# 16.4.7

### Ekadhammapāḷi

### Catutthavagga

(328.)

419. …  Evamevaṃ kho, bhikkhave, appakā te sattā ye sutvā dhammaṃ dhārenti; atha kho eteva sattā bahutarā ye sutvā dhammaṃ na dhārenti.

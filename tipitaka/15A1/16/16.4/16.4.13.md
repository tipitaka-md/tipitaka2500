# 16.4.13

### Ekadhammapāḷi

### Catutthavagga

(334.)

425\. …  Evamevaṃ kho, bhikkhave, appakā te sattā ye annaggarasaggānaṃ lābhino; atha kho eteva sattā bahutarā ye annaggarasaggānaṃ na lābhino, uñchena kapālābhatena yāpenti.

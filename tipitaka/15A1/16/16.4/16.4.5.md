# 16.4.5

### Ekadhammapāḷi

### Catutthavagga

(326.)

417. …  Evamevaṃ kho, bhikkhave, appakā te sattā ye labhanti tathāgataṃ dassanāya; atha kho eteva sattā bahutarā ye na labhanti tathāgataṃ dassanāya.

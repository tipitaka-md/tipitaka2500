# 13.2

### Ekapuggalavagga

(171.)

264. “Ekapuggalassa, bhikkhave, pātubhāvo dullabho lokasmiṃ. Katamassa ekapuggalassa? Tathāgatassa arahato sammāsambuddhassa. Imassa kho, bhikkhave, ekapuggalassa pātubhāvo dullabho lokasmin”ti.

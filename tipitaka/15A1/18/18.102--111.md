# 18.102--111

### Aparaaccharāsaṅghātavagga

(483--492.)

455. Paṭhamajjhānasahagataṃ saddhindriyaṃ bhāveti…  vīriyindriyaṃ bhāveti…  satindriyaṃ bhāveti…  samādhindriyaṃ bhāveti…  paññindriyaṃ bhāveti…  saddhābalaṃ bhāveti…  vīriyabalaṃ bhāveti…  satibalaṃ bhāveti…  samādhibalaṃ bhāveti…  paññābalaṃ bhāveti… .

# 18.21--25

### Aparaaccharāsaṅghātavagga

(402--406.)

445. Saddhindriyaṃ bhāveti…  vīriyindriyaṃ bhāveti…  satindriyaṃ bhāveti…  samādhindriyaṃ bhāveti…  paññindriyaṃ bhāveti… .

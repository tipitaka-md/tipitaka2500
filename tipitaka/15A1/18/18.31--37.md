# 18.31--37

* Aparaaccharāsaṅghātavagga

(412--418.)

447\. Satisambojjhaṅgaṃ bhāveti…  dhammavicayasambojjhaṅgaṃ bhāveti…  vīriyasambojjhaṅgaṃ bhāveti…  pītisambojjhaṅgaṃ bhāveti…  passaddhisambojjhaṅgaṃ bhāveti…  samādhisambojjhaṅgaṃ bhāveti…  upekkhāsambojjhaṅgaṃ bhāveti… .

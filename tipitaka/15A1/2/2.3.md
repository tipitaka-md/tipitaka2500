# 2.3

* Nīvaraṇappahānavagga

(13.)

28\. “Nāhaṃ, bhikkhave, aññaṃ ekadhammampi samanupassāmi yena anuppannaṃ vā thinamiddhaṃ uppajjati uppannaṃ vā thinamiddhaṃ bhiyyobhāvāya vepullāya saṃvattati yathayidaṃ, bhikkhave, arati tandī vijambhitā bhattasammado cetaso ca līnattaṃ. Līnacittassa, bhikkhave, anuppannañceva thinamiddhaṃ uppajjati uppannañca thinamiddhaṃ bhiyyobhāvāya vepullāya saṃvattatī”ti.

---

29\. Tatiyaṃ.

# 3.9

### Akammaniyavagga

(29.)

61. “Nāhaṃ, bhikkhave, aññaṃ ekadhammampi samanupassāmi yaṃ evaṃ abhāvitaṃ abahulīkataṃ dukkhādhivahaṃ hoti yathayidaṃ, bhikkhave, cittaṃ. Cittaṃ, bhikkhave, abhāvitaṃ abahulīkataṃ dukkhādhivahaṃ hotī”ti.

===

62. Navamaṃ.

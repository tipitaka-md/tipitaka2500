# 3.2

### Akammaniyavagga

(22.)

47. “Nāhaṃ, bhikkhave, aññaṃ ekadhammampi samanupassāmi yaṃ evaṃ bhāvitaṃ kammaniyaṃ hoti yathayidaṃ, bhikkhave, cittaṃ. Cittaṃ, bhikkhave, bhāvitaṃ kammaniyaṃ hotī”ti.

---

48. Dutiyaṃ.

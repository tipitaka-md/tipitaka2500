# 15.1.7

### Aṭṭhānapāḷi

### Paṭhamavagga

(274.)

364. “Aṭṭhānametaṃ, bhikkhave, anavakāso yaṃ diṭṭhisampanno puggalo tathāgatassa paduṭṭhacitto lohitaṃ uppādeyya. Netaṃ ṭhānaṃ vijjati. Ṭhānañca kho etaṃ, bhikkhave, vijjati yaṃ puthujjano tathāgatassa paduṭṭhacitto lohitaṃ uppādeyya. Ṭhānametaṃ vijjatī”ti.

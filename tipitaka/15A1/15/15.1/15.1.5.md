# 15.1.5

* Aṭṭhānapāḷi

* Paṭhamavagga

(272.)

362\. “Aṭṭhānametaṃ, bhikkhave, anavakāso yaṃ diṭṭhisampanno puggalo pitaraṃ jīvitā voropeyya. Netaṃ ṭhānaṃ vijjati. Ṭhānañca kho etaṃ, bhikkhave, vijjati yaṃ puthujjano pitaraṃ jīvitā voropeyya. Ṭhānametaṃ vijjatī”ti.

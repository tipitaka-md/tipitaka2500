# 15.2.8--9

### Aṭṭhānapāḷi

### Dutiyavagga

(285--286.)

374. “Aṭṭhānametaṃ, bhikkhave, anavakāso yaṃ vacīduccaritassa…pe…  yaṃ manoduccaritassa iṭṭho kanto manāpo vipāko nibbatteyya. Netaṃ ṭhānaṃ vijjati. Ṭhānañca kho etaṃ, bhikkhave, vijjati yaṃ manoduccaritassa aniṭṭho akanto amanāpo vipāko nibbatteyya. Ṭhānametaṃ vijjatī”ti.

===

375. Vaggo dutiyo.

# 15.2.7

### Aṭṭhānapāḷi

### Dutiyavagga

(284.)

373. “Aṭṭhānametaṃ, bhikkhave, anavakāso yaṃ kāyaduccaritassa iṭṭho kanto manāpo vipāko nibbatteyya. Netaṃ ṭhānaṃ vijjati. Ṭhānañca kho etaṃ, bhikkhave, vijjati yaṃ kāyaduccaritassa aniṭṭho akanto amanāpo vipāko nibbatteyya. Ṭhānametaṃ vijjatī”ti.

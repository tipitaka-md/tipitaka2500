# 8.10

### Kalyāṇamittādivagga

(80.)

168. “Appamattikā esā, bhikkhave, parihāni yadidaṃ yasoparihāni. Etaṃ patikiṭṭhaṃ, bhikkhave, parihānīnaṃ yadidaṃ paññāparihānī”ti.

---

169. Dasamaṃ.

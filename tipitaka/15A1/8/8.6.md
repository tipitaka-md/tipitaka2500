# 8.6

### Kalyāṇamittādivagga

(76.)

160. “Appamattikā esā, bhikkhave, parihāni yadidaṃ ñātiparihāni. Etaṃ patikiṭṭhaṃ, bhikkhave, parihānīnaṃ yadidaṃ paññāparihānī”ti.

===

161. Chaṭṭhaṃ.

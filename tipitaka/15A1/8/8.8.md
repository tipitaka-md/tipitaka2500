# 8.8

### Kalyāṇamittādivagga

(78.)

164. “Appamattikā esā, bhikkhave, parihāni yadidaṃ bhogaparihāni. Etaṃ patikiṭṭhaṃ, bhikkhave, parihānīnaṃ yadidaṃ paññāparihānī”ti.

===

165. Aṭṭhamaṃ.

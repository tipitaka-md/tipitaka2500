# 9.6

### Pamādādivagga

(87.)

183. “Nāhaṃ, bhikkhave, aññaṃ ekadhammampi samanupassāmi yo evaṃ mahato atthāya saṃvattati yathayidaṃ, bhikkhave, appicchatā. Appicchatā, bhikkhave, mahato atthāya saṃvattatī”ti.

---

184. Chaṭṭhaṃ.

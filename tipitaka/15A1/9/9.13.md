# 9.13

### Pamādādivagga

(94.)

197. “Nāhaṃ, bhikkhave, aññaṃ ekadhammampi samanupassāmi yo evaṃ mahato anatthāya saṃvattati yathayidaṃ, bhikkhave, pāpamittatā. Pāpamittatā, bhikkhave, mahato anatthāya saṃvattatī”ti.

===

198. Terasamaṃ.

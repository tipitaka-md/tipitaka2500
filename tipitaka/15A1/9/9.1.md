# 9.1

### Pamādādivagga

(82.)

173. “Nāhaṃ, bhikkhave, aññaṃ ekadhammampi samanupassāmi yo evaṃ mahato anatthāya saṃvattati yathayidaṃ, bhikkhave, pamādo. Pamādo, bhikkhave, mahato anatthāya saṃvattatī”ti.

===

174. Paṭhamaṃ.

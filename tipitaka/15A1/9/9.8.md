# 9.8

* Pamādādivagga

(89.)

187\. “Nāhaṃ, bhikkhave, aññaṃ ekadhammampi samanupassāmi yo evaṃ mahato atthāya saṃvattati yathayidaṃ, bhikkhave, santuṭṭhitā. Santuṭṭhitā, bhikkhave, mahato atthāya saṃvattatī”ti.

---

188\. Aṭṭhamaṃ.

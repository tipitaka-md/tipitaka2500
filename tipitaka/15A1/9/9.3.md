# 9.3

### Pamādādivagga

(84.)

177. “Nāhaṃ, bhikkhave, aññaṃ ekadhammampi samanupassāmi yo evaṃ mahato anatthāya saṃvattati yathayidaṃ, bhikkhave, kosajjaṃ. Kosajjaṃ, bhikkhave, mahato anatthāya saṃvattatī”ti.

===

178. Tatiyaṃ.

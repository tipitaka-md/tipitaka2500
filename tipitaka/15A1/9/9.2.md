# 9.2

### Pamādādivagga

(83.)

175. “Nāhaṃ, bhikkhave, aññaṃ ekadhammampi samanupassāmi yo evaṃ mahato atthāya saṃvattati yathayidaṃ, bhikkhave, appamādo. Appamādo, bhikkhave, mahato atthāya saṃvattatī”ti.

===

176. Dutiyaṃ.

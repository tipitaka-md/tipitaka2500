# 9.7

* Pamādādivagga

(88.)

185\. “Nāhaṃ, bhikkhave, aññaṃ ekadhammampi samanupassāmi yo evaṃ mahato anatthāya saṃvattati yathayidaṃ, bhikkhave, asantuṭṭhitā. Asantuṭṭhitā, bhikkhave, mahato anatthāya saṃvattatī”ti.

---

186\. Sattamaṃ.

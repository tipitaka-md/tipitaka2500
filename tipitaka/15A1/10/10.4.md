# 10.4

### Dutiyapamādādivagga

(101.)

212. “Ajjhattikaṃ, bhikkhave, aṅganti karitvā nāññaṃ ekaṅgampi samanupassāmi yaṃ evaṃ mahato atthāya saṃvattati yathayidaṃ, bhikkhave, vīriyārambho. Vīriyārambho, bhikkhave, mahato atthāya saṃvattatī”ti.

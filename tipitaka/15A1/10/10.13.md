# 10.13

* Dutiyapamādādivagga

(110.)

215\. “Bāhiraṃ, bhikkhave, aṅganti karitvā nāññaṃ ekaṅgampi samanupassāmi yaṃ evaṃ mahato anatthāya saṃvattati yathayidaṃ, bhikkhave, pāpamittatā. Pāpamittatā, bhikkhave, mahato anatthāya saṃvattatī”ti.

---

216\. Terasamaṃ.

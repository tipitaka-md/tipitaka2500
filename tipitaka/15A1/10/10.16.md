# 10.16

* Dutiyapamādādivagga

(113.)

221\. “Ajjhattikaṃ, bhikkhave, aṅganti karitvā nāññaṃ ekaṅgampi samanupassāmi yaṃ evaṃ mahato atthāya saṃvattati yathayidaṃ, bhikkhave, anuyogo kusalānaṃ dhammānaṃ, ananuyogo akusalānaṃ dhammānaṃ. Anuyogo, bhikkhave, kusalānaṃ dhammānaṃ, ananuyogo akusalānaṃ dhammānaṃ mahato atthāya saṃvattatī”ti.

---

222\. Soḷasamaṃ.

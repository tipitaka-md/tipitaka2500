# 10.2

* Dutiyapamādādivagga

(99.)

208\. “Ajjhattikaṃ, bhikkhave, aṅganti karitvā nāññaṃ ekaṅgampi samanupassāmi yaṃ evaṃ mahato atthāya saṃvattati yathayidaṃ, bhikkhave, appamādo. Appamādo, bhikkhave, mahato atthāya saṃvattatī”ti.

---

209\. Dutiyaṃ.

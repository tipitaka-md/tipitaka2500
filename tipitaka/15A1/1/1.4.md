# 1.4

### Rūpādivagga

(4.)

9. “Nāhaṃ, bhikkhave, aññaṃ ekarasampi samanupassāmi yaṃ evaṃ purisassa cittaṃ pariyādāya tiṭṭhati yathayidaṃ, bhikkhave, itthiraso. Itthiraso, bhikkhave, purisassa cittaṃ pariyādāya tiṭṭhatī”ti.

===

10. Catutthaṃ.

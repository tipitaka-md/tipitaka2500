# 1.3

* Rūpādivagga

(3.)

7\. “Nāhaṃ, bhikkhave, aññaṃ ekagandhampi samanupassāmi yaṃ evaṃ purisassa cittaṃ pariyādāya tiṭṭhati yathayidaṃ, bhikkhave, itthigandho. Itthigandho, bhikkhave, purisassa cittaṃ pariyādāya tiṭṭhatī”ti.

---

8\. Tatiyaṃ.

# 4.8

* Adantavagga

(38.)

80\. “Nāhaṃ, bhikkhave, aññaṃ ekadhammampi samanupassāmi yaṃ evaṃ saṃvutaṃ mahato atthāya saṃvattati yathayidaṃ, bhikkhave, cittaṃ. Cittaṃ, bhikkhave, saṃvutaṃ mahato atthāya saṃvattatī”ti.

---

81\. Aṭṭhamaṃ.

# 4.1

### Adantavagga

(31.)

66. “Nāhaṃ, bhikkhave, aññaṃ ekadhammampi samanupassāmi yaṃ evaṃ adantaṃ mahato anatthāya saṃvattati yathayidaṃ, bhikkhave, cittaṃ. Cittaṃ, bhikkhave, adantaṃ mahato anatthāya saṃvattatī”ti.

===

67. Paṭhamaṃ.

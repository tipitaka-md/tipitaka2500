# 4.7

### Adantavagga

(37.)

78. “Nāhaṃ, bhikkhave, aññaṃ ekadhammampi samanupassāmi yaṃ evaṃ asaṃvutaṃ mahato anatthāya saṃvattati yathayidaṃ, bhikkhave, cittaṃ. Cittaṃ, bhikkhave, asaṃvutaṃ mahato anatthāya saṃvattatī”ti.

===

79. Sattamaṃ.

# 4.6

### Adantavagga

(36.)

76. “Nāhaṃ, bhikkhave, aññaṃ ekadhammampi samanupassāmi yaṃ evaṃ rakkhitaṃ mahato atthāya saṃvattati yathayidaṃ, bhikkhave, cittaṃ. Cittaṃ, bhikkhave, rakkhitaṃ mahato atthāya saṃvattatī”ti.

===

77. Chaṭṭhaṃ.

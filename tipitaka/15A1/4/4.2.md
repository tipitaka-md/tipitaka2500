# 4.2

* Adantavagga

(32.)

68\. “Nāhaṃ, bhikkhave, aññaṃ ekadhammampi samanupassāmi yaṃ evaṃ dantaṃ mahato atthāya saṃvattati yathayidaṃ, bhikkhave, cittaṃ. Cittaṃ, bhikkhave, dantaṃ mahato atthāya saṃvattatī”ti.

---

69\. Dutiyaṃ.

# 14.1.1--10

* Etadaggavagga

* Paṭhamavagga

(188.)

271\. “Etadaggaṃ, bhikkhave, mama sāvakānaṃ bhikkhūnaṃ rattaññūnaṃ yadidaṃ aññāsikoṇḍañño. (1)

(189.)

272\. …  Mahāpaññānaṃ yadidaṃ sāriputto. (2)

(190.)

273\. …  Iddhimantānaṃ yadidaṃ mahāmoggallāno. (3)

(191.)

274\. …  Dhutavādānaṃ yadidaṃ mahākassapo. (4)

(192.)

275\. …  Dibbacakkhukānaṃ yadidaṃ anuruddho. (5)

(193.)

276\. …  Uccākulikānaṃ yadidaṃ bhaddiyo kāḷigodhāya putto. (6)

(194.)

277\. …  Mañjussarānaṃ yadidaṃ lakuṇḍaka bhaddiyo. (7)

(195.)

278\. …  Sīhanādikānaṃ yadidaṃ piṇḍolabhāradvājo. (8)

(196.)

279\. …  Dhammakathikānaṃ yadidaṃ puṇṇo mantāṇiputto. (9)

(197.)

280\. …  Saṃkhittena bhāsitassa vitthārena atthaṃ vibhajantānaṃ yadidaṃ mahākaccāno”ti. (10)

---

281\. Vaggo paṭhamo.

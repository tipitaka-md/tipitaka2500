# 6.7

### Accharāsaṅghātavagga

(57.)

120. “Ye keci, bhikkhave, dhammā kusalā kusalabhāgiyā kusalapakkhikā, sabbe te manopubbaṅgamā. Mano tesaṃ dhammānaṃ paṭhamaṃ uppajjati, anvadeva kusalā dhammā”ti.

===

121. Sattamaṃ.

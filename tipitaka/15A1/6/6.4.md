# 6.4

### Accharāsaṅghātavagga

(54.)

114. “Accharāsaṅghātamattampi ce, bhikkhave, bhikkhu mettācittaṃ bhāveti; ayaṃ vuccati, bhikkhave—  ‘bhikkhu arittajjhāno viharati satthusāsanakaro ovādapatikaro, amoghaṃ raṭṭhapiṇḍaṃ bhuñjati’. Ko pana vādo ye naṃ bahulīkarontī”ti.

---

115. Catutthaṃ.

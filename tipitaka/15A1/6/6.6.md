# 6.6

### Accharāsaṅghātavagga

(56.)

118. “Ye keci, bhikkhave, dhammā akusalā akusalabhāgiyā akusalapakkhikā, sabbe te manopubbaṅgamā. Mano tesaṃ dhammānaṃ paṭhamaṃ uppajjati, anvadeva akusalā dhammā”ti.

---

119. Chaṭṭhaṃ.

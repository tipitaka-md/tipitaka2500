### Saṅkhyāvāra

### Suddha

(56.)

243. Nahetuyā tettiṃsa, naārammaṇe satta, naadhipatiyā sattatiṃsa, naanantare nasamanantare naaññamaññe naupanissaye satta, napurejāte napacchājāte naāsevane sattatiṃsa, nakamme ekādasa, navipāke sattatiṃsa, naāhāre ekaṃ, naindriye ekaṃ, najhāne ekaṃ, namagge tettiṃsa, nasampayutte satta, navippayutte ekādasa, nonatthiyā satta, novigate satta.

# 7.5.4 Paccayapaccanīyānuloma

### Vitakkattika

### Saṃsaṭṭhavāra

### Paccayapaccanīyānuloma

(65.)

268\. Nahetupaccayā ārammaṇe cha…pe…  purejāte cha, āsevane pañca, kamme cha…pe…  jhāne cha, magge tīṇi, sampayutte cha…pe…  avigate cha.

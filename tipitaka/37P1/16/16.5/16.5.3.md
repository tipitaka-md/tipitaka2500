# 16.5.3 Paccayānulomapaccanīya

* Micchattaniyatattika

* Saṃsaṭṭhavāra

* Paccayānulomapaccanīya

(34.)

2339\. Hetupaccayā naadhipatiyā tīṇi, napurejāte dve, napacchājāte tīṇi, naāsevane ekaṃ, nakamme tīṇi, navipāke tīṇi, navippayutte dve. (Evaṃ gaṇetabbaṃ.)

---

2340\. Anulomapaccanīyaṃ.

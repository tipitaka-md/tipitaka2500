2319. Micchattaniyataṃ dhammaṃ saṃsaṭṭho micchattaniyato dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā—  micchattaniyataṃ ekaṃ khandhaṃ saṃsaṭṭhā tayo khandhā…pe…  dve khandhe…pe… . (1)

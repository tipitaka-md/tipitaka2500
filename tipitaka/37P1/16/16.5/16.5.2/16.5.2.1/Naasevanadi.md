### Naāsevanādi

(32.)

2332\. Aniyataṃ dhammaṃ saṃsaṭṭho aniyato dhammo uppajjati naāsevanapaccayā—  aniyataṃ ekaṃ khandhaṃ saṃsaṭṭhā tayo khandhā…pe…  dve khandhe…pe…  paṭisandhikkhaṇe…pe… . (1)

2333\. Micchattaniyataṃ dhammaṃ saṃsaṭṭho micchattaniyato dhammo uppajjati nakammapaccayā, navipākapaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

2334\. Aniyataṃ dhammaṃ saṃsaṭṭho aniyato dhammo uppajjati najhānapaccayā—  pañcaviññāṇaṃ…pe…  namaggapaccayā—  ahetukaṃ aniyataṃ…pe… .

2335\. Sammattaniyataṃ dhammaṃ saṃsaṭṭho sammattaniyato dhammo uppajjati navippayuttapaccayā—  arūpe sammattaniyataṃ ekaṃ khandhaṃ saṃsaṭṭhā tayo khandhā…pe…  dve khandhe…pe… . (1)

2336\. Aniyataṃ dhammaṃ saṃsaṭṭho aniyato dhammo uppajjati navippayuttapaccayā—  arūpe aniyataṃ ekaṃ khandhaṃ saṃsaṭṭhā tayo khandhā…pe…  dve khandhe…pe… . (1)

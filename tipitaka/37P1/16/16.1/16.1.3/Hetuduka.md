# Hetuduka

### Micchattaniyatattika

### Paṭiccavāra

### Paccayānulomapaccanīya

### Hetuduka

(12.)

2283. Hetupaccayā naārammaṇe pañca, naadhipatiyā tīṇi, naanantare nasamanantare naaññamaññe naupanissaye pañca, napurejāte cha, napacchājāte nava, naāsevane pañca, nakamme tīṇi, navipāke nava, nasampayutte pañca, navippayutte dve, nonatthiyā pañca, novigate pañca. (Evaṃ gaṇetabbaṃ.)

===

2284. Anulomapaccanīyaṃ.

1551. Ācayagāmiṃ dhammaṃ saṃsaṭṭho ācayagāmī dhammo uppajjati naadhipatipaccayā…  napurejātapaccayā…  napacchājātapaccayā…  naāsevanapaccayā…  ācayagāmiṃ ekaṃ khandhaṃ saṃsaṭṭhā tayo khandhā…pe…  dve khandhe…pe… .

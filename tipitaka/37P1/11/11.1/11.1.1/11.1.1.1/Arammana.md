1432\. Ācayagāmiṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca ācayagāmī dhammo uppajjati ārammaṇapaccayā—  ācayagāmiṃ ekaṃ khandhaṃ paṭicca tayo khandhā…pe…  dve khandhe…pe… . (1)

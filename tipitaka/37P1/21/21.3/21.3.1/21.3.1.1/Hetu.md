(18.)

2794. Ajjhattaṃ dhammaṃ paccayā ajjhatto dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā—  ajjhattaṃ ekaṃ khandhaṃ paccayā tayo khandhā cittasamuṭṭhānañca rūpaṃ…pe…  dve khandhe…pe…  paṭisandhikkhaṇe (paripuṇṇaṃ). Ekaṃ mahābhūtaṃ…pe…  vatthuṃ paccayā ajjhattā khandhā. (1)

2795. Bahiddhā dhammaṃ paccayā bahiddhā dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā—  bahiddhā ekaṃ khandhaṃ paccayā tayo khandhā…pe…  paṭisandhikkhaṇe…pe…  ekaṃ mahābhūtaṃ…pe…  vatthuṃ paccayā bahiddhā khandhā. (1)

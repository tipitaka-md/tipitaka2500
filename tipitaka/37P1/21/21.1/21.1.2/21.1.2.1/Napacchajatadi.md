(11.)

2777. Ajjhattaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca ajjhatto dhammo uppajjati napacchājātapaccayā, naāsevanapaccayā, nakammapaccayā—  ajjhatte khandhe paṭicca ajjhattā cetanā. Āhārasamuṭṭhānaṃ…  utusamuṭṭhānaṃ…pe… . (1)

2785\. Ajjhattaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca ajjhatto dhammo uppajjati namaggapaccayā…  (nahetusadisaṃ. Moho natthi) nasampayuttapaccayā…  navippayuttapaccayā—  arūpe…pe…  āhārasamuṭṭhānaṃ…  utusamuṭṭhānaṃ…  asaññasattānaṃ…pe… . (1)

2779. Ajjhattaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca ajjhatto dhammo uppajjati navipākapaccayā (paṭisandhi natthi), naāhārapaccayā—  utusamuṭṭhānaṃ…  asaññasattānaṃ…pe… . (1)

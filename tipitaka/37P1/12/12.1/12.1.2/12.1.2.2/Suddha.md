### Paṭiccavāra

### Paccayapaccanīya

### Saṅkhyāvāra

### Suddha

(15.)

1697. Nahetuyā ekaṃ, naārammaṇe pañca, naadhipatiyā tīṇi, naanantare nasamanantare naaññamaññe naupanissaye pañca, napurejāte satta, napacchājāte nava, naāsevane nava, nakamme dve, navipāke pañca, naāhāre ekaṃ, naindriye ekaṃ, najhāne ekaṃ, namagge ekaṃ, nasampayutte pañca, navippayutte tīṇi, nonatthiyā pañca, novigate pañca. (Evaṃ gaṇetabbaṃ.)

===

1698. Paccanīyaṃ.

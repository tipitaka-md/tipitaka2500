### Paccayapaccanīyānuloma

### Nahetuduka

(17.)

1701\. Nahetupaccayā ārammaṇe ekaṃ, anantare samanantare sahajāte aññamaññe nissaye upanissaye purejāte āsevane kamme vipāke āhāre indriye jhāne magge sampayutte vippayutte atthiyā natthiyā vigate avigate ekaṃ. (Evaṃ gaṇetabbaṃ.)

---

1702\. Paccanīyānulomaṃ.

  
Paṭiccavāro.

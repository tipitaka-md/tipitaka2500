# Paccanīyuddhāra

* Sekkhattika

* Pañhāvāra

* Paccanīyuddhāra

(69.)

1845\. Sekkho dhammo sekkhassa dhammassa sahajātapaccayena paccayo…  upanissayapaccayena paccayo. (1)

1846\. Sekkho dhammo asekkhassa dhammassa upanissayapaccayena paccayo. (2)

1847\. Sekkho dhammo nevasekkhanāsekkhassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo…  sahajātapaccayena paccayo…  upanissayapaccayena paccayo…  pacchājātapaccayena paccayo. (3)

1848\. Sekkho dhammo sekkhassa ca nevasekkhanāsekkhassa ca dhammassa sahajātapaccayena paccayo. (4)

(70.)

1849\. Asekkho dhammo asekkhassa dhammassa sahajātapaccayena paccayo…  upanissayapaccayena paccayo. (1)

1850\. Asekkho dhammo nevasekkhanāsekkhassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo…  sahajātapaccayena paccayo…  upanissayapaccayena paccayo…  pacchājātapaccayena paccayo. (2)

1851\. Asekkho dhammo asekkhassa ca nevasekkhanāsekkhassa ca dhammassa sahajātapaccayena paccayo. (3)

(71.)

1852\. Nevasekkhanāsekkho dhammo nevasekkhanāsekkhassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo…  sahajātapaccayena paccayo…  upanissayapaccayena paccayo…  purejātapaccayena paccayo…  pacchājātapaccayena paccayo…  kammapaccayena paccayo…  āhārapaccayena paccayo…  indriyapaccayena paccayo. (1)

1853\. Nevasekkhanāsekkho dhammo sekkhassa dhammassa upanissayapaccayena paccayo…  purejātapaccayena paccayo. (2)

1854\. Nevasekkhanāsekkho dhammo asekkhassa dhammassa upanissayapaccayena paccayo…  purejātapaccayena paccayo. (3)

(72.)

1855\. Sekkho ca nevasekkhanāsekkho ca dhammā sekkhassa dhammassa sahajātaṃ, purejātaṃ. (1)

1856\. Sekkho ca nevasekkhanāsekkho ca dhammā nevasekkhanāsekkhassa dhammassa sahajātaṃ, pacchājātaṃ, āhāraṃ, indriyaṃ. (2)

1857\. Asekkho ca nevasekkhanāsekkho ca dhammā asekkhassa dhammassa sahajātaṃ, purejātaṃ. (1)

1858\. Asekkho ca nevasekkhanāsekkho ca dhammā nevasekkhanāsekkhassa dhammassa sahajātaṃ, pacchājātaṃ, āhāraṃ, indriyaṃ. (2)

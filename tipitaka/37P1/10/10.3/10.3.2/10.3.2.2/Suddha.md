### Saṅkhyāvāra

### Suddha

(60.)

1228. Nahetuyā pañca, naārammaṇe pañca, naadhipatiyā sattarasa, naanantare pañca, nasamanantare pañca, naaññamaññe pañca, naupanissaye pañca, napurejāte terasa, napacchājāte sattarasa, naāsevane sattarasa, nakamme satta, navipāke sattarasa, naāhāre ekaṃ, naindriye ekaṃ, najhāne ekaṃ, namagge ekaṃ, nasampayutte pañca, navippayutte ekādasa, nonatthiyā pañca, novigate pañca. (Evaṃ gaṇetabbaṃ.)

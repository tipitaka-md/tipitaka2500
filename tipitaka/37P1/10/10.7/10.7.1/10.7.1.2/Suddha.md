### Saṅkhyāvāra

### Suddha

(107.)

1389. Hetuyā ekādasa, ārammaṇe ekavīsa, adhipatiyā dasa, anantare sattarasa, samanantare sattarasa, sahajāte sattarasa, aññamaññe ekādasa, nissaye sattarasa, upanissaye ekavīsa, purejāte pañca, pacchājāte pañca, āsevane sattarasa, kamme satta, vipāke ekaṃ, āhāre satta, indriye satta, jhāne satta, magge satta, sampayutte ekādasa, vippayutte nava, atthiyā sattarasa, natthiyā sattarasa, vigate sattarasa, avigate sattarasa. (Evaṃ gaṇetabbaṃ.)

1390. Anulomaṃ.

# 10.1.3 Paccayānulomapaccanīya

### Dassanenapahātabbahetukattika

### Paṭiccavāra

### Paccayānulomapaccanīya

(33.)

1139. Hetupaccayā naārammaṇe pañca, naadhipatiyā sattarasa, naanantare pañca, nasamanantare pañca, naaññamaññe pañca, naupanissaye pañca, napurejāte terasa, napacchājāte sattarasa, naāsevane sattarasa, nakamme satta, navipāke sattarasa, nasampayutte pañca, navippayutte ekādasa, nonatthiyā pañca, novigate pañca. (Evaṃ gaṇetabbaṃ.)

===

1140. Anulomapaccanīyaṃ.

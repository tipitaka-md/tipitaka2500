1099\. Dassanena pahātabbahetukaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nevadassanena nabhāvanāya pahātabbahetuko dhammo uppajjati nahetupaccayā—  vicikicchāsahagate khandhe paṭicca vicikicchāsahagato moho. (1)

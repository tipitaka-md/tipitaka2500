(5.)

2636\. Atīto dhammo paccuppannassa dhammassa anantarapaccayena paccayo—  purimā purimā atītā khandhā pacchimānaṃ pacchimānaṃ paccuppannānaṃ khandhānaṃ anantarapaccayena paccayo. Anulomaṃ gotrabhussa…  anulomaṃ vodānassa…  gotrabhu maggassa…  vodānaṃ maggassa…  maggo phalassa…  phalaṃ phalassa…  anulomaṃ phalasamāpattiyā…  nirodhā vuṭṭhahantassa nevasaññānāsaññāyatanaṃ phalasamāpattiyā anantarapaccayena paccayo.

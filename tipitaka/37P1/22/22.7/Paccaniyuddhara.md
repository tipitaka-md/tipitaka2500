# Paccanīyuddhāra

### Ajjhattārammaṇattika

### Pañhāvāra

### Paccanīyuddhāra

(25.)

2919. Ajjhattārammaṇo dhammo ajjhattārammaṇassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo…  sahajātapaccayena paccayo…  upanissayapaccayena paccayo…  kammapaccayena paccayo. (1)

2920. Ajjhattārammaṇo dhammo bahiddhārammaṇassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo…  upanissayapaccayena paccayo…  kammapaccayena paccayo. (2)

2921. Bahiddhārammaṇo dhammo bahiddhārammaṇassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo…  sahajātapaccayena paccayo…  upanissayapaccayena paccayo…  kammapaccayena paccayo. (1)

2922. Bahiddhārammaṇo dhammo ajjhattārammaṇassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo…  upanissayapaccayena paccayo…  kammapaccayena paccayo. (2)

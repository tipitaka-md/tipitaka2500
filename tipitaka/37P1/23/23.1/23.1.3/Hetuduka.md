### Hetuduka

(27.)

2996. Hetupaccayā naārammaṇe ekavīsa, naadhipatiyā ekavīsa…pe…  nakamme ekaṃ, navipāke ekavīsa, nasampayutte ekavīsa, navippayutte ekaṃ, nonatthiyā ekavīsa, novigate ekavīsa. (Evaṃ gaṇetabbaṃ.)

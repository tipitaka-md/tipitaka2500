### Saṅkhyāvāra

### Suddha

(56.)

3061. Hetuyā satta, ārammaṇe tīṇi, adhipatiyā nava, anantare ekaṃ, samanantare ekaṃ, sahajāte ekavīsa, aññamaññe cha, nissaye ekavīsa, upanissaye tīṇi, purejāte cha, pacchājāte satta, āsevane ekaṃ, kamme satta, vipāke satta, āhāre satta, indriye nava, jhāne satta, magge satta, sampayutte ekaṃ, vippayutte aṭṭha, atthiyā pañcavīsa, natthiyā ekaṃ, vigate ekaṃ, avigate pañcavīsa.

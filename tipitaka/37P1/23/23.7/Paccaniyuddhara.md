# Paccanīyuddhāra

* Sanidassanasappaṭighattika

* Pañhāvāra

* Paccanīyuddhāra

(59.)

3066\. Sanidassanasappaṭigho dhammo anidassanaappaṭighassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo…  upanissayapaccayena paccayo…  purejātapaccayena paccayo. (1)

3067\. Anidassanasappaṭigho dhammo anidassanasappaṭighassa dhammassa sahajātapaccayena paccayo. (1)

3068\. Anidassanasappaṭigho dhammo sanidassanasappaṭighassa dhammassa sahajātapaccayena paccayo. (2)

3069\. Anidassanasappaṭigho dhammo anidassanaappaṭighassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo…  sahajātapaccayena paccayo…  upanissayapaccayena paccayo…  purejātapaccayena paccayo. (3)

3070\. Anidassanasappaṭigho dhammo sanidassanasappaṭighassa ca anidassanaappaṭighassa ca dhammassa sahajātapaccayena paccayo. (4)

3071\. Anidassanasappaṭigho dhammo anidassanasappaṭighassa ca anidassanaappaṭighassa ca dhammassa sahajātapaccayena paccayo. (5)

3072\. Anidassanasappaṭigho dhammo sanidassanasappaṭighassa ca anidassanasappaṭighassa ca dhammassa sahajātapaccayena paccayo. (6)

3073\. Anidassanasappaṭigho dhammo sanidassanasappaṭighassa ca anidassanasappaṭighassa ca anidassanaappaṭighassa ca dhammassa sahajātapaccayena paccayo. (7)

(60.)

3074\. Anidassanaappaṭigho dhammo anidassanaappaṭighassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo…  sahajātapaccayena paccayo…  upanissayapaccayena paccayo…  purejātapaccayena paccayo…  pacchājātapaccayena paccayo…  kammapaccayena paccayo…  āhārapaccayena paccayo…  indriyapaccayena paccayo. (1)

3075\. Anidassanaappaṭigho dhammo sanidassanasappaṭighassa dhammassa sahajātapaccayena paccayo…  pacchājātapaccayena paccayo…  kammapaccayena paccayo…  āhārapaccayena paccayo…  indriyapaccayena paccayo. (2)

3076\. Anidassanaappaṭigho dhammo anidassanasappaṭighassa dhammassa sahajātapaccayena paccayo…  pacchājātapaccayena paccayo…  kammapaccayena paccayo…  āhārapaccayena paccayo…  indriyapaccayena paccayo. (3)

3077\. Anidassanaappaṭigho dhammo sanidassanasappaṭighassa ca anidassanaappaṭighassa ca dhammassa sahajātapaccayena paccayo…  pacchājātapaccayena paccayo…  kammapaccayena paccayo…  āhārapaccayena paccayo…  indriyapaccayena paccayo. (4)

3078\. Anidassanaappaṭigho dhammo anidassanasappaṭighassa ca anidassanaappaṭighassa ca dhammassa sahajātapaccayena paccayo…  pacchājātapaccayena paccayo…  kammapaccayena paccayo…  āhārapaccayena paccayo…  indriyapaccayena paccayo. (5)

3079\. Anidassanaappaṭigho dhammo sanidassanasappaṭighassa ca anidassanasappaṭighassa ca dhammassa sahajātapaccayena paccayo…  pacchājātapaccayena paccayo…  kammapaccayena paccayo…  āhārapaccayena paccayo…  indriyapaccayena paccayo. (6)

3080\. Anidassanaappaṭigho dhammo sanidassanasappaṭighassa ca anidassanasappaṭighassa ca anidassanaappaṭighassa ca dhammassa sahajātapaccayena paccayo…  pacchājātapaccayena paccayo…  kammapaccayena paccayo…  āhārapaccayena paccayo…  indriyapaccayena paccayo. (7)

(61.)

3081\. Sanidassanasappaṭigho ca anidassanaappaṭigho ca dhammā anidassanaappaṭighassa dhammassa purejātaṃ. (1)

3082\. Anidassanasappaṭigho ca anidassanaappaṭigho ca dhammā sanidassanasappaṭighassa dhammassa sahajātapaccayena paccayo. (1)

3083\. Anidassanasappaṭigho ca anidassanaappaṭigho ca dhammā anidassanasappaṭighassa dhammassa sahajātapaccayena paccayo. (2)

3084\. Anidassanasappaṭigho ca anidassanaappaṭigho ca dhammā anidassanaappaṭighassa dhammassa sahajātaṃ, purejātaṃ. (3)

3085\. Anidassanasappaṭigho ca anidassanaappaṭigho ca dhammā sanidassanasappaṭighassa ca anidassanaappaṭighassa ca dhammassa sahajātapaccayena paccayo. (4)

3086\. Anidassanasappaṭigho ca anidassanaappaṭigho ca dhammā anidassanasappaṭighassa ca anidassanaappaṭighassa ca dhammassa sahajātapaccayena paccayo. (5)

3087\. Anidassanasappaṭigho ca anidassanaappaṭigho ca dhammā sanidassanasappaṭighassa ca anidassanasappaṭighassa ca dhammassa sahajātapaccayena paccayo. (6)

3088\. Anidassanasappaṭigho ca anidassanaappaṭigho ca dhammā sanidassanasappaṭighassa ca anidassanasappaṭighassa ca anidassanaappaṭighassa ca dhammassa sahajātapaccayena paccayo. (7)

(62.)

3089\. Sanidassanasappaṭigho ca anidassanasappaṭigho ca dhammā anidassanaappaṭighassa dhammassa purejātaṃ. (1)

3090\. Sanidassanasappaṭigho ca anidassanasappaṭigho ca anidassanaappaṭigho ca dhammā anidassanaappaṭighassa dhammassa sahajātapaccayena paccayo…  purejātapaccayena paccayo. (1)

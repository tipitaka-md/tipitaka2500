(80.)

978. Dassanena pahātabbo dhammo dassanena pahātabbassa dhammassa aññamaññapaccayena paccayo—  dassanena pahātabbo eko khandho tiṇṇannaṃ khandhānaṃ…pe… . (1)

979. Bhāvanāya pahātabbo dhammo bhāvanāya pahātabbassa dhammassa…pe…  bhāvanāya pahātabbo eko khandho tiṇṇannaṃ khandhānaṃ…pe… . (1)

980. Nevadassanena nabhāvanāya pahātabbo dhammo nevadassanena nabhāvanāya pahātabbassa dhammassa…pe…  nevadassanena nabhāvanāya pahātabbo eko khandho tiṇṇannaṃ khandhānaṃ aññamaññapaccayena paccayo…pe…  dve khandhā…pe…  paṭisandhikkhaṇe nevadassanena nabhāvanāya pahātabbo eko khandho tiṇṇannaṃ khandhānaṃ vatthussa ca aññamaññapaccayena paccayo…pe…  dve khandhā…pe…  khandhā vatthussa…pe…  vatthu khandhānaṃ…pe…  ekaṃ mahābhūtaṃ tiṇṇannaṃ mahābhūtānaṃ aññamaññapaccayena paccayo…pe…  asaññasattānaṃ…pe… . (1)

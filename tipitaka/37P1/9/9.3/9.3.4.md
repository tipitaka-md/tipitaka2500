# 9.3.4 Paccayapaccanīyānuloma

### Dassanenapahātabbattika

### Paccayavāra

### Paccayapaccanīyānuloma

(59.)

920. Nahetupaccayā ārammaṇe satta, anantare satta, samanantare satta, sahajāte satta, aññamaññe satta, nissaye satta, upanissaye satta, purejāte satta, āsevane satta, kamme satta, vipāke ekaṃ, āhāre satta, indriye satta, jhāne satta, magge cha, sampayutte satta, vippayutte satta, atthiyā satta, natthiyā satta, vigate satta, avigate satta. (Evaṃ gaṇetabbaṃ.)

===

921. Paccanīyānulomaṃ.

  
Paccayavāro.

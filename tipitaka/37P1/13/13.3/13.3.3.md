# 13.3.3 Paccayānulomapaccanīya

* Parittattika

* Paccayavāra

* Paccayānulomapaccanīya

(40.)

1991\. Hetupaccayā naārammaṇe pañca, naadhipatiyā dvādasa, naanantare pañca, nasamanantare pañca, naaññamaññe pañca, naupanissaye pañca, napurejāte dvādasa, napacchājāte sattarasa, naāsevane sattarasa, nakamme satta, navipāke sattarasa, nasampayutte pañca, navippayutte tīṇi, nonatthiyā pañca, novigate pañca. (Evaṃ gaṇetabbaṃ.)

---

1992\. Anulomapaccanīyaṃ.

### Hetu

(42.)

1996. Parittaṃ dhammaṃ saṃsaṭṭho paritto dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā—  parittaṃ ekaṃ khandhaṃ saṃsaṭṭhā tayo khandhā…pe…  dve khandhe…pe…  paṭisandhikkhaṇe…pe… . (1)

1997. Mahaggataṃ dhammaṃ saṃsaṭṭho mahaggato dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā—  mahaggataṃ ekaṃ khandhaṃ saṃsaṭṭhā tayo khandhā…pe…  dve khandhe…pe…  paṭisandhikkhaṇe…pe… . (1)

1998. Appamāṇaṃ dhammaṃ saṃsaṭṭho appamāṇo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā—  appamāṇaṃ ekaṃ khandhaṃ saṃsaṭṭhā tayo khandhā…pe…  dve khandhe…pe… . (1)

# 8 Kusalattika, Upādānadukādi

### Kusalattika, Upādānadukādi

(40.)

88. Nakusalaṃ noupādānaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca… . (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā ekūnatiṃsa, ārammaṇe catuvīsa…pe…  vipāke nava…pe…  avigate ekūnatiṃsa.

89. Nakusalaṃ nanoupādānaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca… . (Saṃkhittaṃ. Sabbattha nava. Vipākaṃ natthi.)

(41.)

90. Nakusalaṃ nokilesaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca… . (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā ekūnatiṃsa, ārammaṇe catuvīsa…pe…  vipāke nava…pe…  avigate ekūnatiṃsa.

91. Nakusalaṃ nanokilesaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca… . (Saṃkhittaṃ. Sabbattha nava. Vipākaṃ natthi.)

# 1.1.1.6.1 Paccanīkānuloma

### Mahāpaṇṇāsaka

### Paṭhamavagga

### Puggalakathā

### Kālasaccikaṭṭha

### Paccanīkānuloma

(15.)

56. × Puggalo nupalabbhati saccikaṭṭhaparamatthenāti? Āmantā. Sabbadā puggalo nupalabbhati saccikaṭṭhaparamatthenāti? Na hevaṃ vattabbe.

57. Ājānāhi niggahaṃ. Hañci puggalo nupalabbhati saccikaṭṭhaparamatthena, tena vata re vattabbe—  “sabbadā puggalo nupalabbhati saccikaṭṭhaparamatthenā”ti. Yaṃ tattha vadesi—  “vattabbe kho—  ‘puggalo nupalabbhati saccikaṭṭhaparamatthena’, no ca vattabbe—  ‘sabbadā puggalo nupalabbhati saccikaṭṭhaparamatthenā’”ti micchā.

58. No ce pana vattabbe—  “sabbadā puggalo nupalabbhati saccikaṭṭhaparamatthenā”ti, no ca vata re vattabbe—  “puggalo nupalabbhati saccikaṭṭhaparamatthenā”ti. Yaṃ tattha vadesi—  “vattabbe kho—  ‘puggalo nupalabbhati saccikaṭṭhaparamatthena’, no ca vattabbe—  ‘sabbadā puggalo nupalabbhati saccikaṭṭhaparamatthenā’”ti micchā…pe… .

---

59. Sattamo niggaho.

# 2.1.9 Paṭhamapuggalasutta

### Dutiyapaṇṇāsaka

### Gotamīvagga

### Paṭhamapuggalasutta

(59.)

610. “Aṭṭhime, bhikkhave, puggalā āhuneyyā pāhuneyyā dakkhiṇeyyā añjalikaraṇīyā anuttaraṃ puññakkhettaṃ lokassa? Katame aṭṭha? Sotāpanno, sotāpattiphalasacchikiriyāya paṭipanno, sakadāgāmī, sakadāgāmiphalasacchikiriyāya paṭipanno, anāgāmī, anāgāmiphalasacchikiriyāya paṭipanno, arahā, arahattāya paṭipanno. Ime kho, bhikkhave, aṭṭha puggalā āhuneyyā…pe…  anuttaraṃ puññakkhettaṃ lokassāti.

611. _Cattāro ca paṭipannā,_  
_cattāro ca phale ṭhitā;_  
_Esa saṃgho ujubhūto,_  
_paññāsīlasamāhito._  


612. _Yajamānānaṃ manussānaṃ,_  
_Puññapekkhāna pāṇinaṃ;_  
_Karotaṃ opadhikaṃ puññaṃ,_  
_Saṃghe dinnaṃ mahapphalan”ti._  


===

613. Navamaṃ.

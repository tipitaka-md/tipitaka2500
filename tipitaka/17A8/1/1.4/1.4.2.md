# 1.4.2 Dutiyadānasutta

### Paṭhamapaṇṇāsaka

### Dānavagga

### Dutiyadānasutta

(32.)

324\. _“Saddhā hiriyaṃ kusalañca dānaṃ,_  
_Dhammā ete sappurisānuyātā;_  
_Etañhi maggaṃ diviyaṃ vadanti,_  
_Etena hi gacchati devalokan”ti._  


---

325\. Dutiyaṃ.

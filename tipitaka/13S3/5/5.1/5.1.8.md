# 5.1.8 Taṇhāsutta

### Uppādasaṃyutta

### Uppādavagga

### Taṇhāsutta

(319.)

1083. Sāvatthinidānaṃ. “Yo kho, bhikkhave, rūpataṇhāya uppādo ṭhiti…pe…  jarāmaraṇassa pātubhāvo…pe…  yo dhammataṇhāya uppādo ṭhiti abhinibbatti pātubhāvo, dukkhasseso uppādo, rogānaṃ ṭhiti, jarāmaraṇassa pātubhāvo. Yo ca kho, bhikkhave, rūpataṇhāya nirodho…pe…  jarāmaraṇassa atthaṅgamo…pe…  yo dhammataṇhāya nirodho vūpasamo atthaṅgamo, dukkhasseso nirodho, rogānaṃ vūpasamo, jarāmaraṇassa atthaṅgamo”ti.

===

1084. Aṭṭhamaṃ.

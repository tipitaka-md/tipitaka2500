# 3.2.20 Arūpīattāsutta

* Diṭṭhisaṃyutta

* Dutiyagamanavagga

* Arūpīattāsutta

(243.)

994\. Sāvatthinidānaṃ. “Kismiṃ nu kho, bhikkhave, sati, kiṃ upādāya, kiṃ abhinivissa evaṃ diṭṭhi uppajjati—  ‘arūpī attā hoti arogo paraṃ maraṇā’”ti? (Peyyālo.)

---

995\. Vīsatimaṃ.

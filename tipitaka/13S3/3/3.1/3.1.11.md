# 3.1.11 Antavāsutta

* Diṭṭhisaṃyutta

* Sotāpattivagga

* Antavāsutta

(216.)

957\. Sāvatthinidānaṃ. “Kismiṃ nu kho, bhikkhave, sati, kiṃ upādāya, kiṃ abhinivissa evaṃ diṭṭhi uppajjati—  ‘antavā loko’”ti? “Bhagavaṃmūlakā no, bhante, dhammā…pe…  niyato sambodhiparāyano”ti.

---

958\. Ekādasamaṃ.

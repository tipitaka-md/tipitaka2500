# 3.1.12 Anantavāsutta

### Diṭṭhisaṃyutta

### Sotāpattivagga

### Anantavāsutta

(217.)

959. Sāvatthinidānaṃ. “Kismiṃ nu kho, bhikkhave, sati, kiṃ upādāya, kiṃ abhinivissa evaṃ diṭṭhi uppajjati—  ‘anantavā loko’”ti? “Bhagavaṃmūlakā no, bhante, dhammā…pe…  niyato sambodhiparāyano”ti.

===

960. Dvādasamaṃ.

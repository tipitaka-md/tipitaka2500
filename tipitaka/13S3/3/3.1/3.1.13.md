# 3.1.13 Taṃjīvaṃtaṃsarīraṃsutta

### Diṭṭhisaṃyutta

### Sotāpattivagga

### Taṃjīvaṃtaṃsarīraṃsutta

(218.)

961. Sāvatthinidānaṃ. “Kismiṃ nu kho, bhikkhave, sati, kiṃ upādāya, kiṃ abhinivissa evaṃ diṭṭhi uppajjati—  ‘taṃ jīvaṃ taṃ sarīran’”ti? “Bhagavaṃmūlakā no, bhante, dhammā…pe…  niyato sambodhiparāyano”ti.

===

962. Terasamaṃ.

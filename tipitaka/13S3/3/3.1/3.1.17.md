# 3.1.17 Hoticanacahotitathāgatosutta

### Diṭṭhisaṃyutta

### Sotāpattivagga

### Hoticanacahotitathāgatosutta

(222.)

969. Sāvatthinidānaṃ. “Kismiṃ nu kho, bhikkhave, sati, kiṃ upādāya, kiṃ abhinivissa evaṃ diṭṭhi uppajjati—  ‘hoti ca na ca hoti tathāgato paraṃ maraṇā’”ti? “Bhagavaṃmūlakā no, bhante, dhammā…pe…  niyato sambodhiparāyano”ti.

===

970. Sattarasamaṃ.

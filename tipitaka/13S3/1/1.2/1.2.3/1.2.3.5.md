# 1.2.3.5 Dutiyaarahantasutta

* Khandhasaṃyutta

* Majjhimapaṇṇāsaka

* Khajjanīyavagga

* Dutiyaarahantasutta

(77.)

397\. Sāvatthinidānaṃ. “Rūpaṃ, bhikkhave, aniccaṃ. Yadaniccaṃ taṃ dukkhaṃ; yaṃ dukkhaṃ tadanattā; yadanattā taṃ ‘netaṃ mama, nesohamasmi, na meso attā’ti…pe…  evametaṃ yathābhūtaṃ sammappaññāya daṭṭhabbaṃ.

398\. Evaṃ passaṃ, bhikkhave, sutavā ariyasāvako rūpasmimpi nibbindati, vedanāyapi…  saññāyapi…  saṅkhāresupi…  viññāṇasmimpi nibbindati. Nibbindaṃ virajjati; virāgā vimuccati. Vimuttasmiṃ vimuttamiti ñāṇaṃ hoti. ‘Khīṇā jāti, vusitaṃ brahmacariyaṃ, kataṃ karaṇīyaṃ, nāparaṃ itthattāyā’ti pajānāti. Yāvatā, bhikkhave, sattāvāsā, yāvatā bhavaggaṃ, ete aggā, ete seṭṭhā lokasmiṃ yadidaṃ arahanto”ti.

---

399\. Pañcamaṃ.

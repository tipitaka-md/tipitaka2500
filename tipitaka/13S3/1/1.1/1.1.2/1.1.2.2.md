# 1.1.2.2 Dukkhasutta

* Khandhasaṃyutta

* Mūlapaṇṇāsaka

* Aniccavagga

* Dukkhasutta

(13.)

100\. Sāvatthinidānaṃ. “Rūpaṃ, bhikkhave, dukkhaṃ, vedanā dukkhā, saññā dukkhā, saṅkhārā dukkhā, viññāṇaṃ dukkhaṃ. Evaṃ passaṃ…pe…  nāparaṃ itthattāyāti pajānātī”ti.

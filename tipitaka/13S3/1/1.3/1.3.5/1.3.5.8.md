# 1.3.5.8 Abhinivesasutta

### Khandhasaṃyutta

### Uparipaṇṇāsaka

### Diṭṭhivagga

### Abhinivesasutta

(157.)

807. Sāvatthinidānaṃ. “Kismiṃ nu kho, bhikkhave, sati, kiṃ upādāya, kiṃ abhinivissa uppajjanti saṃyojanābhinivesavinibandhā”ti? Bhagavaṃmūlakā no, bhante, dhammā…pe…  “rūpe kho, bhikkhave, sati, rūpaṃ upādāya, rūpaṃ abhinivissa uppajjanti saṃyojanābhinivesavinibandhā. Vedanāya sati…  saññāya sati…  saṅkhāresu sati…  viññāṇe sati, viññāṇaṃ upādāya, viññāṇaṃ abhinivissa uppajjanti saṃyojanābhinivesavinibandhā. Taṃ kiṃ maññatha, bhikkhave, rūpaṃ niccaṃ vā aniccaṃ vā”ti? “Aniccaṃ, bhante”. “Yaṃ panāniccaṃ…pe…  api nu taṃ anupādāya uppajjeyyuṃ saṃyojanābhinivesavinibandhā”ti? “No hetaṃ, bhante” …pe…  “evaṃ passaṃ…pe…  nāparaṃ itthattāyāti pajānātī”ti.

===

808. Aṭṭhamaṃ.

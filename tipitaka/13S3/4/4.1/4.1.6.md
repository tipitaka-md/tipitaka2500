# 4.1.6 Rūpasaññāsutta

### Okkantasaṃyutta

### Cakkhuvagga

### Rūpasaññāsutta

(307.)

1053\. Sāvatthinidānaṃ. “Rūpasaññā, bhikkhave, aniccā vipariṇāmī aññathābhāvī; saddasaññā…  gandhasaññā…  rasasaññā…  phoṭṭhabbasaññā…  dhammasaññā aniccā vipariṇāmī aññathābhāvī. Yo, bhikkhave, ime dhamme evaṃ saddahati adhimuccati, ayaṃ vuccati ‘saddhānusārī…pe…  sambodhiparāyano’”ti.

---

1054\. Chaṭṭhaṃ.

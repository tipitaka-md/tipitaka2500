# 4.1.8 Rūpataṇhāsutta

* Okkantasaṃyutta

* Cakkhuvagga

* Rūpataṇhāsutta

(309.)

1057\. Sāvatthinidānaṃ. “Rūpataṇhā, bhikkhave, aniccā vipariṇāmī aññathābhāvī; saddataṇhā…  gandhataṇhā…  rasataṇhā…  phoṭṭhabbataṇhā…  dhammataṇhā aniccā vipariṇāmī aññathābhāvī. Yo, bhikkhave, ime dhamme evaṃ saddahati adhimuccati, ayaṃ vuccati ‘saddhānusārī…pe…  sambodhiparāyano’”ti.

---

1058\. Aṭṭhamaṃ.

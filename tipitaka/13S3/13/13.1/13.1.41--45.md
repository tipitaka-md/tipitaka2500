# 13.1.41--45 Ārammaṇamūlakagocarasuttādipañcaka

### Jhānasaṃyutta

### Jhānavagga

### Ārammaṇamūlakagocarasuttādipañcaka

(702--706.)

1329. Sāvatthinidānaṃ…  “samādhismiṃ ārammaṇakusalo hoti, na samādhismiṃ gocarakusalo…  samādhismiṃ gocarakusalo hoti, na samādhismiṃ ārammaṇakusalo…  neva samādhismiṃ ārammaṇakusalo hoti, na ca samādhismiṃ gocarakusalo…  samādhismiṃ ārammaṇakusalo ca hoti, samādhismiṃ gocarakusalo ca. Tatra, bhikkhave, yvāyaṃ jhāyī…pe…  uttamo ca pavaro cā”ti.

===

1330. Ekacattālīsamaṃ.



===

# 2.2.8 Anattadhammasutta

* Rādhasaṃyutta

* Dutiyamāravagga

* Anattadhammasutta

(177.)

862\. Sāvatthinidānaṃ. Ekamantaṃ nisinno kho āyasmā rādho bhagavantaṃ etadavoca—  “‘anattadhammo, anattadhammo’ti, bhante, vuccati. Katamo nu kho, bhante, anattadhammo”ti? “Rūpaṃ kho, rādha, anattadhammo, vedanā anattadhammo, saññā anattadhammo, saṅkhārā anattadhammo, viññāṇaṃ anattadhammo. Evaṃ passaṃ…pe…  nāparaṃ itthattāyāti pajānātī”ti.

---

863\. Aṭṭhamaṃ.

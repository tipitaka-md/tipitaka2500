# 2.1.7 Sotāpannasutta

### Rādhasaṃyutta

### Paṭhamamāravagga

### Sotāpannasutta

(166.)

837. Sāvatthinidānaṃ. Ekamantaṃ nisinnaṃ kho āyasmantaṃ rādhaṃ bhagavā etadavoca—  “pañcime, rādha, upādānakkhandhā. Katame pañca? Rūpupādānakkhandho…pe…  viññāṇupādānakkhandho. Yato kho, rādha, ariyasāvako imesaṃ pañcannaṃ upādānakkhandhānaṃ samudayañca atthaṅgamañca assādañca ādīnavañca nissaraṇañca yathābhūtaṃ pajānāti—  ayaṃ vuccati, rādha, ariyasāvako sotāpanno avinipātadhammo niyato sambodhiparāyano”ti.

===

838. Sattamaṃ.

# 7.1.6 Viññāṇañcāyatanasutta

### Sāriputtasaṃyutta

### Sāriputtavagga

### Viññāṇañcāyatanasutta

(337.)

1132. Sāvatthinidānaṃ. Addasā kho āyasmā ānando…pe…  “idhāhaṃ, āvuso, sabbaso ākāsānañcāyatanaṃ samatikkamma anantaṃ viññāṇanti viññāṇañcāyatanaṃ upasampajja viharāmi…pe…  vuṭṭhitoti vā”ti.

===

1133. Chaṭṭhaṃ.

# 9.2 Appamādavagga

### Jhānasaṃyutta

### Appamādavagga

1631. (Appamādavaggo vitthāretabbo.)

===

1632. Tassuddānaṃ



1633. _Tathāgataṃ padaṃ kūṭaṃ,_  
_mūlaṃ sāro ca vassikaṃ;_  
_Rājā candimasūriyā,_  
_vatthena dasamaṃ padanti._

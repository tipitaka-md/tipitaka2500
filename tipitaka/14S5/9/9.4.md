# 9.4 Esanāvagga

### Jhānasaṃyutta

### Esanāvagga

1637\. (Esanāvaggo vitthāretabbo.)

---

1638\. Tassuddānaṃ



1639\. _Esanā vidhā āsavo,_  
_Bhavo ca dukkhatā tisso;_  
_Khilaṃ malañca nīgho ca,_  
_Vedanā taṇhā tasinā cāti._

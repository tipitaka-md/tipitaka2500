# 6.2 Appamādavagga

### Balasaṃyutta

### Appamādavagga

1315. (Appamādavaggo vitthāretabbo.)

===

1316. Tassuddānaṃ



1317. _Tathāgataṃ padaṃ kūṭaṃ,_  
_mūlaṃ sārena vassikaṃ;_  
_Rājā candimasūriyā,_  
_vatthena dasamaṃ padanti._

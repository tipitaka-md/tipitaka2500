# 6.4 Esanāvagga

* Balasaṃyutta

* Esanāvagga

1321\. (Esanāvaggo vitthāretabbo.)

---

1322\. Tassuddānaṃ



1323\. _Esanā vidhā āsavo,_  
_Bhavo ca dukkhatā tisso;_  
_Khilaṃ malañca nīgho ca,_  
_Vedanā taṇhā tasinā cāti._

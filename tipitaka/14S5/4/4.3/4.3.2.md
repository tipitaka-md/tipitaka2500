# 4.3.2 Jīvitindriyasutta

### Indriyasaṃyutta

### Chaḷindriyavagga

### Jīvitindriyasutta

(492.)

1063. “Tīṇimāni, bhikkhave, indriyāni. Katamāni tīṇi? Itthindriyaṃ, purisindriyaṃ, jīvitindriyaṃ—  imāni kho, bhikkhave, tīṇi indriyānī”ti.

===

1064. Dutiyaṃ.

# 4.4.1 Suddhikasutta

* Indriyasaṃyutta

* Sukhindriyavagga

* Suddhikasutta

(501.)

1085\. “Pañcimāni, bhikkhave, indriyāni. Katamāni pañca? Sukhindriyaṃ, dukkhindriyaṃ, somanassindriyaṃ, domanassindriyaṃ, upekkhindriyaṃ—  imāni kho, bhikkhave, pañcindriyānī”ti.

---

1086\. Paṭhamaṃ.

# 4.2.10 Āsavakkhayasutta

* Indriyasaṃyutta

* Mudutaravagga

* Āsavakkhayasutta

(490.)

1056\. “Pañcimāni, bhikkhave, indriyāni. Katamāni pañca? Saddhindriyaṃ…pe…  paññindriyaṃ—  imāni kho, bhikkhave, pañcindriyāni. Imesaṃ kho, bhikkhave, pañcannaṃ indriyānaṃ bhāvitattā bahulīkatattā bhikkhu āsavānaṃ khayā anāsavaṃ cetovimuttiṃ paññāvimuttiṃ diṭṭheva dhamme sayaṃ abhiññā sacchikatvā upasampajja viharatī”ti.

---

1057\. Dasamaṃ.



---

1058\. Mudutaravaggo dutiyo.



---

1059\. Tassuddānaṃ



1060\. _Paṭilābho tayo saṃkhittā,_  
_vitthārā apare tayo;_  
_Paṭipanno ca sampanno,_  
_dasamaṃ āsavakkhayanti._

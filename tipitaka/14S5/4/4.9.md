# 4.9 Appamādavagga

* Indriyasaṃyutta

* Appamādavagga

1258\. (Appamādavaggo vitthāretabbo.)

---

1259\. Tassuddānaṃ



1260\. _Tathāgataṃ padaṃ kūṭaṃ,_  
_mūlaṃ sārena vassikaṃ;_  
_Rājā candimasūriyā,_  
_vatthena dasamaṃ padanti._

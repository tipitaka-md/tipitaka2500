# 4.7.5 Paṭhamaphalasutta

* Indriyasaṃyutta

* Bodhipakkhiyavagga

* Paṭhamaphalasutta

(535.)

1238\. “Pañcimāni, bhikkhave, indriyāni. Katamāni pañca? Saddhindriyaṃ…pe…  paññindriyaṃ—  imāni kho, bhikkhave, pañcindriyāni. Imesaṃ kho, bhikkhave, pañcannaṃ indriyānaṃ bhāvitattā bahulīkatattā dvinnaṃ phalānaṃ aññataraṃ phalaṃ pāṭikaṅkhaṃ—  diṭṭheva dhamme aññā, sati vā upādisese anāgāmitā”ti.

---

1239\. Pañcamaṃ.

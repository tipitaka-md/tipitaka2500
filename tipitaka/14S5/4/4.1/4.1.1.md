# 4.1.1 Suddhikasutta

### Indriyasaṃyutta

### Suddhikavagga

### Suddhikasutta

(471.)

1000. Sāvatthinidānaṃ. Tatra kho bhagavā etadavoca. “Pañcimāni, bhikkhave, indriyāni. Katamāni pañca? Saddhindriyaṃ, vīriyindriyaṃ, satindriyaṃ, samādhindriyaṃ, paññindriyaṃ—  imāni kho, bhikkhave, pañcindriyānī”ti.

===

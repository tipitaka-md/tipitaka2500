# 1.7.2--6 Sīlasampadādisuttapañcaka

* Maggasaṃyutta

* Ekadhammapeyyālavagga

* Sīlasampadādisuttapañcaka

(64--68.)

172\. “Ekadhammo, bhikkhave, bahūpakāro ariyassa aṭṭhaṅgikassa maggassa uppādāya. Katamo ekadhammo? Yadidaṃ—  sīlasampadā…pe…  yadidaṃ—  chandasampadā…pe…  yadidaṃ—  attasampadā…pe…  yadidaṃ—  diṭṭhisampadā…pe…  yadidaṃ—  appamādasampadā…pe… .

---

173\. Chaṭṭhaṃ.

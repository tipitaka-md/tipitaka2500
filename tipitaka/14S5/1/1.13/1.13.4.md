# 1.13.4 Bhavasutta

### Maggasaṃyutta

### Esanāvagga

### Bhavasutta

(164.)

316. “Tayome, bhikkhave, bhavā. Katame tayo? Kāmabhavo, rūpabhavo, arūpabhavo—  ime kho, bhikkhave, tayo bhavā. Imesaṃ kho, bhikkhave, tiṇṇannaṃ bhavānaṃ abhiññāya pariññāya parikkhayāya pahānāya…pe…  ayaṃ ariyo aṭṭhaṅgiko maggo bhāvetabbo”ti.

===

317. Catutthaṃ.

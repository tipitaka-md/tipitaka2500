# 1.4.9 Paṭhamabrahmacariyasutta

### Maggasaṃyutta

### Paṭipattivagga

### Paṭhamabrahmacariyasutta

(39.)

139\. Sāvatthinidānaṃ. “Brahmacariyañca vo, bhikkhave, desessāmi, brahmacariyaphalāni ca. Taṃ suṇātha. Katamañca, bhikkhave, brahmacariyaṃ? Ayameva ariyo aṭṭhaṅgiko maggo, seyyathidaṃ—  sammādiṭṭhi…pe…  sammāsamādhi. Idaṃ vuccati, bhikkhave, brahmacariyaṃ. Katamāni ca, bhikkhave, brahmacariyaphalāni? Sotāpattiphalaṃ, sakadāgāmiphalaṃ, anāgāmiphalaṃ, arahattaphalaṃ—  imāni vuccanti, bhikkhave, brahmacariyaphalānī”ti.

---

140\. Navamaṃ.

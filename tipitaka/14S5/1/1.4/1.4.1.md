# 1.4.1 Paṭhamapaṭipattisutta

* Maggasaṃyutta

* Paṭipattivagga

* Paṭhamapaṭipattisutta

(31.)

117\. Sāvatthinidānaṃ. “Micchāpaṭipattiñca vo, bhikkhave, desessāmi, sammāpaṭipattiñca. Taṃ suṇātha. Katamā ca, bhikkhave, micchāpaṭipatti? Seyyathidaṃ—  micchādiṭṭhi…pe…  micchāsamādhi. Ayaṃ vuccati, bhikkhave, micchāpaṭipatti. Katamā ca, bhikkhave, sammāpaṭipatti? Seyyathidaṃ—  sammādiṭṭhi…pe…  sammāsamādhi. Ayaṃ vuccati, bhikkhave, sammāpaṭipattī”ti.

---

118\. Paṭhamaṃ.

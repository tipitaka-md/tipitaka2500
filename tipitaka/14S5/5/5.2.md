# 5.2 Appamādavagga

### Sammappadhānasaṃyutta

### Appamādavagga

1291. (Appamādavaggo sammappadhānavasena vitthāretabbo.)

===

1292. Tassuddānaṃ



1293. _Tathāgataṃ padaṃ kūṭaṃ,_  
_mūlaṃ sārena vassikaṃ;_  
_Rājā candimasūriyā,_  
_vatthena dasamaṃ padanti._

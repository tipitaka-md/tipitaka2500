# 11.6.1 Sagāthakasutta

* Sotāpattisaṃyutta

* Sappaññavagga

* Sagāthakasutta

(1047.)

2091\. “Catūhi, bhikkhave, dhammehi samannāgato ariyasāvako sotāpanno hoti avinipātadhammo niyato sambodhiparāyaṇo.

2092\. Katamehi catūhi? Idha, bhikkhave, ariyasāvako buddhe aveccappasādena samannāgato hoti—  itipi so bhagavā…pe…  satthā devamanussānaṃ buddho bhagavāti. Dhamme…pe…  saṃghe…pe…  ariyakantehi sīlehi samannāgato hoti akhaṇḍehi…pe…  samādhisaṃvattanikehi. Imehi kho, bhikkhave, catūhi dhammehi samannāgato ariyasāvako sotāpanno hoti avinipātadhammo niyato sambodhiparāyaṇo”ti. Idamavoca bhagavā. Idaṃ vatvāna sugato athāparaṃ etadavoca satthā—

2093\. _“Yassa saddhā tathāgate,_  
_acalā suppatiṭṭhitā;_  
_Sīlañca yassa kalyāṇaṃ,_  
_ariyakantaṃ pasaṃsitaṃ._  


2094\. _Saṃghe pasādo yassatthi,_  
_ujubhūtañca dassanaṃ;_  
_Adaliddoti taṃ āhu,_  
_amoghaṃ tassa jīvitaṃ._  


2095\. _Tasmā saddhañca sīlañca,_  
_pasādaṃ dhammadassanaṃ;_  
_Anuyuñjetha medhāvī,_  
_saraṃ buddhānasāsanan”ti._  


---

2096\. Paṭhamaṃ.

### Sappaññavagga

### Paññāvepullasutta

(1057.)

2131. “… . Paññāvepullāya…  saṃvattantī”ti.

---

2132. Ekādasamaṃ.



---

2133. Sappaññavaggo chaṭṭho.



---

2134. Tassuddānaṃ



2135. _Sagāthakaṃ vassaṃvutthaṃ,_

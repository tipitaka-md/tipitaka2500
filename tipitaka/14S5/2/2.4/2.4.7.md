# 2.4.7 Buddhisutta

### Bojjhaṅgasaṃyutta

### Nīvaraṇavagga

### Buddhisutta

(218.)

513. “Sattime, bhikkhave, bojjhaṅgā bhāvitā bahulīkatā buddhiyā aparihānāya saṃvattanti. Katame satta? Satisambojjhaṅgo…pe…  upekkhāsambojjhaṅgo. Ime kho, bhikkhave, satta bojjhaṅgā bhāvitā bahulīkatā buddhiyā aparihānāya saṃvattantī”ti.

===

514. Sattamaṃ.

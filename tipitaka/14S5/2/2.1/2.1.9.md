# 2.1.9 Paṭhamauppannasutta

### Bojjhaṅgasaṃyutta

### Pabbatavagga

### Paṭhamauppannasutta

(190.)

416\. “Sattime, bhikkhave, bojjhaṅgā bhāvitā bahulīkatā anuppannā uppajjanti, nāññatra tathāgatassa pātubhāvā arahato sammāsambuddhassa. Katame satta? Satisambojjhaṅgo…pe…  upekkhāsambojjhaṅgo—  ime kho, bhikkhave, satta bojjhaṅgā bhāvitā bahulīkatā anuppannā uppajjanti, nāññatra tathāgatassa pātubhāvā arahato sammāsambuddhassā”ti.

---

417\. Navamaṃ.

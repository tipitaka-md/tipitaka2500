# 2.5.6 Daliddasutta

### Bojjhaṅgasaṃyutta

### Cakkavattivagga

### Daliddasutta

(227.)

542. “‘Daliddo, daliddo’ti, bhante, vuccati. Kittāvatā nu kho, bhante, ‘daliddo’ti vuccatī”ti? “Sattannaṃ kho, bhikkhu, bojjhaṅgānaṃ abhāvitattā abahulīkatattā ‘daliddo’ti vuccati. Katamesaṃ sattannaṃ? Satisambojjhaṅgassa…pe…  upekkhāsambojjhaṅgassa—  imesaṃ kho, bhikkhu, sattannaṃ bojjhaṅgānaṃ abhāvitattā abahulīkatattā ‘daliddo’ti vuccatī”ti.

===

543. Chaṭṭhaṃ.

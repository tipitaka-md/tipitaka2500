# 2.5.3 Mārasutta

### Bojjhaṅgasaṃyutta

### Cakkavattivagga

### Mārasutta

(224.)

536. “Mārasenappamaddanaṃ vo, bhikkhave, maggaṃ desessāmi; taṃ suṇātha. Katamo ca, bhikkhave, mārasenappamaddano maggo? Yadidaṃ—  satta bojjhaṅgā. Katame satta? Satisambojjhaṅgo…pe…  upekkhāsambojjhaṅgo—  ayaṃ kho, bhikkhave, mārasenappamaddano maggo”ti.

===

537. Tatiyaṃ.

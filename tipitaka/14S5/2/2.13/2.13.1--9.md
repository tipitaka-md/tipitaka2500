# 2.13.1--9 Oghādisutta

### Bojjhaṅgasaṃyutta

### Oghavagga

### Oghādisutta

(302--310.)

719. “Cattārome, bhikkhave, oghā. Katame cattāro? Kāmogho, bhavogho, diṭṭhogho, avijjogho”ti vitthāretabbaṃ.

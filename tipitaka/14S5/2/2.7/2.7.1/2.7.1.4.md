# 2.7.1.4 Saṃvegasutta

### Bojjhaṅgasaṃyutta

### Ānāpānavagga

### Aṭṭhikamahapphalasutta

### Saṃvegasutta

669\. “Aṭṭhikasaññā, bhikkhave, bhāvitā bahulīkatā mahato saṃvegāya saṃvattati. Kathaṃ bhāvitā ca, bhikkhave, aṭṭhikasaññā kathaṃ bahulīkatā mahato saṃvegāya saṃvattati? Idha, bhikkhave, bhikkhu aṭṭhikasaññāsahagataṃ satisambojjhaṅgaṃ bhāveti…pe…  aṭṭhikasaññāsahagataṃ upekkhāsambojjhaṅgaṃ bhāveti vivekanissitaṃ virāganissitaṃ nirodhanissitaṃ vossaggapariṇāmiṃ. Evaṃ bhāvitā kho, bhikkhave, aṭṭhikasaññā evaṃ bahulīkatā mahato saṃvegāya saṃvattatī”ti.

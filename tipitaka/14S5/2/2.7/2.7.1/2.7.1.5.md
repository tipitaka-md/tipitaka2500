# 2.7.1.5 Phāsuvihārasutta

### Bojjhaṅgasaṃyutta

### Ānāpānavagga

### Aṭṭhikamahapphalasutta

### Phāsuvihārasutta

670. “Aṭṭhikasaññā, bhikkhave, bhāvitā bahulīkatā mahato phāsuvihārāya saṃvattati. Kathaṃ bhāvitā ca, bhikkhave, aṭṭhikasaññā kathaṃ bahulīkatā mahato phāsuvihārāya saṃvattati? Idha, bhikkhave, bhikkhu aṭṭhikasaññāsahagataṃ satisambojjhaṅgaṃ bhāveti…pe…  aṭṭhikasaññāsahagataṃ upekkhāsambojjhaṅgaṃ bhāveti vivekanissitaṃ virāganissitaṃ nirodhanissitaṃ vossaggapariṇāmiṃ. Evaṃ bhāvitā kho, bhikkhave, aṭṭhikasaññā evaṃ bahulīkatā mahato phāsuvihārāya saṃvattatī”ti.

---

671. Paṭhamaṃ.

# 2.2.9 Ariyasutta

### Bojjhaṅgasaṃyutta

### Gilānavagga

### Ariyasutta

(200.)

455. “Sattime, bhikkhave, bojjhaṅgā bhāvitā bahulīkatā ariyā niyyānikā nīyanti takkarassa sammā dukkhakkhayāya. Katame satta? Satisambojjhaṅgo…pe…  upekkhāsambojjhaṅgo—  ime kho, bhikkhave, satta bojjhaṅgā bhāvitā bahulīkatā ariyā niyyānikā nīyanti takkarassa sammā dukkhakkhayāyā”ti.

===

456. Navamaṃ.

# 2.18.1--10 Punaoghādisutta

* Bojjhaṅgasaṃyutta

* Punaoghavagga

* Punaoghādisutta

(357--366.)

744\. …pe…

---

745\. Bojjhaṅgasaṃyuttassa punaoghavaggo aṭṭhārasamo.



---

746\. Uddānaṃ



747\. _Ogho yogo upādānaṃ,_  
_Ganthā anusayena ca;_  
_Kāmaguṇā nīvaraṇā,_  
_Khandhā oruddhambhāgiyānīti._  


748\. (Rāgavinayapariyosāna-dosavinayapariyosāna-mohavinayapariyosānavaggo vitthāretabbo.) (Yadapi maggasaṃyuttaṃ vitthāretabbaṃ, tadapi bojjhaṅgasaṃyuttaṃ vitthāretabbaṃ.)

---

749\. Bojjhaṅgasaṃyuttaṃ dutiyaṃ.

# 7.5 Appamādavagga

### Iddhipādasaṃyutta

### Appamādavagga

1532. (Appamādavaggo vitthāretabbo.)

---

1533. Tassuddānaṃ



1534. _Tathāgataṃ padaṃ kūṭaṃ,_  
_mūlaṃ sāro ca vassikaṃ;_  
_Rājā candimasūriyā,_  
_vatthena dasamaṃ padanti._

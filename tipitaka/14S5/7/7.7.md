# 7.7 Esanāvagga

### Iddhipādasaṃyutta

### Esanāvagga

1538. (Esanāvaggo vitthāretabbo.)

===

1539. Tassuddānaṃ



1540. _Esanā vidhā āsavo,_  
_Bhavo ca dukkhatā tisso;_  
_Khilaṃ malañca nīgho ca,_  
_Vedanā taṇhā tasinā cāti._

### Dutiyaāmakadhaññapeyyālavagga

### Gandhavilepanasutta

(1150.)

2480. Tassuddānaṃ



2481. _Pāṇaṃ adinnaṃ kāmesu,_  
_musāvādañca pesuññaṃ;_

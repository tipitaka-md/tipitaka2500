# 12.10.6--11 Chedanādisutta

### Saccasaṃyutta

### Catutthaāmakadhaññapeyyālavagga

### Chedanādisutta

(1166--1171.)

2515. …  “Evameva kho, bhikkhave, appakā te sattā ye chedanavadhabandhanaviparāmosaālopasahasākārā paṭiviratā; atha kho eteva bahutarā sattā ye chedanavadhabandhanaviparāmosaālopasahasākārā appaṭiviratā. Taṃ kissa hetu? Adiṭṭhattā bhikkhave, catunnaṃ ariyasaccānaṃ. Katamesaṃ catunnaṃ? Dukkhassa ariyasaccassa…pe…  dukkhanirodhagāminiyā paṭipadāya ariyasaccassa.

2516. Tasmātiha, bhikkhave, ‘idaṃ dukkhan’ti yogo karaṇīyo…pe…  ‘ayaṃ dukkhanirodhagāminī paṭipadā’ti yogo karaṇīyo”ti.

===

2517. Ekādasamaṃ.



===

2518. Catutthaāmakadhaññapeyyālavaggo dasamo.



===

2519. Tassuddānaṃ



2520. _Khettaṃ kāyaṃ dūteyyañca,_  
_tulākūṭaṃ ukkoṭanaṃ;_  
_Chedanaṃ vadhabandhanaṃ,_  
_viparālopaṃ sāhasanti._

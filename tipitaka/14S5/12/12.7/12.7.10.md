# 12.7.10 Pacāyikasutta

### Saccasaṃyutta

### Paṭhamaāmakadhaññapeyyālavagga

### Pacāyikasutta

(1140.)

2454. …  “Evameva kho, bhikkhave, appakā te sattā ye kule jeṭṭhāpacāyino; atha kho eteva bahutarā sattā ye kule ajeṭṭhāpacāyinoti…pe… .

---

2455. Dasamaṃ.



---

2456. Paṭhamaāmakadhaññapeyyālavaggo sattamo.



---

2457. Tassuddānaṃ



2458. _Aññatra paccantaṃ paññā,_  
_Surāmerayaodakā;_  
_Matteyya petteyyā cāpi,_  
_Sāmaññaṃ brahmapacāyikanti._

# 8.2.9 Nānādhimuttisutta

* Anuruddhasaṃyutta

* Dutiyavagga

* Nānādhimuttisutta

(917.)

1609\. “Imesañca panāhaṃ, āvuso, catunnaṃ satipaṭṭhānānaṃ bhāvitattā bahulīkatattā sattānaṃ nānādhimuttikataṃ yathābhūtaṃ pajānāmī”ti.

---

1610\. Navamaṃ.

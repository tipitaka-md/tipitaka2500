# 8.2.7 Sabbatthagāminisutta

### Anuruddhasaṃyutta

### Dutiyavagga

### Sabbatthagāminisutta

(915.)

1605. “Imesañca panāhaṃ, āvuso, catunnaṃ satipaṭṭhānānaṃ bhāvitattā bahulīkatattā sabbatthagāminippaṭipadaṃ yathābhūtaṃ pajānāmī”ti.

===

1606. Sattamaṃ.

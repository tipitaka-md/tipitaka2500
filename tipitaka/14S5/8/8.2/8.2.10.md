# 8.2.10 Indriyaparopariyattasutta

### Anuruddhasaṃyutta

### Dutiyavagga

### Indriyaparopariyattasutta

(918.)

1611. “Imesañca panāhaṃ, āvuso, catunnaṃ satipaṭṭhānānaṃ bhāvitattā bahulīkatattā parasattānaṃ parapuggalānaṃ indriyaparopariyattaṃ yathābhūtaṃ pajānāmī”ti.

===

1612. Dasamaṃ.

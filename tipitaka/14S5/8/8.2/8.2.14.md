# 8.2.14 Āsavakkhayasutta

### Anuruddhasaṃyutta

### Dutiyavagga

### Āsavakkhayasutta

(922.)

1619. “Imesañca panāhaṃ, āvuso, catunnaṃ satipaṭṭhānānaṃ bhāvitattā bahulīkatattā āsavānaṃ khayā anāsavaṃ cetovimuttiṃ paññāvimuttiṃ diṭṭheva dhamme sayaṃ abhiññā sacchikatvā upasampajja viharāmī”ti.

---

1620. Cuddasamaṃ.



---

1621. Dutiyo vaggo.



---

1622. Tassuddānaṃ



1623. _Mahābhiññaṃ iddhi dibbaṃ,_  
_Cetopariyaṃ ṭhānaṃ kammaṃ;_  
_Sabbatthadhātudhimutti,_  
_Indriyaṃ jhānaṃ tisso vijjāti._  


---

1624. Anuruddhasaṃyuttaṃ aṭṭhamaṃ.

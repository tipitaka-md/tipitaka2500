# 9.2.1.4 Suññatasamādhi

### Asaṅkhatasaṃyutta

### Dutiyavagga

### Asaṅkhatasutta

### Suññatasamādhi

1699. Katamo ca, bhikkhave, asaṅkhatagāmimaggo? Suññato samādhi. Ayaṃ vuccati, bhikkhave, asaṅkhatagāmimaggo…pe…  katamo ca, bhikkhave, asaṅkhatagāmimaggo? Animitto samādhi. Ayaṃ vuccati, bhikkhave, asaṅkhatagāmimaggo…pe…  katamo ca, bhikkhave, asaṅkhatagāmimaggo? Appaṇihito samādhi. Ayaṃ vuccati, bhikkhave, asaṅkhatagāmimaggo…pe… . (1.6--8)

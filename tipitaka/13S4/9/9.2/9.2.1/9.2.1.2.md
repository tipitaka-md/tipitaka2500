# 9.2.1.2 Vipassanā

### Asaṅkhatasaṃyutta

### Dutiyavagga

### Asaṅkhatasutta

### Vipassanā

1697. Asaṅkhatañca vo, bhikkhave, desessāmi asaṅkhatagāmiñca maggaṃ. Taṃ suṇātha. Katamañca, bhikkhave, asaṅkhataṃ? Yo, bhikkhave, rāgakkhayo dosakkhayo mohakkhayo—  idaṃ vuccati, bhikkhave, asaṅkhataṃ. Katamo ca, bhikkhave, asaṅkhatagāmimaggo? Vipassanā. Ayaṃ vuccati, bhikkhave, asaṅkhatagāmimaggo. Iti kho, bhikkhave, desitaṃ vo mayā asaṅkhataṃ…pe…  ayaṃ vo amhākaṃ anusāsanīti. (1.2)

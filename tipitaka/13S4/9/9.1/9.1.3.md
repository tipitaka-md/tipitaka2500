# 9.1.3 Savitakkasavicārasutta

* Asaṅkhatasaṃyutta

* Paṭhamavagga

* Savitakkasavicārasutta

(368.)

1675\. “Katamo ca, bhikkhave, asaṅkhatagāmimaggo? Savitakkasavicāro samādhi, avitakkavicāramatto samādhi, avitakkaavicāro samādhi—  ayaṃ vuccati, bhikkhave, asaṅkhatagāmimaggo…pe… .

---

1676\. Tatiyaṃ.

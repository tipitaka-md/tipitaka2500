# 1.3.1.6 Saṃyojaniyasutta

### Saḷāyatanasaṃyutta

### Tatiyapaṇṇāsaka

### Yogakkhemivagga

### Saṃyojaniyasutta

(109.)

598. “Saṃyojaniye ca, bhikkhave, dhamme desessāmi saṃyojanañca. Taṃ suṇātha. Katame ca, bhikkhave, saṃyojaniyā dhammā, katamañca saṃyojanaṃ? Cakkhuṃ, bhikkhave, saṃyojaniyo dhammo. Yo tattha chandarāgo, taṃ tattha saṃyojanaṃ…pe…  jivhā saṃyojaniyo dhammo…pe…  mano saṃyojaniyo dhammo. Yo tattha chandarāgo, taṃ tattha saṃyojanaṃ. Ime vuccanti, bhikkhave, saṃyojaniyā dhammā, idaṃ saṃyojanan”ti.

===

599. Chaṭṭhaṃ.

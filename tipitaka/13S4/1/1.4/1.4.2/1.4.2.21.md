# 1.4.2.21 Ajjhattapaccuppannāniccasutta

* Saḷāyatanasaṃyutta

* Catutthapaṇṇāsaka

* Saṭṭhipeyyālavagga

* Ajjhattapaccuppannāniccasutta

(188.)

900\. “Cakkhu, bhikkhave, aniccaṃ paccuppannaṃ…pe…  jivhā aniccā paccuppannā…pe…  mano anicco paccuppanno. Evaṃ passaṃ…pe…  nāparaṃ itthattāyāti pajānātī”ti.

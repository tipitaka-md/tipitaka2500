### Ajjhattāyatanaaniccasutta

(222.)

914. “Cakkhu, bhikkhave, aniccaṃ…pe…  jivhā aniccā…pe…  mano anicco. Evaṃ passaṃ…pe…  nāparaṃ itthattāyāti pajānātī”ti.

### Ajjhattāyatanadukkhasutta

(223.)

915. “Cakkhu, bhikkhave, dukkhaṃ…pe…  jivhā dukkhā…pe…  mano dukkho. Evaṃ passaṃ…pe…  nāparaṃ itthattāyāti pajānātī”ti.

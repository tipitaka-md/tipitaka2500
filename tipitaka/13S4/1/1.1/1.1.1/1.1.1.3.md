# 1.1.1.3 Ajjhattānattasutta

### Saḷāyatanasaṃyutta

### Paṭhamapaṇṇāsaka

### Aniccavagga

### Ajjhattānattasutta

(3.)

7. “Cakkhuṃ, bhikkhave, anattā. Yadanattā taṃ ‘netaṃ mama, nesohamasmi, na meso attā’ti evametaṃ yathābhūtaṃ sammappaññāya daṭṭhabbaṃ. Sotaṃ anattā…pe…  ghānaṃ anattā…  jivhā anattā…  kāyo anattā…  mano anattā. Yadanattā taṃ ‘netaṃ mama, nesohamasmi, na meso attā’ti evametaṃ yathābhūtaṃ sammappaññāya daṭṭhabbaṃ. Evaṃ passaṃ…pe…  nāparaṃ itthattāyāti pajānātī”ti.

===

8. Tatiyaṃ.

# 1.1.1.2 Ajjhattadukkhasutta

* Saḷāyatanasaṃyutta

* Paṭhamapaṇṇāsaka

* Aniccavagga

* Ajjhattadukkhasutta

(2.)

5\. “Cakkhuṃ, bhikkhave, dukkhaṃ. Yaṃ dukkhaṃ tadanattā; yadanattā taṃ ‘netaṃ mama, nesohamasmi, na meso attā’ti evametaṃ yathābhūtaṃ sammappaññāya daṭṭhabbaṃ. Sotaṃ dukkhaṃ…pe…  ghānaṃ dukkhaṃ…  jivhā dukkhā…  kāyo dukkho…  mano dukkho. Yaṃ dukkhaṃ tadanattā; yadanattā taṃ ‘netaṃ mama, nesohamasmi, na meso attā’ti evametaṃ yathābhūtaṃ sammappaññāya daṭṭhabbaṃ. Evaṃ passaṃ…pe…  nāparaṃ itthattāyāti pajānātī”ti.

---

6\. Dutiyaṃ.

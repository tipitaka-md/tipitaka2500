# 2.1.2 Sukhasutta

### Vedanāsaṃyutta

### Sagāthāvagga

### Sukhasutta

(250.)

1098\. “Tisso imā, bhikkhave, vedanā. Katamā tisso? Sukhā vedanā, dukkhā vedanā, adukkhamasukhā vedanā—  imā kho, bhikkhave, tisso vedanāti.

1099\. _Sukhaṃ vā yadi vā dukkhaṃ,_  
_adukkhamasukhaṃ saha;_  
_Ajjhattañca bahiddhā ca,_  
_yaṃ kiñci atthi veditaṃ._  


1100\. _Etaṃ dukkhanti ñatvāna,_  
_mosadhammaṃ palokinaṃ;_  
_Phussa phussa vayaṃ passaṃ,_  
_evaṃ tattha virajjatī”ti._

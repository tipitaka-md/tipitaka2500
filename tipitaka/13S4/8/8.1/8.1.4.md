### Gāmaṇisaṃyutta

### Gāmaṇivagga

### Hatthārohasutta

(356.)

1556. Atha kho hatthāroho gāmaṇi yena bhagavā tenupasaṅkami; upasaṅkamitvā…pe…  ajjatagge pāṇupetaṃ saraṇaṃ gatanti.

===

1557. Catutthaṃ.

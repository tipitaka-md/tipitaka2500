# 3.2.3 Anissukīsutta

### Mātugāmasaṃyutta

### Dutiyapeyyālavagga

### Anissukīsutta

(296.)

1278. “Pañcahi, anuruddha, dhammehi samannāgato mātugāmo kāyassa bhedā paraṃ maraṇā sugatiṃ saggaṃ lokaṃ upapajjati. Katamehi pañcahi? Saddho ca hoti, hirimā ca hoti, ottappī ca hoti, anissukī ca hoti, paññavā ca hoti…pe… .

===

1279. Tatiyaṃ.

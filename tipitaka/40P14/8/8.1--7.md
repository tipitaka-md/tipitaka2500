# 8.1--7 Paṭiccādivāra

### Sanidassanaduka

### Paṭiccādivāra

(11.)

35. Anidassanaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca naanidassano dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Anidassanaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nasanidassano dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Anidassanaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nasanidassano ca naanidassano ca dhammā uppajjanti hetupaccayā. (3) (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

36. Hetuyā tīṇi, ārammaṇe ekaṃ…pe…  avigate tīṇi. (Sabbattha vitthāro.)

# 25 Kilesagocchaka

### Kilesagocchaka

(34.)

111. Kilesaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nokileso dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

112. Hetuyā nava…pe…  vipāke ekaṃ…pe…  avigate nava.

# 21.1--7 Paṭiccādivāra

### Sārammaṇaduka

### Paṭiccādivāra

(24.)

81. Sārammaṇaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nasārammaṇo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā…  tīṇi.

82. Anārammaṇaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca naanārammaṇo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā…  tīṇi.

83. Sārammaṇañca anārammaṇañca dhammaṃ paṭicca nasārammaṇo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā…  tīṇi. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

84. Hetuyā nava, ārammaṇe tīṇi, adhipatiyā pañca…pe…  aññamaññe cha…pe…  purejāte āsevane ekaṃ…pe…  avigate nava. (Sabbattha vitthāro.)

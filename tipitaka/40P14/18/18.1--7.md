# 18.1--7 Paṭiccādivāra

### Āsavavippayuttasāsavaduka

### Paṭiccādivāra

(21.)

72. Āsavavippayuttaṃ sāsavaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca āsavavippayutto naanāsavo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (1)

73. Āsavavippayuttaṃ anāsavaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca āsavavippayutto naanāsavo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā…  tīṇi.

74. Āsavavippayuttaṃ sāsavañca āsavavippayuttaṃ anāsavañca dhammaṃ paṭicca āsavavippayutto nanoanāsavo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (1)

75. Hetuyā pañca, ārammaṇe dve…pe…  avigate pañca. (Sabbattha vitthāro.)

===

76. Āsavagocchakaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.

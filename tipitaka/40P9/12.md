# 12 Mahantaraduka, Kusalattika

### Mahantaraduka, Kusalattika

(56.)

146. Nasārammaṇaṃ nakusalaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca…pe…  naanārammaṇaṃ nakusalaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca… . (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

147. Hetuyā nava, ārammaṇe tīṇi, adhipatiyā pañca…pe…  aññamaññe cha…pe…  purejāte āsevane ekaṃ…pe…  avigate nava.

(57.)

148. Nacittaṃ nakusalaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca…pe…  nanocittaṃ nakusalaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca… . (Saṃkhittaṃ. Sabbattha pañca.)

(58.)

149. Nacetasikaṃ nakusalaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca… . (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

150. Hetuyā nava…pe…  purejāte āsevane pañca…pe…  avigate nava.

(59.)

151. Nacittasampayuttaṃ nakusalaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca… . (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

152. Hetuyā nava, ārammaṇe tīṇi, adhipatiyā pañca…pe…  aññamaññe cha…pe…  purejāte āsevane ekaṃ…pe…  avigate nava.

(60.)

153. Nacittasaṃsaṭṭhaṃ nakusalaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca… . (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

154. Hetuyā nava, ārammaṇe tīṇi, adhipatiyā pañca…pe…  aññamaññe cha…pe…  purejāte āsevane ekaṃ…pe…  avigate nava.

(61.)

155. Nocittasamuṭṭhānaṃ nakusalaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca… . (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

156. Hetuyā nava, ārammaṇe nava, adhipatiyā pañca…pe…  purejāte āsevane pañca…pe…  avigate nava.

(62.)

157. Nocittasahabhuṃ nakusalaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca… . (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

158. Hetuyā nava…pe…  purejāte āsevane pañca…pe…  avigate nava.

(63.)

159. Nocittānuparivattiṃ nakusalaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca…pe… . Nocittasaṃsaṭṭhasamuṭṭhānaṃ nakusalaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca…pe…  nocittasaṃsaṭṭhasamuṭṭhānasahabhuṃ nakusalaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca…pe…  nocittasaṃsaṭṭhasamuṭṭhānānuparivattiṃ nakusalaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca… . (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

160. Hetuyā nava…pe…  purejāte āsevane pañca…pe…  avigate nava.

(64.)

161. Naajjhattikaṃ nakusalaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca…pe…  nabāhiraṃ nakusalaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca… . (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

162. Hetuyā nava, ārammaṇe pañca, adhipatiyā pañca…pe…  avigate nava.

(65.)

163. Naupādā nakusalaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca… . (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

164. Hetuyā pañca, ārammaṇe tīṇi, adhipatiyā tīṇi…pe…  purejāte āsevane ekaṃ…pe…  avigate pañca.

(66.)

165. Naupādinnaṃ nakusalaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca… . (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

166. Hetuyā pañca, ārammaṇe dve, adhipatiyā ekaṃ…pe…  āsevane ekaṃ…pe…  avigate pañca.

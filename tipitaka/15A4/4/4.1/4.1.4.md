# 4.1.4 Satibalasutta

### Catutthapaṇṇāsaka

### Indriyavagga

### Satibalasutta

(154.)

913. “Cattārimāni, bhikkhave, balāni. Katamāni cattāri? Satibalaṃ, samādhibalaṃ, anavajjabalaṃ, saṅgahabalaṃ—  imāni kho, bhikkhave, cattāri balānī”ti.

===

914. Catutthaṃ.

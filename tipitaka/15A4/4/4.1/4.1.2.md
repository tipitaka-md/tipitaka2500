# 4.1.2 Saddhābalasutta

### Catutthapaṇṇāsaka

### Indriyavagga

### Saddhābalasutta

(152.)

909. “Cattārimāni, bhikkhave, balāni. Katamāni cattāri? Saddhābalaṃ, vīriyabalaṃ, satibalaṃ, samādhibalaṃ—  imāni kho, bhikkhave, cattāri balānī”ti.

===

910. Dutiyaṃ.

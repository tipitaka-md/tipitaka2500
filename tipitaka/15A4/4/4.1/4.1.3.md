# 4.1.3 Paññābalasutta

### Catutthapaṇṇāsaka

### Indriyavagga

### Paññābalasutta

(153.)

911. “Cattārimāni, bhikkhave, balāni. Katamāni cattāri? Paññābalaṃ, vīriyabalaṃ, anavajjabalaṃ, saṅgahabalaṃ—  imāni kho, bhikkhave, cattāri balānī”ti.

---

912. Tatiyaṃ.

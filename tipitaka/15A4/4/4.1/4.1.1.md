# 4.1.1 Indriyasutta

### Catutthapaṇṇāsaka

### Indriyavagga

### Indriyasutta

(151.)

907. “Cattārimāni, bhikkhave, indriyāni. Katamāni cattāri? Saddhindriyaṃ, vīriyindriyaṃ, satindriyaṃ, samādhindriyaṃ—  imāni kho, bhikkhave, cattāri indriyānī”ti.

---

908. Paṭhamaṃ.

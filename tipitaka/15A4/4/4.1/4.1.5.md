# 4.1.5 Paṭisaṅkhānabalasutta

### Catutthapaṇṇāsaka

### Indriyavagga

### Paṭisaṅkhānabalasutta

(155.)

915. “Cattārimāni, bhikkhave, balāni. Katamāni cattāri? Paṭisaṅkhānabalaṃ, bhāvanābalaṃ, anavajjabalaṃ, saṅgahabalaṃ—  imāni kho, bhikkhave, cattāri balānī”ti.

===

916. Pañcamaṃ.

# 3.2.10 Dutiyabhayasutta

* Tatiyapaṇṇāsaka

* Kesivagga

* Dutiyabhayasutta

(120.)

778\. “Cattārimāni, bhikkhave, bhayāni. Katamāni cattāri? Aggibhayaṃ, udakabhayaṃ, rājabhayaṃ, corabhayaṃ—  imāni kho, bhikkhave, cattāri bhayānī”ti.

---

779\. Dasamaṃ.



---

780\. Kesivaggo dutiyo.



---

781\. Tassuddānaṃ



782\. _Kesi javo patodo ca,_  
_Nāgo ṭhānena pañcamaṃ;_  
_Appamādo ca ārakkho,_  
_Saṃvejanīyañca dve bhayāti._

# 3.5.4 Obhāsasutta

### Tatiyapaṇṇāsaka

### Ābhāvagga

### Obhāsasutta

(144.)

888. “Cattārome, bhikkhave, obhāsā. Katame cattāro? Candobhāso, sūriyobhāso, aggobhāso, paññobhāso—  ime kho, bhikkhave, cattāro obhāsā. Etadaggaṃ, bhikkhave, imesaṃ catunnaṃ obhāsānaṃ yadidaṃ paññobhāso”ti.

===

889. Catutthaṃ.

# 2.5.8 Attahitasutta

### Dutiyapaṇṇāsaka

### Asuravagga

### Attahitasutta

(98.)

655. “Cattārome, bhikkhave, puggalā santo saṃvijjamānā lokasmiṃ. Katame cattāro? Attahitāya paṭipanno no parahitāya, parahitāya paṭipanno no attahitāya, nevattahitāya paṭipanno no parahitāya, attahitāya ceva paṭipanno parahitāya ca. Ime kho, bhikkhave, cattāro puggalā santo saṃvijjamānā lokasmin”ti.

---

656. Aṭṭhamaṃ.

### Dutiyapaṇṇāsaka

### Apaṇṇakavagga

### Paṭhamaaggasutta

(74.)

537. “Cattārimāni, bhikkhave, aggāni. Katamāni cattāri? Sīlaggaṃ, samādhiggaṃ, paññāggaṃ, vimuttaggaṃ—  imāni kho, bhikkhave, cattāri aggānī”ti.

# 2.3.5 Dutiyaaggasutta

### Dutiyapaṇṇāsaka

### Apaṇṇakavagga

### Dutiyaaggasutta

(75.)

539. “Cattārimāni, bhikkhave, aggāni. Katamāni cattāri? Rūpaggaṃ, vedanāggaṃ, saññāggaṃ, bhavaggaṃ—  imāni kho, bhikkhave, cattāri aggānī”ti.

---

540. Pañcamaṃ.

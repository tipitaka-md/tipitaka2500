# 5.3.6 Dutiyamaggasutta

### Pañcamapaṇṇāsaka

### Duccaritavagga

### Dutiyamaggasutta

(226.)

1411\. …pe…  Micchāājīvo hoti, micchāvāyāmo hoti, micchāsati hoti, micchāsamādhi hoti…pe…  sammāājīvo hoti, sammāvāyāmo hoti, sammāsati hoti, sammāsamādhi hoti…pe… .

---

1412\. Chaṭṭhaṃ.

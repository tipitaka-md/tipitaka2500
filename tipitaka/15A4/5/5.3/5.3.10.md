# 5.3.10 Duppaññasutta

### Pañcamapaṇṇāsaka

### Duccaritavagga

### Duppaññasutta

(230.)

1418. …pe…  Assaddho hoti, dussīlo hoti, kusīto hoti, duppañño hoti…pe…  saddho hoti, sīlavā hoti, āraddhavīriyo hoti, paññavā hoti—  imehi kho, bhikkhave, catūhi dhammehi samannāgato paṇḍito viyatto sappuriso akkhataṃ anupahataṃ attānaṃ pariharati, anavajjo ca hoti ananuvajjo viññūnaṃ, bahuñca puññaṃ pasavatī”ti.

===

1419. Dasamaṃ.

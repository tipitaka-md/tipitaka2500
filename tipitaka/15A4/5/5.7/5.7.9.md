# 5.7.9 Byāpannacittasutta

### Pañcamapaṇṇāsaka

### Kammapathavagga

### Byāpannacittasutta

(272.)

1592. …pe…  Attanā ca byāpannacitto hoti, parañca byāpāde samādapeti, byāpāde ca samanuñño hoti, byāpādassa ca vaṇṇaṃ bhāsati—  imehi…pe… .

1593. Attanā ca abyāpannacitto hoti, parañca abyāpāde samādapeti, abyāpāde ca samanuñño hoti, abyāpādassa ca vaṇṇaṃ bhāsati—  imehi…pe… .

===

1594. Navamaṃ.

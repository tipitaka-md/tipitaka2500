# 5.7.2 Adinnādāyīsutta

### Pañcamapaṇṇāsaka

### Kammapathavagga

### Adinnādāyīsutta

(265.)

1571. “Catūhi, bhikkhave, dhammehi samannāgato yathābhataṃ nikkhitto evaṃ niraye. Katamehi catūhi? Attanā ca adinnādāyī hoti, parañca adinnādāne samādapeti, adinnādāne ca samanuñño hoti, adinnādānassa ca vaṇṇaṃ bhāsati—  imehi kho…pe… .

1572. Attanā ca adinnādānā paṭivirato hoti, parañca adinnādānā veramaṇiyā samādapeti, adinnādānā veramaṇiyā ca samanuñño hoti, adinnādānā veramaṇiyā ca vaṇṇaṃ bhāsati—  imehi kho, bhikkhave…pe… .

===

1573. Dutiyaṃ.

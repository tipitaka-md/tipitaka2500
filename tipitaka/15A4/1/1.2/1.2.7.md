# 1.2.7 Paṭhamaagatisutta

### Paṭhamapaṇṇāsaka

### Caravagga

### Paṭhamaagatisutta

(17.)

112. “Cattārimāni, bhikkhave, agatigamanāni. Katamāni cattāri? Chandāgatiṃ gacchati, dosāgatiṃ gacchati, mohāgatiṃ gacchati, bhayāgatiṃ gacchati—  imāni kho, bhikkhave, cattāri agatigamanānīti.

113. _Chandā dosā bhayā mohā,_  
_yo dhammaṃ ativattati;_  
_Nihīyati tassa yaso,_  
_kāḷapakkheva candimā”ti._  


===

114. Sattamaṃ.

# 1.3.5 Brahmacariyasutta

### Paṭhamapaṇṇāsaka

### Uruvelavagga

### Brahmacariyasutta

(25.)

176. “Nayidaṃ, bhikkhave, brahmacariyaṃ vussati janakuhanatthaṃ, na janalapanatthaṃ, na lābhasakkārasilokānisaṃsatthaṃ, na itivādappamokkhānisaṃsatthaṃ, na ‘iti maṃ jano jānātū’ti. Atha kho idaṃ, bhikkhave, brahmacariyaṃ vussati saṃvaratthaṃ pahānatthaṃ virāgatthaṃ nirodhatthanti.

177. _Saṃvaratthaṃ pahānatthaṃ,_  
_brahmacariyaṃ anītihaṃ;_  
_Adesayi so bhagavā,_  
_nibbānogadhagāminaṃ;_  
_Esa maggo mahantehi,_  
_anuyāto mahesibhi._  


178. _Ye ca taṃ paṭipajjanti,_  
_yathā buddhena desitaṃ;_  
_Dukkhassantaṃ karissanti,_  
_satthusāsanakārino”ti._  


===

179. Pañcamaṃ.

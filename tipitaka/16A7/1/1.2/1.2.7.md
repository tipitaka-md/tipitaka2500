# 1.2.7 Dukkhānupassīsutta

### Paṭhamapaṇṇāsaka

### Anusayavagga

### Dukkhānupassīsutta

(17.)

82\. Sattime, bhikkhave, puggalā āhuneyyā…pe…  anuttaraṃ puññakkhettaṃ lokassa. Katame satta? Idha, bhikkhave, ekacco puggalo sabbasaṅkhāresu dukkhānupassī viharati…pe… .

---

83\. Sattamaṃ.

# 2.6.3

### Dutiyapaṇṇāsaka

### Rāgapeyyāla

(625.)

677. “Rāgassa, bhikkhave, abhiññāya satta dhammā bhāvetabbā. Katame satta? Asubhasaññā, maraṇasaññā, āhāre paṭikūlasaññā, sabbaloke anabhiratasaññā, aniccasaññā, anicce dukkhasaññā, dukkhe anattasaññā—  rāgassa, bhikkhave, abhiññāya ime satta dhammā bhāvetabbā”ti.

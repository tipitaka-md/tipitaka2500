# 2.6.1

### Dutiyapaṇṇāsaka

### Rāgapeyyāla

(623.)

675. “Rāgassa, bhikkhave, abhiññāya satta dhammā bhāvetabbā. Katame satta? Satisambojjhaṅgo…pe…  upekkhāsambojjhaṅgo—  rāgassa, bhikkhave, abhiññāya ime satta dhammā bhāvetabbā”ti.

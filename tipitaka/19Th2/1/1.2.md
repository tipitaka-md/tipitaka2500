# 1.2 Muttātherīgāthā

### Ekakanipāta

### Muttātherīgāthā

(2.)

4. _“Mutte muccassu yogehi,_  
_cando rāhuggahā iva;_  
_Vippamuttena cittena,_  
_anaṇā bhuñja piṇḍakan”ti._  


5. Itthaṃ sudaṃ bhagavā muttaṃ sikkhamānaṃ imāya gāthāya abhiṇhaṃ ovadatīti.

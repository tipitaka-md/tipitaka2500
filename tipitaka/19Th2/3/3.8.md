# 3.8 Somātherīgāthā

* Tikanipāta

* Somātherīgāthā

(60.)

98\. _“Yaṃ taṃ isīhi pattabbaṃ,_  
_ṭhānaṃ durabhisambhavaṃ;_  
_Na taṃ dvaṅgulapaññāya,_  
_sakkā pappotumitthiyā”._  


(61.)

99\. _“Itthibhāvo no kiṃ kayirā,_  
_cittamhi susamāhite;_  
_Ñāṇamhi vattamānamhi,_  
_sammā dhammaṃ vipassato._  


(62.)

100\. _Sabbattha vihatā nandī,_  
_Tamokhandho padālito;_  
_Evaṃ jānāhi pāpima,_  
_Nihato tvamasi antakā”ti._

# 2.1.9 Dutiyasūcivimānavatthu

* Purisavimāna

* Mahārathavagga

* Dutiyasūcivimānavatthu

(952.)

796\. _“Uccamidaṃ maṇithūṇaṃ vimānaṃ,_  
_Samantato dvādasa yojanāni;_  
_Kūṭāgārā sattasatā uḷārā,_  
_Veḷuriyathambhā rucakatthatā subhā._  


(953.)

797\. _Tatthacchasi pivasi khādasi ca,_  
_Dibbā ca vīṇā pavadanti vagguṃ;_  
_Dibbā rasā kāmaguṇettha pañca,_  
_Nāriyo ca naccanti suvaṇṇachannā._  


(954--955.)

798\. _Kena tetādiso vaṇṇo,_  
_…pe…_  
_Vaṇṇo ca te sabbadisā pabhāsatī”ti._  


(956.)

799\. _So devaputto attamano,_  
_…pe…_  
_yassa kammassidaṃ phalaṃ._  


(957.)

800\. _“Ahaṃ manussesu manussabhūto,_  
_Purimajātiyā manussaloke._  


(958.)

801\. _Addasaṃ virajaṃ bhikkhuṃ,_  
_vippasannamanāvilaṃ;_  
_Tassa adāsahaṃ sūciṃ,_  
_pasanno sehi pāṇibhi._  


(959--960.)

802\. _Tena metādiso vaṇṇo,_  
_…pe…_  
_Vaṇṇo ca me sabbadisā pabhāsatī”ti._  


---

803\. Dutiyasūcivimānaṃ navamaṃ.

# 2.2.1 Paṭhamaagāriyavimānavatthu

* Purisavimāna

* Pāyāsivagga

* Paṭhamaagāriyavimānavatthu

(1048.)

896\. _“Yathā vanaṃ cittalataṃ pabhāsati,_  
_Uyyānaseṭṭhaṃ tidasānamuttamaṃ;_  
_Tathūpamaṃ tuyhamidaṃ vimānaṃ,_  
_Obhāsayaṃ tiṭṭhati antalikkhe._  


(1049.)

897\. _Deviddhipattosi mahānubhāvo,_  
_Manussabhūto kimakāsi puññaṃ;_  
_Kenāsi evaṃ jalitānubhāvo,_  
_Vaṇṇo ca te sabbadisā pabhāsatī”ti._  


(1050.)

898\. _So devaputto attamano,_  
_moggallānena pucchito;_  
_Pañhaṃ puṭṭho viyākāsi,_  
_yassa kammassidaṃ phalaṃ._  


(1051.)

899\. _“Ahañca bhariyā ca manussaloke,_  
_Opānabhūtā gharamāvasimha;_  
_Annañca pānañca pasannacittā,_  
_Sakkacca dānaṃ vipulaṃ adamha._  


(1052--1053.)

900\. _Tena metādiso vaṇṇo,_  
_…pe…_  
_Vaṇṇo ca me sabbadisā pabhāsatī”ti._  


---

901\. Paṭhamaagāriyavimānaṃ paṭhamaṃ.

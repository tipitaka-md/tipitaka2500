# 15 Saraṇaduka, Cūḷantaraduka

* Saraṇaduka, Cūḷantaraduka

(46.)

149\. Nasaraṇaṃ naappaccayaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca…pe…  nasaraṇaṃ naasaṅkhataṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca… . (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā pañca, ārammaṇe dve…pe…  vipāke ekaṃ…pe…  avigate pañca.

150\. Nasaraṇaṃ nasanidassanaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca… . (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā pañca, ārammaṇe dve…pe…  vipāke ekaṃ…pe…  avigate pañca.

151\. Nasaraṇaṃ nasappaṭighaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca… . (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā pañca, ārammaṇe dve…pe…  vipāke ekaṃ…pe…  avigate pañca.

152\. Nasaraṇaṃ naappaṭighaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca… . (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā ekaṃ…pe…  avigate ekaṃ.

153\. Nasaraṇaṃ narūpiṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca… . (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā dve…pe…  vipāke ekaṃ…pe…  avigate dve.

154\. Nasaraṇaṃ naarūpiṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca… . (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā ekaṃ…pe…  avigate ekaṃ.

155\. Nasaraṇaṃ nalokiyaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca… . (Saṃkhittaṃ. Sabbattha ekaṃ.)

156\. Nasaraṇaṃ nalokuttaraṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca… . (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā pañca, ārammaṇe dve…pe…  vipāke ekaṃ…pe…  avigate pañca.

157\. Nasaraṇaṃ nakenaci viññeyyaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca…pe…  nasaraṇaṃ nanakenaci viññeyyaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca… . (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā pañca, ārammaṇe dve…pe…  vipāke ekaṃ…pe…  avigate pañca.

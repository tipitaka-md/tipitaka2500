# 22.1.1--6 Paṭiccādivāra

* Saraṇaduka, Piṭṭhiduka

* Sauttarapada

* Paṭiccādivāra

(86.)

181\. Nasaraṇaṃ nadassanena pahātabbaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca saraṇo dassanena pahātabbo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā ekaṃ…pe…  avigate ekaṃ.

182\. Naaraṇaṃ nanadassanena pahātabbaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca araṇo nadassanena pahātabbo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā ekaṃ, adhipatiyā ekaṃ…pe…  avigate ekaṃ.

(87.)

183\. Nasaraṇaṃ nasauttaraṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca araṇo sauttaro dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā ekaṃ, adhipatiyā ekaṃ…pe…  avigate ekaṃ.

184\. (Sahajātavārampi paccayavārampi nissayavārampi saṃsaṭṭhavārampi sampayuttavārampi paṭiccavārasadisaṃ.)

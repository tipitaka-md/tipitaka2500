# 2.6.3 Dhātu

### Niddesa

### Chaṭṭhanaya Sampayogavippayogapadaniddesa

### Dhātu

(233.)

310. Cakkhudhātu…pe…  phoṭṭhabbadhātu…pe…  sampayuttāti? Natthi. Katihi vippayuttā? Catūhi khandhehi ekenāyatanena sattahi dhātūhi vippayuttā; ekenāyatanena ekāya dhātuyā kehici vippayuttā.

(234.)

311. Cakkhuviññāṇadhātu…pe…  manodhātu…  manoviññāṇadhātu tīhi khandhehi sampayuttā; ekenāyatanena ekāya dhātuyā kehici sampayuttā. Katihi vippayuttā? Ekena khandhena dasahāyatanehi soḷasahi dhātūhi vippayuttā; ekenāyatanena ekāya dhātuyā kehici vippayuttā.

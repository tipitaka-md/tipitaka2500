# 2.9.2.3 Āsavagocchaka

### Niddesa

### Navamanaya Sampayuttenasampayuttapadaniddesa

### Duka

### Āsavagocchaka

(344.)

430\. Āsavehi dhammehi ye dhammā…  āsavehi ceva sāsavehi ca dhammehi ye dhammā…  āsavehi ceva āsavasampayuttehi ca dhammehi ye dhammā sampayuttā, tehi dhammehi ye dhammā sampayuttā…  te dhammā tīhi khandhehi ekenāyatanena ekāya dhātuyā sampayuttā; ekena khandhena ekenāyatanena ekāya dhātuyā kehici sampayuttā.

(345.)

431\. Āsavasampayuttehi ceva no ca āsavehi dhammehi ye dhammā sampayuttā, tehi dhammehi ye dhammā sampayuttā…  te dhammā ekena khandhena ekenāyatanena ekāya dhātuyā kehici sampayuttā.

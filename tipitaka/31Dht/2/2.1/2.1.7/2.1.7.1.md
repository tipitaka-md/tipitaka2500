# 2.1.7.1 Kusalattika

* Niddesa

* Paṭhamanaya saṅgahāsaṅgahapadaniddesa

* Tika

* Kusalattika

(77.)

123\. Kusalā dhammā…  akusalā dhammā katihi khandhehi katihāyatanehi katihi dhātūhi saṅgahitā? Kusalā dhammā…  akusalā dhammā catūhi khandhehi dvīhāyatanehi dvīhi dhātūhi saṅgahitā. Katihi asaṅgahitā? Ekena khandhena dasahāyatanehi soḷasahi dhātūhi asaṅgahitā.

(78.)

124\. Abyākatā dhammā asaṅkhataṃ khandhato ṭhapetvā pañcahi khandhehi dvādasahāyatanehi aṭṭhārasahi dhātūhi saṅgahitā. Katihi asaṅgahitā? Na kehici khandhehi na kehici āyatanehi na kāhici dhātūhi asaṅgahitā.

# 2.1.7.5 Saṃkiliṭṭhattika

### Niddesa

### Paṭhamanaya saṅgahāsaṅgahapadaniddesa

### Tika

### Saṃkiliṭṭhattika

(87.)

133. Asaṃkiliṭṭhasaṃkilesikā dhammā pañcahi khandhehi dvādasahāyatanehi aṭṭhārasahi dhātūhi saṅgahitā. Katihi asaṅgahitā? Na kehici khandhehi na kehici āyatanehi na kāhici dhātūhi asaṅgahitā.

# 2.12 Dvādasamanaya Sampayuttenasaṅgahitāsaṅgahitapadaniddesa

* Niddesa

* Dvādasamanaya Sampayuttenasaṅgahitāsaṅgahitapadaniddesa

(417.)

513\. Vedanākkhandhena ye dhammā…  saññākkhandhena ye dhammā…  saṅkhārakkhandhena ye dhammā sampayuttā, te dhammā katihi khandhehi katihāyatanehi katihi dhātūhi saṅgahitā? Te dhammā tīhi khandhehi dvīhāyatanehi aṭṭhahi dhātūhi saṅgahitā. Katihi asaṅgahitā? Dvīhi khandhehi dasahāyatanehi dasahi dhātūhi asaṅgahitā.

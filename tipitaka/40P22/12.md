# 12 Kusalattika, Pariyāpannaduka

### Kusalattika, Pariyāpannaduka

(32.)

73. Nakusalaṃ napariyāpannaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca abyākato pariyāpanno dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Naakusalaṃ napariyāpannaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca abyākato pariyāpanno dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Naabyākataṃ napariyāpannaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca abyākato pariyāpanno dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā pañca, adhipatiyā pañca…pe…  vipāke tīṇi…pe…  avigate pañca.

74. Nakusalaṃ naapariyāpannaṃ dhammaṃ paccayā kusalo apariyāpanno dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Nakusalaṃ naapariyāpannaṃ dhammaṃ paccayā abyākato apariyāpanno dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā…  dve. (Akusalamūle dve, dukamūle dve.) Hetuyā cha…  vipāke tīṇi…pe…  avigate cha.

# 20 Saṃkiliṭṭhattika, Hetuduka

### Saṃkiliṭṭhattika, Hetuduka

(40.)

99. Nasaṃkiliṭṭhasaṃkilesikaṃ nahetuṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca asaṃkiliṭṭhasaṃkilesiko hetu dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

100. Hetuyā nava…pe…  vipāke cha…pe…  avigate nava.

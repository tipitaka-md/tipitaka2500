# 2.4.2.12.6 Kilesasaṃkiliṭṭhaduka

### Niddesa

### Aṭṭhakathākaṇḍa

### Dukaatthuddhāra

### Kilesagocchaka

### Kilesasaṃkiliṭṭhaduka

(1575.)

2286. Katame dhammā kilesā ceva saṃkiliṭṭhā ca? Teva kilesā kilesā ceva saṃkiliṭṭhā ca.

(1576.)

2287. Katame dhammā saṃkiliṭṭhā ceva no ca kilesā? Ṭhapetvā kilese avasesaṃ akusalaṃ—  ime dhammā saṃkiliṭṭhā ceva no ca kilesā. Asaṃkiliṭṭhā dhammā na vattabbā—  kilesā ceva saṃkiliṭṭhā cātipi, saṃkiliṭṭhā ceva no ca kilesātipi.

# 2.4.2.10.7 Cittasahabhūduka

* Niddesa

* Aṭṭhakathākaṇḍa

* Dukaatthuddhāra

* Mahantaraduka

* Cittasahabhūduka

(1537.)

2248\. Katame dhammā cittasahabhuno? Vedanākkhandho, saññākkhandho, saṅkhārakkhandho, kāyaviññatti, vacīviññatti—  ime dhammā cittasahabhuno.

(1538.)

2249\. Katame dhammā no cittasahabhuno? Cittañca, avasesañca rūpaṃ, nibbānañca—  ime dhammā no cittasahabhuno.

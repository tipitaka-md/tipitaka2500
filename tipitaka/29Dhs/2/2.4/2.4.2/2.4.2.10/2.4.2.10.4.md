# 2.4.2.10.4 Cittasampayuttaduka

### Niddesa

### Aṭṭhakathākaṇḍa

### Dukaatthuddhāra

### Mahantaraduka

### Cittasampayuttaduka

(1531.)

2242. Katame dhammā cittasampayuttā? Vedanākkhandho, saññākkhandho, saṅkhārakkhandho—  ime dhammā cittasampayuttā.

(1532.)

2243. Katame dhammā cittavippayuttā? Rūpañca, nibbānañca—  ime dhammā cittavippayuttā. Cittaṃ na vattabbaṃ—  cittena sampayuttantipi, cittena vippayuttantipi.

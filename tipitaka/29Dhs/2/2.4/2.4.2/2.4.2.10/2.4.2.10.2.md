# 2.4.2.10.2 Cittaduka

### Niddesa

### Aṭṭhakathākaṇḍa

### Dukaatthuddhāra

### Mahantaraduka

### Cittaduka

(1527.)

2238. Katame dhammā cittā? Cakkhuviññāṇaṃ, sotaviññāṇaṃ, ghānaviññāṇaṃ, jivhāviññāṇaṃ, kāyaviññāṇaṃ, manodhātu, manoviññāṇadhātu—  ime dhammā cittā.

(1528.)

2239. Katame dhammā no cittā? Vedanākkhandho, saññākkhandho, saṅkhārakkhandho, rūpañca, nibbānañca—  ime dhammā no cittā.

# 2.4.2.10.11 Cittasaṃsaṭṭhasamuṭṭhānānuparivattiduka

* Niddesa

* Aṭṭhakathākaṇḍa

* Dukaatthuddhāra

* Mahantaraduka

* Cittasaṃsaṭṭhasamuṭṭhānānuparivattiduka

(1545.)

2256\. Katame dhammā cittasaṃsaṭṭhasamuṭṭhānānuparivattino? Vedanākkhandho, saññākkhandho, saṅkhārakkhandho—  ime dhammā cittasaṃsaṭṭhasamuṭṭhānānuparivattino.

(1546.)

2257\. Katame dhammā no cittasaṃsaṭṭhasamuṭṭhānānuparivattino? Cittañca, rūpañca, nibbānañca—  ime dhammā no cittasaṃsaṭṭhasamuṭṭhānānuparivattino.

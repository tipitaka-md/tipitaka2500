### Ajjhattikaduka

(1547.)

2258. Katame dhammā ajjhattikā? Cakkhāyatanaṃ…pe…  manāyatanaṃ—  ime dhammā ajjhattikā.

(1548.)

2259. Katame dhammā bāhirā? Rūpāyatanaṃ…pe…  dhammāyatanaṃ—  ime dhammā bāhirā.

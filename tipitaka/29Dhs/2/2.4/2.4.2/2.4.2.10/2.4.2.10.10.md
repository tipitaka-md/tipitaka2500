### Mahantaraduka

### Cittasaṃsaṭṭhasamuṭṭhānasahabhūduka

(1543.)

2254. Katame dhammā cittasaṃsaṭṭhasamuṭṭhānasahabhuno? Vedanākkhandho, saññākkhandho, saṅkhārakkhandho—  ime dhammā cittasaṃsaṭṭhasamuṭṭhānasahabhuno.

(1544.)

2255. Katame dhammā no cittasaṃsaṭṭhasamuṭṭhānasahabhuno? Cittañca, rūpañca, nibbānañca—  ime dhammā no cittasaṃsaṭṭhasamuṭṭhānasahabhuno.

# 2.4.1.20 Ajjhattattika

* Niddesa

* Aṭṭhakathākaṇḍa

* Tikaatthuddhāra

* Ajjhattattika

(1435.)

2141\. Anindriyabaddharūpañca nibbānañca ṭhapetvā, sabbe dhammā siyā ajjhattā, siyā bahiddhā, siyā ajjhattabahiddhā. Anindriyabaddharūpañca nibbānañca bahiddhā.

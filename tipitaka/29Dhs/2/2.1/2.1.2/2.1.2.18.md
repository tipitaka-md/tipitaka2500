# 2.1.2.18 Tīṇi vimokkhāni soḷasakkhattukāni

### Niddesa

### Cittuppādakaṇḍa

### Rūpāvacarakusala

## 691. Tīṇi vimokkhāni soḷasakkhattukāni

(248.)

692. Katame dhammā kusalā? Yasmiṃ samaye rūpūpapattiyā maggaṃ bhāveti rūpī rūpāni passati vivicceva kāmehi…pe…  paṭhamaṃ jhānaṃ upasampajja viharati, tasmiṃ samaye phasso hoti…pe…  avikkhepo hoti…pe…  ime dhammā kusalā.

(249.)

693. Katame dhammā kusalā? Yasmiṃ samaye rūpūpapattiyā maggaṃ bhāveti ajjhattaṃ arūpasaññī bahiddhā rūpāni passati vivicceva kāmehi…pe…  paṭhamaṃ jhānaṃ upasampajja viharati, tasmiṃ samaye phasso hoti…pe…  avikkhepo hoti…pe…  ime dhammā kusalā.

(250.)

694. Katame dhammā kusalā? Yasmiṃ samaye rūpūpapattiyā maggaṃ bhāveti subhanti vivicceva kāmehi…pe…  paṭhamaṃ jhānaṃ upasampajja viharati, tasmiṃ samaye phasso hoti…pe…  avikkhepo hoti…pe…  ime dhammā kusalā.

===

695. Imānipi tīṇi vimokkhāni soḷasakkhattukāni.

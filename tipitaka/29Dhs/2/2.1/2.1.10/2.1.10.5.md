# 2.1.10.5 Rūpāvacarakiriyā

### Niddesa

### Cittuppādakaṇḍa

### Ahetukakiriyāabyākata

### Rūpāvacarakiriyā

(577.)

1086. Katame dhammā abyākatā? Yasmiṃ samaye rūpāvacaraṃ jhānaṃ bhāveti kiriyaṃ neva kusalaṃ nākusalaṃ na ca kammavipākaṃ diṭṭhadhammasukhavihāraṃ vivicceva kāmehi…pe…  paṭhamaṃ jhānaṃ upasampajja viharati pathavīkasiṇaṃ, tasmiṃ samaye phasso hoti…pe…  avikkhepo hoti…pe…  ime dhammā abyākatā.

(578.)

1087. Katame dhammā abyākatā? Yasmiṃ samaye rūpāvacaraṃ jhānaṃ bhāveti kiriyaṃ neva kusalaṃ nākusalaṃ na ca kammavipākaṃ diṭṭhadhammasukhavihāraṃ vitakkavicārānaṃ vūpasamā…pe…  dutiyaṃ jhānaṃ…pe…  tatiyaṃ jhānaṃ…pe…  catutthaṃ jhānaṃ…pe…  paṭhamaṃ jhānaṃ…pe…  pañcamaṃ jhānaṃ upasampajja viharati pathavīkasiṇaṃ, tasmiṃ samaye phasso hoti…pe…  avikkhepo hoti…pe…  ime dhammā abyākatā.

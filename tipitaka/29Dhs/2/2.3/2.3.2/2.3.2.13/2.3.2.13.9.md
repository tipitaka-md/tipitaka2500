### Piṭṭhiduka

### Sukhasahagataduka

(1283.)

1984. Katame dhammā sukhasahagatā? Sukhabhūmiyaṃ kāmāvacare rūpāvacare apariyāpanne, sukhaṃ ṭhapetvā, taṃsampayutto saññākkhandho, saṅkhārakkhandho, viññāṇakkhandho—  ime dhammā sukhasahagatā.

(1284.)

1985. Katame dhammā na sukhasahagatā? Na sukhabhūmiyaṃ kāmāvacare rūpāvacare arūpāvacare apariyāpanne; vedanākkhandho…pe…  viññāṇakkhandho; sukhañca, sabbañca rūpaṃ, asaṅkhatā ca dhātu—  ime dhammā na sukhasahagatā.

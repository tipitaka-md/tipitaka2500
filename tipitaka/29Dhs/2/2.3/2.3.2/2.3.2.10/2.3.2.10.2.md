### Cittaduka

(1193.)

1889. Katame dhammā cittā? Cakkhuviññāṇaṃ, sotaviññāṇaṃ, ghānaviññāṇaṃ, jivhāviññāṇaṃ, kāyaviññāṇaṃ, manodhātu, manoviññāṇadhātu—  ime dhammā cittā.

(1194.)

1890. Katame dhammā no cittā? Vedanākkhandho, saññākkhandho, saṅkhārakkhandho, sabbañca rūpaṃ, asaṅkhatā ca dhātu—  ime dhammā no cittā.

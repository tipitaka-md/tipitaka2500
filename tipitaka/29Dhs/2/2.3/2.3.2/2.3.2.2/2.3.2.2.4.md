### Sappaṭighaduka

(1095.)

1764. Katame dhammā sappaṭighā? Cakkhāyatanaṃ…pe…  phoṭṭhabbāyatanaṃ—  ime dhammā sappaṭighā.

(1096.)

1765. Katame dhammā appaṭighā? Vedanākkhandho…pe…  viññāṇakkhandho, yañca rūpaṃ anidassanaṃ appaṭighaṃ dhammāyatanapariyāpannaṃ, asaṅkhatā ca dhātu—  ime dhammā appaṭighā.

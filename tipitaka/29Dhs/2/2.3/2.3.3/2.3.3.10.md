# 2.3.3.10 Avijjāduka

### Niddesa

### Nikkhepakaṇḍa

### Suttantikadukanikkhepa

### Avijjāduka

(1318.)

2023. Tattha katamā avijjā? Yaṃ aññāṇaṃ adassanaṃ…pe…  avijjālaṅgī moho akusalamūlaṃ—  ayaṃ vuccati avijjā.

(1319.)

2024. Tattha katamā bhavataṇhā? Yo bhavesu bhavachando…pe…  bhavajjhosānaṃ—  ayaṃ vuccati bhavataṇhā.

# 2.3.3.19 Āpattikusalatāduka

### Niddesa

### Nikkhepakaṇḍa

### Suttantikadukanikkhepa

### Āpattikusalatāduka

(1336.)

2041\. Tattha katamā āpattikusalatā? Pañcapi āpattikkhandhā āpattiyo, sattapi āpattikkhandhā āpattiyo. Yā tāsaṃ āpattīnaṃ āpattikusalatā paññā pajānanā…pe…  amoho dhammavicayo sammādiṭṭhi—  ayaṃ vuccati āpattikusalatā.

(1337.)

2042\. Tattha katamā āpattivuṭṭhānakusalatā? Yā tāhi āpattīhi vuṭṭhānakusalatā paññā pajānanā…pe…  amoho dhammavicayo sammādiṭṭhi—  ayaṃ vuccati āpattivuṭṭhānakusalatā.

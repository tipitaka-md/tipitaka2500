# 2.2.2.11 Ekādasaka

* Niddesa

* Rūpakaṇḍa

* Mātikā

* Ekādasaka

(593.)

1231\. Ekādasavidhena rūpasaṅgaho—

1232\. Cakkhāyatanaṃ, sotāyatanaṃ, ghānāyatanaṃ, jivhāyatanaṃ, kāyāyatanaṃ, rūpāyatanaṃ, saddāyatanaṃ, gandhāyatanaṃ, rasāyatanaṃ, phoṭṭhabbāyatanaṃ, yañca rūpaṃ anidassanaappaṭighaṃ dhammāyatanapariyāpannaṃ.

---

1233\. Evaṃ ekādasavidhena rūpasaṅgaho.

  
Ekādasakaṃ.

  
Mātikā.

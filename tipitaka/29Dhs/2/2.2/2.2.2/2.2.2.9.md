# 2.2.2.9 Navaka

* Niddesa

* Rūpakaṇḍa

* Mātikā

* Navaka

(591.)

1225\. Navavidhena rūpasaṅgaho—

1226\. Cakkhundriyaṃ, sotindriyaṃ, ghānindriyaṃ, jivhindriyaṃ, kāyindriyaṃ, itthindriyaṃ, purisindriyaṃ, jīvitindriyaṃ, yañca rūpaṃ na indriyaṃ.

---

1227\. Evaṃ navavidhena rūpasaṅgaho.

  
Navakaṃ.

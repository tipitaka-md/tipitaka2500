# 2.2.2.8 Aṭṭhaka

* Niddesa

* Rūpakaṇḍa

* Mātikā

* Aṭṭhaka

(590.)

1222\. Aṭṭhavidhena rūpasaṅgaho—

1223\. Cakkhuviññeyyaṃ rūpaṃ, sotaviññeyyaṃ rūpaṃ, ghānaviññeyyaṃ rūpaṃ, jivhāviññeyyaṃ rūpaṃ, kāyaviññeyyaṃ rūpaṃ, atthi sukhasamphassaṃ, atthi dukkhasamphassaṃ, manodhātuviññeyyaṃ rūpaṃ, manoviññāṇadhātuviññeyyaṃ rūpaṃ.

---

1224\. Evaṃ aṭṭhavidhena rūpasaṅgaho.

  
Aṭṭhakaṃ.

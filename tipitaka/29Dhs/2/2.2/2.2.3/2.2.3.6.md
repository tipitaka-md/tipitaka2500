# 2.2.3.6 Chakka

### Niddesa

### Rūpakaṇḍa

### Rūpavibhatti

### Chakka

(972.)

1630. Rūpāyatanaṃ cakkhuviññeyyaṃ rūpaṃ, saddāyatanaṃ sotaviññeyyaṃ rūpaṃ, gandhāyatanaṃ ghānaviññeyyaṃ rūpaṃ, rasāyatanaṃ jivhāviññeyyaṃ rūpaṃ, phoṭṭhabbāyatanaṃ kāyaviññeyyaṃ rūpaṃ, sabbaṃ rūpaṃ manoviññeyyaṃ rūpaṃ.

===

1631. Evaṃ chabbidhena rūpasaṅgaho.

  
Chakkaṃ.

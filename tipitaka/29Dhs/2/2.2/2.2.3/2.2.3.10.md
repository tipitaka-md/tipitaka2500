# 2.2.3.10 Dasaka

### Niddesa

### Rūpakaṇḍa

### Rūpavibhatti

### Dasaka

(978.)

1640. Katamaṃ taṃ rūpaṃ cakkhundriyaṃ? Yaṃ cakkhu catunnaṃ mahābhūtānaṃ upādāya pasādo…pe…  suñño gāmopeso—  idaṃ taṃ rūpaṃ cakkhundriyaṃ.

(979.)

1641. Katamaṃ taṃ rūpaṃ sotindriyaṃ…pe…  ghānindriyaṃ…pe…  jivhindriyaṃ…pe…  kāyindriyaṃ…pe…  itthindriyaṃ…pe…  purisindriyaṃ…pe…  jīvitindriyaṃ? Yo tesaṃ rūpīnaṃ dhammānaṃ āyu ṭhiti yapanā yāpanā iriyanā vattanā pālanā jīvitaṃ jīvitindriyaṃ—  idaṃ taṃ rūpaṃ jīvitindriyaṃ.

(980.)

1642. Katamaṃ taṃ rūpaṃ na indriyaṃ sappaṭighaṃ? Rūpāyatanaṃ…pe…  phoṭṭhabbāyatanaṃ—  idaṃ taṃ rūpaṃ na indriyaṃ sappaṭighaṃ.

(981.)

1643. Katamaṃ taṃ rūpaṃ na indriyaṃ appaṭighaṃ? Kāyaviññatti…pe…  kabaḷīkāro āhāro—  idaṃ taṃ rūpaṃ na indriyaṃ appaṭighaṃ.

===

1644. Evaṃ dasavidhena rūpasaṅgaho.

  
Dasakaṃ.

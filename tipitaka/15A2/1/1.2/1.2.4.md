# 1.2.4

### Paṭhamapaṇṇāsaka

### Adhikaraṇavagga

(14.)

36\. “Dvemā, bhikkhave, tathāgatassa dhammadesanā. Katamā dve? Saṃkhittena ca vitthārena ca. Imā kho, bhikkhave, dve tathāgatassa dhammadesanā”ti.

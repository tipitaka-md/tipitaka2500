# 3.2.9

### Tatiyapaṇṇāsaka

### Āyācanavagga

(139.)

229. “Dveme, bhikkhave, dhammā. Katame dve? Sacittavodānañca na ca kiñci loke upādiyati. Ime kho, bhikkhave, dve dhammā”ti.

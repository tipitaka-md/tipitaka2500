# 3.2.11

* Tatiyapaṇṇāsaka

* Āyācanavagga

(141.)

231\. “Dveme, bhikkhave, dhammā. Katame dve? Kodhavinayo ca upanāhavinayo ca. Ime kho, bhikkhave, dve dhammā”ti.

---

232\. Āyācanavaggo dutiyo.

# 3.5.16

* Tatiyapaṇṇāsaka

* Samāpattivagga

(179.)

272\. “Dveme, bhikkhave, dhammā. Katame dve? Muṭṭhassaccañca asampajaññañca. Ime kho, bhikkhave, dve dhammā”.

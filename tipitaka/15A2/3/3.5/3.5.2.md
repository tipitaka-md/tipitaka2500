# 3.5.2

### Tatiyapaṇṇāsaka

### Samāpattivagga

(165.)

258. “Dveme, bhikkhave, dhammā. Katame dve? Ajjavañca maddavañca. Ime kho, bhikkhave, dve dhammā”.

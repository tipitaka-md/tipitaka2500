# 3.5.8

### Tatiyapaṇṇāsaka

### Samāpattivagga

(171.)

264. “Dveme, bhikkhave, dhammā. Katame dve? Paṭisaṅkhānabalañca bhāvanābalañca. Ime kho, bhikkhave, dve dhammā”.

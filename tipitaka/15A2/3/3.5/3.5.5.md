# 3.5.5

### Tatiyapaṇṇāsaka

### Samāpattivagga

(168.)

261. “Dveme, bhikkhave, dhammā. Katame dve? Avihiṃsā ca soceyyañca. Ime kho, bhikkhave, dve dhammā”.

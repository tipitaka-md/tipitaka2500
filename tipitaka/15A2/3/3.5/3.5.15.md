# 3.5.15

### Tatiyapaṇṇāsaka

### Samāpattivagga

(178.)

271. “Dveme, bhikkhave, dhammā. Katame dve? Asantuṭṭhitā ca kusalesu dhammesu, appaṭivānitā ca padhānasmiṃ. Ime kho, bhikkhave, dve dhammā”.

# 3.5.4

### Tatiyapaṇṇāsaka

### Samāpattivagga

(167.)

260. “Dveme, bhikkhave, dhammā. Katame dve? Sākhalyañca paṭisanthāro ca. Ime kho, bhikkhave, dve dhammā”.

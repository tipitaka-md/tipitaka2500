# 3.5.3

### Tatiyapaṇṇāsaka

### Samāpattivagga

(166.)

259. “Dveme, bhikkhave, dhammā. Katame dve? Khanti ca soraccañca. Ime kho, bhikkhave, dve dhammā”.

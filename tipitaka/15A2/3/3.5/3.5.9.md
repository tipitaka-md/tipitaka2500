# 3.5.9

### Tatiyapaṇṇāsaka

### Samāpattivagga

(172.)

265. “Dveme, bhikkhave, dhammā. Katame dve? Satibalañca samādhibalañca. Ime kho, bhikkhave, dve dhammā”.

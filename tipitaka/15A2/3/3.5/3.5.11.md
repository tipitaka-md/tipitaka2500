# 3.5.11

### Tatiyapaṇṇāsaka

### Samāpattivagga

(174.)

267. “Dveme, bhikkhave, dhammā. Katame dve? Sīlavipatti ca diṭṭhivipatti ca. Ime kho, bhikkhave, dve dhammā”.

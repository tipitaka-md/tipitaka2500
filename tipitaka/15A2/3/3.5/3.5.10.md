# 3.5.10

### Tatiyapaṇṇāsaka

### Samāpattivagga

(173.)

266. “Dveme, bhikkhave, dhammā. Katame dve? Samatho ca vipassanā ca. Ime kho, bhikkhave, dve dhammā”.

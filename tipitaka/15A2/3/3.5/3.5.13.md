# 3.5.13

### Tatiyapaṇṇāsaka

### Samāpattivagga

(176.)

269. “Dveme, bhikkhave, dhammā. Katame dve? Sīlavisuddhi ca diṭṭhivisuddhi ca. Ime kho, bhikkhave, dve dhammā”.

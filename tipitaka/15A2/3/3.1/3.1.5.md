# 3.1.5

### Tatiyapaṇṇāsaka

### Āsāduppajahavagga

(123.)

208. “Dveme, bhikkhave, puggalā sutappayā. Katame dve? Yo ca laddhaṃ laddhaṃ na nikkhipati, yo ca laddhaṃ laddhaṃ na vissajjeti. Ime kho, bhikkhave, dve puggalā sutappayā”ti.

# 3.1.3

### Tatiyapaṇṇāsaka

### Āsāduppajahavagga

(121.)

206. “Dveme, bhikkhave, puggalā dullabhā lokasmiṃ. Katame dve? Titto ca tappetā ca. Ime kho, bhikkhave, dve puggalā dullabhā lokasmin”ti.

# 3.1.11

* Tatiyapaṇṇāsaka

* Āsāduppajahavagga

(129.)

214\. “Dvemā, bhikkhave, āpattiyo. Katamā dve? Duṭṭhullā ca āpatti, aduṭṭhullā ca āpatti. Imā kho, bhikkhave, dve āpattiyo”ti.

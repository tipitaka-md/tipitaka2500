# 3.1.4

### Tatiyapaṇṇāsaka

### Āsāduppajahavagga

(122.)

207. “Dveme, bhikkhave, puggalā duttappayā. Katame dve? Yo ca laddhaṃ laddhaṃ nikkhipati, yo ca laddhaṃ laddhaṃ vissajjeti. Ime kho, bhikkhave, dve puggalā duttappayā”ti.

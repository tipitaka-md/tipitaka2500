# 3.1.2

### Tatiyapaṇṇāsaka

### Āsāduppajahavagga

(120.)

205. “Dveme, bhikkhave, puggalā dullabhā lokasmiṃ. Katame dve? Yo ca pubbakārī, yo ca kataññū katavedī. Ime kho, bhikkhave, dve puggalā dullabhā lokasmin”ti.

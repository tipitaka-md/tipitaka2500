# 3.4.11

### Tatiyapaṇṇāsaka

### Santhāravagga

(162.)

254. “Dveme, bhikkhave, sannicayā. Katame dve? Āmisasannicayo ca dhammasannicayo ca. Ime kho, bhikkhave, dve sannicayā. Etadaggaṃ, bhikkhave, imesaṃ dvinnaṃ sannicayānaṃ yadidaṃ dhammasannicayo”ti.

# 3.4.8

### Tatiyapaṇṇāsaka

### Santhāravagga

(159.)

251. “Dvemā, bhikkhave, iddhiyo. Katamā dve? Āmisiddhi ca dhammiddhi ca. Imā kho, bhikkhave, dve iddhiyo. Etadaggaṃ, bhikkhave, imāsaṃ dvinnaṃ iddhīnaṃ yadidaṃ dhammiddhī”ti.

# 3.4.7

* Tatiyapaṇṇāsaka

* Santhāravagga

(158.)

250\. “Dvemāni, bhikkhave, ātitheyyāni. Katamāni dve? Āmisātitheyyañca dhammātitheyyañca. Imāni kho, bhikkhave, dve ātitheyyāni. Etadaggaṃ, bhikkhave, imesaṃ dvinnaṃ ātitheyyānaṃ yadidaṃ dhammātitheyyan”ti.

# 2.2.12

* Dutiyapaṇṇāsaka

* Sukhavagga

(76.)

156\. “Dvemāni, bhikkhave, sukhāni. Katamāni dve? Sātārammaṇañca sukhaṃ upekkhārammaṇañca sukhaṃ. Imāni kho, bhikkhave, dve sukhāni. Etadaggaṃ, bhikkhave, imesaṃ dvinnaṃ sukhānaṃ yadidaṃ upekkhārammaṇaṃ sukhan”ti.

# 2.2.3

### Dutiyapaṇṇāsaka

### Sukhavagga

(67.)

147. “Dvemāni, bhikkhave, sukhāni. Katamāni dve? Upadhisukhañca nirupadhisukhañca. Imāni kho, bhikkhave, dve sukhāni. Etadaggaṃ, bhikkhave, imesaṃ dvinnaṃ sukhānaṃ yadidaṃ nirupadhisukhan”ti.

# 2.2.1

### Dutiyapaṇṇāsaka

### Sukhavagga

(65.)

145. “Dvemāni, bhikkhave, sukhāni. Katamāni dve? Gihisukhañca pabbajitasukhañca. Imāni kho, bhikkhave, dve sukhāni. Etadaggaṃ, bhikkhave, imesaṃ dvinnaṃ sukhānaṃ yadidaṃ pabbajitasukhan”ti.

# 2.2.6

### Dutiyapaṇṇāsaka

### Sukhavagga

(70.)

150. “Dvemāni, bhikkhave, sukhāni. Katamāni dve? Ariyasukhañca anariyasukhañca. Imāni kho, bhikkhave, dve sukhāni. Etadaggaṃ, bhikkhave, imesaṃ dvinnaṃ sukhānaṃ yadidaṃ ariyasukhan”ti.

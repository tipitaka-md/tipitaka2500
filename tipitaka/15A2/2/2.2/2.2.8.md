# 2.2.8

### Dutiyapaṇṇāsaka

### Sukhavagga

(72.)

152. “Dvemāni, bhikkhave, sukhāni. Katamāni dve? Sappītikañca sukhaṃ nippītikañca sukhaṃ. Imāni kho, bhikkhave, dve sukhāni. Etadaggaṃ, bhikkhave, imesaṃ dvinnaṃ sukhānaṃ yadidaṃ nippītikaṃ sukhan”ti.

# 2.2.10

### Dutiyapaṇṇāsaka

### Sukhavagga

(74.)

154. “Dvemāni, bhikkhave, sukhāni. Katamāni dve? Samādhisukhañca asamādhisukhañca. Imāni kho, bhikkhave, dve sukhāni. Etadaggaṃ, bhikkhave, imesaṃ dvinnaṃ sukhānaṃ yadidaṃ samādhisukhan”ti.

# 2.2.11

### Dutiyapaṇṇāsaka

### Sukhavagga

(75.)

155. “Dvemāni, bhikkhave, sukhāni. Katamāni dve? Sappītikārammaṇañca sukhaṃ nippītikārammaṇañca sukhaṃ. Imāni kho, bhikkhave, dve sukhāni. Etadaggaṃ, bhikkhave, imesaṃ dvinnaṃ sukhānaṃ yadidaṃ nippītikārammaṇaṃ sukhan”ti.

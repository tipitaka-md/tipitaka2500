# 2.2.5

* Dutiyapaṇṇāsaka

* Sukhavagga

(69.)

149\. “Dvemāni, bhikkhave, sukhāni. Katamāni dve? Sāmisañca sukhaṃ nirāmisañca sukhaṃ. Imāni kho, bhikkhave, dve sukhāni. Etadaggaṃ, bhikkhave, imesaṃ dvinnaṃ sukhānaṃ yadidaṃ nirāmisaṃ sukhan”ti.

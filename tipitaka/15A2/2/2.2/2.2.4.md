# 2.2.4

* Dutiyapaṇṇāsaka

* Sukhavagga

(68.)

148\. “Dvemāni, bhikkhave, sukhāni. Katamāni dve? Sāsavasukhañca anāsavasukhañca. Imāni kho, bhikkhave, dve sukhāni. Etadaggaṃ, bhikkhave, imesaṃ dvinnaṃ sukhānaṃ yadidaṃ anāsavasukhan”ti.

# 2.2.9

### Dutiyapaṇṇāsaka

### Sukhavagga

(73.)

153. “Dvemāni, bhikkhave, sukhāni. Katamāni dve? Sātasukhañca upekkhāsukhañca. Imāni kho, bhikkhave, dve sukhāni. Etadaggaṃ, bhikkhave, imesaṃ dvinnaṃ sukhānaṃ yadidaṃ upekkhāsukhan”ti.

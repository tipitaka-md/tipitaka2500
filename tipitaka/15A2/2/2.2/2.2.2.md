# 2.2.2

* Dutiyapaṇṇāsaka

* Sukhavagga

(66.)

146\. “Dvemāni, bhikkhave, sukhāni. Katamāni dve? Kāmasukhañca nekkhammasukhañca. Imāni kho, bhikkhave, dve sukhāni. Etadaggaṃ, bhikkhave, imesaṃ dvinnaṃ sukhānaṃ yadidaṃ nekkhammasukhan”ti.

# 2.1.5

### Dutiyapaṇṇāsaka

### Puggalavagga

(57.)

133\. “Dveme, bhikkhave, buddhā. Katame dve? Tathāgato ca arahaṃ sammāsambuddho, paccekabuddho ca. Ime kho, bhikkhave, dve buddhā”ti.

# 2.5.16

### Dutiyapaṇṇāsaka

### Bālavagga

(114.)

197. “Dvinnaṃ, bhikkhave, āsavā na vaḍḍhanti. Katamesaṃ dvinnaṃ? Yo ca āpattiyā āpattisaññī, yo ca anāpattiyā anāpattisaññī. Imesaṃ kho, bhikkhave, dvinnaṃ āsavā na vaḍḍhantī”ti.

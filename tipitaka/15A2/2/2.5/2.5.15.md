# 2.5.15

### Dutiyapaṇṇāsaka

### Bālavagga

(113.)

196. “Dvinnaṃ, bhikkhave, āsavā vaḍḍhanti. Katamesaṃ dvinnaṃ? Yo ca āpattiyā anāpattisaññī, yo ca anāpattiyā āpattisaññī. Imesaṃ kho, bhikkhave, dvinnaṃ āsavā vaḍḍhantī”ti.

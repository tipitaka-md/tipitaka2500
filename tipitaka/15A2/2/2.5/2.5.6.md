# 2.5.6

### Dutiyapaṇṇāsaka

### Bālavagga

(104.)

187. “Dveme, bhikkhave, paṇḍitā. Katame dve? Yo ca anāpattiyā anāpattisaññī, yo ca āpattiyā āpattisaññī. Ime kho, bhikkhave, dve paṇḍitā”ti.

# 2.5.8

### Dutiyapaṇṇāsaka

### Bālavagga

(106.)

189. “Dveme, bhikkhave, paṇḍitā. Katame dve? Yo ca dhamme dhammasaññī, yo ca adhamme adhammasaññī. Ime kho, bhikkhave, dve paṇḍitā”ti.

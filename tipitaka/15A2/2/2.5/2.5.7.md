# 2.5.7

* Dutiyapaṇṇāsaka

* Bālavagga

(105.)

188\. “Dveme, bhikkhave, bālā. Katame dve? Yo ca adhamme dhammasaññī, yo ca dhamme adhammasaññī. Ime kho, bhikkhave, dve bālā”ti.

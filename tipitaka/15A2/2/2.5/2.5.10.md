# 2.5.10

### Dutiyapaṇṇāsaka

### Bālavagga

(108.)

191. “Dveme, bhikkhave, paṇḍitā. Katame dve? Yo ca avinaye avinayasaññī, yo ca vinaye vinayasaññī. Ime kho, bhikkhave, dve paṇḍitā”ti.

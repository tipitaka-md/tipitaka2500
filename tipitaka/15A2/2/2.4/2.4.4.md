# 2.4.4

### Dutiyapaṇṇāsaka

### Dhammavagga

(91.)

173. “Dveme, bhikkhave, dhammā. Katame dve? Vijjā ca vimutti ca. Ime kho, bhikkhave, dve dhammā”ti.

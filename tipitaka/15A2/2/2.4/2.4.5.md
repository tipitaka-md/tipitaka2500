# 2.4.5

### Dutiyapaṇṇāsaka

### Dhammavagga

(92.)

174. “Dveme, bhikkhave, dhammā. Katame dve? Bhavadiṭṭhi ca vibhavadiṭṭhi ca. Ime kho, bhikkhave, dve dhammā”ti.

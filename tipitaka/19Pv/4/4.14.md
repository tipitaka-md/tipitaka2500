# 4.14 Bhogasaṃharaṇapetavatthu

### Mahāvagga

### Bhogasaṃharaṇapetavatthu

(801.)

862. _“Mayaṃ bhoge saṃharimhā,_  
_samena visamena ca;_  
_Te aññe paribhuñjanti,_  
_mayaṃ dukkhassa bhāginī”ti._  


---

863. Bhogasaṃharaṇapetavatthu cuddasamaṃ.

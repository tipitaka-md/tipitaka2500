# 5.4.1--7 Paṭiccādivāra

### Kusalattika, Mahantaradukaupādānagocchaka

### Kusalattika, Cittasampayuttadukādi

### Paṭiccādivāra

(17.)

1382. Kusalaṃ cittasampayuttaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca…pe…  kusalaṃ cittasaṃsaṭṭhaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca…pe…  kusalaṃ cittasamuṭṭhānaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca…pe…  kusalaṃ cittasahabhuṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca…pe…  kusalaṃ cittānuparivattiṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca…pe…  kusalaṃ cittasaṃsaṭṭhasamuṭṭhānaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca…pe…  kusalaṃ cittasaṃsaṭṭhasamuṭṭhānasahabhuṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca…pe…  kusalaṃ cittasaṃsaṭṭhasamuṭṭhānānuparivattiṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca kusalo cittasaṃsaṭṭhasamuṭṭhānānuparivatti dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

1383. Hetuyā tīṇi, ārammaṇe tīṇi…pe…  vipāke ekaṃ…pe…  avigate tīṇi. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

1384. (Sahajātavārampi…pe…  sampayuttavārampi pañhāvārampi evaṃ vitthāretabbaṃ.)

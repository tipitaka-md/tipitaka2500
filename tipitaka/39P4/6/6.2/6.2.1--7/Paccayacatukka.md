# Paccayacatukka

* Kusalattika, Kilesagocchaka

* Kusalattika, Saṃkilesikaduka

* Paṭiccādivāra

* Paccayacatukka

(6.)

1448\. Kusalaṃ saṃkilesikaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca kusalo saṃkilesiko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā…  tīṇi.

1449\. Akusalaṃ saṃkilesikaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca akusalo saṃkilesiko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā…  tīṇi.

1450\. Abyākataṃ saṃkilesikaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca abyākato saṃkilesiko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (1)

1451\. Kusalaṃ saṃkilesikañca abyākataṃ saṃkilesikañca dhammaṃ paṭicca abyākato saṃkilesiko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (1)

1452\. Akusalaṃ saṃkilesikañca abyākataṃ saṃkilesikañca dhammaṃ paṭicca abyākato saṃkilesiko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (1)

(7.)

1453\. Hetuyā nava, ārammaṇe tīṇi…pe…  vipāke ekaṃ…pe…  avigate nava. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

1454\. (Sahajātavārampi…pe…  sampayuttavārampi vitthāretabbaṃ.)

(8.)

1455\. Kusalo saṃkilesiko dhammo akusalassa saṃkilesikassa dhammassa hetupaccayena paccayo…  tīṇi.

1456\. Akusalo saṃkilesiko dhammo akusalassa saṃkilesikassa dhammassa hetupaccayena paccayo…  tīṇi.

1457\. Abyākato saṃkilesiko dhammo abyākatassa saṃkilesikassa dhammassa hetupaccayena paccayo. (1) (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

(9.)

1458\. Hetuyā satta, ārammaṇe nava, adhipatiyā nava…pe…  avigate terasa. (Saṃkhittaṃ. Yathā kusalattike pañhāvāraṃ, evaṃ vitthāretabbaṃ.)

(10.)

1459\. Kusalaṃ asaṃkilesikaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca kusalo asaṃkilesiko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (1)

1460\. Abyākataṃ asaṃkilesikaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca abyākato asaṃkilesiko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (1) (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

(11.)

1461\. Hetuyā dve, ārammaṇe dve…pe…  āsevane ekaṃ…pe…  vipāke ekaṃ…pe…  avigate dve. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

1462\. (Sahajātavārampi…pe…  sampayuttavārampi paṭiccavārasadisaṃ.)

(12.)

1463\. Kusalo asaṃkilesiko dhammo kusalassa asaṃkilesikassa dhammassa hetupaccayena paccayo. (1)

1464\. Abyākato asaṃkilesiko dhammo abyākatassa asaṃkilesikassa dhammassa hetupaccayena paccayo. (1)

1465\. Abyākato asaṃkilesiko dhammo abyākatassa asaṃkilesikassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo. Abyākato asaṃkilesiko dhammo kusalassa asaṃkilesikassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo. (2) (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

(13.)

1466\. Hetuyā dve, ārammaṇe dve, adhipatiyā tīṇi, anantare dve…pe…  upanissaye cattāri…pe…  kamme tīṇi, vipāke ekaṃ…pe…  avigate dve. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

1467\. (Yathā kusalattike pañhāvāraṃ, evaṃ vitthāretabbaṃ.)

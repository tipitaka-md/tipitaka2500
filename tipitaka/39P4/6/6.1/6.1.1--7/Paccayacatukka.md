# Paccayacatukka

### Kusalattika, Kilesagocchaka

### Kusalattika, Kilesaduka

### Paṭiccādivāra

### Paccayacatukka

(1.)

1436. Akusalaṃ kilesaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca akusalo kileso dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

1437. Hetuyā ekaṃ, ārammaṇe ekaṃ…pe…  avigate ekaṃ. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

1438. (Sahajātavārepi…pe…  pañhāvārepi sabbattha ekaṃ.)

(2.)

1439. Kusalaṃ nokilesaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca kusalo nokileso dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā…  tīṇi.

1440. Akusalaṃ nokilesaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca akusalo nokileso dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā…  tīṇi.

1441. Abyākataṃ nokilesaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca abyākato nokileso dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (1)

1442. Kusalaṃ nokilesañca abyākataṃ nokilesañca dhammaṃ paṭicca abyākato nokileso dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (1)

1443. Akusalaṃ nokilesañca abyākataṃ nokilesañca dhammaṃ paṭicca abyākato nokileso dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (1) (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

(3.)

1444. Hetuyā nava, ārammaṇe tīṇi, adhipatiyā nava…pe…  vipāke ekaṃ, avigate nava. (Saṃkhittaṃ. Sahajātavārampi…pe…  sampayuttavārampi paṭiccavārasadisaṃ.)

(4.)

1445. Kusalo nokileso dhammo kusalassa nokilesassa dhammassa hetupaccayena paccayo…  tīṇi.

1446. Abyākato nokileso dhammo abyākatassa nokilesassa dhammassa hetupaccayena paccayo. (1) (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

(5.)

1447. Hetuyā cattāri, ārammaṇe nava, adhipatiyā dasa…pe…  avigate terasa. (Saṃkhittaṃ. Yathā kusalattike pañhāvāraṃ, evaṃ vitthāretabbaṃ.)

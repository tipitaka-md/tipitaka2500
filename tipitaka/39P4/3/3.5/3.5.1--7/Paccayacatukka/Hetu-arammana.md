(28.)

1167\. Kusalo arūpī dhammo kusalassa arūpissa dhammassa hetupaccayena paccayo. (1)

1168\. Akusalo arūpī dhammo akusalassa arūpissa dhammassa hetupaccayena paccayo. (1)

1169\. Abyākato arūpī dhammo abyākatassa arūpissa dhammassa hetupaccayena paccayo. (1)

1170\. Kusalo arūpī dhammo kusalassa arūpissa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo…  tīṇi.

1171\. Akusalo arūpī dhammo akusalassa arūpissa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo…  tīṇi.

1172\. Abyākato arūpī dhammo abyākatassa arūpissa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

(29.)

1173\. Hetuyā tīṇi, ārammaṇe nava, adhipatiyā satta, anantare satta…pe…  sahajāte aññamaññe nissaye tīṇi, upanissaye nava, āsevane tīṇi, kamme pañca, vipāke ekaṃ, āhāre…pe…  sampayutte tīṇi…pe…  avigate tīṇi. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

1174\. (Yathā kusalattike pañhāvāraṃ, evaṃ vitthāretabbaṃ.)

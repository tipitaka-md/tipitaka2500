# 3.5.7 Tatiyakhatasutta

### Tatiyapaṇṇāsaka

### Maṅgalavagga

### Tatiyakhatasutta

(153.)

1081. “Tīhi, bhikkhave…pe…  visamena kāyakammena, visamena vacīkammena, visamena manokammena…pe… .

1082. Tīhi, bhikkhave…pe…  samena kāyakammena, samena vacīkammena, samena manokammena…pe… .

===

1083. Sattamaṃ.

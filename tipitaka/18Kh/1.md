# 1 Saraṇattaya

* Saraṇattaya

(1.)

2\. _Buddhaṃ saraṇaṃ gacchāmi,_  
_Dhammaṃ saraṇaṃ gacchāmi,_  
_Saṃghaṃ saraṇaṃ gacchāmi._  


3\. _Dutiyampi buddhaṃ saraṇaṃ gacchāmi,_  
_Dutiyampi dhammaṃ saraṇaṃ gacchāmi,_  
_Dutiyampi saṃghaṃ saraṇaṃ gacchāmi._  


4\. _Tatiyampi buddhaṃ saraṇaṃ gacchāmi,_  
_Tatiyampi dhammaṃ saraṇaṃ gacchāmi,_  
_Tatiyampi saṃghaṃ saraṇaṃ gacchāmi._  


---

5\. Saraṇattayaṃ.

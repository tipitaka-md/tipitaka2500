# 2.2.3.6 Paccanīkapuggalokāsa

* Pavattivāra

* Nirodhavāra

* Anāgatavāra

* Paccanīkapuggalokāsa

(104.)

536\. »  Yassa yattha dukkhasaccaṃ na nirujjhissati tassa tattha samudayasaccaṃ na nirujjhissatīti? Āmantā.

537\. «  Yassa vā pana yattha samudayasaccaṃ na nirujjhissati tassa tattha dukkhasaccaṃ na nirujjhissatīti?

538\. Aggamaggasamaṅgīnaṃ arahantānaṃ yassa cittassa anantarā aggamaggaṃ paṭilabhissanti asaññasattānaṃ tesaṃ tattha samudayasaccaṃ na nirujjhissati, no ca tesaṃ tattha dukkhasaccaṃ na nirujjhissati. Pacchimacittassa bhaṅgakkhaṇe tesaṃ tattha samudayasaccañca na nirujjhissati dukkhasaccañca na nirujjhissati.

539\. »  Yassa yattha dukkhasaccaṃ na nirujjhissati tassa tattha maggasaccaṃ na nirujjhissatīti? Āmantā.

540\. «  Yassa vā pana yattha maggasaccaṃ na nirujjhissati tassa tattha dukkhasaccaṃ na nirujjhissatīti?

541\. Aggamaggassa bhaṅgakkhaṇe arahantānaṃ ye ca puthujjanā maggaṃ na paṭilabhissanti āpāyikānaṃ asaññasattānaṃ tesaṃ tattha maggasaccaṃ na nirujjhissati, no ca tesaṃ tattha dukkhasaccaṃ na nirujjhissati. Pacchimacittassa bhaṅgakkhaṇe tesaṃ tattha maggasaccañca na nirujjhissati dukkhasaccañca na nirujjhissati.

(105.)

542\. »  Yassa yattha samudayasaccaṃ na nirujjhissati tassa tattha maggasaccaṃ na nirujjhissatīti?

543\. Aggamaggassa uppādakkhaṇe yassa cittassa anantarā aggamaggaṃ paṭilabhissanti tesaṃ tattha samudayasaccaṃ na nirujjhissati, no ca tesaṃ tattha maggasaccaṃ na nirujjhissati. Aggamaggassa bhaṅgakkhaṇe arahantānaṃ asaññasattānaṃ tesaṃ tattha samudayasaccañca na nirujjhissati maggasaccañca na nirujjhissati.

544\. «  Yassa vā pana yattha maggasaccaṃ na nirujjhissati tassa tattha samudayasaccaṃ na nirujjhissatīti?

545\. Āpāyikānaṃ ye ca puthujjanā maggaṃ na paṭilabhissanti tesaṃ tattha maggasaccaṃ na nirujjhissati, no ca tesaṃ tattha samudayasaccaṃ na nirujjhissati. Aggamaggassa bhaṅgakkhaṇe arahantānaṃ asaññasattānaṃ tesaṃ tattha maggasaccañca na nirujjhissati samudayasaccañca na nirujjhissati.

### Anulomapuggalokāsa

(100.)

522. »  Yassa yattha dukkhasaccaṃ nirujjhissati tassa tattha samudayasaccaṃ nirujjhissatīti?

523. Aggamaggasamaṅgīnaṃ arahantānaṃ yassa cittassa anantarā aggamaggaṃ paṭilabhissanti asaññasattānaṃ tesaṃ tattha dukkhasaccaṃ nirujjhissati, no ca tesaṃ tattha samudayasaccaṃ nirujjhissati. Itaresaṃ catuvokārānaṃ pañcavokārānaṃ tesaṃ tattha dukkhasaccañca nirujjhissati samudayasaccañca nirujjhissati…pe… .

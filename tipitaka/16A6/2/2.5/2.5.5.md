# 2.5.5 Anattasutta

### Dutiyapaṇṇāsaka

### Ānisaṃsavagga

### Anattasutta

(100.)

723. “So vata, bhikkhave, bhikkhu kañci dhammaṃ attato samanupassanto…pe…  sabbadhamme anattato samanupassanto…pe…  ṭhānametaṃ vijjati”.

===

724. Pañcamaṃ.

# 2.5.4 Dukkhasutta

* Dutiyapaṇṇāsaka

* Ānisaṃsavagga

* Dukkhasutta

(99.)

721\. “So vata, bhikkhave, bhikkhu kañci saṅkhāraṃ sukhato samanupassanto…pe…  sabbasaṅkhāre dukkhato samanupassanto…pe…  ṭhānametaṃ vijjati”.

722\. Catutthaṃ.

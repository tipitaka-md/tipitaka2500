# 2.8.1

### Dutiyapaṇṇāsaka

### Rāgapeyyāla

(140.)

775. “Rāgassa, bhikkhave, abhiññāya cha dhammā bhāvetabbā. Katame cha? Dassanānuttariyaṃ, savanānuttariyaṃ, lābhānuttariyaṃ, sikkhānuttariyaṃ, pāricariyānuttariyaṃ, anussatānuttariyaṃ. Rāgassa, bhikkhave, abhiññāya ime cha dhammā bhāvetabbā”ti.

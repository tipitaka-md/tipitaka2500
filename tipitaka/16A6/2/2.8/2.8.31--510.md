# 2.8.31--510

### Dutiyapaṇṇāsaka

### Rāgapeyyāla

(170--649.)

779. “Dosassa…pe…  mohassa…  kodhassa…  upanāhassa…  makkhassa…  paḷāsassa…  issāya…  macchariyassa…  māyāya…  sāṭheyyassa…  thambhassa…  sārambhassa…  mānassa…  atimānassa…  madassa…  pamādassa abhiññāya…pe…  pariññāya…  parikkhayāya…  pahānāya…  khayāya…  vayāya…  virāgāya…  nirodhāya…  cāgāya…  paṭinissaggāya…pe…  ime cha dhammā bhāvetabbā”ti. Idamavoca bhagavā. Attamanā te bhikkhū bhagavato bhāsitaṃ abhinandunti.

---

780. Rāgapeyyālaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.



---

781. Chakkanipātapāḷi niṭṭhitā.

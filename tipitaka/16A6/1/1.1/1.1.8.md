# 1.1.8 Anuttariyasutta

### Paṭhamapaṇṇāsaka

### Āhuneyyavagga

### Anuttariyasutta

(8.)

28. “Chayimāni, bhikkhave, anuttariyāni. Katamāni cha? Dassanānuttariyaṃ, savanānuttariyaṃ, lābhānuttariyaṃ, sikkhānuttariyaṃ, pāricariyānuttariyaṃ, anussatānuttariyaṃ. Imāni kho, bhikkhave, cha anuttariyānī”ti.

===

29. Aṭṭhamaṃ.

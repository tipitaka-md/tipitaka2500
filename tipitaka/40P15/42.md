# 42 Cittasaṃsaṭṭhaduka, Kusalattika

### Cittasaṃsaṭṭhaduka, Kusalattika

(51.)

150. Cittasaṃsaṭṭhaṃ kusalaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nacittasaṃsaṭṭho nakusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Hetuyā ekaṃ…pe…  avigate ekaṃ.

151. Cittasaṃsaṭṭhaṃ akusalaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nacittasaṃsaṭṭho naakusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Hetuyā ekaṃ…pe…  avigate ekaṃ. (Abyākatamūlaṃ tīṇiyeva paccayavasena.)

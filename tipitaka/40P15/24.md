# 24 Hetusampayuttaduka, Kusalattika

### Hetusampayuttaduka, Kusalattika

(31.)

86. Hetusampayuttaṃ kusalaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nahetusampayutto nakusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Hetuyā ekaṃ…pe…  avigate ekaṃ.

87. Hetusampayuttaṃ akusalaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nahetusampayutto naakusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā… . Hetuyā tīṇi, adhipatiyā ekaṃ…pe…  avigate tīṇi.

88. Hetuvippayuttaṃ abyākataṃ dhammaṃ paccayā nahetuvippayutto naabyākato dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Hetuyā ekaṃ, ārammaṇe tīṇi, adhipatiyā ekaṃ…pe…  avigate tīṇi.

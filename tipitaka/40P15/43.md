# 43 Cittasamuṭṭhānaduka, Kusalattika

### Cittasamuṭṭhānaduka, Kusalattika

(52.)

152. Cittasamuṭṭhānaṃ kusalaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nanocittasamuṭṭhāno nakusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā… . Hetuyā tīṇi…pe…  avigate tīṇi.

153. Cittasamuṭṭhānaṃ akusalaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nanocittasamuṭṭhāno naakusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā… . Hetuyā tīṇi…pe…  avigate tīṇi. (Abyākatamūlaṃ tīṇiyeva paccayavasena.)

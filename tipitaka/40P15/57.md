# 57 Savitakkaduka, Kusalattika

### Savitakkaduka, Kusalattika

(66.)

185. Savitakkaṃ kusalaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nasavitakko nakusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā… . Hetuyā tīṇi…pe…  avigate tīṇi.

186. Savitakkaṃ akusalaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nasavitakko naakusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā… . Hetuyā tīṇi…pe…  avigate tīṇi. (Abyākatamūlaṃ tīṇiyeva paccayavasena.)

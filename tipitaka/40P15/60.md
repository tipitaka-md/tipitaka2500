# 60 Pītisahagataduka, Kusalattika

### Pītisahagataduka, Kusalattika

(69.)

191. Pītisahagataṃ kusalaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca napītisahagato nakusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā… . Hetuyā tīṇi…pe…  avigate tīṇi.

192. Pītisahagataṃ akusalaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca napītisahagato naakusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā… . Hetuyā tīṇi…pe…  avigate tīṇi. (Abyākatamūlaṃ tīṇiyeva paccayavasena.)

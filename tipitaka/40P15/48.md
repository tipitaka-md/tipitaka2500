(57.)

162. Cittasaṃsaṭṭhasamuṭṭhānānuparivattiṃ kusalaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nocittasaṃsaṭṭhasamuṭṭhānānuparivattī nakusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā… . Hetuyā tīṇi…pe…  avigate tīṇi.

# 75 Saraṇaduka, Vitakkattika

### Saraṇaduka, Vitakkattika

(86.)

232\. Saraṇaṃ savitakkasavicāraṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nasaraṇo nasavitakkasavicāro dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā…  tīṇi.

233\. Araṇaṃ savitakkasavicāraṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca naaraṇo nasavitakkasavicāro dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā…  ekaṃ.

234\. Hetuyā cattāri, ārammaṇe dve…pe…  vipāke ekaṃ…pe…  avigate cattāri.

(87.)

235\. Saraṇaṃ avitakkavicāramattaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nasaraṇo naavitakkavicāramatto dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā… . Hetuyā cattāri, ārammaṇe dve…pe…  vipāke ekaṃ…pe…  avigate cattāri.

236\. Araṇaṃ avitakkaavicāraṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nasaraṇo naavitakkaavicāro dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Hetuyā ekaṃ…pe…  avigate ekaṃ.

# 51 Upādinnaduka, Kusalattika

### Upādinnaduka, Kusalattika

(60.)

168. Anupādinnaṃ kusalaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca naupādinno nakusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Hetuyā ekaṃ…pe…  avigate ekaṃ.

169. Anupādinnaṃ akusalaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca naupādinno naakusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Hetuyā ekaṃ…pe…  avigate ekaṃ. (Abyākatamūlaṃ ekaṃyeva paccayavasena.)

===

170. Mahantaradukaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.

# 72 Saraṇaduka, Vipākattika

* Saraṇaduka, Vipākattika

(83.)

224\. Araṇaṃ vipākaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nasaraṇo navipāko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Hetuyā ekaṃ…pe…  avigate ekaṃ.

225\. Saraṇaṃ vipākadhammadhammaṃ paṭicca nasaraṇo navipākadhammadhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Araṇaṃ vipākadhammadhammaṃ paṭicca nasaraṇo navipākadhammadhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Hetuyā dve…pe…  avigate dve.

226\. Araṇaṃ nevavipākanavipākadhammadhammaṃ paṭicca nasaraṇo nanevavipākanavipākadhammadhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Hetuyā ekaṃ…pe…  avigate ekaṃ.

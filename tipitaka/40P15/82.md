# 82 Saraṇaduka, Parittārammaṇattika

### Saraṇaduka, Parittārammaṇattika

(94.)

259. Saraṇaṃ parittārammaṇaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nasaraṇo naparittārammaṇo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Araṇaṃ parittārammaṇaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nasaraṇo naparittārammaṇo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Hetuyā dve…pe…  avigate dve.

260. Saraṇaṃ mahaggatārammaṇaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nasaraṇo namahaggatārammaṇo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Araṇaṃ mahaggatārammaṇaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nasaraṇo namahaggatārammaṇo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Hetuyā dve…pe…  avigate dve.

261. Araṇaṃ appamāṇārammaṇaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nasaraṇo naappamāṇārammaṇo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Hetuyā ekaṃ…pe…  avigate ekaṃ.

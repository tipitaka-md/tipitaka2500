# 78 Saraṇaduka, Dassanahetuttika

### Saraṇaduka, Dassanahetuttika

(90.)

247. Saraṇaṃ dassanena pahātabbahetukaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nasaraṇo nadassanena pahātabbahetuko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Hetuyā ekaṃ, ārammaṇe ekaṃ…pe…  sahajāte tīṇi…pe…  avigate tīṇi.

248. Saraṇaṃ bhāvanāya pahātabbahetukaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nasaraṇo nabhāvanāya pahātabbahetuko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Hetuyā ekaṃ, ārammaṇe ekaṃ…pe…  sahajāte tīṇi…pe…  avigate tīṇi.

249. Araṇaṃ nevadassanena nabhāvanāya pahātabbahetukaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca naaraṇo nanevadassanena nabhāvanāya pahātabbahetuko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Hetuyā ekaṃ…pe…  avigate ekaṃ.

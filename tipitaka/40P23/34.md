# 34 Maggārammaṇattika, Kusalattika

### Maggārammaṇattika, Kusalattika

(43.)

136. Namaggārammaṇaṃ nakusalaṃ dhammaṃ paccayā maggārammaṇo kusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

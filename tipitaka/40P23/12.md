# 12 Kusalattika, Parittārammaṇattika

### Kusalattika, Parittārammaṇattika

(16.)

57. Nakusalaṃ naparittārammaṇaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca abyākato parittārammaṇo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi…pe…  avigate tīṇi.

58. Nakusalaṃ namahaggatārammaṇaṃ dhammaṃ paccayā kusalo mahaggatārammaṇo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā nava…pe…  vipāke tīṇi…pe…  avigate nava.

59. Nakusalaṃ naappamāṇārammaṇaṃ dhammaṃ paccayā kusalo appamāṇārammaṇo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā cha…pe…  vipāke tīṇi…pe…  avigate cha.

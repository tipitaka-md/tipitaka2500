# 30 Parittattika, Kusalattika

* Parittattika, Kusalattika

(39.)

124\. Namahaggataṃ nakusalaṃ dhammaṃ paccayā mahaggato kusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā nava…pe…  avigate nava.

125\. Namahaggataṃ naakusalaṃ dhammaṃ paccayā paritto akusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi…pe…  avigate tīṇi.

126\. Naparittaṃ naabyākataṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca paritto abyākato dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā cha, adhipatiyā cha…pe…  avigate cha.

# 11 Kusalattika, Parittattika

### Kusalattika, Parittattika

(15.)

54. Nakusalaṃ naparittaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca abyākato paritto dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā pañca, adhipatiyā pañca…pe…  aññamaññe tīṇi…pe…  vipāke tīṇi…pe…  avigate pañca.

55. Nakusalaṃ namahaggataṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca abyākato mahaggato dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi…pe…  avigate tīṇi.

56. Nakusalaṃ naappamāṇaṃ dhammaṃ paccayā kusalo appamāṇo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā cha…pe…  āsevane tīṇi…pe…  vipāke tīṇi…pe…  avigate cha.

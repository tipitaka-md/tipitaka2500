# 35 Uppannattikādi, Kusalattika

### Uppannattikādi, Kusalattika

(44.)

138. Nauppanno nakusalo dhammo uppannassa kusalassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo. Ārammaṇe satta, adhipatiyā cha, upanissaye satta.

139. Nauppanno naakusalo dhammo uppannassa akusalassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo. Ārammaṇe cha, adhipatiyā cha, upanissaye cha.

140. Naanupanno naabyākato dhammo uppannassa abyākatassa dhammassa hetupaccayena paccayo…  tīṇi.

141. Nauppanno naabyākato dhammo uppannassa abyākatassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

142. Hetuyā tīṇi, ārammaṇe satta, adhipatiyā cattāri, sahajāte nissaye tīṇi, upanissaye satta…pe…  avigate tīṇi.

===

143. (Atītattikaṃ uppannattikasadisaṃ.)

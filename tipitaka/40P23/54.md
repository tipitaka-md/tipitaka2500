# 54 Sanidassanattika, Maggārammaṇattika

### Sanidassanattika, Maggārammaṇattika

(63.)

198. Nasanidassanasappaṭighaṃ namaggārammaṇaṃ dhammaṃ paccayā anidassanaappaṭigho maggārammaṇo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi…pe…  avigate tīṇi.

199. Nasanidassanasappaṭighaṃ namaggahetukaṃ dhammaṃ paccayā anidassanaappaṭigho maggahetuko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi…pe…  avigate tīṇi.

200. Nasanidassanasappaṭighaṃ namaggādhipatiṃ dhammaṃ paccayā anidassanaappaṭigho maggādhipati dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi…pe…  avigate tīṇi.

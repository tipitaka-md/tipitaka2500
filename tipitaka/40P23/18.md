# 18 Kusalattika, Ajjhattattikadvaya

### Kusalattika, Ajjhattattikadvaya

(23.)

76. Nakusalo naajjhatto dhammo ajjhattassa kusalassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Ārammaṇe aṭṭhārasa, adhipatiyā soḷasa, upanissaye aṭṭhārasa, purejāte atthiyā avigate nava.

77. Nakusalo nabahiddhā dhammo bahiddhā kusalassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Ārammaṇe aṭṭhārasa, adhipatiyā cha, upanissaye aṭṭhārasa, purejāte atthiyā avigate nava.

(24.)

78. Nakusalaṃ naajjhattārammaṇaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca abyākato ajjhattārammaṇo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi…pe…  avigate tīṇi.

79. Nakusalaṃ nabahiddhārammaṇaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca abyākato bahiddhārammaṇo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi…pe…  avigate tīṇi.

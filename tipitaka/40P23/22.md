# 22 Upādinnattika, Kusalattika

### Upādinnattika, Kusalattika

(31.)

100\. Naanupādinnupādāniyaṃ nakusalaṃ dhammaṃ paccayā anupādinnupādāniyo kusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā cha…pe…  avigate cha.

101\. Naanupādinnupādāniyaṃ naakusalaṃ dhammaṃ paccayā anupādinnupādāniyo akusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi…pe…  avigate tīṇi.

102\. Naupādinnupādāniyaṃ naabyākataṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca anupādinnupādāniyo abyākato dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā pañca, adhipatiyā pañca…pe…  avigate pañca.

# 45 Sanidassanattika, Pītittika

### Sanidassanattika, Pītittika

(54.)

171. Nasanidassanasappaṭighaṃ napītisahagataṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca anidassanaappaṭigho pītisahagato dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi…pe…  avigate tīṇi.

172. Nasanidassanasappaṭighaṃ nasukhasahagataṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca anidassanaappaṭigho sukhasahagato dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi…pe…  avigate tīṇi.

173. Nasanidassanasappaṭighaṃ naupekkhāsahagataṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca anidassanaappaṭigho upekkhāsahagato dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi…pe…  avigate tīṇi.

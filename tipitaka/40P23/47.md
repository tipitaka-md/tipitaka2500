# 47 Sanidassanattika, Dassanahetuttika

### Sanidassanattika, Dassanahetuttika

(56.)

177. Nasanidassanasappaṭighaṃ nadassanena pahātabbahetukaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca anidassanaappaṭigho dassanena pahātabbahetuko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi…pe…  avigate tīṇi.

178. Nasanidassanasappaṭighaṃ nabhāvanāya pahātabbahetukaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca anidassanaappaṭigho bhāvanāya pahātabbahetuko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi…pe…  avigate tīṇi.

179. Nasanidassanasappaṭighaṃ nanevadassanena nabhāvanāya pahātabbahetukaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca sanidassanasappaṭigho nevadassanena nabhāvanāya pahātabbahetuko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā ekavīsa, ārammaṇe tīṇi…pe…  avigate ekavīsa.

# 9 Kusalattika, Ācayagāmittika

* Kusalattika, Ācayagāmittika

(13.)

48\. Nakusalaṃ naācayagāmiṃ dhammaṃ paccayā kusalo ācayagāmī dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā cha…pe…  avigate cha.

49\. Nakusalaṃ naapacayagāmiṃ dhammaṃ paccayā kusalo apacayagāmī dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi…pe…  avigate tīṇi.

50\. Nakusalaṃ nanevācayagāmināpacayagāmiṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca abyākato nevācayagāmināpacayagāmī dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā pañca, adhipatiyā pañca…pe…  avigate pañca.

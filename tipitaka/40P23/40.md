# 40 Sanidassanattika, Vedanāttika

### Sanidassanattika, Vedanāttika

(49.)

156. Nasanidassanasappaṭighaṃ nasukhāya vedanāya sampayuttaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca anidassanaappaṭigho sukhāya vedanāya sampayutto dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi…pe…  avigate tīṇi.

157. Nasanidassanasappaṭighaṃ nadukkhāya vedanāya sampayuttaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca anidassanaappaṭigho dukkhāya vedanāya sampayutto dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi…pe…  avigate tīṇi.

158. Nasanidassanasappaṭighaṃ naadukkhamasukhāya vedanāya sampayuttaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca anidassanaappaṭigho adukkhamasukhāya vedanāya sampayutto dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi…pe…  avigate tīṇi.

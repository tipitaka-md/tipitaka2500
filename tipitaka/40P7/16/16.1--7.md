# 16.1--7 Paṭiccādivāra

### Maggārammaṇattika

### Paṭiccādivāra

(29.)

61. Namaggārammaṇaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca…pe…  namaggahetukaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca…pe…  namaggādhipatiṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca… . (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

62. Hetuyā ekūnapaññāsa…pe…  avigate ekūnapaññāsa.

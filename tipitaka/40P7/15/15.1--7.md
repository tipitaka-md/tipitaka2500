# 15.1--7 Paṭiccādivāra

### Micchattattika

### Paṭiccādivāra

(28.)

59. Namicchattaniyataṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca…pe…  nasammattaniyataṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca…pe…  naaniyataṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca… . (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

60. Hetuyā ekūnatiṃsa, ārammaṇe catuvīsa…pe…  vipāke nava…pe…  avigate ekūnatiṃsa.

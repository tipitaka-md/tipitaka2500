# 50 Saṃkilesikaduka

### Saṃkilesikaduka

(69.)

214. Nasaṃkilesikaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nasaṃkilesiko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Nasaṃkilesikaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca naasaṃkilesiko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Nasaṃkilesikaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nasaṃkilesiko ca naasaṃkilesiko ca dhammā uppajjanti hetupaccayā. (3)

215. Naasaṃkilesikaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca naasaṃkilesiko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (1)

216. Nasaṃkilesikañca naasaṃkilesikañca dhammaṃ paṭicca naasaṃkilesiko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (1) (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

217. Hetuyā pañca, ārammaṇe dve…pe…  avigate pañca. (Sabbattha vitthāro.)

# 39 Cittasamuṭṭhānaduka

### Cittasamuṭṭhānaduka

(58.)

184. Nacittasamuṭṭhānaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nacittasamuṭṭhāno dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā… . (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

185. Hetuyā nava…pe…  adhipatiyā pañca…pe…  purejāte āsevane pañca…pe…  avigate nava.

(61.)

190. Nacittasaṃsaṭṭhasamuṭṭhānaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nacittasaṃsaṭṭhasamuṭṭhāno dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā… . (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

191. Hetuyā nava…pe…  avigate nava.

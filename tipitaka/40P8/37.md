# 37 Cittasampayuttaduka

### Cittasampayuttaduka

(56.)

179. Nacittasampayuttaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nacittasampayutto dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā—  cittaṃ paṭicca cittasamuṭṭhānaṃ rūpaṃ. Nacittasampayuttaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nacittavippayutto dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā—  cittaṃ paṭicca cittasampayuttakā khandhā.

180. (Paccanīyadukamattāni na vattabbaṃ dhammaṃ pūretuṃ nava nava pañhāni karotu.)

181. Hetuyā nava, ārammaṇe tīṇi, adhipatiyā pañca…pe…  aññamaññe cha…pe…  purejāte āsevane ekaṃ…pe…  avigate nava.

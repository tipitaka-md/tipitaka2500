# 11.3.1 Chetvāsutta

* Sakkasaṃyutta

* Tatiyavagga

* Chetvāsutta

(267.)

1683\. Sāvatthiyaṃ jetavane. Atha kho sakko devānamindo yena bhagavā tenupasaṅkami; upasaṅkamitvā bhagavantaṃ abhivādetvā ekamantaṃ aṭṭhāsi. Ekamantaṃ ṭhito kho sakko devānamindo bhagavantaṃ gāthāya ajjhabhāsi—

1684\. _“Kiṃsu chetvā sukhaṃ seti,_  
_kiṃsu chetvā na socati;_  
_Kissassu ekadhammassa,_  
_vadhaṃ rocesi gotamā”ti._  


1685\. _“Kodhaṃ chetvā sukhaṃ seti,_  
_kodhaṃ chetvā na socati;_  
_Kodhassa visamūlassa,_  
_madhuraggassa vāsava;_  
_Vadhaṃ ariyā pasaṃsanti,_  
_tañhi chetvā na socatī”ti._

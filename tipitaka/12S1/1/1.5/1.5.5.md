# 1.5.5 Anomasutta

### Devatāsaṃyutta

### Ādittavagga

### Anomasutta

(45.)

250. _“Anomanāmaṃ nipuṇatthadassiṃ,_  
_Paññādadaṃ kāmālaye asattaṃ;_  
_Taṃ passatha sabbaviduṃ sumedhaṃ,_  
_Ariye pathe kamamānaṃ mahesin”ti._

# 1.6.5 Paṭhamajanasutta

### Devatāsaṃyutta

### Jarāvagga

### Paṭhamajanasutta

(55.)

296. _“Kiṃsu janeti purisaṃ,_  
_kiṃsu tassa vidhāvati;_  
_Kiṃsu saṃsāramāpādi,_  
_kiṃsu tassa mahabbhayan”ti._  


297. _“Taṇhā janeti purisaṃ,_  
_cittamassa vidhāvati;_  
_Satto saṃsāramāpādi,_  
_dukkhamassa mahabbhayan”ti._

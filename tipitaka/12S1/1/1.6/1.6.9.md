# 1.6.9 Dutiyasutta

### Devatāsaṃyutta

### Jarāvagga

### Dutiyasutta

(59.)

304. _“Kiṃsu dutiyā purisassa hoti,_  
_Kiṃsu cenaṃ pasāsati;_  
_Kissa cābhirato macco,_  
_Sabbadukkhā pamuccatī”ti._  


305. _“Saddhā dutiyā purisassa hoti,_  
_Paññā cenaṃ pasāsati;_  
_Nibbānābhirato macco,_  
_Sabbadukkhā pamuccatī”ti._

# 1.6.6 Dutiyajanasutta

* Devatāsaṃyutta

* Jarāvagga

* Dutiyajanasutta

(56.)

298\. _“Kiṃsu janeti purisaṃ,_  
_kiṃsu tassa vidhāvati;_  
_Kiṃsu saṃsāramāpādi,_  
_kismā na parimuccatī”ti._  


299\. _“Taṇhā janeti purisaṃ,_  
_cittamassa vidhāvati;_  
_Satto saṃsāramāpādi,_  
_dukkhā na parimuccatī”ti._

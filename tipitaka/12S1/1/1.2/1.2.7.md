# 1.2.7 Dukkarasutta

### Devatāsaṃyutta

### Nandanavagga

### Dukkarasutta

(17.)

55. _“Dukkaraṃ duttitikkhañca,_  
_abyattena ca sāmaññaṃ;_  
_Bahūhi tattha sambādhā,_  
_yattha bālo visīdatī”ti._  


56. _“Katihaṃ careyya sāmaññaṃ,_  
_cittañce na nivāraye;_  
_Pade pade visīdeyya,_  
_saṅkappānaṃ vasānugoti._  


57. _Kummova aṅgāni sake kapāle,_  
_Samodahaṃ bhikkhu manovitakke;_  
_Anissito aññamaheṭhayāno,_  
_Parinibbuto nūpavadeyya kañcī”ti._

# 1.2.5 Saṇamānasutta

### Devatāsaṃyutta

### Nandanavagga

### Saṇamānasutta

(15.)

51. _“Ṭhite majjhanhike kāle,_  
_sannisīvesu pakkhisu;_  
_Saṇateva brahāraññaṃ,_  
_taṃ bhayaṃ paṭibhāti man”ti._  


52. _“Ṭhite majjhanhike kāle,_  
_sannisīvesu pakkhisu;_  
_Saṇateva brahāraññaṃ,_  
_sā rati paṭibhāti man”ti._

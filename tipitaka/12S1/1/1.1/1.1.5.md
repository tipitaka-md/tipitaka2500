# 1.1.5 Katichindasutta

### Devatāsaṃyutta

### Naḷavagga

### Katichindasutta

(5.)

16\. Sāvatthinidānaṃ. Ekamantaṃ ṭhitā kho sā devatā bhagavato santike imaṃ gāthaṃ abhāsi—

17\. _“Kati chinde kati jahe,_  
_kati cuttari bhāvaye;_  
_Kati saṅgātigo bhikkhu,_  
_oghatiṇṇoti vuccatī”ti._  


18\. _“Pañca chinde pañca jahe,_  
_pañca cuttari bhāvaye;_  
_Pañca saṅgātigo bhikkhu,_  
_oghatiṇṇoti vuccatī”ti._

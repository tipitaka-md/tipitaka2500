# 1.1.4 Accentisutta

### Devatāsaṃyutta

### Naḷavagga

### Accentisutta

(4.)

13. Sāvatthinidānaṃ. Ekamantaṃ ṭhitā kho sā devatā bhagavato santike imaṃ gāthaṃ abhāsi—

14. _“Accenti kālā tarayanti rattiyo,_  
_Vayoguṇā anupubbaṃ jahanti;_  
_Etaṃ bhayaṃ maraṇe pekkhamāno,_  
_Puññāni kayirātha sukhāvahānī”ti._  


15. _“Accenti kālā tarayanti rattiyo,_  
_Vayoguṇā anupubbaṃ jahanti;_  
_Etaṃ bhayaṃ maraṇe pekkhamāno,_  
_Lokāmisaṃ pajahe santipekkho”ti._

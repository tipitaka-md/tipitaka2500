# 1.7.8 Pihitasutta

* Devatāsaṃyutta

* Addhavagga

* Pihitasutta

(68.)

325\. _“Kenassu pihito loko,_  
_kismiṃ loko patiṭṭhito;_  
_Kenassu uḍḍito loko,_  
_kenassu parivārito”ti._  


326\. _“Maccunā pihito loko,_  
_dukkhe loko patiṭṭhito;_  
_Taṇhāya uḍḍito loko,_  
_jarāya parivārito”ti._

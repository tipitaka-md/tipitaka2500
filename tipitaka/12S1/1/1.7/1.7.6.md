# 1.7.6 Attahatasutta

### Devatāsaṃyutta

### Addhavagga

### Attahatasutta

(66.)

321. _“Kenassubbhāhato loko,_  
_kenassu parivārito;_  
_Kena sallena otiṇṇo,_  
_kissa dhūpāyito sadā”ti._  


322. _“Maccunābbhāhato loko,_  
_jarāya parivārito;_  
_Taṇhāsallena otiṇṇo,_  
_icchādhūpāyito sadā”ti._

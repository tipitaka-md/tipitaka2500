# 1.7.1 Nāmasutta

* Devatāsaṃyutta

* Addhavagga

* Nāmasutta

(61.)

311\. _“Kiṃsu sabbaṃ addhabhavi,_  
_kismā bhiyyo na vijjati;_  
_Kissassu ekadhammassa,_  
_sabbeva vasamanvagū”ti._  


312\. _“Nāmaṃ sabbaṃ addhabhavi,_  
_nāmā bhiyyo na vijjati;_  
_Nāmassa ekadhammassa,_  
_sabbeva vasamanvagū”ti._

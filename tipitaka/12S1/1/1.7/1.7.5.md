# 1.7.5 Bandhanasutta

### Devatāsaṃyutta

### Addhavagga

### Bandhanasutta

(65.)

319. _“Kiṃsu sambandhano loko,_  
_kiṃsu tassa vicāraṇaṃ;_  
_Kissassu vippahānena,_  
_sabbaṃ chindati bandhanan”ti._  


320. _“Nandīsambandhano loko,_  
_vitakkassa vicāraṇaṃ;_  
_Taṇhāya vippahānena,_  
_sabbaṃ chindati bandhanan”ti._

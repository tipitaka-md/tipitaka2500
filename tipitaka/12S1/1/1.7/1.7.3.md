# 1.7.3 Taṇhāsutta

### Devatāsaṃyutta

### Addhavagga

### Taṇhāsutta

(63.)

315. _“Kenassu nīyati loko,_  
_kenassu parikassati;_  
_Kissassu ekadhammassa,_  
_sabbeva vasamanvagū”ti._  


316. _“Taṇhāya nīyati loko,_  
_taṇhāya parikassati;_  
_Taṇhāya ekadhammassa,_  
_sabbeva vasamanvagū”ti._

# 1.7.9 Icchāsutta

### Devatāsaṃyutta

### Addhavagga

### Icchāsutta

(69.)

327. _“Kenassu bajjhatī loko,_  
_kissa vinayāya muccati;_  
_Kissassu vippahānena,_  
_sabbaṃ chindati bandhanan”ti._  


328. _“Icchāya bajjhatī loko,_  
_icchāvinayāya muccati;_  
_Icchāya vippahānena,_  
_sabbaṃ chindati bandhanan”ti._

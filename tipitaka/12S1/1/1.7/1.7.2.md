# 1.7.2 Cittasutta

### Devatāsaṃyutta

### Addhavagga

### Cittasutta

(62.)

313. _“Kenassu nīyati loko,_  
_kenassu parikassati;_  
_Kissassu ekadhammassa,_  
_sabbeva vasamanvagū”ti._  


314. _“Cittena nīyati loko,_  
_cittena parikassati;_  
_Cittassa ekadhammassa,_  
_sabbeva vasamanvagū”ti._

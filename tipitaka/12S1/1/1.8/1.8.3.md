# 1.8.3 Vittasutta

* Devatāsaṃyutta

* Chetvāvagga

* Vittasutta

(73.)

339\. _“Kiṃsūdha vittaṃ purisassa seṭṭhaṃ,_  
_Kiṃsu suciṇṇo sukhamāvahati;_  
_Kiṃsu have sādutaraṃ rasānaṃ,_  
_Kathaṃjīviṃ jīvitamāhu seṭṭhan”ti._  


340\. _“Saddhīdha vittaṃ purisassa seṭṭhaṃ,_  
_Dhammo suciṇṇo sukhamāvahati;_  
_Saccaṃ have sādutaraṃ rasānaṃ,_  
_Paññājīviṃ jīvitamāhu seṭṭhan”ti._

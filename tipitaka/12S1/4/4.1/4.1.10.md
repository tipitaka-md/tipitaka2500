# 4.1.10 Dutiyaāyusutta

* Mārasaṃyutta

* Paṭhamavagga

* Dutiyaāyusutta

(146.)

814\. Evaṃ me sutaṃ—  ekaṃ samayaṃ bhagavā rājagahe viharati veḷuvane kalandakanivāpe. Tatra kho bhagavā…pe…  etadavoca—

815\. “Appamidaṃ, bhikkhave, manussānaṃ āyu. Gamanīyo samparāyo, kattabbaṃ kusalaṃ, caritabbaṃ brahmacariyaṃ. Natthi jātassa amaraṇaṃ. Yo, bhikkhave, ciraṃ jīvati, so vassasataṃ appaṃ vā bhiyyo”ti.

816\. Atha kho māro pāpimā yena bhagavā tenupasaṅkami; upasaṅkamitvā bhagavantaṃ gāthāya ajjhabhāsi—

817\. _“Nāccayanti ahorattā,_  
_Jīvitaṃ nūparujjhati;_  
_Āyu anupariyāyati maccānaṃ,_  
_Nemīva rathakubbaran”ti._  


818\. _“Accayanti ahorattā,_  
_jīvitaṃ uparujjhati;_  
_Āyu khīyati maccānaṃ,_  
_kunnadīnaṃva odakan”ti._  


819\. Atha kho māro pāpimā “jānāti maṃ bhagavā, jānāti maṃ sugato”ti dukkhī dummano tatthevantaradhāyīti.

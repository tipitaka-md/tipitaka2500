# 4.1.9 Paṭhamaāyusutta

### Mārasaṃyutta

### Paṭhamavagga

### Paṭhamaāyusutta

(145.)

808. Evaṃ me sutaṃ—  ekaṃ samayaṃ bhagavā rājagahe viharati veḷuvane kalandakanivāpe. Tatra kho bhagavā bhikkhū āmantesi—  “bhikkhavo”ti. “Bhadante”ti te bhikkhū bhagavato paccassosuṃ. Bhagavā etadavoca—

809. “Appamidaṃ, bhikkhave, manussānaṃ āyu. Gamanīyo samparāyo, kattabbaṃ kusalaṃ, caritabbaṃ brahmacariyaṃ. Natthi jātassa amaraṇaṃ. Yo, bhikkhave, ciraṃ jīvati, so vassasataṃ appaṃ vā bhiyyo”ti.

810. Atha kho māro pāpimā yena bhagavā tenupasaṅkami; upasaṅkamitvā bhagavantaṃ gāthāya ajjhabhāsi—

811. _“Dīghamāyu manussānaṃ,_  
_na naṃ hīḷe suporiso;_  
_Careyya khīramattova,_  
_natthi maccussa āgamo”ti._  


812. _“Appamāyu manussānaṃ,_  
_hīḷeyya naṃ suporiso;_  
_Careyyādittasīsova,_  
_natthi maccussa nāgamo”ti._  


813. Atha kho māro…pe…  tatthevantaradhāyīti.

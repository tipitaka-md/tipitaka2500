# 2.3.7 Nandasutta

### Devaputtasaṃyutta

### Nānātitthiyavagga

### Nandasutta

(108.)

535. Ekamantaṃ ṭhito kho nando devaputto bhagavato santike imaṃ gāthaṃ abhāsi—

536. _“Accenti kālā tarayanti rattiyo,_  
_Vayoguṇā anupubbaṃ jahanti;_  
_Etaṃ bhayaṃ maraṇe pekkhamāno,_  
_Puññāni kayirātha sukhāvahānī”ti._  


537. _“Accenti kālā tarayanti rattiyo,_  
_Vayoguṇā anupubbaṃ jahanti;_  
_Etaṃ bhayaṃ maraṇe pekkhamāno,_  
_Lokāmisaṃ pajahe santipekkho”ti._

# 2.1.3 Māghasutta

* Devaputtasaṃyutta

* Paṭhamavagga

* Māghasutta

(84.)

376\. Sāvatthinidānaṃ. Atha kho māgho devaputto abhikkantāya rattiyā abhikkantavaṇṇo kevalakappaṃ jetavanaṃ obhāsetvā yena bhagavā tenupasaṅkami; upasaṅkamitvā bhagavantaṃ abhivādetvā ekamantaṃ aṭṭhāsi. Ekamantaṃ ṭhito kho māgho devaputto bhagavantaṃ gāthāya ajjhabhāsi—

377\. _“Kiṃsu chetvā sukhaṃ seti,_  
_kiṃsu chetvā na socati;_  
_Kissassu ekadhammassa,_  
_vadhaṃ rocesi gotamā”ti._  


378\. _“Kodhaṃ chetvā sukhaṃ seti,_  
_kodhaṃ chetvā na socati;_  
_Kodhassa visamūlassa,_  
_madhuraggassa vatrabhū;_  
_Vadhaṃ ariyā pasaṃsanti,_  
_tañhi chetvā na socatī”ti._

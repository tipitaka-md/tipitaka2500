# 1.5.9 Sarīraṭṭhadhammasutta

### Paṭhamapaṇṇāsaka

### Akkosavagga

### Sarīraṭṭhadhammasutta

(49.)

349. “Dasayime, bhikkhave, dhammā sarīraṭṭhā. Katame dasa? Sītaṃ, uṇhaṃ, jighacchā, pipāsā, uccāro, passāvo, kāyasaṃvaro, vacīsaṃvaro, ājīvasaṃvaro, ponobhaviko bhavasaṅkhāro—  ime kho, bhikkhave, dasa dhammā sarīraṭṭhā”ti.

===

350. Navamaṃ.

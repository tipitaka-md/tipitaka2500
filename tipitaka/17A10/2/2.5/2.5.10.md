# 2.5.10 Abhabbasutta

### Dutiyapaṇṇāsaka

### Upālivagga

### Abhabbasutta

(100.)

918. “Dasayime, bhikkhave, dhamme appahāya abhabbo arahattaṃ sacchikātuṃ. Katame dasa? Rāgaṃ, dosaṃ, mohaṃ, kodhaṃ, upanāhaṃ, makkhaṃ, paḷāsaṃ, issaṃ, macchariyaṃ, mānaṃ—  ime kho, bhikkhave, dasa dhamme appahāya abhabbo arahattaṃ sacchikātuṃ.

919. Dasayime, bhikkhave, dhamme pahāya bhabbo arahattaṃ sacchikātuṃ. Katame dasa? Rāgaṃ, dosaṃ, mohaṃ, kodhaṃ, upanāhaṃ, makkhaṃ, paḷāsaṃ, issaṃ, macchariyaṃ, mānaṃ—  ime kho, bhikkhave, dasa dhamme pahāya bhabbo arahattaṃ sacchikātun”ti.

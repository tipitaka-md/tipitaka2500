# 4.3.3 Kusalasutta

### Catutthapaṇṇāsaka

### Sādhuvagga

### Kusalasutta

(180.)

1327. “Kusalañca vo, bhikkhave, desessāmi akusalañca. Taṃ suṇātha…pe…  katamañca, bhikkhave, akusalaṃ? Pāṇātipāto…pe…  micchādiṭṭhi—  idaṃ vuccati, bhikkhave, akusalaṃ.

1328. Katamañca, bhikkhave, kusalaṃ? Pāṇātipātā veramaṇī…pe…  sammādiṭṭhi—  idaṃ vuccati, bhikkhave, kusalan”ti.

===

1329. Tatiyaṃ.

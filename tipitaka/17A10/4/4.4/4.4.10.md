# 4.4.10 Sacchikātabbasutta

* Catutthapaṇṇāsaka

* Ariyamaggavagga

* Sacchikātabbasutta

(198.)

1382\. “Sacchikātabbañca vo, bhikkhave, dhammaṃ desessāmi na sacchikātabbañca. Taṃ suṇātha…pe…  katamo ca, bhikkhave, na sacchikātabbo dhammo? Pāṇātipāto…pe…  micchādiṭṭhi—  ayaṃ vuccati, bhikkhave, na sacchikātabbo dhammo.

1383\. Katamo ca, bhikkhave, sacchikātabbo dhammo? Pāṇātipātā veramaṇī…pe…  sammādiṭṭhi—  ayaṃ vuccati, bhikkhave, sacchikātabbo dhammo”ti.

---

1384\. Dasamaṃ.



---

1385\. Ariyamaggavaggo catuttho.

# 4.4.5 Uppādetabbadhammasutta

### Catutthapaṇṇāsaka

### Ariyamaggavagga

### Uppādetabbadhammasutta

(193.)

1367\. “Uppādetabbañca vo, bhikkhave, dhammaṃ desessāmi na uppādetabbañca. Taṃ suṇātha…pe…  katamo ca, bhikkhave, na uppādetabbo dhammo? Pāṇātipāto…pe…  micchādiṭṭhi—  ayaṃ vuccati, bhikkhave, na uppādetabbo dhammo.

1368\. Katamo ca, bhikkhave, uppādetabbo dhammo? Pāṇātipātā veramaṇī…pe…  sammādiṭṭhi—  ayaṃ vuccati, bhikkhave, uppādetabbo dhammo”ti.

---

1369\. Pañcamaṃ.

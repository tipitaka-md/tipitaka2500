# 4.4.4 Sappurisadhammasutta

### Catutthapaṇṇāsaka

### Ariyamaggavagga

### Sappurisadhammasutta

(192.)

1364\. “Sappurisadhammañca vo, bhikkhave, desessāmi asappurisadhammañca. Taṃ suṇātha…pe…  katamo ca, bhikkhave, asappurisadhammo? Pāṇātipāto…pe…  micchādiṭṭhi—  ayaṃ vuccati, bhikkhave, asappurisadhammo.

1365\. Katamo ca, bhikkhave, sappurisadhammo? Pāṇātipātā veramaṇī…pe…  sammādiṭṭhi—  ayaṃ vuccati, bhikkhave, sappurisadhammo”ti.

---

1366\. Catutthaṃ.

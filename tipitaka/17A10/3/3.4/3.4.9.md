# 3.4.9 Ācayagāmisutta

### Tatiyapaṇṇāsaka

### Sādhuvagga

### Ācayagāmisutta

(142.)

1122. “Ācayagāmiñca vo, bhikkhave, dhammaṃ desessāmi apacayagāmiñca. Taṃ suṇātha…pe…  katamo ca, bhikkhave, ācayagāmī dhammo? Micchādiṭṭhi…pe…  micchāvimutti—  ayaṃ vuccati, bhikkhave, ācayagāmī dhammo. Katamo ca, bhikkhave, apacayagāmī dhammo? Sammādiṭṭhi…pe…  sammāvimutti—  ayaṃ vuccati, bhikkhave, apacayagāmī dhammo”ti.

---

1123. Navamaṃ.

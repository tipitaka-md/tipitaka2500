# 11 Vedanāttika, Vipākattika

* Vedanāttika, Vipākattika

(43.)

103\. Nasukhāya vedanāya sampayuttaṃ navipākaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nasukhāya vedanāya sampayutto navipāko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

# 1.4.11--170

### Paṭhamapaṇṇāsaka

### Rāgapeyyāla

(513--672.)

228. “Dosassa…pe…  mohassa…  kodhassa…  upanāhassa…  makkhassa…  paḷāsassa…  issāya…  macchariyassa…  māyāya…  sāṭheyyassa…  thambhassa…  sārambhassa…  mānassa…  atimānassa…  madassa…  pamādassa abhiññāya…pe…  pariññāya…  parikkhayāya…  pahānāya…  khayāya…  vayāya…  virāgāya…  nirodhāya…  cāgāya…  paṭinissaggāya ime ekādasa dhammā bhāvetabbā”ti.

229. Idamavoca bhagavā. Attamanā te bhikkhū bhagavato bhāsitaṃ abhinandunti.

===

230. Rāgapeyyālaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.



231. _Nava suttasahassāni,_  
_bhiyyo pañcasatāni ca;_  
_Sattapaññāsa suttantā,_  
_aṅguttarasamāyutāti._  


===

232. Ekādasakanipātapāḷi niṭṭhitā.



===

233. Aṅguttaranikāyo samatto.

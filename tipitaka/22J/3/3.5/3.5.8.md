# 3.5.8 Udumbarajātaka

### Tikanipāta

### Kumbhavagga

### Udumbarajātaka

(142.)

866. _“Udumbarā cime pakkā,_  
_nigrodhā ca kapitthanā;_  
_Ehi nikkhama bhuñjassu,_  
_kiṃ jighacchāya miyyasi”._  


(143.)

867. _“Evaṃ so suhito hoti,_  
_yo vuḍḍhamapacāyati;_  
_Yathāhamajja suhito,_  
_dumapakkāni māsito”._  


(144.)

868. _“Yaṃ vanejo vanejassa,_  
_vañceyya kapino kapi;_  
_Daharo kapi saddheyya,_  
_na hi jiṇṇo jarākapī”ti._  


===

869. Udumbarajātakaṃ aṭṭhamaṃ.

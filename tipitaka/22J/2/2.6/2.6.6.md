# 2.6.6 Kuruṅgamigajātaka

* Dukanipāta

* Nataṃdaḷhavagga

* Kuruṅgamigajātaka

(111.)

521\. _“Iṅgha vaṭṭamayaṃ pāsaṃ,_  
_chinda dantehi kacchapa;_  
_Ahaṃ tathā karissāmi,_  
_yathā nehiti luddako”._  


(112.)

522\. _“Kacchapo pāvisī vāriṃ,_  
_kuruṅgo pāvisī vanaṃ;_  
_Satapatto dumaggamhā,_  
_dūre putte apānayī”ti._  


---

523\. Kuruṅgamigajātakaṃ chaṭṭhaṃ.

# 2.6.7 Assakajātaka

### Dukanipāta

### Nataṃdaḷhavagga

### Assakajātaka

(113.)

524. _“Ayamassakarājena,_  
_deso vicarito mayā;_  
_Anukāmayakāmena,_  
_piyena patinā saha._  


(114.)

525. _Navena sukhadukkhena,_  
_porāṇaṃ apidhīyati;_  
_Tasmā assakaraññāva,_  
_kīṭo piyataro mamā”ti._  


===

526. Assakajātakaṃ sattamaṃ.

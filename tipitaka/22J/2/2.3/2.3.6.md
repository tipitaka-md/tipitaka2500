# 2.3.6 Kaḷāyamuṭṭhijātaka

### Dukanipāta

### Kalyāṇavagga

### Kaḷāyamuṭṭhijātaka

(51.)

421\. _“Bālo vatāyaṃ dumasākhagocaro,_  
_Paññā janinda nayimassa vijjati;_  
_Kaḷāyamuṭṭhiṃ avakiriya kevalaṃ,_  
_Ekaṃ kaḷāyaṃ patitaṃ gavesati”._  


(52.)

422\. _“Evameva mayaṃ rāja,_  
_ye caññe atilobhino;_  
_Appena bahuṃ jiyyāma,_  
_kaḷāyeneva vānaro”ti._  


---

423\. Kaḷāyamuṭṭhijātakaṃ chaṭṭhaṃ.

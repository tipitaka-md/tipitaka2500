# 2.8.3 Puṭabhattajātaka

* Dukanipāta

* Kāsāvavagga

* Puṭabhattajātaka

(145.)

576\. _“Name namantassa bhaje bhajantaṃ,_  
_Kiccānukubbassa kareyya kiccaṃ;_  
_Nānatthakāmassa kareyya atthaṃ,_  
_Asambhajantampi na sambhajeyya._  


(146.)

577\. _Caje cajantaṃ vanathaṃ na kayirā,_  
_Apetacittena na sambhajeyya;_  
_Dijo dumaṃ khīṇaphalanti ñatvā,_  
_Aññaṃ samekkheyya mahā hi loko”ti._  


---

578\. Puṭabhattajātakaṃ tatiyaṃ.

# 2.9.2 Vīṇāguṇajātaka

### Dukanipāta

### Upāhanavagga

### Vīṇāguṇajātaka

(163.)

605. _“Ekacintito yamattho,_  
_bālo apariṇāyako;_  
_Na hi khujjena vāmena,_  
_bhoti saṅgantumarahasi”._  


(164.)

606. _“Purisūsabhaṃ maññamānā,_  
_ahaṃ khujjamakāmayiṃ;_  
_Soyaṃ saṅkuṭito seti,_  
_chinnatanti yathā viṇā”ti._  


===

607. Vīṇāguṇajātakaṃ dutiyaṃ.

# 2.2.9 Arakajātaka

### Dukanipāta

### Santhavavagga

### Arakajātaka

(37.)

398. _“Yo ve mettena cittena,_  
_sabbalokānukampati;_  
_Uddhaṃ adho ca tiriyaṃ,_  
_appamāṇena sabbaso._  


(38.)

399. _Appamāṇaṃ hitaṃ cittaṃ,_  
_paripuṇṇaṃ subhāvitaṃ;_  
_Yaṃ pamāṇakataṃ kammaṃ,_  
_na taṃ tatrāvasissatī”ti._  


===

400. Arakajātakaṃ navamaṃ.

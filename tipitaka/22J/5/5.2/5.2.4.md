# 5.2.4 Khajjopanakajātaka

* Pañcakanipāta

* Vaṇṇārohavagga

* Khajjopanakajātaka

(75.)

1235\. _“Ko nu santamhi pajjote,_  
_aggipariyesanaṃ caraṃ;_  
_Addakkhi ratti khajjotaṃ,_  
_jātavedaṃ amaññatha._  


(76.)

1236\. _Svassa gomayacuṇṇāni,_  
_abhimatthaṃ tiṇāni ca;_  
_Viparītāya saññāya,_  
_nāsakkhi pajjaletave._  


(77.)

1237\. _Evampi anupāyena,_  
_atthaṃ na labhate migo;_  
_Visāṇato gavaṃ dohaṃ,_  
_yattha khīraṃ na vindati._  


(78.)

1238\. _Vividhehi upāyehi,_  
_atthaṃ papponti māṇavā;_  
_Niggahena amittānaṃ,_  
_mittānaṃ paggahena ca._  


(79.)

1239\. _Senāmokkhapalābhena,_  
_vallabhānaṃ nayena ca;_  
_Jagatiṃ jagatipālā,_  
_āvasanti vasundharan”ti._  


---

1240\. Khajjopanakajātakaṃ catutthaṃ.

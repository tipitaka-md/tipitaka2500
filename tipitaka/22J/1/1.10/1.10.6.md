# 1.10.6 Telapattajātaka

* Ekakanipāta

* Littavagga

* Telapattajātaka

(96.)

212\. _“Samatittikaṃ anavasekaṃ,_  
_Telapattaṃ yathā parihareyya;_  
_Evaṃ sacittamanurakkhe,_  
_Patthayāno disaṃ agatapubban”ti._  


---

213\. Telapattajātakaṃ chaṭṭhaṃ.

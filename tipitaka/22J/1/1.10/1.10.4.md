# 1.10.4 Lomahaṃsajātaka

### Ekakanipāta

### Littavagga

### Lomahaṃsajātaka

(94.)

208. _Sotatto sosindo ceva,_  
_eko bhiṃsanake vane;_  
_Naggo na caggimāsīno,_  
_esanāpasuto munī”ti._  


---

209. Lomahaṃsajātakaṃ catutthaṃ.

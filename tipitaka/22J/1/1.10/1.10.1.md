# 1.10.1 Littajātaka

* Ekakanipāta

* Littavagga

* Littajātaka

(91.)

202\. _“Littaṃ paramena tejasā,_  
_Gilamakkhaṃ puriso na bujjhati;_  
_Gila re gila pāpadhuttaka,_  
_Pacchā te kaṭukaṃ bhavissatī”ti._  


---

203\. Littajātakaṃ paṭhamaṃ.

# 1.14.7 Babbujātaka

### Ekakanipāta

### Asampadānavagga

### Babbujātaka

(137.)

303. _“Yattheko labhate babbu,_  
_dutiyo tattha jāyati;_  
_Tatiyo ca catuttho ca,_  
_idaṃ te babbukā bilan”ti._  


===

304. Babbujātakaṃ sattamaṃ.

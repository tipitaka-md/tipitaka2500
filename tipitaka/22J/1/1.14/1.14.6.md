# 1.14.6 Suvaṇṇahaṃsajātaka

* Ekakanipāta

* Asampadānavagga

* Suvaṇṇahaṃsajātaka

(136.)

301\. _“Yaṃ laddhaṃ tena tuṭṭhabbaṃ,_  
_Atilobho hi pāpako;_  
_Haṃsarājaṃ gahetvāna,_  
_Suvaṇṇā parihāyathā”ti._  


---

302\. Suvaṇṇahaṃsajātakaṃ chaṭṭhaṃ.

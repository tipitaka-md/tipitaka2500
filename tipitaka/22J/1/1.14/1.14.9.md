# 1.14.9 Ubhatobhaṭṭhajātaka

### Ekakanipāta

### Asampadānavagga

### Ubhatobhaṭṭhajātaka

(139.)

307. _“Akkhī bhinnā paṭo naṭṭho,_  
_sakhigehe ca bhaṇḍanaṃ;_  
_Ubhato paduṭṭhā kammantā,_  
_udakamhi thalamhi cā”ti._  


===

308. Ubhatobhaṭṭhajātakaṃ navamaṃ.

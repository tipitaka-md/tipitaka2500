# 1.14.5 Candābhajātaka

### Ekakanipāta

### Asampadānavagga

### Candābhajātaka

(135.)

299. _“Candābhaṃ sūriyābhañca,_  
_yodha paññāya gādha”ti._  
_Avitakkena jhānena,_  
_hoti ābhassarūpagoti._  


===

300. Candābhajātakaṃ pañcamaṃ.

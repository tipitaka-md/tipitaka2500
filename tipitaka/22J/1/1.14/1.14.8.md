# 1.14.8 Godhajātaka

* Ekakanipāta

* Asampadānavagga

* Godhajātaka

(138.)

305\. _“Kiṃ te jaṭāhi dummedha,_  
_kiṃ te ajinasāṭiyā;_  
_Abbhantaraṃ te gahanaṃ,_  
_bāhiraṃ parimajjasī”ti._  


---

306\. Godhajātakaṃ aṭṭhamaṃ.

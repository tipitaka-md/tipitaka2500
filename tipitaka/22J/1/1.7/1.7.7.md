# 1.7.7 Ucchaṅgajātaka

### Ekakanipāta

### Itthivagga

### Ucchaṅgajātaka

(67.)

147. _“Ucchaṅge deva me putto,_  
_Pathe dhāvantiyā pati;_  
_Tañca desaṃ na passāmi,_  
_Yato sodariyamānayeti._  


===

148. Ucchaṅgajātakaṃ sattamaṃ.

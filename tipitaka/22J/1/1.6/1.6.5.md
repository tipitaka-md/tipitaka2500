# 1.6.5 Pañcāvudhajātaka

* Ekakanipāta

* Āsīsavagga

* Pañcāvudhajātaka

(55.)

121\. _“Yo alīnena cittena,_  
_alīnamanaso naro;_  
_Bhāveti kusalaṃ dhammaṃ,_  
_yogakkhemassa pattiyā;_  
_Pāpuṇe anupubbena,_  
_sabbasaṃyojanakkhayan”ti._  


---

122\. Pañcāvudhajātakaṃ pañcamaṃ.

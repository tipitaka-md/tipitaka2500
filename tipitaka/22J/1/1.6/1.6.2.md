# 1.6.2 Cūḷajanakajātaka

* Ekakanipāta

* Āsīsavagga

* Cūḷajanakajātaka

(52.)

115\. _“Vāyametheva puriso,_  
_na nibbindeyya paṇḍito;_  
_Passāmi vohaṃ attānaṃ,_  
_udakā thalamubbhatan”ti._  


---

116\. Cūḷajanakajātakaṃ dutiyaṃ.

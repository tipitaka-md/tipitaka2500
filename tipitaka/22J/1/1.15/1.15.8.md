# 1.15.8 Siṅgālajātaka

* Ekakanipāta

* Kakaṇṭakavagga

* Siṅgālajātaka

(148.)

327\. _“Nāhaṃ punaṃ na ca punaṃ,_  
_na cāpi apunappunaṃ;_  
_Hatthibondiṃ pavekkhāmi,_  
_tathā hi bhayatajjito”ti._  


---

328\. Siṅgālajātakaṃ aṭṭhamaṃ.

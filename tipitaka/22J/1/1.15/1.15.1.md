# 1.15.1 Godhajātaka

### Ekakanipāta

### Kakaṇṭakavagga

### Godhajātaka

(141.)

313. _“Na pāpajanasaṃsevī,_  
_accantasukhamedhati;_  
_Godhākulaṃ kakaṇṭāva,_  
_kaliṃ pāpeti attanan”ti._  


---

314. Godhajātakaṃ paṭhamaṃ.

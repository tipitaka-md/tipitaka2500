# 1.15.4 Naṅguṭṭhajātaka

### Ekakanipāta

### Kakaṇṭakavagga

### Naṅguṭṭhajātaka

(144.)

319\. _“Bahumpetaṃ asabbhi jātaveda,_  
_Yaṃ taṃ vāladhinābhipūjayāma;_  
_Maṃsārahassa natthajja maṃsaṃ,_  
_Naṅguṭṭhampi bhavaṃ paṭiggahātū”ti._  


---

320\. Naṅguṭṭhajātakaṃ catutthaṃ.

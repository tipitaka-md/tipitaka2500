# 1.15.7 Puppharattajātaka

### Ekakanipāta

### Kakaṇṭakavagga

### Puppharattajātaka

(147.)

325. _“Nayidaṃ dukkhaṃ aduṃ dukkhaṃ,_  
_Yaṃ maṃ tudati vāyaso;_  
_Yaṃ sāmā puppharattena,_  
_Kattikaṃ nānubhossatī”ti._  


===

326. Puppharattajātakaṃ sattamaṃ.

# 1.15.9 Ekapaṇṇajātaka

### Ekakanipāta

### Kakaṇṭakavagga

### Ekapaṇṇajātaka

(149.)

329\. _“Ekapaṇṇo ayaṃ rukkho,_  
_na bhūmyā caturaṅgulo;_  
_Phalena visakappena,_  
_mahāyaṃ kiṃ bhavissatī”ti._  


---

330\. Ekapaṇṇajātakaṃ navamaṃ.

# 1.2.1 Lakkhaṇamigajātaka

### Ekakanipāta

### Sīlavagga

### Lakkhaṇamigajātaka

(11.)

24\. _“Hoti sīlavataṃ attho,_  
_paṭisanthāravuttinaṃ;_  
_Lakkhaṇaṃ passa āyantaṃ,_  
_ñātisaṅghapurakkhataṃ;_  
_Atha passasimaṃ kāḷaṃ,_  
_suvihīnaṃva ñātibhī”ti._  


---

25\. Lakkhaṇamigajātakaṃ paṭhamaṃ.

# 1.11.5 Dubbalakaṭṭhajātaka

### Ekakanipāta

### Parosatavagga

### Dubbalakaṭṭhajātaka

(105.)

233. _“Bahumpetaṃ vane kaṭṭhaṃ,_  
_vāto bhañjati dubbalaṃ;_  
_Tassa ce bhāyasī nāga,_  
_kiso nūna bhavissasī”ti._  


---

234. Dubbalakaṭṭhajātakaṃ pañcamaṃ.

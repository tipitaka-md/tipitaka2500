# 1.3.1 Kuruṅgamigajātaka

### Ekakanipāta

### Kuruṅgavagga

### Kuruṅgamigajātaka

(21.)

46\. _“Ñātametaṃ kuruṅgassa,_  
_yaṃ tvaṃ sepaṇṇi siyyasi;_  
_Aññaṃ sepaṇṇi gacchāmi,_  
_na me te ruccate phalan”ti._  


---

47\. Kuruṅgamigajātakaṃ paṭhamaṃ.

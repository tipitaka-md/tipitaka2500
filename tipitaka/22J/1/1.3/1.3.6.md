# 1.3.6 Mahiḷāmukhajātaka

### Ekakanipāta

### Kuruṅgavagga

### Mahiḷāmukhajātaka

(26.)

56\. _“Purāṇacorāna vaco nisamma,_  
_Mahiḷāmukho pothayamanvacārī;_  
_Susaññatānañhi vaco nisamma,_  
_Gajuttamo sabbaguṇesu aṭṭhā”ti._  


---

57\. Mahiḷāmukhajātakaṃ chaṭṭhaṃ.

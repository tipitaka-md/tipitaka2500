# 1.12.8 Vaṭṭakajātaka

* Ekakanipāta

* Haṃcivagga

* Vaṭṭakajātaka

(118.)

261\. _“Nācintayanto puriso,_  
_visesamadhigacchati;_  
_Cintitassa phalaṃ passa,_  
_muttosmi vadhabandhanā”ti._  


---

262\. Vaṭṭakajātakaṃ aṭṭhamaṃ.

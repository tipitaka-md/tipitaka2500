# 1.8.3 Saccaṃkirajātaka

### Ekakanipāta

### Varuṇavagga

### Saccaṃkirajātaka

(73.)

161. _“Saccaṃ kirevamāhaṃsu,_  
_narā ekacciyā idha;_  
_Kaṭṭhaṃ niplavitaṃ seyyo,_  
_na tvevekacciyo naro”ti._  


===

162. Saccaṃkirajātakaṃ tatiyaṃ.

# 1.8.4 Rukkhadhammajātaka

* Ekakanipāta

* Varuṇavagga

* Rukkhadhammajātaka

(74.)

163\. _“Sādhū sambahulā ñātī,_  
_api rukkhā araññajā;_  
_Vāto vahati ekaṭṭhaṃ,_  
_brahantampi vanappatin”ti._  


---

164\. Rukkhadhammajātakaṃ catutthaṃ.

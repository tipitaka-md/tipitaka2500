# 1.8.6 Asaṃkiyajātaka

### Ekakanipāta

### Varuṇavagga

### Asaṃkiyajātaka

(76.)

167. _“Asaṅkiyomhi gāmamhi,_  
_araññe natthi me bhayaṃ;_  
_Ujuṃ maggaṃ samārūḷho,_  
_mettāya karuṇāya cā”ti._  


===

168. Asaṃkiyajātakaṃ chaṭṭhaṃ.

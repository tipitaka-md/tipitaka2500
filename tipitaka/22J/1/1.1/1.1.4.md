# 1.1.4 Cūḷaseṭṭhijātaka

### Ekakanipāta

### Apaṇṇakavagga

### Cūḷaseṭṭhijātaka

(4.)

8\. _“Appakenapi medhāvī,_  
_pābhatena vicakkhaṇo;_  
_Samuṭṭhāpeti attānaṃ,_  
_aṇuṃ aggiṃva sandhaman”ti._  


---

9\. Cūḷaseṭṭhijātakaṃ catutthaṃ.

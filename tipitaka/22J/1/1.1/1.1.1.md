# 1.1.1 Apaṇṇakajātaka

### Ekakanipāta

### Apaṇṇakavagga

### Apaṇṇakajātaka

(1.)

2. _“Apaṇṇakaṃ ṭhānameke,_  
_Dutiyaṃ āhu takkikā;_  
_Etadaññāya medhāvī,_  
_Taṃ gaṇhe yadapaṇṇakan”ti._  


===

3. Apaṇṇakajātakaṃ paṭhamaṃ.

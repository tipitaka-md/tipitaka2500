# 1.1.6 Devadhammajātaka

### Ekakanipāta

### Apaṇṇakavagga

### Devadhammajātaka

(6.)

12. _“Hiriottappasampannā,_  
_sukkadhammasamāhitā;_  
_Santo sappurisā loke,_  
_devadhammāti vuccare”ti._  


---

13. Devadhammajātakaṃ chaṭṭhaṃ.

# 1.5.8 Vedabbajātaka

* Ekakanipāta

* Atthakāmavagga

* Vedabbajātaka

(48.)

104\. _“Anupāyena yo atthaṃ,_  
_Icchati so vihaññati;_  
_Cetā haniṃsu vedabbaṃ,_  
_Sabbe te byasanamajjhagūti._  


---

105\. Vedabbajātakaṃ aṭṭhamaṃ.

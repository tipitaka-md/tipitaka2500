# 1.5.4 Makasajātaka

### Ekakanipāta

### Atthakāmavagga

### Makasajātaka

(44.)

96. _“Seyyo amitto matiyā upeto,_  
_Na tveva mitto mativippahīno;_  
_Makasaṃ vadhissanti hi eḷamūgo,_  
_Putto pitu abbhidā uttamaṅgan”ti._  


===

97. Makasajātakaṃ catutthaṃ.

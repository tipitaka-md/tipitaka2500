# 1.9.8 Sārambhajātaka

### Ekakanipāta

### Apāyimhavagga

### Sārambhajātaka

(88.)

194\. _“Kalyāṇimeva muñceyya,_  
_na hi muñceyya pāpikaṃ;_  
_Mokkho kalyāṇiyā sādhu,_  
_mutvā tappati pāpikan”ti._  


---

195\. Sārambhajātakaṃ aṭṭhamaṃ.

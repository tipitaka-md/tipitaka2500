# 1.4.6 Sakuṇajātaka

### Ekakanipāta

### Kulāvakavagga

### Sakuṇajātaka

(36.)

78. _“Yaṃ nissitā jagatiruhaṃ vihaṅgamā,_  
_Svāyaṃ aggiṃ pamuñcati;_  
_Disā bhajatha vakkaṅgā,_  
_Jātaṃ saraṇato bhayan”ti._  


===

79. Sakuṇajātakaṃ chaṭṭhaṃ.

# 1.13.6 Asilakkhaṇajātaka

### Ekakanipāta

### Kusanāḷivagga

### Asilakkhaṇajātaka

(126.)

279. _“Tadevekassa kalyāṇaṃ,_  
_tadevekassa pāpakaṃ;_  
_Tasmā sabbaṃ na kalyāṇaṃ,_  
_sabbaṃ vāpi na pāpakan”ti._  


===

280. Asilakkhaṇajātakaṃ chaṭṭhaṃ.

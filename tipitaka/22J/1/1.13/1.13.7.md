# 1.13.7 Kalaṇḍukajātaka

* Ekakanipāta

* Kusanāḷivagga

* Kalaṇḍukajātaka

(127.)

281\. _“Te desā tāni vatthūni,_  
_ahañca vanagocaro;_  
_Anuvicca kho taṃ gaṇheyyuṃ,_  
_piva khīraṃ kalaṇḍukā”ti._  


---

282\. Kalaṇḍukajātakaṃ sattamaṃ.

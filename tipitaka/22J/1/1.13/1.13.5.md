# 1.13.5 Kaṭāhakajātaka

### Ekakanipāta

### Kusanāḷivagga

### Kaṭāhakajātaka

(125.)

277. _“Bahumpi so vikattheyya,_  
_aññaṃ janapadaṃ gato;_  
_Anvāgantvāna dūseyya,_  
_bhuñja bhoge kaṭāhakā”ti._  


---

278. Kaṭāhakajātakaṃ pañcamaṃ.

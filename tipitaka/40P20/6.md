# 6 Upādānagocchaka

### Upādānagocchaka

(36.)

99. Naupādānaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca upādāno dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

100. Hetuyā nava…pe…  vipāke ekaṃ…pe…  avigate nava. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

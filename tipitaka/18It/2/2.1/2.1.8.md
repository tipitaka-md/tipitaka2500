# 2.1.8 Paṭhamajananakuhanasutta

### Dukanipāta

### Paṭhamavagga

### Paṭhamajananakuhanasutta

(35.)

214. Vuttañhetaṃ bhagavatā vuttamarahatāti me sutaṃ—

215. “Nayidaṃ, bhikkhave, brahmacariyaṃ vussati janakuhanatthaṃ, na janalapanatthaṃ, na lābhasakkārasilokānisaṃsatthaṃ, na ‘iti maṃ jano jānātū’ti. Atha kho idaṃ, bhikkhave, brahmacariyaṃ vussati saṃvaratthañceva pahānatthañcā”ti. Etamatthaṃ bhagavā avoca. Tatthetaṃ iti vuccati—

216. _“Saṃvaratthaṃ pahānatthaṃ,_  
_brahmacariyaṃ anītihaṃ;_  
_Adesayi so bhagavā,_  
_nibbānogadhagāminaṃ._  


217. _Esa maggo mahattehi,_  
_anuyāto mahesibhi;_  
_Ye ye taṃ paṭipajjanti,_  
_yathā buddhena desitaṃ;_  
_Dukkhassantaṃ karissanti,_  
_satthusāsanakārino”ti._  


218. Ayampi attho vutto bhagavatā, iti me sutanti.

===

219. Aṭṭhamaṃ.

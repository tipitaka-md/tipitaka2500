# 3.1.5 Paṭhamaesanāsutta

### Tikanipāta

### Paṭhamavagga

### Paṭhamaesanāsutta

(54.)

350. Vuttañhetaṃ bhagavatā vuttamarahatāti me sutaṃ—

351. “Tisso imā, bhikkhave, esanā. Katamā tisso? Kāmesanā, bhavesanā, brahmacariyesanā—  imā kho, bhikkhave, tisso esanā”ti. Etamatthaṃ bhagavā avoca. Tatthetaṃ iti vuccati—

352. _“Samāhito sampajāno,_  
_sato buddhassa sāvako;_  
_Esanā ca pajānāti,_  
_esanānañca sambhavaṃ._  


353. _Yattha cetā nirujjhanti,_  
_maggañca khayagāminaṃ;_  
_Esanānaṃ khayā bhikkhu,_  
_nicchāto parinibbuto”ti._  


354. Ayampi attho vutto bhagavatā, iti me sutanti.

===

355. Pañcamaṃ.

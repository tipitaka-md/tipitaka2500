# 1.1.3 Mohasutta

* Ekakanipāta

* Paṭhamavagga

* Mohasutta

(3.)

12\. Vuttañhetaṃ bhagavatā vuttamarahatāti me sutaṃ—

13\. “Ekadhammaṃ, bhikkhave, pajahatha; ahaṃ vo pāṭibhogo anāgāmitāya. Katamaṃ ekadhammaṃ? Mohaṃ, bhikkhave, ekadhammaṃ pajahatha; ahaṃ vo pāṭibhogo anāgāmitāyā”ti. Etamatthaṃ bhagavā avoca. Tatthetaṃ iti vuccati—

14\. _“Yena mohena mūḷhāse,_  
_sattā gacchanti duggatiṃ;_  
_Taṃ mohaṃ sammadaññāya,_  
_pajahanti vipassino;_  
_Pahāya na punāyanti,_  
_imaṃ lokaṃ kudācanan”ti._  


15\. Ayampi attho vutto bhagavatā, iti me sutanti.

---

16\. Tatiyaṃ.

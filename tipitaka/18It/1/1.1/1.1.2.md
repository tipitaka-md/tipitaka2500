# 1.1.2 Dosasutta

* Ekakanipāta

* Paṭhamavagga

* Dosasutta

(2.)

7\. Vuttañhetaṃ bhagavatā vuttamarahatāti me sutaṃ—

8\. “Ekadhammaṃ, bhikkhave, pajahatha; ahaṃ vo pāṭibhogo anāgāmitāya. Katamaṃ ekadhammaṃ? Dosaṃ, bhikkhave, ekadhammaṃ pajahatha; ahaṃ vo pāṭibhogo anāgāmitāyā”ti. Etamatthaṃ bhagavā avoca. Tatthetaṃ iti vuccati—

9\. _“Yena dosena duṭṭhāse,_  
_sattā gacchanti duggatiṃ;_  
_Taṃ dosaṃ sammadaññāya,_  
_pajahanti vipassino;_  
_Pahāya na punāyanti,_  
_imaṃ lokaṃ kudācanan”ti._  


10\. Ayampi attho vutto bhagavatā, iti me sutanti.

---

11\. Dutiyaṃ.

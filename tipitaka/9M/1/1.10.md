# 1.10 Mahāsatipaṭṭhānasutta

* Mūlapariyāyavagga

* Mahāsatipaṭṭhānasutta

(105.)

301\. Evaṃ me sutaṃ—  ekaṃ samayaṃ bhagavā kurūsu viharati kammāsadhammaṃ nāma kurūnaṃ nigamo. Tatra kho bhagavā bhikkhū āmantesi—  “bhikkhavo”ti. “Bhadante”ti te bhikkhū bhagavato paccassosuṃ. Bhagavā etadavoca—

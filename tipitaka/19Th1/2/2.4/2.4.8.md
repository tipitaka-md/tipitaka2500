# 2.4.8 Nisabhattheragāthā

### Dukanipāta

### Catutthavagga

### Nisabhattheragāthā

(195.)

391. _“Pañca kāmaguṇe hitvā,_  
_Piyarūpe manorame;_  
_Saddhāya gharā nikkhamma,_  
_Dukkhassantakaro bhave._  


(196.)

392. _Nābhinandāmi maraṇaṃ,_  
_nābhinandāmi jīvitaṃ;_  
_Kālañca paṭikaṅkhāmi,_  
_sampajāno patissato”ti._  


===

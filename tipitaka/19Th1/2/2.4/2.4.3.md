# 2.4.3 Upavāṇattheragāthā

### Dukanipāta

### Catutthavagga

### Upavāṇattheragāthā

(185.)

376. _“Arahaṃ sugato loke,_  
_vātehābādhiko muni;_  
_Sace uṇhodakaṃ atthi,_  
_munino dehi brāhmaṇa._  


(186.)

377. _Pūjito pūjaneyyānaṃ,_  
_sakkareyyāna sakkato;_  
_Apacitopaceyyānaṃ,_  
_tassa icchāmi hātave”ti._

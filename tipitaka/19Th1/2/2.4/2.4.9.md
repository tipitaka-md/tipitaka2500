# 2.4.9 Usabhattheragāthā

* Dukanipāta

* Catutthavagga

* Usabhattheragāthā

(197.)

394\. _“Ambapallavasaṅkāsaṃ,_  
_aṃse katvāna cīvaraṃ;_  
_Nisinno hatthigīvāyaṃ,_  
_gāmaṃ piṇḍāya pāvisiṃ._  


(198.)

395\. _Hatthikkhandhato oruyha,_  
_saṃvegaṃ alabhiṃ tadā;_  
_Sohaṃ ditto tadā santo,_  
_patto me āsavakkhayo”ti._

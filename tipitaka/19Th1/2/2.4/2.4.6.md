# 2.4.6 Nitakattheragāthā

### Dukanipāta

### Catutthavagga

### Nitakattheragāthā

(191.)

385. _“Kassa selūpamaṃ cittaṃ,_  
_ṭhitaṃ nānupakampati;_  
_Virattaṃ rajanīyesu,_  
_kuppanīye na kuppati;_  
_Yassevaṃ bhāvitaṃ cittaṃ,_  
_kuto taṃ dukkhamessati._  


(192.)

386. _Mama selūpamaṃ cittaṃ,_  
_ṭhitaṃ nānupakampati;_  
_Virattaṃ rajanīyesu,_  
_kuppanīye na kuppati;_  
_Mamevaṃ bhāvitaṃ cittaṃ,_  
_kuto maṃ dukkhamessatī”ti._

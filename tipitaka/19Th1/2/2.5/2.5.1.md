# 2.5.1 Kumārakassapattheragāthā

### Dukanipāta

### Pañcamavagga

### Kumārakassapattheragāthā

(201.)

402. _“Aho buddhā aho dhammā,_  
_aho no satthu sampadā;_  
_Yattha etādisaṃ dhammaṃ,_  
_sāvako sacchikāhiti._  


(202.)

403. _Asaṅkheyyesu kappesu,_  
_sakkāyādhigatā ahū;_  
_Tesamayaṃ pacchimako,_  
_carimoyaṃ samussayo;_  
_Jātimaraṇasaṃsāro,_  
_natthi dāni punabbhavo”ti._

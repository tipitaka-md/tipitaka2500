# 2.3.2 Bhaddajittheragāthā

### Dukanipāta

### Tatiyavagga

### Bhaddajittheragāthā

(163.)

341. _“Panādo nāma so rājā,_  
_yassa yūpo suvaṇṇayo;_  
_Tiriyaṃ soḷasubbedho,_  
_ubbhamāhu sahassadhā._  


(164.)

342. _Sahassakaṇḍo satageṇḍu,_  
_dhajālu haritāmayo;_  
_Anaccuṃ tattha gandhabbā,_  
_chasahassāni sattadhā”ti._

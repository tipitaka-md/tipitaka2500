# 2.3.3 Sobhitattheragāthā

* Dukanipāta

* Tatiyavagga

* Sobhitattheragāthā

(165.)

344\. _“Satimā paññavā bhikkhu,_  
_āraddhabalavīriyo;_  
_Pañca kappasatānāhaṃ,_  
_ekarattiṃ anussariṃ._  


(166.)

345\. _Cattāro satipaṭṭhāne,_  
_satta aṭṭha ca bhāvayaṃ;_  
_Pañca kappasatānāhaṃ,_  
_ekarattiṃ anussarin”ti._  


---

346\. …  Sobhito thero… .

# 2.2.8 Kimilattheragāthā

* Dukanipāta

* Dutiyavagga

* Kimilattheragāthā

(155.)

_sakyaputtā sahāyakā;_  
_Pahāyānappake bhoge,_  
_uñchāpattāgate ratā._  


(156.)

_Niccaṃ daḷhaparakkamā;_

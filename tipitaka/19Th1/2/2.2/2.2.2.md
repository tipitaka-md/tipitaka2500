# 2.2.2 Jotidāsattheragāthā

* Dukanipāta

* Dutiyavagga

* Jotidāsattheragāthā

(143.)

309\. _“Ye kho te veṭhamissena,_  
_nānattena ca kammunā;_  
_Manusse uparundhanti,_  
_pharusūpakkamā janā;_  
_Tepi tattheva kīranti,_  
_na hi kammaṃ panassati._  


(144.)

310\. _Yaṃ karoti naro kammaṃ,_  
_Kalyāṇaṃ yadi pāpakaṃ;_  
_Tassa tasseva dāyādo,_  
_Yaṃ yaṃ kammaṃ pakubbatī”ti._

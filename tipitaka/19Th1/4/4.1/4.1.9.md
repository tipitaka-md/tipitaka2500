# 4.1.9 Candanattheragāthā

### Catukkanipāta

### Paṭhamavagga

### Candanattheragāthā

(299.)

543. _“Jātarūpena sañchannā,_  
_dāsīgaṇapurakkhatā;_  
_Aṅkena puttamādāya,_  
_bhariyā maṃ upāgami._  


(300.)

544. _Tañca disvāna āyantiṃ,_  
_sakaputtassa mātaraṃ;_  
_Alaṅkataṃ suvasanaṃ,_  
_maccupāsaṃva oḍḍitaṃ._  


(301.)

545. _Tato me manasīkāro,_  
_yoniso udapajjatha;_  
_Ādīnavo pāturahu,_  
_nibbidā samatiṭṭhatha._  


(302.)

546. _Tato cittaṃ vimucci me,_  
_passa dhammasudhammataṃ;_  
_Tisso vijjā anuppattā,_  
_kataṃ buddhassa sāsanan”ti._  


===

547. …  Candano thero… .

# 1.2.8 Siṅgālapituttheragāthā

* Ekakanipāta

* Dutiyavagga

* Siṅgālapituttheragāthā

(18.)

42\. _“Ahu buddhassa dāyādo,_  
_bhikkhu bhesakaḷāvane;_  
_Kevalaṃ aṭṭhisaññāya,_  
_apharī pathaviṃ imaṃ;_  
_Maññehaṃ kāmarāgaṃ so,_  
_khippameva pahissatī”ti._

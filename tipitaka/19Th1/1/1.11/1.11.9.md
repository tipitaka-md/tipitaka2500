# 1.11.9 Saṃgharakkhitattheragāthā

* Ekakanipāta

* Ekādasamavagga

* Saṃgharakkhitattheragāthā

(109.)

243\. _“Na nūnāyaṃ paramahitānukampino,_  
_Rahogato anuvigaṇeti sāsanaṃ;_  
_Tathāhayaṃ viharati pākatindriyo,_  
_Migī yathā taruṇajātikā vane”ti._

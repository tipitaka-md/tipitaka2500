# 1.11.1 Belaṭṭhānikattheragāthā

### Ekakanipāta

### Ekādasamavagga

### Belaṭṭhānikattheragāthā

(101.)

227. _“Hitvā gihittaṃ anavositatto,_  
_Mukhanaṅgalī odariko kusīto;_  
_Mahāvarāhova nivāpapuṭṭho,_  
_Punappunaṃ gabbhamupeti mando”ti._

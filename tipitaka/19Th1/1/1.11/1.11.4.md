# 1.11.4 Khitakattheragāthā

### Ekakanipāta

### Ekādasamavagga

### Khitakattheragāthā

(104.)

233\. _“Lahuko vata me kāyo,_  
_Phuṭṭho ca pītisukhena vipulena;_  
_Tūlamiva eritaṃ mālutena,_  
_Pilavatīva me kāyo”ti._

# 1.11.3 Bandhurattheragāthā

### Ekakanipāta

### Ekādasamavagga

### Bandhurattheragāthā

(103.)

231. _“Nāhaṃ etena atthiko,_  
_Sukhito dhammarasena tappito;_  
_Pitvā rasaggamuttamaṃ,_  
_Na ca kāhāmi visena santhavan”ti._

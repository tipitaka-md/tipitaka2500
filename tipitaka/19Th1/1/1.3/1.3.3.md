# 1.3.3 Gosālattheragāthā

* Ekakanipāta

* Tatiyavagga

* Gosālattheragāthā

(23.)

54\. _“Ahaṃ kho veḷugumbasmiṃ,_  
_bhutvāna madhupāyasaṃ;_  
_Padakkhiṇaṃ sammasanto,_  
_khandhānaṃ udayabbayaṃ;_  
_Sānuṃ paṭigamissāmi,_  
_vivekamanubrūhayan”ti._

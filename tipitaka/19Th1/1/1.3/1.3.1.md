# 1.3.1 Nigrodhattheragāthā

### Ekakanipāta

### Tatiyavagga

### Nigrodhattheragāthā

(21.)

50. _“Nāhaṃ bhayassa bhāyāmi,_  
_Satthā no amatassa kovido;_  
_Yattha bhayaṃ nāvatiṭṭhati,_  
_Tena maggena vajanti bhikkhavo”ti._

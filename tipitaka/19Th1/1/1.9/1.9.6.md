# 1.9.6 Nāgitattheragāthā

* Ekakanipāta

* Navamavagga

* Nāgitattheragāthā

(86.)

193\. _“Ito bahiddhā puthu aññavādinaṃ,_  
_Maggo na nibbānagamo yathā ayaṃ;_  
_Itissu saṃghaṃ bhagavānusāsati,_  
_Satthā sayaṃ pāṇitaleva dassayan”ti._

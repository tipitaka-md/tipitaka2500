# 1.9.9 (Paṭhama) Devasabhattheragāthā

### Ekakanipāta

### Navamavagga

### (Paṭhama) Devasabhattheragāthā

(89.)

199\. _“Uttiṇṇā paṅkapalipā,_  
_pātālā parivajjitā;_  
_Mutto oghā ca ganthā ca,_  
_sabbe mānā visaṃhatā”ti._

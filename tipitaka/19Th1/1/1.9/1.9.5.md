# 1.9.5 Sunāgattheragāthā

### Ekakanipāta

### Navamavagga

### Sunāgattheragāthā

(85.)

191. _“Cittanimittassa kovido,_  
_Pavivekarasaṃ vijāniya;_  
_Jhāyaṃ nipako patissato,_  
_Adhigaccheyya sukhaṃ nirāmisan”ti._  


===

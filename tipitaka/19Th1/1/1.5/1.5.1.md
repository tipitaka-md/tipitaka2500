### Ekakanipāta

### Pañcamavagga

### Sirivaḍḍhattheragāthā

(41.)

94. _“Vivaramanupatanti vijjutā,_  
_Vebhārassa ca paṇḍavassa ca;_  
_Nagavivaragato ca jhāyati,_  
_Putto appaṭimassa tādino”ti._

# 1.5.6 Samiddhittheragāthā

### Ekakanipāta

### Pañcamavagga

### Samiddhittheragāthā

(46.)

105. _“Saddhāyāhaṃ pabbajito,_  
_Agārasmānagāriyaṃ;_  
_Sati paññā ca me vuḍḍhā,_  
_Cittañca susamāhitaṃ;_  
_Kāmaṃ karassu rūpāni,_  
_Neva maṃ byādhayissasī”ti._

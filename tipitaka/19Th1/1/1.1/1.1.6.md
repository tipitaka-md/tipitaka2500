# 1.1.6 Sītavaniyattheragāthā

* Ekakanipāta

* Paṭhamavagga

* Sītavaniyattheragāthā

(6.)

15\. _“Yo sītavanaṃ upagā bhikkhu,_  
_Eko santusito samāhitatto;_  
_Vijitāvī apetalomahaṃso,_  
_Rakkhaṃ kāyagatāsatiṃ dhitimā”ti._  


16\. Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā sītavaniyo thero gāthaṃ abhāsitthāti.

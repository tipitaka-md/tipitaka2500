# 1.1.4 Puṇṇattheragāthā

* Ekakanipāta

* Paṭhamavagga

* Puṇṇattheragāthā

(4.)

11\. _“Sabbhireva samāsetha,_  
_paṇḍitehatthadassibhi;_  
_Atthaṃ mahantaṃ gambhīraṃ,_  
_duddasaṃ nipuṇaṃ aṇuṃ;_  
_Dhīrā samadhigacchanti,_  
_appamattā vicakkhaṇā”ti._  


12\. Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā puṇṇo mantāṇiputto thero gāthaṃ abhāsitthāti.

# 1.1.8 Vīrattheragāthā

### Ekakanipāta

### Paṭhamavagga

### Vīrattheragāthā

(8.)

19. _“Yo duddamiyo damena danto,_  
_Vīro santusito vitiṇṇakaṅkho;_  
_Vijitāvī apetalomahaṃso,_  
_Vīro so parinibbuto ṭhitatto”ti._  


20. Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā vīro thero gāthaṃ abhāsitthāti.

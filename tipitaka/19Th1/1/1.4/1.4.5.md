### Catutthavagga

### Sāmaññakānittheragāthā

(35.)

80\. _“Sukhaṃ sukhattho labhate tadācaraṃ,_  
_Kittiñca pappoti yasassa vaḍḍhati;_  
_Yo ariyamaṭṭhaṅgikamañjasaṃ ujuṃ,_  
_Bhāveti maggaṃ amatassa pattiyā”ti._

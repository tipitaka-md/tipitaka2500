# 1.4.8 Gavampatittheragāthā

### Ekakanipāta

### Catutthavagga

### Gavampatittheragāthā

(38.)

86. _“Yo iddhiyā sarabhuṃ aṭṭhapesi,_  
_So gavampati asito anejo;_  
_Taṃ sabbasaṅgātigataṃ mahāmuniṃ,_  
_Devā namassanti bhavassa pāragun”ti._

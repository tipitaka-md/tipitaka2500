# 1.4.10 Vaḍḍhamānattheragāthā

### Ekakanipāta

### Catutthavagga

### Vaḍḍhamānattheragāthā

(40.)

90. _“Sattiyā viya omaṭṭho,_  
_ḍayhamānova matthake;_  
_Bhavarāgappahānāya,_  
_sato bhikkhu paribbaje”ti._  


===

91. …  Vaḍḍhamāno thero… .

  
Vaggo catuttho.



===

92. Tassuddānaṃ



93. _Gahvaratīriyo suppiyo,_  
_sopāko ceva posiyo;_  
_Sāmaññakāni kumāputto,_  
_kumāputtasahāyako;_  
_Gavampati tissatthero,_  
_vaḍḍhamāno mahāyasoti._

# 1.12.1 Jentattheragāthā

### Ekakanipāta

### Dvādasamavagga

### Jentattheragāthā

(111.)

249. _“Duppabbajjaṃ ve duradhivāsā gehā,_  
_Dhammo gambhīro duradhigamā bhogā;_  
_Kicchā vutti no itarītareneva,_  
_Yuttaṃ cintetuṃ satatamaniccatan”ti._

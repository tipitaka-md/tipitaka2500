# 1.12.8 Kimilattheragāthā

### Ekakanipāta

### Dvādasamavagga

### Kimilattheragāthā

(118.)

_Vayo rūpaṃ aññamiva tatheva santaṃ;_  
_Tasseva sato avippavasato,_  
_Aññasseva sarāmi attānan”ti._

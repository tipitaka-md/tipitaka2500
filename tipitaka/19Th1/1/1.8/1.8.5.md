# 1.8.5 Susāradattheragāthā

### Ekakanipāta

### Aṭṭhamavagga

### Susāradattheragāthā

(75.)

169. _“Sādhu suvihitāna dassanaṃ,_  
_Kaṅkhā chijjati buddhi vaḍḍhati;_  
_Bālampi karonti paṇḍitaṃ,_  
_Tasmā sādhu sataṃ samāgamo”ti._  


===

170. …  Susārado thero… .

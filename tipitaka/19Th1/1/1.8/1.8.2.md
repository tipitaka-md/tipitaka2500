# 1.8.2 Ātumattheragāthā

### Ekakanipāta

### Aṭṭhamavagga

### Ātumattheragāthā

(72.)

163. _“Yathā kaḷīro susu vaḍḍhitaggo,_  
_Dunnikkhamo hoti pasākhajāto;_  
_Evaṃ ahaṃ bhariyāyānitāya,_  
_Anumaññaṃ maṃ pabbajitomhi dānī”ti._

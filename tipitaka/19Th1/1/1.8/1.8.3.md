# 1.8.3 Māṇavattheragāthā

### Ekakanipāta

### Aṭṭhamavagga

### Māṇavattheragāthā

(73.)

165. _“Jiṇṇañca disvā dukhitañca byādhitaṃ,_  
_Matañca disvā gatamāyusaṅkhayaṃ;_  
_Tato ahaṃ nikkhamitūna pabbajiṃ,_  
_Pahāya kāmāni manoramānī”ti._

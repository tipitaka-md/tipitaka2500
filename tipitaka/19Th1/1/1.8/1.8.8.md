# 1.8.8 Meṇḍasirattheragāthā

### Ekakanipāta

### Aṭṭhamavagga

### Meṇḍasirattheragāthā

(78.)

175\. _“Anekajātisaṃsāraṃ,_  
_sandhāvissaṃ anibbisaṃ;_  
_Tassa me dukkhajātassa,_  
_dukkhakkhandho aparaddho”ti._

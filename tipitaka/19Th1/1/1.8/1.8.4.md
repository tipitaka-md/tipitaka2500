# 1.8.4 Suyāmanattheragāthā

### Ekakanipāta

### Aṭṭhamavagga

### Suyāmanattheragāthā

(74.)

_Thinamiddhañca bhikkhuno;_  
_Uddhaccaṃ vicikicchā ca,_  
_Sabbasova na vijjatī”ti._

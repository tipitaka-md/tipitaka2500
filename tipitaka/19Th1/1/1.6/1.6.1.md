# 1.6.1 Godhikattheragāthā

### Ekakanipāta

### Chaṭṭhavagga

### Godhikattheragāthā

(51.)

117. _“Vassati devo yathā sugītaṃ,_  
_Channā me kuṭikā sukhā nivātā;_  
_Cittaṃ susamāhitañca mayhaṃ,_  
_Atha ce patthayasi pavassa devā”ti._

# 1.6.7 Dutiyakuṭivihārittheragāthā

### Ekakanipāta

### Chaṭṭhavagga

### Dutiyakuṭivihārittheragāthā

(57.)

129\. _“Ayamāhu purāṇiyā kuṭi,_  
_Aññaṃ patthayase navaṃ kuṭiṃ;_  
_Āsaṃ kuṭiyā virājaya,_  
_Dukkhā bhikkhu puna navā kuṭī”ti._

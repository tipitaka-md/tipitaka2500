# 1.6.5 Añjanavaniyattheragāthā

### Ekakanipāta

### Chaṭṭhavagga

### Añjanavaniyattheragāthā

(55.)

125. _“Āsandiṃ kuṭikaṃ katvā,_  
_ogayha añjanaṃ vanaṃ;_  
_Tisso vijjā anuppattā,_  
_kataṃ buddhassa sāsanan”ti._  


===

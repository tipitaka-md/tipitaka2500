# 1.6.2 Subāhuttheragāthā

### Ekakanipāta

### Chaṭṭhavagga

### Subāhuttheragāthā

(52.)

119. _“Vassati devo yathā sugītaṃ,_  
_Channā me kuṭikā sukhā nivātā;_  
_Cittaṃ susamāhitañca kāye,_  
_Atha ce patthayasi pavassa devā”ti._

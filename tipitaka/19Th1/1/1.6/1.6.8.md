# 1.6.8 Ramaṇīyakuṭikattheragāthā

### Ekakanipāta

### Chaṭṭhavagga

### Ramaṇīyakuṭikattheragāthā

(58.)

131. _“Ramaṇīyā me kuṭikā,_  
_Saddhādeyyā manoramā;_  
_Na me attho kumārīhi,_  
_Yesaṃ attho tahiṃ gacchatha nāriyo”ti._

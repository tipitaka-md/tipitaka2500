# 1.6.3 Valliyattheragāthā

### Ekakanipāta

### Chaṭṭhavagga

### Valliyattheragāthā

(53.)

121. _“Vassati devo yathā sugītaṃ,_  
_Channā me kuṭikā sukhā nivātā;_  
_Tassaṃ viharāmi appamatto,_  
_Atha ce patthayasi pavassa devā”ti._  


===

122. …  Valliyo thero… .

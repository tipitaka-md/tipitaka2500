# 1.6.4 Uttiyattheragāthā

### Ekakanipāta

### Chaṭṭhavagga

### Uttiyattheragāthā

(54.)

123. _“Vassati devo yathā sugītaṃ,_  
_Channā me kuṭikā sukhā nivātā;_  
_Tassaṃ viharāmi adutiyo,_  
_Atha ce patthayasi pavassa devā”ti._  


===

124. …  Uttiyo thero… .

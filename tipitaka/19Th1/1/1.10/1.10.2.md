# 1.10.2 Vijayattheragāthā

### Ekakanipāta

### Dasamavagga

### Vijayattheragāthā

(92.)

207. _“Yassāsavā parikkhīṇā,_  
_āhāre ca anissito;_  
_Suññato animitto ca,_  
_vimokkho yassa gocaro;_  
_Ākāseva sakuntānaṃ,_  
_padaṃ tassa durannayan”ti._  


===

# 1.10.9 Uttiyattheragāthā

### Ekakanipāta

### Dasamavagga

### Uttiyattheragāthā

(99.)

221. _“Saddaṃ sutvā sati muṭṭhā,_  
_Piyaṃ nimittaṃ manasikaroto;_  
_Sārattacitto vedeti,_  
_Tañca ajjhosa tiṭṭhati;_  
_Tassa vaḍḍhanti āsavā,_  
_Saṃsāraṃ upagāmino”ti._

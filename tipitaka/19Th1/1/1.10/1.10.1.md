# 1.10.1 Paripuṇṇakattheragāthā

### Ekakanipāta

### Dasamavagga

### Paripuṇṇakattheragāthā

(91.)

205. _“Na tathā mataṃ satarasaṃ,_  
_Sudhannaṃ yaṃ mayajja paribhuttaṃ;_  
_Aparimitadassinā gotamena,_  
_Buddhena desito dhammo”ti._

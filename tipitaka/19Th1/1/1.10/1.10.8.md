# 1.10.8 Abhayattheragāthā

### Ekakanipāta

### Dasamavagga

### Abhayattheragāthā

(98.)

219. _“Rūpaṃ disvā sati muṭṭhā,_  
_Piyaṃ nimittaṃ manasikaroto;_  
_Sārattacitto vedeti,_  
_Tañca ajjhosa tiṭṭhati;_  
_Tassa vaḍḍhanti āsavā,_  
_Bhavamūlopagāmino”ti._

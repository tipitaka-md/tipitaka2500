# 1.7.2 Vajjiputtattheragāthā

### Ekakanipāta

### Sattamavagga

### Vajjiputtattheragāthā

(62.)

141\. _“Ekakā mayaṃ araññe viharāma,_  
_Apaviddhaṃva vanasmiṃ dārukaṃ;_  
_Tassa me bahukā pihayanti,_  
_Nerayikā viya saggagāminan”ti._

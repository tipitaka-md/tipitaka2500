# 1.7.7 Ekadhammasavanīyattheragāthā

### Ekakanipāta

### Sattamavagga

### Ekadhammasavanīyattheragāthā

(67.)

151. _“Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,_  
_bhavā sabbe samūhatā;_  
_Vikkhīṇo jātisaṃsāro,_  
_natthi dāni punabbhavo”ti._  


===

152. …  Ekadhammasavanīyo thero… .

# 1.7.6 Meghiyattheragāthā

### Ekakanipāta

### Sattamavagga

### Meghiyattheragāthā

(66.)

149\. _“Anusāsi mahāvīro,_  
_sabbadhammāna pāragū;_  
_Tassāhaṃ dhammaṃ sutvāna,_  
_vihāsiṃ santike sato;_  
_Tisso vijjā anuppattā,_  
_kataṃ buddhassa sāsanan”ti._

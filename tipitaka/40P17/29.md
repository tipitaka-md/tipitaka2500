# 29 Sanidassanattika, Vipākattika

### Sanidassanattika, Vipākattika

(46.)

129. Anidassanaappaṭighaṃ vipākaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca naanidassanaappaṭigho navipāko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā cha, adhipatiyā cha…pe…  aññamaññe tīṇi…pe…  avigate cha.

130. Anidassanaappaṭighaṃ vipākadhammadhammaṃ paṭicca naanidassanaappaṭigho navipākadhammadhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā cha, adhipatiyā cha…pe…  avigate cha.

131. Anidassanaappaṭighaṃ nevavipākanavipākadhammadhammaṃ paṭicca nasanidassanasappaṭigho nanevavipākanavipākadhammadhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi…pe…  avigate tīṇi.

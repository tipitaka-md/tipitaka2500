# 10 Kusalattika, Sekkhattika

### Kusalattika, Sekkhattika

(22.)

57. Kusalaṃ sekkhaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nakusalo nasekkho dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Dve mūlāni.) Hetuyā cha, adhipatiyā cha…pe…  vipāke tīṇi…pe…  avigate cha.

58. Abyākataṃ asekkhaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nakusalo naasekkho dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi, adhipatiyā tīṇi…pe…  avigate tīṇi.

59. Abyākataṃ nevasekkhanāsekkhaṃ dhammaṃ paccayā naabyākato nanevasekkhanāsekkho dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā pañca…pe…  āsevane tīṇi…pe…  vipāke tīṇi…pe…  avigate pañca.

# 77 Sanidassanattika, Upādānadukādi

### Sanidassanattika, Upādānadukādi

(126.)

304. Anidassanaappaṭighaṃ upādānaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca naanidassanaappaṭigho noupādāno dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

305. Hetuyā cha, ārammaṇe tīṇi…pe…  avigate cha.

(127.)

306. Anidassanaappaṭighaṃ kilesaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca naanidassanaappaṭigho nokileso dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

307. Hetuyā cha, ārammaṇe tīṇi…pe…  avigate cha.

# 22 Kusalattika, Cittānuparivattadukādi

### Kusalattika, Cittānuparivattadukādi

(42.)

119. Kusalaṃ cittānuparivattiṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca…  (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā terasa, ārammaṇe nava…pe…  vipāke tīṇi…pe…  avigate terasa.

120. Kusalaṃ nocittānuparivattiṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca…  (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā terasa, ārammaṇe nava…pe…  vipāke tīṇi…pe…  avigate terasa. (Ete saṃkhittā, dukattayaṃ cittadukasadisaṃ.)

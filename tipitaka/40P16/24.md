# 24 Kusalattika, Upādānadukādi

### Kusalattika, Upādānadukādi

(46.)

128. Akusalaṃ upādānaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca naakusalo noupādāno dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

129. Akusalaṃ kilesaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca naakusalo nokileso dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

130. Hetuyā pañca, ārammaṇe tīṇi…pe…  avigate pañca.

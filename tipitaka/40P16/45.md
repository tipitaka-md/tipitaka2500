# 45 Ajjhattārammaṇattika, Hetuduka

### Ajjhattārammaṇattika, Hetuduka

(87.)

221. Ajjhattārammaṇaṃ hetuṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca naajjhattārammaṇo nahetu dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā cha…pe…  purejāte āsevane dve…pe…  avigate cha.

222. Ajjhattārammaṇaṃ nahetuṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nabahiddhārammaṇo nanahetu dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā dve…pe…  avigate dve.

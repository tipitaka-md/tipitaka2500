# 12 Saññojanādichagocchaka, Kusalattika

### Saññojanādichagocchaka, Kusalattika

(22.)

60. Nasaññojanaṃ naakusalaṃ dhammaṃ paccayā saññojano akusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

61. Hetuyā tīṇi…pe…  avigate tīṇi.

(23.)

62. Naganthaṃ naakusalaṃ dhammaṃ paccayā gantho akusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

63. Hetuyā tīṇi…pe…  avigate tīṇi.

(24.)

64. Naoghaṃ naakusalaṃ dhammaṃ paccayā ogho akusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

65. Hetuyā tīṇi…pe…  avigate tīṇi.

(25.)

66. Nayogaṃ naakusalaṃ dhammaṃ paccayā yogo akusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

67. Hetuyā tīṇi…pe…  avigate tīṇi.

(26.)

68. Nanīvaraṇaṃ naakusalaṃ dhammaṃ paccayā nīvaraṇo akusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

69. Hetuyā tīṇi…pe…  avigate tīṇi.

(27.)

70. Naparāmāsaṃ naakusalaṃ dhammaṃ paccayā parāmāso akusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

71. Hetuyā tīṇi…pe…  avigate tīṇi.

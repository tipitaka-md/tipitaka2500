# 43 Rūpīduka, Sanidassanattika

### Rūpīduka, Sanidassanattika

(91.)

232\. Narūpiṃ nasanidassanasappaṭighaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca rūpī sanidassanasappaṭigho dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi, adhipatiyā tīṇi…pe…  avigate tīṇi.

233\. Narūpiṃ naanidassanasappaṭighaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca rūpī anidassanasappaṭigho dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi, adhipatiyā tīṇi…pe…  aññamaññe ekaṃ…pe…  avigate tīṇi.

234\. Naarūpiṃ naanidassanaappaṭighaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca rūpī anidassanaappaṭigho dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā ekaṃ, adhipatiyā ekaṃ…pe…  avigate ekaṃ.

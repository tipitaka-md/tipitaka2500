# 27 Hetuduka, Parittattika

### Hetuduka, Parittattika

(74.)

177. Nahetuṃ naparittaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nahetu paritto dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Nanahetuṃ naparittaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nahetu paritto dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi, adhipatiyā tīṇi…pe…  avigate tīṇi.

178. Nahetuṃ namahaggataṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca hetu mahaggato dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi…pe…  avigate tīṇi.

179. Nahetuṃ naappamāṇaṃ dhammaṃ paccayā hetu appamāṇo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi…pe…  avigate tīṇi.

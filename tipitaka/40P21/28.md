# 28 Hetuduka, Parittārammaṇattika

* Hetuduka, Parittārammaṇattika

(75.)

180\. Nahetuṃ naparittārammaṇaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca hetu parittārammaṇo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi…pe…  avigate tīṇi.

181\. Nahetuṃ namahaggatārammaṇaṃ dhammaṃ paccayā hetu mahaggatārammaṇo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi…pe…  avigate tīṇi.

182\. Nahetuṃ naappamāṇārammaṇaṃ dhammaṃ paccayā hetu appamāṇārammaṇo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi…pe…  avigate tīṇi.

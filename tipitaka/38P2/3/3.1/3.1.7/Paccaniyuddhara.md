# Paccanīyuddhāra

### Āsavagocchaka

### Āsavaduka

### Pañhāvāra

### Paccanīyuddhāra

(41.)

1073. Āsavo dhammo āsavassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo…  sahajātapaccayena paccayo…  upanissayapaccayena paccayo. (1)

1074. Āsavo dhammo noāsavassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo…  sahajātapaccayena paccayo…  upanissayapaccayena paccayo…  pacchājātapaccayena paccayo. (2)

1075. Āsavo dhammo āsavassa ca noāsavassa ca dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo…  sahajātapaccayena paccayo…  upanissayapaccayena paccayo. (3)

(42.)

1076. Noāsavo dhammo noāsavassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo…  sahajātapaccayena paccayo…  upanissayapaccayena paccayo…  purejātapaccayena paccayo…  pacchājātapaccayena paccayo…  kammapaccayena paccayo…  āhārapaccayena paccayo…  indriyapaccayena paccayo. (1)

1077. Noāsavo dhammo āsavassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo…  sahajātapaccayena paccayo…  upanissayapaccayena paccayo…  purejātapaccayena paccayo. (2)

1078. Noāsavo dhammo āsavassa ca noāsavassa ca dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo…  sahajātapaccayena paccayo…  upanissayapaccayena paccayo…  purejātapaccayena paccayo. (3)

(43.)

1079. Āsavo ca noāsavo ca dhammā āsavassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo…  sahajātapaccayena paccayo…  upanissayapaccayena paccayo. (1)

1080. Āsavo ca noāsavo ca dhammā noāsavassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo…  sahajātapaccayena paccayo…  upanissayapaccayena paccayo…  pacchājātapaccayena paccayo. (2)

1081. Āsavo ca noāsavo ca dhammā āsavassa ca noāsavassa ca dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo…  sahajātapaccayena paccayo…  upanissayapaccayena paccayo. (3)

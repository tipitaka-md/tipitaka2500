# 3.1.5.1--4 Paccayānulomādi

### Āsavagocchaka

### Āsavaduka

### Saṃsaṭṭhavāra

### Paccayānulomādi

(15.)

1005. Āsavaṃ dhammaṃ saṃsaṭṭho āsavo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā—  kāmāsavaṃ saṃsaṭṭho diṭṭhāsavo avijjāsavo. (Cakkaṃ. Saṃkhittaṃ.)

1006. Hetuyā nava, ārammaṇe nava (sabbattha nava), vipāke ekaṃ…pe…  avigate nava.

1007. Nahetuyā dve, naadhipatiyā nava, napurejāte nava, napacchājāte nava, naāsevane nava, nakamme tīṇi, navipāke nava, najhāne ekaṃ, namagge ekaṃ, navippayutte nava.

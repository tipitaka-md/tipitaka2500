2156\. Noparāmāso dhammo noparāmāsassa dhammassa hetupaccayena paccayo—  noparāmāsā hetū sampayuttakānaṃ khandhānaṃ cittasamuṭṭhānānañca rūpānaṃ hetupaccayena paccayo; paṭisandhikkhaṇe…pe… . (1)

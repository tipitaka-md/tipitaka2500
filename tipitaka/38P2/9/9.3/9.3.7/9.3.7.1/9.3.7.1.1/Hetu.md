2245. Parāmāsasampayutto dhammo parāmāsasampayuttassa dhammassa hetupaccayena paccayo—  parāmāsasampayuttā hetū sampayuttakānaṃ khandhānaṃ hetupaccayena paccayo. (Mūlaṃ kātabbaṃ.)

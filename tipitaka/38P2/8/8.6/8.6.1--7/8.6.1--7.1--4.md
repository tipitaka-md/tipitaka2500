# 8.6.1--7.1--4 Paccayānulomādi

### Nīvaraṇagocchaka

### Nīvaraṇavippayuttanīvaraṇiyaduka

### Paṭiccādivāra

### Paccayānulomādi

(111.)

2125. Nīvaraṇavippayuttaṃ nīvaraṇiyaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nīvaraṇavippayutto nīvaraṇiyo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā—  nīvaraṇavippayuttaṃ nīvaraṇiyaṃ ekaṃ khandhaṃ paṭicca tayo khandhā cittasamuṭṭhānañca rūpaṃ…pe…  dve khandhe…pe…  paṭisandhikkhaṇe…pe…  (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

2126. (Yathā cūḷantaraduke lokiyadukaṃ, evaṃ kātabbaṃ ninnānākaraṇaṃ.)

===

2127. Nīvaraṇavippayuttanīvaraṇiyadukaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.



===

2128. Nīvaraṇagocchakaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.

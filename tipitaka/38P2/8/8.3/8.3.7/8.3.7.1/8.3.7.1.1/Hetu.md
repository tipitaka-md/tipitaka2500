(66.)

2030. Nīvaraṇasampayutto dhammo nīvaraṇasampayuttassa dhammassa hetupaccayena paccayo—  nīvaraṇasampayuttā hetū sampayuttakānaṃ khandhānaṃ hetupaccayena paccayo. (Mūlaṃ pucchitabbaṃ.)

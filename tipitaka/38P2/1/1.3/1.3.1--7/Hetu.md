### Paṭiccādivāra

### Hetu

(126.)

349\. Hetusampayuttaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca hetusampayutto dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā—  hetusampayuttaṃ ekaṃ khandhaṃ paṭicca tayo khandhā…pe…  dve khandhe…pe…  paṭisandhikkhaṇe…pe… . (1)

350\. Hetusampayuttaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca hetuvippayutto dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā—  hetusampayutte khandhe paṭicca cittasamuṭṭhānaṃ rūpaṃ; paṭisandhikkhaṇe…pe… . (2)

351\. (Iminā kāraṇena vitthāretabbaṃ yathā sahetukadukaṃ ninnānākaraṇaṃ.)

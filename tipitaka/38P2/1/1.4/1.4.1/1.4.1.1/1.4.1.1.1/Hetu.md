### Hetu

(127.)

353\. Hetuñceva sahetukañca dhammaṃ paṭicca hetu ceva sahetuko ca dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā—  alobhaṃ paṭicca adoso amoho (cakkaṃ). Lobhaṃ paṭicca moho (cakkaṃ); paṭisandhikkhaṇe alobhaṃ paṭicca adoso amoho. (Cakkaṃ.) (1)

354\. Hetuñceva sahetukañca dhammaṃ paṭicca sahetuko ceva na ca hetu dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā—  hetuṃ paṭicca sampayuttakā khandhā; paṭisandhikkhaṇe…pe… . (2)

355\. Hetuñceva sahetukañca dhammaṃ paṭicca hetu ceva sahetuko ca sahetuko ceva na ca hetu ca dhammā uppajjanti hetupaccayā—  alobhaṃ paṭicca adoso amoho sampayuttakā ca khandhā (cakkaṃ). Lobhaṃ paṭicca moho sampayuttakā ca khandhā (cakkaṃ); paṭisandhikkhaṇe…pe… . (3)

(128.)

356\. Sahetukañceva na ca hetuṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca sahetuko ceva na ca hetu dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā—  sahetukañceva na ca hetuṃ ekaṃ khandhaṃ paṭicca tayo khandhā…pe…  dve khandhe…pe…  paṭisandhikkhaṇe…pe… . (1)

357\. Sahetukañceva na ca hetuṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca hetu ceva sahetuko ca dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā—  sahetuke ceva na ca hetū khandhe paṭicca hetū; paṭisandhikkhaṇe…pe… . (2)

358\. Sahetukañceva na ca hetuṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca hetu ceva sahetuko ca sahetuko ceva na ca hetu ca dhammā uppajjanti hetupaccayā—  sahetukañceva na ca hetuṃ ekaṃ khandhaṃ paṭicca tayo khandhā hetu ca…pe…  dve khandhe…pe…  paṭisandhikkhaṇe…pe… . (3)

(129.)

359\. Hetuñceva sahetukañca sahetukañceva na ca hetuñca dhammaṃ paṭicca hetu ceva sahetuko ca dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā—  alobhañca sampayuttake ca khandhe paṭicca adoso amoho (cakkaṃ). Lobhañca sampayuttake ca khandhe paṭicca moho (cakkaṃ); paṭisandhikkhaṇe…pe… . (1)

360\. Hetuñceva sahetukañca sahetukañceva na ca hetuñca dhammaṃ paṭicca sahetuko ceva na ca hetu dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā—  sahetukañceva na ca hetuṃ ekaṃ khandhañca hetuñca paṭicca tayo khandhā…pe…  dve khandhe…pe…  paṭisandhikkhaṇe…pe… . (2)

361\. Hetuñceva sahetukañca sahetukañceva na ca hetuñca dhammaṃ paṭicca hetu ceva sahetuko ca sahetuko ceva na ca hetu ca dhammā uppajjanti hetupaccayā—  sahetukañceva na ca hetuṃ ekaṃ khandhañca alobhañca paṭicca tayo khandhā adoso amoho ca…pe…  dve khandhe…pe…  paṭisandhikkhaṇe…pe… . (3)

362\. (Saṃkhittaṃ. Evaṃ vitthāretabbaṃ.)

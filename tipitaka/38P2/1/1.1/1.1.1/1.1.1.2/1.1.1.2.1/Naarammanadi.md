16. Hetuṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nahetu dhammo uppajjati naārammaṇapaccayā—  hetuṃ paṭicca cittasamuṭṭhānaṃ rūpaṃ; paṭisandhikkhaṇe…pe… . (1)

### Paccanīyuddhāra

(93.)

4046. Kileso ceva saṃkiliṭṭho ca dhammo kilesassa ceva saṃkiliṭṭhassa ca dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo…  sahajātapaccayena paccayo…  upanissayapaccayena paccayo. (Navapi, tīṇiyeva padā kātabbā.)

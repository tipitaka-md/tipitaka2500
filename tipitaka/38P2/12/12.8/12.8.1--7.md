# 12.8.1--7 Paṭiccādivāra

### Kilesagocchaka

### Kilesavippayuttasaṃkilesikaduka

### Paṭiccādivāra

(98.)

4053. Kilesavippayuttaṃ saṃkilesikaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca kilesavippayutto saṃkilesiko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā—  kilesavippayuttaṃ saṃkilesikaṃ ekaṃ khandhaṃ paṭicca tayo khandhā cittasamuṭṭhānañca rūpaṃ…pe…  dve khandhe…pe… .

===

4054. (Yathā lokiyadukaṃ, evaṃ ninnānākaraṇaṃ.)

  
Kilesavippayuttasaṃkilesikadukaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.



===

4055. Kilesagocchakaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.

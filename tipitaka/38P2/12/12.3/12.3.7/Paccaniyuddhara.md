# Paccanīyuddhāra

### Kilesagocchaka

### Saṃkiliṭṭhaduka

### Pañhāvāra

### Paccanīyuddhāra

(68.)

3989. Saṃkiliṭṭho dhammo saṃkiliṭṭhassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo…  sahajātapaccayena paccayo…  upanissayapaccayena paccayo. (1)

3990. Saṃkiliṭṭho dhammo asaṃkiliṭṭhassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo…  sahajātapaccayena paccayo…  upanissayapaccayena paccayo…  pacchājātapaccayena paccayo…  kammapaccayena paccayo. (2)

3991. Saṃkiliṭṭho dhammo saṃkiliṭṭhassa ca asaṃkiliṭṭhassa ca dhammassa sahajātapaccayena paccayo. (3)

(69.)

3992. Asaṃkiliṭṭho dhammo asaṃkiliṭṭhassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo…  sahajātapaccayena paccayo…  upanissayapaccayena paccayo…  purejātapaccayena paccayo…  pacchājātapaccayena paccayo…  kammapaccayena paccayo…  āhārapaccayena paccayo…  indriyapaccayena paccayo. (1)

3993. Asaṃkiliṭṭho dhammo saṃkiliṭṭhassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo…  upanissayapaccayena paccayo…  purejātapaccayena paccayo. (2)

3994. Saṃkiliṭṭho ca asaṃkiliṭṭho ca dhammā saṃkiliṭṭhassa dhammassa sahajātaṃ…  purejātaṃ. (1)

3995. Saṃkiliṭṭho ca asaṃkiliṭṭho ca dhammā asaṃkiliṭṭhassa dhammassa sahajātaṃ…  pacchājātaṃ…  āhāraṃ…  indriyaṃ. (2)

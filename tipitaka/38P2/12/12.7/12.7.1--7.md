# 12.7.1--7 Paṭiccādivāra

### Kilesagocchaka

### Kilesakilesasampayuttaduka

### Paṭiccādivāra

(97.)

4051. Kilesañceva kilesasampayuttañca dhammaṃ paṭicca kileso ceva kilesasampayutto ca dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā—  lobhaṃ paṭicca moho diṭṭhi thinaṃ uddhaccaṃ ahirikaṃ anottappaṃ. (Kilesasaṃkiliṭṭhadukasadisaṃ ninnānākaraṇaṃ, sabbe vārā.)

===

4052. Kilesakilesasampayuttadukaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.

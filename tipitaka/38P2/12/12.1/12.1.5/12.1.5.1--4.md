# 12.1.5.1--4 Paccayānulomādi

### Kilesagocchaka

### Kilesaduka

### Saṃsaṭṭhavāra

### Paccayānulomādi

(15.)

3849. Kilesaṃ dhammaṃ saṃsaṭṭho kileso dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā—  lobhaṃ saṃsaṭṭho moho diṭṭhi thinaṃ uddhaccaṃ ahirikaṃ anottappaṃ. (Cakkaṃ. Evaṃ nava pañhā kātabbā.)

3850. Hetuyā nava, ārammaṇe nava (sabbattha nava), vipāke ekaṃ…pe…  avigate nava.

===

3851. Anulomaṃ.



3852. Kilesaṃ dhammaṃ saṃsaṭṭho kileso dhammo uppajjati nahetupaccayā (evaṃ nahetupañhā cattāri kātabbā.)

3853. Nahetuyā cattāri, naadhipatiyā nava, napurejāte nava, napacchājāte nava, naāsevane nava, nakamme tīṇi, navipāke nava, najhāne ekaṃ, namagge ekaṃ, navippayutte nava.

===

3854. Paccanīyaṃ.

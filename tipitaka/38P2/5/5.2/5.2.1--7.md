# 5.2.1--7 Vārasattaka

* Ganthaniyaduka

* Vārasattaka

(44.)

1716\. Ganthaniyaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca ganthaniyo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā—  ganthaniyaṃ ekaṃ khandhaṃ. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

1717\. (Yathā cūḷantaraduke lokiyadukaṃ, evaṃ vibhajitabbaṃ ninnānākaraṇaṃ.)

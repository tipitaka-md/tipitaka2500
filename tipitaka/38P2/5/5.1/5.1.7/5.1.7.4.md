* Ganthagocchaka

* Ganthaduka

* Pañhāvāra

* Paccayapaccanīyānuloma

(43.)

1714\. Nahetupaccayā ārammaṇe nava, adhipatiyā nava (anulomapadāni paripuṇṇāni kātabbāni), avigate nava.

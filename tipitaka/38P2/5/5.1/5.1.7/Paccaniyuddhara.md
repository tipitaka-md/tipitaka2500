# Paccanīyuddhāra

* Ganthagocchaka

* Ganthaduka

* Pañhāvāra

* Paccanīyuddhāra

(38.)

1703\. Gantho dhammo ganthassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo…  sahajātapaccayena paccayo…  upanissayapaccayena paccayo. (1)

1704\. Gantho dhammo noganthassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo…  sahajātapaccayena paccayo…  upanissayapaccayena paccayo…  pacchājātapaccayena paccayo. (2)

1705\. Gantho dhammo ganthassa ca noganthassa ca dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo…  sahajātapaccayena paccayo…  upanissayapaccayena paccayo. (3)

(39.)

1706\. Nogantho dhammo noganthassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo…  sahajātapaccayena paccayo…  upanissayapaccayena paccayo…  purejātapaccayena paccayo…  pacchājātapaccayena paccayo…  kammapaccayena paccayo…  āhārapaccayena paccayo…  indriyapaccayena paccayo. (1)

1707\. Nogantho dhammo ganthassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo…  sahajātapaccayena paccayo…  upanissayapaccayena paccayo…  purejātapaccayena paccayo. (2)

1708\. Nogantho dhammo ganthassa ca noganthassa ca dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo…  sahajātapaccayena paccayo…  upanissayapaccayena paccayo…  purejātapaccayena paccayo. (3)

(40.)

1709\. Gantho ca nogantho ca dhammā ganthassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo…  sahajātapaccayena paccayo…  upanissayapaccayena paccayo. (1)

1710\. Gantho ca nogantho ca dhammā noganthassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo…  sahajātapaccayena paccayo…  upanissayapaccayena paccayo…  pacchājātapaccayena paccayo. (2)

1711\. Gantho ca nogantho ca dhammā ganthassa ca noganthassa ca dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo…  sahajātapaccayena paccayo…  upanissayapaccayena paccayo. (3)

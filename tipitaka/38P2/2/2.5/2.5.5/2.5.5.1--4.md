### Paccayānulomādi

(110.)

798. Arūpiṃ dhammaṃ saṃsaṭṭho arūpī dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā—  arūpiṃ ekaṃ khandhaṃ saṃsaṭṭhā tayo khandhā…pe…  dve khandhe…pe…  paṭisandhikkhaṇe…pe… .

799. Hetuyā ekaṃ…pe…  avigate ekaṃ. (Evaṃ paccanīyādīni gaṇanāpi sampayuttavārepi sabbe kātabbā. Ekoyeva pañho.)

# 4.2.1--7 Paṭiccādivāra

### Saññojanagocchaka

### Saññojaniyaduka

### Paṭiccādivāra

(39.)

1416. Saññojaniyaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca saññojaniyo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā—  saññojaniyaṃ ekaṃ khandhaṃ paṭicca tayo khandhā cittasamuṭṭhānañca rūpaṃ…pe…  dve khandhe…pe…  paṭisandhikkhaṇe…pe…  khandhe paṭicca vatthu, vatthuṃ paṭicca khandhā, ekaṃ mahābhūtaṃ…pe… .

1417. (Cūḷantaraduke lokiyadukasadisaṃ, ninnānākaraṇaṃ.)

---

1418. Saññojaniyadukaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.

(109.)

1598. Saññojano ceva saññojanasampayutto ca dhammo saññojanassa ceva saññojanasampayuttassa ca dhammassa anantarapaccayena paccayo…  nava (ninnānākaraṇaṃ, vibhajanā natthi.

# 13.17.1--7 Paṭiccādivāra

### Piṭṭhiduka

### Sauttaraduka

### Paṭiccādivāra

(345.)

4791. Sauttaraṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca sauttaro dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā—  sauttaraṃ ekaṃ khandhaṃ paṭicca tayo khandhā cittasamuṭṭhānañca rūpaṃ…pe…  dve khandhe…pe…  paṭisandhikkhaṇe…pe… . (Yāva ajjhattikā mahābhūtā. Yathā cūḷantaraduke lokiyadukasadisaṃ ninnānākaraṇaṃ.)

===

4792. Sauttaradukaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.

# 13.4.1 Paṭiccādivāra

### Piṭṭhiduka

### Bhāvanāyapahātabbahetukaduka

### Paṭiccādivāra

(93.)

4266. Bhāvanāya pahātabbahetukaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca bhāvanāya pahātabbahetuko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā—  bhāvanāya pahātabbahetukaṃ ekaṃ khandhaṃ paṭicca tayo khandhā…pe…  dve khandhe…pe… .

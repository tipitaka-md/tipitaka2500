# 13.15.5.1--4 Paccayānulomādi

* Piṭṭhiduka

* Niyyānikaduka

* Saṃsaṭṭhavāra

* Paccayānulomādi

(312.)

4737\. Niyyānikaṃ dhammaṃ saṃsaṭṭho niyyāniko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā—  niyyānikaṃ ekaṃ khandhaṃ saṃsaṭṭhā tayo khandhā…pe…  dve khandhe…pe… . (1)

4738\. Aniyyānikaṃ dhammaṃ saṃsaṭṭho aniyyāniko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā—  aniyyānikaṃ ekaṃ khandhaṃ saṃsaṭṭhā tayo khandhā…pe…  dve khandhe…pe…  paṭisandhikkhaṇe…pe… . (1)

4739\. Hetuyā dve, ārammaṇe dve (sabbattha dve), vipāke ekaṃ…pe…  avigate dve. (Anulomaṃ.)

4740\. Nahetuyā ekaṃ, naadhipatiyā dve, napurejāte dve, napacchājāte dve, naāsevane ekaṃ, nakamme dve, navipāke dve, najhāne ekaṃ, namagge ekaṃ, navippayutte dve. (Paccanīyaṃ.)

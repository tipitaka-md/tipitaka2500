# Ārammaṇa

### Piṭṭhiduka

### Dassanenapahātabbahetukaduka

### Saṃsaṭṭhavāra

### Paccayacatukka

### Ārammaṇa

(65.)

4211. Dassanena pahātabbahetukaṃ dhammaṃ saṃsaṭṭho dassanena pahātabbahetuko dhammo uppajjati ārammaṇapaccayā…  tīṇi. (Paṭiccasadisā.)

4212. Nadassanena pahātabbahetukaṃ dhammaṃ saṃsaṭṭho nadassanena pahātabbahetuko dhammo uppajjati ārammaṇapaccayā—  nadassanena pahātabbahetukaṃ ekaṃ khandhaṃ saṃsaṭṭhā tayo khandhā…pe…  dve khandhe…pe…  paṭisandhikkhaṇe…pe… . (1)

4213. Nadassanena pahātabbahetukaṃ dhammaṃ saṃsaṭṭho dassanena pahātabbahetuko dhammo uppajjati ārammaṇapaccayā. (Paṭiccasadisaṃ.) (2)

4214. Dassanena pahātabbahetukañca nadassanena pahātabbahetukañca dhammaṃ saṃsaṭṭho dassanena pahātabbahetuko dhammo uppajjati ārammaṇapaccayā. (Paṭiccasadisaṃ, saṃkhittaṃ.) (1)

(66.)

4215. Hetuyā cattāri, ārammaṇe cha, adhipatiyā dve, anantare cha (sabbattha cha), vipāke ekaṃ…pe…  avigate cha.

===

4216. Anulomaṃ.

### Pañhāvāra

### Paccayānulomapaccanīya

(142.)

2667. Hetupaccayā naārammaṇe tīṇi, naadhipatiyā tīṇi, naanantare tīṇi, nasamanantare tīṇi, naaññamaññe ekaṃ, naupanissaye tīṇi (sabbattha tīṇi), namagge tīṇi, nasampayutte ekaṃ, navippayutte tīṇi, nonatthiyā tīṇi, novigate tīṇi.

### Paccayānulomapaccanīya

(253.)

2941. Hetupaccayā naārammaṇe nava (sabbattha nava), nakamme tīṇi, navipāke nava, nasampayutte nava, navippayutte pañca, nonatthiyā nava, novigate nava.

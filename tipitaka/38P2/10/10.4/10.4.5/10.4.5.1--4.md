# 10.4.5.1--4 Paccayānulomādi

* Mahantaraduka

* Cittasampayuttaduka

* Saṃsaṭṭhavāra

* Paccayānulomādi

(162.)

2712\. Cittasampayuttaṃ dhammaṃ saṃsaṭṭho cittasampayutto dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā—  cittasampayuttaṃ ekaṃ khandhaṃ saṃsaṭṭhā dve khandhā, dve khandhe…pe…  paṭisandhikkhaṇe…pe… .

2713\. Hetuyā ekaṃ, ārammaṇe ekaṃ, adhipatiyā ekaṃ (sabbattha ekaṃ), avigate ekaṃ.

2714\. Nahetuyā ekaṃ, naadhipatiyā ekaṃ, napurejāte ekaṃ, napacchājāte ekaṃ, naāsevane ekaṃ, nakamme ekaṃ, navipāke ekaṃ, najhāne ekaṃ, namagge ekaṃ, navippayutte ekaṃ.

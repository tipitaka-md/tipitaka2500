(384.)

3283. Hetupaccayā naārammaṇe tīṇi, naadhipatiyā pañca…pe…  nakamme ekaṃ, navipāke tīṇi…pe…  nasampayutte tīṇi, navippayutte ekaṃ, nonatthiyā tīṇi, novigate tīṇi.

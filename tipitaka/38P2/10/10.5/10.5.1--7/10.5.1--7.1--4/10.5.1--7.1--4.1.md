# 10.5.1--7.1--4.1 Vibhaṅgavāra

* Mahantaraduka

* Cittasaṃsaṭṭhaduka

* Paṭiccādivāra

* Paccayānulomādi

* Vibhaṅgavāra

(185.)

2763\. Cittasaṃsaṭṭhaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca cittasaṃsaṭṭho dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā—  cittasaṃsaṭṭhaṃ ekaṃ khandhaṃ paṭicca dve khandhā, dve khandhe…pe…  paṭisandhikkhaṇe…pe… . (1)

2764\. Cittasaṃsaṭṭhaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca cittavisaṃsaṭṭho dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā—  cittasaṃsaṭṭhe khandhe paṭicca cittasamuṭṭhānaṃ rūpaṃ; paṭisandhikkhaṇe…pe… . (2)

2765\. (Cittasaṃsaṭṭhadukaṃ yathā cittasampayuttadukaṃ evaṃ kātabbaṃ, ninnānākaraṇaṃ.)

# Paccanīyuddhāra

* Mahantaraduka

* Upādinnaduka

* Pañhāvāra

* Paccanīyuddhāra

(467.)

3513\. Upādinno dhammo upādinnassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo…  sahajātapaccayena paccayo…  upanissayapaccayena paccayo…  purejātapaccayena paccayo…  pacchājātapaccayena paccayo…  āhārapaccayena paccayo…  indriyapaccayena paccayo. (1)

3514\. Upādinno dhammo anupādinnassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo…  sahajātapaccayena paccayo…  upanissayapaccayena paccayo…  purejātapaccayena paccayo…  pacchājātapaccayena paccayo…  āhārapaccayena paccayo. (2)

3515\. Upādinno dhammo upādinnassa ca anupādinnassa ca dhammassa sahajātapaccayena paccayo…  pacchājātapaccayena paccayo…  āhārapaccayena paccayo. (3)

(468.)

3516\. Anupādinno dhammo anupādinnassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo…  sahajātapaccayena paccayo…  upanissayapaccayena paccayo…  pacchājātapaccayena paccayo…  āhārapaccayena paccayo. (1)

3517\. Anupādinno dhammo upādinnassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo…  upanissayapaccayena paccayo…  pacchājātapaccayena paccayo…  kammapaccayena paccayo…  āhārapaccayena paccayo. (2)

3518\. Anupādinno dhammo upādinnassa ca anupādinnassa ca dhammassa pacchājātapaccayena paccayo…  āhārapaccayena paccayo. (3)

3519\. Upādinno ca anupādinno ca dhammā upādinnassa dhammassa purejātaṃ, pacchājātaṃ, āhāraṃ, indriyaṃ. (1)

3520\. Upādinno ca anupādinno ca dhammā anupādinnassa dhammassa sahajātaṃ, purejātaṃ, pacchājātaṃ, āhāraṃ. (2)

3521\. Upādinno ca anupādinno ca dhammā upādinnassa ca anupādinnassa ca dhammassa āhārapaccayena paccayo. (3)

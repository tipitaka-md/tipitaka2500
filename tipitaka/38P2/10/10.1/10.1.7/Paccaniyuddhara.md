# Paccanīyuddhāra

* Mahantaraduka

* Sārammaṇaduka

* Pañhāvāra

* Paccanīyuddhāra

(45.)

2411\. Sārammaṇo dhammo sārammaṇassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo…  sahajātapaccayena paccayo…  upanissayapaccayena paccayo…  kammapaccayena paccayo. (1)

2412\. Sārammaṇo dhammo anārammaṇassa dhammassa sahajātapaccayena paccayo…  pacchājātapaccayena paccayo…  kammapaccayena paccayo. (2)

2413\. Sārammaṇo dhammo sārammaṇassa ca anārammaṇassa ca dhammassa sahajātapaccayena paccayo…  kammapaccayena paccayo. (3)

(46.)

2414\. Anārammaṇo dhammo anārammaṇassa dhammassa sahajātapaccayena paccayo…  āhārapaccayena paccayo…  indriyapaccayena paccayo. (1)

2415\. Anārammaṇo dhammo sārammaṇassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo…  sahajātapaccayena paccayo…  upanissayapaccayena paccayo…  purejātapaccayena paccayo. (2)

2416\. Sārammaṇo ca anārammaṇo ca dhammā sārammaṇassa dhammassa sahajātaṃ, purejātaṃ. (1)

2417\. Sārammaṇo ca anārammaṇo ca dhammā anārammaṇassa dhammassa sahajātaṃ, pacchājātaṃ, āhāraṃ, indriyaṃ. (2)

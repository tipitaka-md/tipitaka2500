2375. Sārammaṇo dhammo sārammaṇassa dhammassa hetupaccayena paccayo—  sārammaṇā hetū sampayuttakānaṃ khandhānaṃ hetupaccayena paccayo; paṭisandhikkhaṇe…pe… . (1)

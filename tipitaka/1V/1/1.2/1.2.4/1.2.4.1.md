# 1.2.4.1 Vinītavatthuuddānagāthā

### Mahāvibhaṅga

### Saṃghādisesakaṇḍa

### Attakāmapāricariyasikkhāpada

### Vinītavatthuuddānagāthā

1211. _Kathaṃ vañjhā labhe puttaṃ,_  
_Piyā ca subhagā siyaṃ;_  
_Kiṃ dajjaṃ kenupaṭṭheyyaṃ,_  
_Kathaṃ gaccheyyaṃ suggatinti._

# 2.1.1.13 Niruddhanirujjhamānavāra

### Niddesa

### Suddhacittasāmañña

### Puggalavāra

### Niruddhanirujjhamānavāra

(82.)

366. »  Niruddhaṃ nirujjhamānanti? No.

367. «  Nirujjhamānaṃ niruddhanti? No.

368. »  Na niruddhaṃ na nirujjhamānanti?

369. Bhaṅgakkhaṇe na niruddhaṃ, no ca na nirujjhamānaṃ. Uppādakkhaṇe anāgatañca cittaṃ na ceva niruddhaṃ na ca nirujjhamānaṃ.

370. «  Na nirujjhamānaṃ na niruddhanti?

371. Atītaṃ cittaṃ na nirujjhamānaṃ, no ca na niruddhaṃ. Uppādakkhaṇe anāgatañca cittaṃ na ceva nirujjhamānaṃ na ca niruddhaṃ.

# 1.3 Abhidhammacittamissakavisesa

* Uddesa

* Abhidhammacittamissakavisesa

(62.)

255\. »  Yassa kusalaṃ cittaṃ uppajjati…pe…  yassa akusalaṃ cittaṃ uppajjati…  yassa abyākataṃ cittaṃ uppajjati…  yassa sukhāya vedanāya sampayuttaṃ cittaṃ uppajjati… ?

256\. (Etena upāyena yāva saraṇaaraṇā uddharitabbā.)

257\. »  Yassa araṇaṃ cittaṃ uppajjati na nirujjhati tassa araṇaṃ cittaṃ nirujjhissati na uppajjissati?

258\. «  Yassa vā pana araṇaṃ cittaṃ nirujjhissati na uppajjissati tassa araṇaṃ cittaṃ uppajjati na nirujjhati?
